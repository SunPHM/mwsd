Summer Courses for Youngsters

for Young Adults 5-16 Years

International Summer camp with Spanish Language Courses, Activities and Sports.

The programme is run in the 3-star hotel Conde de Pallars (Lérida-Spain), in a complex surrounded by mountains and rivers making it an ideal location to enjoy nature, practice all kind of sports, make friends from all over the world and learn Spanish. The hotel has excellent facilities, all of them at the disposal of our students. Double, triples and quadruple bedrooms with on-suite facilities. Activities room, video / TV room, private disco, cinema, games room, Tennis, fronton and basketball courts, indoor and outdoor swimming pool, mini golf, gym and football, volleyball, badminton, cricket and baseball fields. All the meals respond to the necessary protein and vitamin balance for the development of all the activities, according to a healthy diet. The meals will be offered at a free buffet, where the students will choose and repeat whatever they want to. Prices Includes: Full board accommodation, 15h. of Spanish or English lessons and tuition material, activities, sports, adventure, excursions, parties, medical service, supervision and laundry.

There is a qualified medical staff 24h. a day during the stay in the camp.
Furthermore, in the extraordinary case it was needed, the nearest hospital is only 4 Km away, in Sort.

Football, tennis, squash, basketball, volleyball, ping-pong, swimming,... can be practiced every day.
