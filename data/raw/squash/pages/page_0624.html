A review of a couple of the Powerplay Sports new range of squash rackets.

GX5 130 GRAMS

A powerfull racket with an extended sweet spot. It has an even weight distribution and a good balance. A good all round racket for plyers who prefer a lighter weight. A stiff frame allows no vibration when attacking hard.

A very competative price at £85.00

A responsive and powerfull racket providing excellent touch which gives the attacking player the edge when using that all important head speed and whip to kill the ball. A nice wieght and a graphite and titanium frame make this a good choice for the team player A very reasonable price at £80.00
