Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients:

1 butternut squash
1 carrot (cut med.)
2 med. size potatoes (peeled & cut med. size)
5 Red Peppers
1 large onion (cut med.)
2 celery sticks (cut med.)
5 gloves garlic
2 bay leaves
2 liters of chicken stock ( or water & powdered chicken base)
1/4 tbsp. oregano, thyme
1/2 liter whipping cream

Procedure:
Now cover peppers and peeled garlic with oil and place in oven 400* until black . Then peel skin off peppers.
Cut squash into wedges, peel off outer shell and remove guts and seeds, Sear these in a frying pan till "brown ".
In a large soup pot melt a lot of butter (3-4 Tbsp at least)
Add all food ingredients and cook for about 5 mins. on med heat, add dry spices and stir.
Add liquids (not whipping cream) and bring to a boil. Then reduce and cook until potatoes are cooked.
Place in blender and puree' till smooth.


You can strain this but I wouldn't if it is smooth enough
Place back in pot and return to stove and bring it to a slow boil, now add whipping cream and cook and reduce to consistency of your choice (thicker or thinner) salt & pepper to taste.
Too garnish this soup chopped parsley or thin sliced green onions may be added at service time (if it sits in the heat to long the green goes gray)
