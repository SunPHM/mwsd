Butternut Squash Soup

as described and illustrated by

Jeremy Greene (Boston, MA) and Brian Stuss (NYC)

 

Ingredients 

  • Lime, sour cream

  • Water

  • Butter

  • Onions (2-3?)

  • 1 good size Butternut Squash

 

  A.

 

Slice squash lenghtwise, place cut edge down in pan with one inch of water.  BAKE 1 hour @ 375? until browned on outside - mushy on inside.

 

B.

 

Sautee onions in butter.  Would you like to add some pepper?  NO! NO!

 

C.

 

Add squash (mushy).  Don’t add the skin of squash.

 

D.

 

BLEND in pot (with hand blender?), out of pot (in blender) or just mash it up. (add water if needed).

 

E.

 

A little pepper? 

 

F.

 

Serve with thin slice of lime and dollop of sour cream.
