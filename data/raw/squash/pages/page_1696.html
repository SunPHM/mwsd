  • SNOWATER RESORT is situated on 20 acres of natural park land surrounded by the Nooksack River and National Forest.

  • This beautiful family resort has all the on-site amenities that you would want on your vacation. Excellent restaurants, skiing, golf and white-water rafting.

  • The all electric, two-story, two bedroom, two bath townhouse interior features a fireplace, washer/dryer, a complete, kitchen with microwave, dishwasher, range and frig, and a furnished balcony.

  • The resort provides four tennis courts, two indoor heated swimming pools with whirlpools, sauna, squash and racquetball courts, billiard, fitness and game rooms.

  • There is a spacious Clubhouse that offers a lounge with fireplace, kitchen facilities, hot tub, pool, sauna, adult billiard room, raquetball and squash courts. It is the hub of social fun with barbecues, planned events, and adjacent tennis courts.

  • The Recreation Center is complete with another swimming pool, whirlpool, fitness center, billiard table, racquetball/squash courts, and sundecks with lounges.

  • The area has miles of hiking trails that wind their way through the dense forest to the open ridges that provide expansive views of the surrounding mountains.

  • The Heritage Log Cabin adult center with its lounge, fireplace, game rooms, kitchen and dining facilities is a quiet retreat for reading, relaxing, socializing and playing of games.

  • The natural setting of the resort offers a bounty of activities from worldclass downhill skiing, snowboarding, backcountry touring, nordic skiing, snowshoeing, tobogganing, hiking and whitewater rafting.

  • The townhouse resort is twenty miles from the Mt Baker Ski Area. The area has an average snowpack of 750 inches, a vertical rise of 1,550 feet, a top elevation of 5,050 feet, six double chairs, two high-speed quad chairs, and one rope tow.

  • MT. BAKER offers more than 38 marked and groomed ski trails, 9 modern highspeed lifts, superb snow depths and a wide variety of terrain for alpine skiing and snowboarding. The longest run is 1.75 miles. The area is in operation from mid November through early May.

  • The resort is located 34 miles east of Bellingham WA on the Mt Baker Highway, approximately 2 hours north of Seattle and east of Vancouver, Canada. Nearby facilities include restaurants, bistros, live entertainment, hiking trails, fishing, downhill and cross-country skiing.
