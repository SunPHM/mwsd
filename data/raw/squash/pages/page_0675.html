In this game, the players are locked inside a small room with at least one glass wall. They viciously attack the small black ball with the snowshoes that they hold sending it speeding around the room. The goal seems to be making the opponent miss so that the ball can have a rest. When they've made the opponent miss a certain number of times they are finally allowed out of the glass cage so that someone else can be put in.
