ISLAND FEATURES

Dunk Island is Australia's most beautiful tropical rainforest island. This island of peace and plenty offers an amazing variety of activities, facilities and experiences.
With an enormous variety of activities to choose from, it's hard deciding what to do next. You could start the day with a rainforest walk and take in the spectacular views from the summit of Mt Kootaloo. After breakfast, glide round the island on a guided jet ski tour, stopping off for a snorkel over the bommies at Naturalist Beach. During lunch you can plan the afternoon ahead. Perhaps some water skiing followed by a game of tennis. Or should that be parasailing followed by squash?
And that's just one day on Dunk Island. You've still got to fit in a trip to the Great Barrier Reef, a visit to the Artists' Colony, horse riding on the beach, and some fishing! But in between this frenzy of fun you'll still find time to relax and unwind. It's as if there's a powerful tonic in the balmy breeze washing away the stress.
Dunk has a variety of restaurants and eateries designed to match your mood. The Beachcomber Restaurant more than caters for all those a la carte cravings. Then there's EJ's on the Deck, an island favourite with a savvy sidewalk cafe style. And BB's on the Beach is great for a relaxing bite to eat. With a postcard view across the palm-fringed beach to distant islands, the Plantation Bar is perfect for a quiet drink together.
For the family, Dunk is as good as it gets. The Kids Club has its own meal times and activities so that mum and dad can enjoy being a couple again. Imagine the freedom and romance of a sunset cruise, knowing that the kids are in safe hands and having a ball.

    ISLAND DINING

    Beachcomber Restaurant
    The resort's main restaurant, specialises in fresh tropical style cuisine. A buffet breakfast is served from 7.00am to 10.00am and 3 course table d'hote or themed buffet dinner from 6.00pm to 9.00pm . Breakfast and dinner are included in the accommodation tariff. Friday nights feature an extensive Seafood Buffet with a carvery section, while Tuesday nights feature a Mediterranean buffet with a pasta station. Other evenings feature a daily changing 3 course table d'hote menu (a special childrens menu is available on these evenings).
    EJ' s On The Deck
    Offering a Modern Australian Brasserie style menu featuring a selection of gourmet sandwiches, salads, pastas, Asian dishes, fish & chips, cakes, ice-creams, milkshakes, smoothies, coffees and fresh juices. Opening hours are between 11.30am and 4.30pm.
    Plantation Bar
    Overlooking the beachfront pool, it' s the perfect place to soak up the rich, tropical ambience of Dunk. Open for drinks from 11.00am to midnight. Features nightly entertainment.
    Cascade Pool Bar
    Open from 11.00am to 4.30pm serving drinks and light snacks (weather permitting).
    BB' s On The Beach
    BB's features a beach cafe style menu including gourmet pizzas, burgers and a selection of other casual meal options and is fully licenced. Open for lunch and snacks from 11.00am to 5.00pm.
    Picnic Lunches
    A choice of 3 different picnic lunch menus are also available (at a charge).
    Beach Barbecues
    Occassionally other dinner options may include poolside gourmet BBQ's with traditional Aussie fare or the 'So Wok' kitchen & Asian Buffet nights offerring the true Asian kitchen style of fun dining. Weather permitting.
    Please Note: The range and style of dining options available at Dunk Island are dependant on weather conditions and the number of guests at the resort.

    KIDS ACTIVITIES

    When it comes to family fun, Dunk is truly the Island of Peace and Plenty. There's a whole host of things to enjoy together. But when Mum and Dad want a little peace and quiet together, the Kids Club has plenty of great activities for th children. Kids Club caters for children aged 3 to 12 years all year round.
    During Australian school holidays, a Keen Teens program operates for children up to 16 years.
    Under the watchful eye of Dunk's professional staff, the kids enjoy a range of activities during morning and evening sessions.
    They can join a treasure hunt, nature arts and crafts, take a rainforest discovery walk, try turtle spotting, visit Dunk's own farm and the list goes on. In the evening, there's disco parties, stargazing, night walks, movies, night games and more. Kids Club even caters for the kids at dinner time. In fact, the kids will be having so much fun, parents can simply disappear! Now that's cool.
