The Vegetable Garden


Bring home some fresh veggies for your family when you sign up for the Farming interest group. The garden has potatoes, green beans, snap peas, carrots, broccoli, squash, zucchini, and tomatoes. Yummy! Sometimes we make homefries for Cookout Day, using potatoes from the garden.
