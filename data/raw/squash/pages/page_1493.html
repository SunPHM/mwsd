The bestselling Skills of the Game seriesprovides in-depth instruction for players and coaches, written by leading names in the field.

Skills of the Game: Squash is the first squash coaching manual that deals with the technical changes demanded by a new generation of rackets and courts.

  • All aspects of the game covered, from rules to mental play.
  • Tips and advice throughout, tried and tested by England’s national coach.
  • Fully illustrated with instructional photography and line drawings.

If you are looking for a sophisticated source that can get beyond the simple introductions to the sport and help you improve your game --- this guide by David Pearson is what you need.

Contents of the book are:

1. The Last Great Fighting Game - basic elements and techniques.
2. The Life Game - levels of the game
3. The Technical Game - techniques
4. The Practice Game - drills and practice
5. The Tactical Game - tactics
6. The Picture Game - video analysis
7. The Body Game - preparing your body
8. The Mind Game - improving your mental approach.

This book, at 110 pages, is relatively compact but contains a wealth of valuable advise from a master at the game.
