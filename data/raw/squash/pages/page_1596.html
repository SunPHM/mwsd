Who the hell is this weirdo?

Who am I?

Nathan Keynes, presently engaged in gainful employment with Virgin Blue. I was previously (up until the end of 2002 ) studying for my PhD in computer science at the University of Queensland under the supervision of Dr Jaga Indulska, but I came to the conclusions that a) it wasn't really going anywhere, and b) doing next to nothing can eventually get a bit tiresome.

My honours project (2000) involved the automatic generation of left-context in parser/scanner generators, about which some details can be found here. Unfortunately I couldn't think of any way to extend this for my PhD though, which is why I'm in a completely different field...

The other major things soaking up my not-terribly-copious-to-start-with free time are the dreamcast emulator I started working on ages ago (and is finally being revived in recent weeks), and my ongoing bumbling attempts to learn the Japanese language. Also started learning basic Mandarin recently.

Stuff I Read

Doctor Who (The greatest SF series ever made, of course), David Brin (If you haven't read the uplift series... what the heck are you waiting for?), Terry Pratchett, Iain M. Banks, James P. Hogan, Frank Herbert, and just about anything good in the SF/Fantasy genres, really. Incidentally, if you happen to have any rare Doctor Who target novelizations that you'd be willing to sell, please let me know.

Stuff I listen to

In no particular order, I'm moderately fond of the likes of Ani Difranco (Folk-Punk), The Tea Party (Alternative?), Elend (Gothic), Melissa Etheridge (Rock), Brave/Arise from thorns (Erm... I give up), Deep Forest (Ethnic Fusion), and Stoa, among other things.

Anime/Manga

Okay, I admit it, I'm something of an anime otaku, although admittedly my collection is still a little on the small side (all those import DVDs get a little expensive, ne?) Particular favourites include Slayers, Gunnm/Battle Angel, Shoujo Kakumei Utena (Revolutionary girl Utena), and Rurouni Kenshin. I also have been collecting a fair bit of manga recently, although I still read rather slowly (my Japanese needs a lot of work...), and haven't really even started on most of them yet.

Other Interests

The game of Go, Piano, Tennis, Squash (when I can find anyone to play). I also used to collect Jolt bottles, but I have decided that was a silly activity.
