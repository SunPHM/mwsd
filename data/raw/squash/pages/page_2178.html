About Us . . .

Stokes County Growers is a growers co-op that sells primarily to wholesalers but also to retail customers.  For 4 growing seasons, SCG has given the farmers of Stokes County the opportunity to grow and sell high quality produce as alternative products to tobacco.

The first crop grown was sweet potatoes.  Since that time, SCG and it's farmers have grown varieties of many types of popular vegetables and herbs, including squash, green beans, watermelons, peppers, tomatoes, lettuce, cucumbers and others.

Many of our farmers' products have been seen at retail stores such as Lowe's Foods, Food Lion, The Fresh Market and Harris Teeter.  Whole Foods, a national chain specializing in organically grown produce has recently opted to showcase our products as well.  SCG offers direct store delivery to retail stores and fully services the wholesale market.

 

 

Stokes County Growers offers several significant advantages to our customers:

   Our customers demand the very best.  SCG can deliver quicker and fresher.  Most product is delivered within 24 hours of being harvested.

   SCG provides assistance with POP marketing and product placement.  We also assist produce managers with merchandising.

   SCG continually engages in test marketing to enhance profitability of seasonal and specialty products.

   Large enough to service national vendors, yet small enough to take care of retail customers personally and with flexibility, SCG can provide the best in produce to any customer.
