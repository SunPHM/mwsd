Squash

Squash courts are available in nearly all units and this sport is extremely popular with many units having internal leagues and often playing in civilian county leagues. The standard is always improving and selection for the Corps team is very competitive. Unit teams regularly reach Army inter unit finals and the Corps team is one of the top teams in the Army. There are two new glass-backed squash courts at Blandford, making it an idea venue for competitions.
