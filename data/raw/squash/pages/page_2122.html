Food in America

              

      

     In America we have many different kinds of great food. My favorite food is pizza. Pizza is bread with sauce and cheese on it. You can have pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, ham, bacon and other things on your pizza. You don’t have to make pizza because you can order it from restaurants. Pizza is a terrific food.

Another delicious food we have in America is hot dogs. A hot dog is made of pig, but you buy it in stores. A hot dog is shaped like a tube. If you’re going to have a hot dog you have to put it in a bun which is bread. You can put ketchup and mustard on your hot dog, but I like them plain. Hot dogs are great!

Ice cream is my favorite desert. You can have chocolate flavored ice cream, strawberry, vanilla, and tons of other flavors. You can put chocolate syrup and sprinkles on your ice cream too. Ice cream is good on hot days. I love ice cream!

Thanksgiving

Many years ago a group of people came to North America in a small boat. They came because they thought they would have a better life in North America. The first year was very hard because the newcomers had to build some small houses and plant a crop so they would have food and shelter in the winter.

Of course, these were not at all the first people to live in North America. The real first people were the Native American Indians who had lived in North America for a very long time.

History tells us that the Native Americans taught the newcomers many ways to make their lives better in the "new world."

After about a year the settlers wanted to celebrate the fact that they had planted crops and built small dwellings so that they could survive the winter. They invited their Native American friends to join them for the celebration.

Tradition says that their dinner consisted of turkey, squash, and corn. We don't know if that is exactly right but even today some people continue to eat those foods when they give thanks late in the fall.

The holiday is North America's "Giving Thanks" day.

Written by Harry, Grade 9, Petosky Schools, Michigan

Food in America 

Americans have an enormous variety of foods to choose from. This is because America is a land of opportunity that many immigrants travel to and bring their ethnic back-rounds to. 

There is really no such thing as American food because every recipe originated in another country. Some types of food you can enjoy here are: Mexican, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, etc.. There is a fast-food restaurant for about any nationality of food you enjoy.

My favorite foods are lasagna, tacos, and strawberry shortcake! You make lasagna with noodles, tomato sauce, and cheese. Tacos include a tortilla shell, meat sauce, and anything else you want on it.
