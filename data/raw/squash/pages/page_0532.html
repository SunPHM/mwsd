Facilities

 
 

Royal Gardens Estate offers you the most ultra-modern facilities to suit your health and relaxation needs.

World class shopping and entertainment complex
Situated on 10 hectares (100,000 square meters) of land, this will be a large-scale shopping and entertainment complex, offering everything from general consumer goods (electronics, clothes, household items, etc.) to groceries, financial services, restaurants and a fuel station. It will be one of Nigeria's first, with leading Nigerian, South African and global retailers.


Sports Bar
Perfect for family and friends to socialize and relax in leisure rooms.





Swimming pools (Adults & Children)
Well-maintained pools designed with safety in mind, where residents of all ages can enjoy anything from a dip to swimming lessons.




Fitness Club
A gym where you'll find the latest cardiovascular machines, exercise equipment as well as weights to maintain a fit and healthy body.

 



Community Hall
A multi-purpose facility to host wedding receptions, meetings, seminars, workshops and exhibitions.

 

 

Tennis Courts
Have the opportunity to play on our all-weather floodlit tennis courts.

 




Squash Courts
Sweat till you can sweat no more in our state-of-the-art squash courts, whether you are an active, competitive player or a beginner.

 



Basketball Courts
Standard basketball courts, for all levels from beginners to professionals.
