Intro Squash



Formally know as the Belgium school of squash, was set up in the 1985 and Directed by Don Veale (World squash federation Advance Coach & Belgium National Coach 1983-1999) to help in the promotion and development of squash.

By giving quality courses to all level of players; Individual or Group, Team training, Company training, Coaching Clinics and Exhibition Matches (World class players), also providing a wide range of services to organisations, companies, clubs, etc.

Over the years the BSA has trained many players to reach a high standard in Squash. It has also been an important part of their personal development and has helped to improve their Social, Physical, Emotional and Intellectual abilities, at the same time keeping the game fin and enjoyable to play.

Squash is now played all over the world by more than 16 million people. It is one of the largest sports played within buisness communities in all major cities throughout the World (some of the biggest contracts have been signed, after a hard game of squash).

It is also one of the fastest growing fitness sports and major intercollege sport in the U.S.A. today and a top University sport in the U.K.

Through the BSA we will help you to get the most out of this exciting fast game called SQUASH !
