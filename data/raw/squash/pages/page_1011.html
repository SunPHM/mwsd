Bon Appetit & Make A Wish Foundation Event

Morgan Ranch joined Bon Appetit & Make A Wish Foundation Event, on October 18, 2002 at the Navy Pier in Chicago. Morgan Ranch donated enough Morgan Ranch Kobe Carpacchio to prepare over 1,000 plates. Carlos’ Restaurant of Highland Park, IL, prepared Carpacchio made from the center cut sirloin, marinated in Olive oil and fresh herbs then thinly sliced. Served on a wanton crisp with parmesan cheese, micro greens and garlic aioli. Over 1000 plates served in 1 evening!

Morgan Ranch Wagyu Beef Featured in USDA Latin America Trade Mission

Morgan Ranch American Style Wagyu Beef invited to participate in USDA Latin America Trade Mission. Morgan's Meat was the featured item at receptions in Caracas and San Jose. The invited quests at the embassy sponsored events had been informed of the "fancy Wagyu beef being served. Items served were shoulder clod. Slow cooked with sea salt, pepper and mustard rub served thinly sliced on bread or crackers with no sauce. A knuckle slow cooked same rub served rare, thinly sliced showing the differences in flavor. And an eye of round seared, fast cooked over high heat served rare with a light barbeque sauce.  Invitees lined up to sample!
