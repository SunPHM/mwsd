Food Stations are one of the most innovative and exciting concepts in catering today. The visual presentation, the wide selection of choices, the superb quality of freshly cooked food, and the interaction between chef and guest provide for a memorable experience.

Following is a partial listing of the amazing selection available. A typical party consists of three or more stations. Other themes not listed are Crepe, Risotto, Sushi, Tempura, Caviar, Fondue, Fajita, Stir Fry, Ethnic, and anything else your imagination can dream of.
