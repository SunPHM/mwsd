Harvest Haven Market Farm

Lethbridge, Alberta

Summer

This is our squash, corn and broad bean crop this year. Apart from the crops doing quite well, you may notice the abundance of weeds. This is an important part of our farming methodology and philosophy. After years of fighting weeds as though they were our enemies, we finally realized them for the soil-building, ground-covering plants they are. We still weed around the plants, especially at the beginning of the season and then keep the weeds mowed so they do not shade out our crops. We have seen the difference they make in protecting the soil moisture and providing a stable soil environment and temperature for the earthworms and microorganisms.
