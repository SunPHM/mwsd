Hans Merensky Estate, which is the largest thatch property in the world, is located in the heart of the Valley of the Elephants. It offers discerning visitors a unique combination of luxury accommodation, golf, sports and wildlife facilities. All of this is within a stone's throw of one of the world's largest wildlife reserves, namely the Kruger National Park.


The chalets, which speak of luxury and comfort, overlook the golf course and the superbly landscaped surroundings. Each chalet is air-conditioned and modern, and captures the essence of Africa with its exquisitely hand-crafted pottery and Sleeperwood furniture. The rustically-styled bathrooms are particularly inviting after an energetic game of golf.
Hans Merensky has many sports facilities. These include, tennis courts, a fully equipped gym, squash courts, 18 hole Championship Golf Course and fishing. There is also a large swimming pool, club house, pro shop, bush bars and much more.
