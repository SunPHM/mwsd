Marmaris Municipality Sport Center is 5 minute walk away from Marmaris Cruise Port.

With it's 1040 seats, VIP Rooms, Cabins & Showers, Sauna and Sport Cafe and many more facilities the Marmaris Municipality Sport Center offers Fitness, Tennis, Bowling, Basketball, Voleyball, Handball, Step-Aerobic, Squash sport and game opportunities in International standards environment.

The practice of most sports finds an adequate setting in Marmaris. Swimming and all water sports have places on the beaches and pools for their fans. Tennis, basketball and volleyball courts, ski resort, rafting, horseback riding club, jogging and hiking offer a thousand opportunities for pleasure.
