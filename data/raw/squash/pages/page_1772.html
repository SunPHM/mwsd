PIA to sponsor World Men's Junior Championships
15 March 2004

Pakistan International Airlines (PIA) chairman Chaudhry Muhammad Saeed has announced that his organisation would provide all out support to the Pakistan Squash Federation (PSF) to win back lost glory in squash.

He was addressing a press conference at the PSF Base Chaklala here during the sponsorship signing ceremony for the 13th World Junior Men’s Squash Championship to be played at Roshan Khan Squash Complex and Pakistan Sports Complex here from August 17 to 28. The individual event will be played from August 17 to 22 while the team championship will be contested from August 23-28.

PIA will be the main sponsor of the championship in which more than 200 players from 24 World Squash Federation (WSF) member countries will be vying for the top honours. "PIA will provide full support to the PSF to win back country’s lost glory and to raise standard of the sport," Chaudhry Saeed said.

WSF technical director Andrew Shelley, who especially came to inspect the venue for the championship, hailed the complex as a 'world class facility'. "These are quality arenas and there is absolutely no issue of lack of squash facilities in Pakistan," he remarked. He was all praise for Pakistan’s role in the promotion of the game in the world. An organising committee headed by PSF senior vice president Rashid Kaleem has been formed to hold the event in a befitting manner.
