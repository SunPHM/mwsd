October 2003 - Recent Events and Photos!

It is amazing how fast time flies - here are some photos of some recent events over the last few weeks:

The last weekend of great weather has come and gone.  Fortunately, I was able to spend it on a campout with the Scouts hiking to Mirror Lake which is just south of the Ski Resorts in the Snoqualmie Pass.

We hiked to the top of a nearby peak reaching 5300 feet in elevation - which is pretty high when you've been living at sea level for a few years.  There were awesome views of Mount Rainer.

 

This is a shot of Mirror Lake - named for obvious reasons.

As Halloween approached, Myrio (Eddie's work) held a pumpkin carving contest.  Our team went for a re-enactment of a scene from the movie Alien where the Alien comes out of the stomach of one of the crew members during a spaghetti dinner.

That is me holding a Tupperware container full of homemade blood.  Let me know if you want the recipe - its actually quite tasty.

We used a squash as the alien and used little toothpicks for the teeth.  We won second behind an entry depicting each of Myrio's CEOs over the past 3 years :) 

That night, Eddie, Braden, and Tyson carved the home pumpkins.

The next night was Halloween - Braden went as a Scorpion Ninja, Tyson as Wolverine from X-Men, and Zackary as Blue from Blue's Clues.  Now, which of these costumes does not belong?
