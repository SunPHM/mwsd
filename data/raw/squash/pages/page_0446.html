Behind the Seeds
A Special Guided Greenhouse Tour

The Behind the Seeds Tour at The Land will be discontinued at the end of operations on January 3, 2004, for an indefinite period of time, due to the construction of Soarin’.

Go behind the scenes at The Land at Epcot® to explore the wonders of growing food plants with the Disney touch!  Tour greenhouses to discover over 60 crops from around the world, and see some of the latest techniques used to grow and improve plants.  Watch as beneficial insects used at The Land battle pests that can destroy our food supply.  Take home environmentally-friendly ideas for growing your own gardens.  Then venture through the fish farm to see thousands of fish and alligators, too!  Bring your cameras, and have fun!

Additional Information:

  • Tours are led by a member of the Epcot Science Team
  • Flash photography and videotaping are allowed
  • Children are welcome, but must be supervised; strollers are provided for small children
  • Wheelchair accessible (guests in electrical wheelchairs need to transfer to push type)
  • Safety and sanitation rules are strictly enforced in the greenhouses and labs
  • Covered, non-alcoholic drinks are allowed during the tour

Duration: Approximately 1 hour.

Location and Time: 10:30 a.m. - 4:00 p.m. daily.*   Same day reservations must be made at the tour podium in the Green Thumb Emporium, lower level of The Land.

*Tour times are subject to change depending on demand and time of year.

Cost: $10 adults; $8 children (ages 3-9).  Theme Park admission is required. - Effective January 1, 2004.

Discounts:

  • Disney Vacation Club (DVC) members - 15%
  • Cast Members - 50% for the number of guests listed on your maingate or silver pass

Packages:

  • Silver, Gold, and Platinum Plans

Free Handouts: Information on setting up small hydroponic (growing plants without soil) systems for home or school is available to guests after the tour.  A written script of the tour in French, German, Portuguese, and Spanish is available for our international guests and in English for our guests who are hard of hearing.
