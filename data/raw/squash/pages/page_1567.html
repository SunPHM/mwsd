SBAC has about 200 squash players covering male and female, young and old, and every level of ability from complete beginners just starting the game brand new, or converting from a tennis or racquetball past, to some of the top players in Southern California.

Many players show up at the more popular times such as lunchtime and after work looking for pick-up games, but most play in the monthly leagues, where you get to play four other players of your level during the month, at times that suit your schedule and theirs. About 100 people play in these leagues.

Courts may be reserved up to one week in advance by contacting the Front Desk. You are encouraged to reserve courts to avoid disappointment, but also asked to cancel them if you are not going to use them.

This is geared more towards beginner level, and a great way for ladies to get involved in the game.

SBAC holds 4 weekend tournaments each year open to players from outside the club as well as members, and also the in-house club championships for members only. There are also usually two other one day events which differ in style each year. We pride ourselves on the reputation of running the best events in
California, if not the country, particularly our event that is a fundraiser for the Santa Barbara Sheriff's Department's Drug Abuse Resistance Education (DARE) program. This tournament always features some of the top Mens Professional players in the world, and has resulted in over $400,000 being donated to DARE directly from the first 11 years of the event.
