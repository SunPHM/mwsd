Thu 7th September - TOMORROW's THE DAY !!!

I’m sure everyone will be pleased to see the Club open again, after what has been a more or less "squashless" summer for the majority of our Members. Refurbishment works have gone well with, unfortunately, some unexpected problems with the building coming to light during the construction works. To celebrate the re opening, we will be holding an opening night party on Saturday 9th September 2000. The night will include an exhibition match a free buffet and a disco. The festivities will start at 7pm.

We hope you will be able to come along and enjoy this special evening, which will hopefully be the start of a new era for Tynemouth Squash.

Refurbishment news June 4th 2000

The first week into the refurbishment program seems to have passed with no major problems except the digger not turning up on time and the loss of two inches on the length of the front extension ( I don’t think it will be noticed). As everyone is aware with every external building project the weather can play a big part in the progress of the job, and have we had some weather this past week.

Many members may not realise that within this refurbishment program the following renovations and alterations are included. This is not the complete list, just a taster.

 
New higher fence to side and rear of the property. Completed
Clean up and grassing of the side adjacent the sub station. Completed
Tidy up of rear car park - yes, it will be sorted out with the contractor. Completed
New security cameras and monitors. Completed
 
New 3 phase mains supply to building. Been a bit dodgy for years with regards to the loading on the old single phase supply. Completed
 
Rewire of the electrics within the club. You will be able to see light again in the lounge for starters.
New floor on court 2. Still dusty but getting better. Be careful. Completed
Court 3 repairs and sanded. Completed
 
New central heating boilers. Like most of us these old boilers have been around for a while and have been held together with gum and prayers.
Two Saunas, one in the ladies and one in the gents changing rooms. No get togethers there ...
New shower control units in the gents reverting back to individual control of temperature setting on each shower. (Hooray!, Ed)
 
New safer double glazed units to the viewing windows in the lounge. Part of the lounge refurb.
 
Total refurbishment of the lounge.
New furnishings in the lounge.
Complete refit of the bar, which moves back a few feet.
 
Kitchen refit. This time its getting an extractor fan and a new cooker that the doors close on etc.
Designated eating area adjacent to the kitchen.
Larger shop (located behind the bar, along with the new office) with more items for sale, we hope.
 
New Front build to club to include in the ground floor a new cellar, separate boiler room, Ladies sauna and extra shower and increase men’s changing area and allow room for gents sauna where cellar was.
New entrance to club to allow easier access from rear car park.
 
Also in August the courts to get a lick of paint on the side walls to make everything look bright and new.

We hope you will bear with us as this is only the start of the improvements to the club and over the next few years we will try to continue to improve the facilities to the members as the cash becomes available.

Work just starting

 
Development Update #1
 
Work will commence on Tuesday 30th May, Estimated completion date Friday 1st September.
 

Internal works are due  to commence around 10th July and completion six weeks later around 21st August. During this period the lounge and changing rooms will be closed to members.
 

A limited range of refreshments will be available.
 
Courts will be available throughout the period of the refurbishment but certain restrictions may apply from time to time. Check updates.
 

Shower and changing facilities will be restricted especially during the internal works. Check updates.
 

During the internal works, courts will be cleaned at the end of each working day and available from 5.40pm onwards.
 
Parking will be restricted both at the front and rear of the club.
 
Members are requested to have due consideration for our neighbours when parking off the club premises. 
 
Members are requested to comply with any safety notices and site restrictions posted.
 
The Committee apologise for any inconvenience to members during this refurbishment period
