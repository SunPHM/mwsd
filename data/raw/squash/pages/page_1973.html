Risser Marvel Farm Market grows 60 acres of fruits and vegetables on our farm.  We retail this produce and other locally grown produce at the farm market mid May through December.

We are a family owned business dating back to 1924, located in Lebanon County Pennsylvania, on Route 322 (Horseshoe Pike) five miles East of Hershey and 1/2 mile East of Campbelltown. 

In our market we feature seasonal homegrown fruits and vegetables picked fresh daily, a wonderful selection of canned (jars) goods, fresh baked goods from cookies and pies to breads, hand made noodles and pot pie.
 

mid-May
Our Farm Market opens.  Strawberries, asparagus, spinach and spring onions are available along with a full line of preserves, jellies and local honey. Our Farm Market  also has homemade baked goods and a full line of condiments and Pa. Dutch items.
 
late June
Sweet corn season begins and continues through Labor Day.

Along with sweet corn we produce some of the best farm fresh fruits and vegetables.  During this time of year you will find sweet corn, cantaloupe, watermelons, tomatoes, peppers, green beans, peas and most seasonal vegetables. We also offer local peaches and apples in season.
 
September
September brings the fall crops of jack-o-lantern pumpkins, pie pumpkins, all varieties of winter squash, apple cider, gourds, Indian corn and many seasonal items. Broccoli, cauliflower, cabbage and a large selection of chrysanthemums are available in September and October along with corn stalks and straw bales.
 
Late November
We extend our season with Pennsylvania grown Christmas trees.  The Christmas trees arrive Thanksgiving weekend along with wreathes, greens, garland, homemade sand tarts and other bakery items.

 

Risser’s closes for the season just before Christmas.
