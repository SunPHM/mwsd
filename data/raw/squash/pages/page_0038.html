AP - Wed Feb 1, 5:28 PM ET
Autumn Faucher, 10, Pelham, N.H., feeds squash to Beau, a Masai giraffe, at the zoo in Boston, Wednesday, Feb. 1, 2006. Beau has giraffe wasting syndrome, according of the Franklin Park Zoo, and Faucher has raised more than $6,000 for his treatment through fundraisers at both school and her parents' Dracut, Mass., convenience store and lunchtimes spent collecting cans and bottles for redemption. Autumn later met Beau's offspring--Autumn-- who the zoo named in her honor. (AP Photo/Chitose Suzuki)
