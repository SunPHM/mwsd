Squash is a very exciting and fast game which can be enjoyable whatever your ability.

This is an American game that have been adapted to enable it to be played on a Squash Court.
The racket used is shorter than a squash racket and the ball is almost the size of a tennis ball and has more "spring".
The effect of this is to lead to longer rallies and although very energetic racketball creates less impact on the body.
