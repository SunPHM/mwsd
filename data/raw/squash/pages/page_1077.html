WILLIAM! - Week 27

27 October 2002

A gradual return to normality after all the excitement of last week. William has experimented with more food - top of the pops this week were mashed prunes. Sweet potato, gem squash, bananas and butternut have also got the nod.


Aunts Wendy and Mia came to visit William on Saturday afternoon, and tried to feed him. He did not co-operate. His sitting exercises, and new toys, also kept him busy this week.
