Butter-Steamed Squash
Serves 4

6 medium squash (use a variety of Italian, summer, winter..etc)
1 red bell pepper
water
1 Tbs. olive oil or butter
1/4 tsp. cumin
ground pepper

Wash and slice the squash and bell pepper. Steam with water until tender. Drain and put into a hot frying pan greased with olive oil or butter. Season with cumin. Sauté lightly and serve with a sprinkling of pepper.
