Squash Programs

Squash Absolute Beginner
Squash absolute beginner lessons focus on having fun! Learn the proper rules, basic forehand and backhand strokes, and serves. Class runs one evening from two hours. All equipment is provided. Contact Tina Istace 220-3405 for more information. more info...

Squash Group Lessons
You will develop the fundamentals of squash properly. Lessons focus on court movement, serve, and return of serve, forehand and backhand drives and other important elements of squash. Class meets twice a week for two weeks and is recommended for all skill levels. If you need more information on this program, call Tina Istace at 220-3405. more info...

Adult Squash Camps
This intense camp will improve your squash game and your fitness. You will spend 1 hour on court and the other hour will be working on fitness, strength, agility and conditioning more info...

Squash Private Lessons
Book your lessons when it is convenient for you! Learn new skills in these 45-minute private lessons. Fee includes FREE drop-in at the Racquet Centre so you can practice throughout your lessons! All equipment is provided. Call Tina Istace at 220-3405 to arrange lesson times. more info...

Squash Semi-Private Lessons
Book your lessons when it is convenient for you! Learn new skills in these 45-minute semi-private squash lessons. Fee includes FREE drop-in at the Racquet Centre so you can practice throughout your lessons! All equipment is provided. Prices are per person and players are responsible for finding partners. Contact Tina Istace 220-3405 to arrange lessons with instructor. more info...
