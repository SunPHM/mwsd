Leah is 7 months old!

William

Leah is getting bigger and cuter every day. She weighed in at 18 pounds, 27 inches at her 6 month doctor visit. Leah is now sitting up, and she has two prominent bottom teeth (visible in some of the photos in the gallery). She is very serious when it comes to playing with her toys; she is especially fond of the tags.

After the initial shock (click here), Leah has been enjoying many different cereals including: oatmeal, rice, and barley. Mama and Daddy are making homemade baby foods. So far, Leah has experienced summer squash, sweet potatoes, butternut squash, apples, and pears. Her favorite is a toss up between the sweet potatoes and butternut squash. Green vegetables are next -- Oh Boy!
