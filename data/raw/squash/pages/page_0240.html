Squash and Racquetball

Hunts Fitness Centre was originally a world class squash centre and remains one of the larger squash centers in Melbourne.

For those who are looking to play competitive squash, we are always looking for new players to play pennant squash. We have teams catering for players of all levels and practice sessions are held each Saturday morning.

In house squash is held every Friday night and Saturday afternoon. Players of all levels are welcome to come and have social hit with other players and if your keen, enjoy a nice cold beer afterwards to quench your thirst.

Bookings

The squash courts are available for hire to the general public 7-days a week and bookings can be made by contacting the centre by phone on 9419 3636 or in person at reception.
