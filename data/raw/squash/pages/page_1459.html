Welcome to the Commodore Squash Club. Opened in 1976, this unique club is devoted to the great game of squash.

Squash was first played in England over two hundred years ago, and is now popular throughout the world. As a racquet sport, squash provides an exciting alternative to racquetball, tennis, and badminton, often combining the best elements of these games.

Perfect for both beginners and experts, squash adapts to the level and humor of the players.
