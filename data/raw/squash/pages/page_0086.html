   The SQUASH Hotel is located in a picturesque valley with a view of the Czech karst, 10km from the centre of Prague. The surrounding countryside provides a lovely backdrop for a walk all year round. At about 200m from the hotel there is a bus stop. Coach trips to both the capital and to nearby sights such as Karlstejn and Zbraslav castles can be arranged.

All rooms are equipped with toilet, shower, telephone set and TV with satellite reception. Each room is attractively laid out featuring for example sloping ceilings, and extra beds may be provided.

The restaurant and bar, with separate non-smoking area, are open to both hotel guests and the general public. The restaurant provides a wide range of dishes and beverages, and cold fare can be prepared for larger parties.

In the summer you can take advantage of our garden, and parents accompanying children will appreciate the separate children's corner.


The hotel complex includes two mini squash courts equipped with electronics (counting, out lines). Two floors lower you can find squash courts (ASB) with air-conditioning.
Games can be watched from our pleasant restaurants.


To help you unwind, we provide a sauna with a small pool, and our lady guests in particular can enjoy the solarium with its pleasant music.


A small gym is also available for training.
