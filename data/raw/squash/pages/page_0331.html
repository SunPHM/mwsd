About the Club

The club is far more than just a squash club, with a whole range of indoor activities available, including Aerobics, A Fully supervised Fitness Suite, a Solarium, a Hair Salon and a bar which is available for functions.

It has ample parking facilities to the front, and spacious changing and showering rooms.

Opening Times

Monday to Saturday 9.00am to 11.00pm
Sunday 9.00am to 9.00pm

Bank holidays times vary.

The club is owned and run by Guy and Tracey Philpotts, who personally oversee that everyone leaves satisfied.
