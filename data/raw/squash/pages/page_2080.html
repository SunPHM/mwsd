The Critter Corral

We have a number of farm animals for children to interact with and pet. If you stop by at feeding time we'll let you help! George and Tammy are our most popular critters.

They are "Kinder" goats and are gentle and love children and adults alike. They are very much like deer and love to jump and play on the boulders in their pen. Cletus is a friendly grey and white lop eared rabbit. He likes to be petted and fed treats. He has also been know to hop up on the boulders and play King of the Mountain with the goats. Kenny and Dolly are a pair of snow white Silkie chickens. They are super soft to the touch, just like goose down. One of our most unusual farm animals that guests just love is Lyle. He is a Swedish crested runner duck. Wynonna, our Ruen duck hen is setting right now, so we'll have lot's of new ducklings this summer. The animals are taken home from the farm store to our home on the other side of the farm each night where they will be safe from coyotes and foxes. They make the ride daily to the farm store in the back of a pickup with the kids. They enjoy the ride so much, when we open the corral they head for the pickup and jump in on their own, ready for the ride.
