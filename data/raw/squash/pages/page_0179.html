Introduction and Rates

International singles courts: Two state-of-the-art hardwood squash courts with glass back walls and elevated viewing area.

Squash players know that no other racquet sport can give you the cardio benefits, hand-eye coordination, and intense, vigorous physical activity. Whether you are a veteran or new to the game, we can provide you with regularly scheduled matches with someone at your ability level, competition in a squash ladder, or instruction on a modern international singles court.

The guest fees are $10 plus court time. For membership fees, please see the MEMBERSHIP/RATES page. *** Not sure how to get started? Visit Four Seasons and watch a video created by the Squash Racquets Association. Or take to the court for a free demonstration with a friendly veteran squash player. We have loaner racquets; we have balls. You bring the enthusiasm!
*** Ask any squash player: You can't BUY a better workout!

Women's Squash Ladder

Sign up for the squash ladder and be assured of regular matches. Players are ranked by approximate ability. You'll be assigned to play a match against a person preceding you or behind you in the rankings. Work your way up the ladder as you perfect your game and improve your conditioning.

Instruction

Squash lessons. Take to the court with an experienced squash competitor who can help you analyze your game and increase your winning percentage. This is an opportunity for beginning to advanced intermediate players to improve their skills while testing themselves against an experienced player. An excellent activity for young people, an intense workout for adults.

Clinics with David Bishop for beginners and intermediates. Call for days and times. Lesson is free for members, $10 for non-members.

Rac-A-Tac After School Program

Children are bussed from school (Daisy Bronson, Lakeway, and Lafayette Regional only) one day a week to Four Seasons for 2 hours of tennis and squash instruction from 3pm-5pm. Then it's on to the pools until parents arrive to pick up their children (by 6pm). $15/week - call for session dates.
