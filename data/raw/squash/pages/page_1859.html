Counting Calories In Starch, Grains, Pasta & Cereals

Carbs continue to be a very controversial area of dieting.  Though delicious, pasta tends to be very high in calories for what it is.  A healthier alternative is Spaghetti Squash - which tastes pretty close to manufactured spaghetti. Spaghetti is generally prepared with enriched flour, thus lending health benefits.

Grains are an important part of your diet - so be sure to include plenty of those every day.  Foods rich in fiber help combat cholesterol to ensure a healthy heart.  They also are filling which is particularly important to the dieter.
