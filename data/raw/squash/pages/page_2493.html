Camping du Soleil
CH-1854 Leysin, Switzerland

The resort of LEYSIN can be reached in less than 2 hours from Geneva Airport by train or by car. Montreux is only ½ hour drive and 45 min. to Lausanne or Gstaad.

 .

In the winter, 60 Km of prepared tracks attract avid Alpine and cross country skiers as well as snowboarders. 
With the new shuttle link to Glacier 3000, you can get to an altitude of 3000m. for more runs and summer skiing. 

All year round, two sports centers provide: Tennis, Swimming, Ice Skating, and Squash facilities including a Fitness complex.
