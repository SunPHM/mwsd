Welcome to my Web site!

Hello my name is Frank Kwan Lista and I am from Venezuela, a beautiful country located at the northern border of South America mainly known for its oil, natural landscapes and beautiful women. I was born in the city of Lechería, which is located 200 miles east of Caracas, Venezuela's Capital city, and which is famous for its oil and wonderful beaches. I am the oldest son of a couple of university professors, Francisco and Mildred. My youngest siblings are Frederick and Michelle Kwan.

 

I obtained an Industrial Engineering degree from one of Venezuela's top four universities, the Universidad Católica Andrés Bello,  in Caracas, Venezuela, on December 1997. At the moment I live in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where I am pursuing a Master's Degree in Business Administration at Carnegie Mellon University. My school is considered one of the best business schools in the World, #3 worldwide according to The Wall Street Journal. My primary interests are Marketing, Strategy, Human Resources and Finance. My graduation is in May 16th, 2004.

 

Before I moved to the United States, I worked for Conind de Venezuela, a Puffer Sweiven Inc. subsidiary and Emerson Electric exclusive sales representative. During five years I held the positions of Applications Engineer, Sales Engineer, Sales Supervisor and Eastern Region Sales Manager. During this period I underwent my most challenging business and leadership experiences, and also acquired my most significant achievements and business lessons.

 

I have always enjoyed sports, and have practiced a large number of them,. From baseball, basketball and volleyball to ping pong and bowling, from soccer and football to fishing and tennis, from golf, judo and weight lifting, to water skiing, spinning and swimming. This last winter I took advantage of Pittsburgh's weather and learned to ski. I am currently practicing golf, and plan to take squash lessons next winter.

 

I also enjoy traveling and experiencing different environments, cultures and meeting new people. I specially like natural sight watching.  In Venezuela I've traveled from  Caracas  to Canaima and from Cumaná and Margarita to Mérida and Maracaibo. In the United States I've gone from Florida to Iowa, and from New York to California. I've also been in Mexico and Puerto Rico. My next stops, Spain, France, Germany and England.

 

I believe in the existence of a God. I believe that everyone around me has the potential of becoming a close friend and a source of learning, but understand that the most important relations in life are those based in long term commitment. I believe in love, I believe in family, I believe in friends, I believe in dreams. I believe one must learn to see with the heart, as the "essential is invisible to the eyes." (The Little Prince)

Frank.
