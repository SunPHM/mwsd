Sporting and Recreational Facilities

For those who believe in life after golf, Kloof Country Club offers a wide choice of other sporting and recreational facilities.

 
 Cricket (on a traditional village green)


Tennis


Swimming

The squash centre is particularly popular and features three courts (one of which is glass backed) and a cosy bar.

 
Squash

The club also boasts a well equipped Pro-Shop, and an active Bridge table. Besides that, an indigenous tree planting programme has made the club grounds a haven for bird-watchers.
