Puteri Pan Pacific  

Hotel Overview

Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru blends the professionalism of international expertise with the quiet charm of traditional Malaysian hospitality in a rich tapestry of hotel comfort and luxury. Just 25 miles from Singapore's Changi Airport, Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru is also just 20 miles from Sultan Ismail Airport at Senai, Johor.

Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru has 500 hotel guest rooms, apartments and suites, with amenities including IDD telephone with bathroom extension, individually controlled air conditioning, colour television and in-house videos in every hotel room. Non-smoking and no-alcohol floors are also available at Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru.


Facilities & Services

Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru offers a wealth of recreational options. You can relax by the swimming pool, enjoy a game of tennis or squash, or work out in the best equipped sports and fitness centre in town. The centre, managed by Clark Hatch International, comes complete with gym, male and female saunas, jacuzzi, a steam room and a spa.

Meetings and Banquets - Our conference and banquet facilities feature nine function rooms, including a magnificent ballroom. Whether it is a high-level meeting for eight or an international function for 800, you can count on us to provide committed support and professional solutions.

Attention to detail is complemented by state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems and full audio-visual and presentation facilities.


Restaurants

The options for dining and entertainment at The Puteri Pan Pacific Hotel range from casual to cosmopolitan.

Paolo Restaurant - The Paolo Restaurant offers exotic cuisine from the Mediterranean region in a cosy ambience. One of Paolo's specialities is its seafood, including prawns, lobsters, fresh oysters, fish and more.

The Hai-Tien-Lo - The Hai-Tien-Lo, our Chinese restaurant, provides a sophisticated setting in which to enjoy the best Malaysian Chinese cuisine. Skillfully created, its menu encompasses a generous choice of seafood, shark's fin, abalone, barbecued and roast meats, soups, noodles, desserts and even dim sum.

The Newsroom Café - Beckoning from the lobby, The Newsroom Café is just the place for a business meeting or a social rendezvous. Bright and airy, this popular coffee shop offers an attractive spread of international selections and local favourites.

Lobby Lounge - The Puteri Pan Pacific Hotel's Lobby Lounge is the perfect place to sit back and relax after a hard day's work. Sink into comfortable chairs and enjoy the best easy- listening music in the city as you sip your favourite drink.


Accommodation

Puteri Pan Pacific Johor Bahru offers 500 guest rooms, suites and full-service apartments, all thoughtfully appointed with a range of modern amenities. Two rooms have been specially designed to meet the needs of physically challenged guests. Non-smoking and no-alcohol floors are also available.

  • IDD telephone with bathroom extension
  • Mini - refrigerator
  • Complimentary coffee and tea making facilities
  • In-room electronic safe
  • Colour TV with CNN and in-house movie channel
  • Individually controlled air conditioning
  • Radio
  • Hairdryer

Princess Deluxe - Rooms are located on the 4th to 15th floors of the Princess Wing, facing south-west with views of the city or the sea. The size of each room is 33 square metres, with room furnishings in mahogany wood with pinkish tones. Bathtub is fitted with a hand shower.

Imperial Deluxe - Rooms are located on the 6th to 12th floors of the newer Imperial Wing, facing south-east with views of the city or the sea. The size of each room is 37 square metres. Apartment suites occupy the 13th and 14th floors. Room furnishings are in walnut wood with bluish tones. Bathroom has bathtub with separate shower cubicle.

Pacific Floor - Designed for business executives, the rooms, measuring 33 square metres, and a Junior Suite are located on the 16th floor of the Princess Wing facing south-west with a view of the city and the sea. Room furnishings are in walnut wood with apple-white tones. Bathtub is fitted with a hand shower. Guest privileges include private check-in and check-out services, audio, video and facsimile services, complimentary laundry / dry-cleaning of three items per day and private lounge serving complimentary breakfast and evening cocktails.

Junior Suite - Located on the 4th to 16th floors of the Princess Wing, the Junior Suites comprise one bedroom with king-size bed and adjoining living room, measuring 58 square metres. The location gives a panoramic view of the historic Sultan Ibrahim Building and the sea. Suites are accorded with VIP bathroom amenities, VIP fruits and flowers and two television sets. Bathtub is fitted with a hand shower. Suites are furnished in mahogany wood with pinkish tones.

Location 

Located in the heart of the city's commercial and government centre. Overlooks the Straits of Johor and 5 minutes drive from the Causeway to Singapore.

Hotel Address: Jalan Abdullah Ibrahim, 'The Kotaraya', PO Box 293,Johor Bahru, Johor, 80730  Malaysia

Access 

  • Sultan Ismail (domestic) Airport - 32 kilometers (20 miles)
  • Singapore Changi International Airport - 40 kilometers (25 miles)
