Farmer's Markets 2004

The Products The Producers Farmer's Markets

The Peak of Summer's Harvest is just beginning! Vine Ripe Tomatoes, Corn, Melons, Peaches, Peppers, Pears, Potatoes, Squash - "back to the land" market gardeners rub elbows with 4th generation old-timers - all have the best summer can offer!

Our Markets stretch across Mendocino County from Fort Bragg to Ukiah, Gualala to Laytonville.
