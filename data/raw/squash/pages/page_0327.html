Barbados Squash Club

The Barbados Squash Club is where fun loving players go to break a sweat and sip a few beers afterwards.

The club has three international squash courts, two of which are glass-backed. There is a lounge area with T.V., video, stereo, couches, dominoes/cards tables and a small gym.

The bar is always well stocked with alcoholoic and non-alcoholic drinks, snacks, cigarettes, squash equipment and accessories.

If you plan on staying in Barbados for a while, you may be like to play in one of our leagues, ladders or tournaments. Drop us a line is you know your travelling dates.

If you enjoy squash, call the club, ask for Tony and he will set up a game for you with a player of similar standard.
