The new shop is a purpose built "Sussex Cart Lodge" type of building, and opened in May 2001. Carol Hilsdon runs the shop, and her policy is to stock the shelves with, in order of preference
  • Sharnfold Grown 
  • Local produce 
  • Regional produce 
  • From abroad only if not available in the UK
Much of the produce is grown at Sharnfold, grown for flavour, not appearance and shelf life, and can often go from field to fork within hours, giving our patrons the freshest, tastiest fruit and vegetables.
The shop sells not only fresh tasty fruit and vegetables, but also cakes, pies, and bread is baked on site daily.
 A limited range of organic produce will be available for the 2002 season
There are also preserves, cheeses and dairy products, apple juice (eight varieties), eggs, honey, ice cream, stationery and cards, and self serve frozen fruit and vegetables. There is also a compact Plant Area with quality plants and shrubs on offer, also wooden bird tables, bird boxes and garden furniture made at Sharnfold. 
The shop and PYO opens from 9.30am until 6.00pm from 26th April until the end of October. Own grown Christmas trees and wreaths are sold in December. There are disabled parking spaces and ramps for easy access. 
The large picnic and play area is located close to both the shop and the small animals, for the benefit of the young and the not so young. Sharnfold has it's own mailing list, we write no more than 3 times a year including right at the start of the PYO season in May, giving approximate picking dates.
