Despite the dry weather we picked about 300 pounds each day we worked at the Farm.  Beans, summer squash and tomatoes have done well.  So far we have harvested over 5,000 pounds of food at the RI Community Farm in East Greenwich.  As of September 15, we are still harvesting tomatoes, eggplant, melons, peppers and beans.

 

   Boy Scout Troop 51 from Warwick gave us a great harvest one weekend of over 300 pounds.  The summer squash is beautiful.

 

Contact steve.connors@rifarm.org if you can join us or for information.

We harvested over 20,000 pounds of vegetables from the Farm last season, distributed to hungry people through the RI Community Food Bank.  Last year's harvest consisted of beans, peas, tomatoes, cucumbers, summer squash, butternut squash, broccoli, pumpkins and eggplant.  This year we will be adding sweet potatoes and other vegetables.
