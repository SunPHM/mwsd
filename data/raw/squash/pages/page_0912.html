Alphabet Pasta with Butternut Squash Sauce

Ingredients

100g Alphabet pasta, cooked in boiling water
Half a butternut squash
1 onion, sliced
olive oil
75g cheddar cheese
200ml vegetable or chicken stock
assorted cooked vegetables, carrots, broccoli, peas etc
salt and pepper
12 cherry tomatoes, halved

Method:

  1. Peel and deseed the butternut squash.
  2. Cut into small chunks.
  3. Place on a baking tray, drizzle with oil and salt and pepper, cover with foil and bake in a medium oven until soft - about 20 minutes.
  4. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a saucepan and cook the onion until soft.
  5. Add the squash and stock and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the cheese and blitz in a blender.
  7. Return to saucepan and add vegetables, cherry tomatoes, and pasta.
  8. Warm through and serve.
