Several pre-primary schools, 60 primary and secondary schools in jurisdiction area. University of Venda, Northern Training Trust, Several Teacher's Training Colleges, Satelite campusses for UNISA and Potchefstroom University, Technicon RSA satelite campus. 2 New private schools approved by Council.
