RULES OF SQUASH

The World Squash Federation has a number of great tools for the average player:

  • the Abbreviated rules, giving a simplified overview to help players with the basics,
  • Let Please, advice on what to do when someone asks for a let,
  • the Idiot's Guide to some of the changes in the latest rules.
Link to http://www.worldsquash.org/rules.html

If you can't find the answer to your question, send it to squash.canada@squash.ca
