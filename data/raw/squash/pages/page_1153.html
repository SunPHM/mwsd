Nashville Farmers Market


Many of the vegetable stands at the Nashville Farmers Market are arranged like those in the beautiful open-air street markets in Paris, with orderly pyramids of tomatoes edged prettily by green and golden peppers. If you need a recipe for preparing something, be sure and ask--the people who grow the stuff usually are pretty good chefs at cooking it up.

Other Sources of Fresh Produce in Nashville:

Produce Place at two locations: 4000 Murphy Road (615-383-2664) and 7101 Highway 70 (615-662-1184) Superior source for fresh fruits and vegetables. Tennessee home-grown tomatoes in the summertime. Reverse-osmosis water available here also (bring your own container). Like having the Farmer's Market in your own neighborhood.
