Squash Courts

Two full sized glass backed courts are available for booking.

Cookstown Leisure Centre has a very successful Squash team, playing in division 3.

The Club normally meet on Wednesday and Friday nights with matches played on Tuesday nights, and always welcome new members and encourage enthusiasm in this sport..
