Juniors   September Tennis Updates

Juniors are an important part of Jericho's growing membership. The Club offers juniors the opportunity to play tennis and squash in a fun environment.

Junior members may participate in both tennis and squash programs run by the professional coaching staff throughout the year. Every level of play is supported and we encourage our juniors to participate in club activities and tournaments. There are also popular summer programs where these members can enjoy various sports activities including tennis, squash and swimming. Family activities are also offered which help to familiarize children with the facilities and encourage them to be our future members.

Jericho hosts such junior events as Theme Nights, Junior camps and clinics and Family-Junior Match Play.

Theme nights include Teen Night, Parent/Child Tourney and Interclub exchanges. These theme nights include round robin tennis followed by dinner.

Junior camps involve broad instruction geared to Beginner, Secondary and Intermediate players, while junior clinics involve specific instruction geared to the level of the player. The small, clinic groups focus on learning the skills essential to move on to the next level.

The family-junior match play allows for members to join in for organized fun play for all levels with parents playing with or against juniors in team games, standard singles and doubles.
