Sport

Oriel has a fine tradition of sporting excellence. Past members of the College have represented the University, and frequently have played for one of the many successful College teams at rugby, football (soccer), cricket, hockey, squash, tennis, table-tennis, basketball, American football, golf, fencing and darts. Many have rowed for the College in the inter-collegiate races which take place twice a year on the nearby Thames. In rowing, one of Oxford's most popular sports, Oriel has been pre-eminent, generally providing the most successful crews in Torpids and in Summer Eights since the 1970s. The College has its own sports ground and pavilion, with football and rugby pitches and a squash court, as well as its own boathouse. Teams from the College have twice been finalists in consecutive years on TV's University Challenge recently. In short, the cultural, social and sporting activities of Oriel students are as diverse as the students who take part in them.
