Player Profiles...

Andrea Goodridge

Andrea's natural athletic ability has kept her at the top of her squash game for many years. Her long legs allow her to cover the court with ease, and should she choose to work on her fitness and mental strength, she could be a top contender at the regional level.

Andrea has won three national squash titles in the past five years and is ranked at #5 in the Caribbean.
