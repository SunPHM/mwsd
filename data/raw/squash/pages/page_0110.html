Thursday, January 19, 2006

Pakistan to figure in Commonwealth Squash

ISLAMABAD: Pakistan will figure in both the men’s singles and doubles squash events of the Commonwealth Games 2006 to be held at Melbourne from March 15 to 26.

Shahid Zaman, Mansoor Zaman and Majid Khan have already been selected to play the singles event while for the doubles event four pairs have been invited for trials for selection of one pair. The pairs include Amjad Khan & Safeerullah, Yasir Butt & Basit Ashfaq, Aamir Atlas & Farhan Mehboob and Khayal Muhammad & Farrukh Zaman. The final trials will be held at Mushaf Squash Complex from January 23 to 25. app
