Madeleine keeps on getting bigger.  Her first tooth is pretty well established and the second one is on its way in to her mouth.

Madeleine is also starting to get around.  She can pull herself to sit up and even stand up if everything is right.  She is starting to try to crawl around as well, so Mom and Dad are in for trouble real soon now!

June 29, 2002, Madeleine holds herself in the standing position without help for the first time.  She stayed there long enough for daddy to grab the camera and take a picture.

Of course, a first try like that was bound to end up on the ground.  Still, Madeleine seemed quite happy with the whole attempt.

Madeleine and mommy went up to aunt Mary's house on a hot day to try out the pool.

Cousin Catherine and Madeleine in the water.

Madeleine liked splashing in the water.

Madeleine had a good time up with Aunt Mary and Cousin Catherine in the pool.

The solid food fun continued.  Here is daddy feeding Madeleine squash, which she seemed to like to eat.

Of course, she seemed to like to wear the squash as well.

Grandma Nancy came over from Maui to visit.  Madeleine greeted her enthusiastically.

Here is Madeleine with Oma Brigitte and Grandma Nancy.

Madeleine had such a good time up at Aunt Mary's house that we went up there again.  Here is Madeleine with Catherine and Mary.

Uncle David, Grandma Nancy, Daddy, and Madeleine together.

Of course, there was more fun in the pool.  This time Madeleine and daddy went for a swim.

Madeleine had a good time in the pool with dad. (Those bubbles are from the pool filter system, I swear!)

Out in the middle of the pool.

Afterwards, toweling off with mom and looking pretty sleepy!

Madeleine saying good-bye to Grandma Nancy at the end of her visit.

Bob, Madeleine, and Grandma Nancy pose for one final picture before Bob and Nancy have to head back to Maui.
