Product Overview                Product Catalogue
  
 

  • Our products help gardeners grow plants high in energy, higher in minerals and vitamins, high in brix levels,  higher health and vitality, and higher yields.  For all fruits, vegetables, ornamentals and turf grasses. 
     

  • We offer Biosol Mix 7-2-3 Organic Fertilizer and Planters II Trace Mineral Fertilizer as microbe and plant sustaining nutrient sources that are applied to the soils with standard spreader equipment (drop spreaders or broadcast spreaders).  Biosol Mix 7-2-3 will promote the growth of beneficial fungi and bacteria in the soils.  Planters II Trace Mineral Fertilizer provides all the important trace minerals needed by plants and brought into the plants by mycorrhiza fungi.
     

  • BioVam contains mycorrhiza fungi, beneficial bacteria, and trichoderma fungi and is applied under seeds, onto plant roots, or into the ground via probe holes to make contact with the roots of established plants.  Plants who's roots are colonized with mycorrhiza fungi have higher mineral quantities, have vibrant colors (higher vitamin content), produce high quality fruits / flowers / tubers / roots each year, have resistance to environmental stresses, and have higher resistance to soil diseases and destructive insects. 
     

  • Natures Own Microbe Tea Brewing Kits are used to produce beneficial bacteria and fungi to be applied to plant surfaces and as a soil drench.  These organisms are responsible for make nutrients available to plants. 
     

  • Yucca Extract is added to the Microbe Tea after the Microbe Tea is brewed and helps plant cells be more permeable to nutrient and water uptake. 
     

  • Garden Sales Kit:  All of the above products can be purchased at a 25% discount when you purchase our garden sales kit. 

Two kinds of resources are added to soils in order to grow high quality plants. 

1 - Soil organisms are added to process nutrients for food and into food for the plants. (BioVam and Microbe tea + Yucca Extract)

2 - Fertilizer nutrients are added as food for soil organisms and are processed into plant food. (Biosol and Planters II).

The result: Plants achieve greater health, productivity, size, yields, brix levels, nutrient value, taste and after harvest sustainability.

Natures Own Growing System Tea Brewers are manufactured by T&J Enterprises.

The ingredients in Natures Own Growing System Microbe Tea Brewers consist of high performing Aerobic and faculative soil bacteria which fix nitrogen from the air, solubilize minerals, and reduce organic material into plant food.  Trichoderma fungi are also present in our Tea Brewing Kits. This product was designed by T&J Enterprises to work in conjunction with our BioVam product which feeds the nutrients into your plants' roots and with our two Organic fertilizers (Biosol Mix 7-2-3 All Purpose Fertilizer and Planters II Trace Mineral Fertilizer).
