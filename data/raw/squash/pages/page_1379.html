If you are in 7th grade, a senior in high school, or anywhere in between, we have a place for you. We welcome you to see what makes us an exciting place for students. We believe in the importance of connecting with other Christians your age. Sunday School, Evangelist Events, YMT, Squash ins, is how we meet the need of unifying our students for one purpose: reaching up, reaching out and reaching in as we grow in our relationship with Jesus Christ!

Hello. My Name is Adam Ganong. I have the privilege of being the Youth Pastor   at Devon Park Baptist Church in Fredericton New Brunswick. If you you live in the area feel free to drop in and visit! Or if you have MSN Messenger you can drop me a message at  adamdpb2003@hotmail.com

God's word says: “Let no man despise thy youth; but be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity.” 1 Timothy 4:12

We want to see young people come to know Jesus Christ and then be used by him to reach others. That is why I chose 1 Timothy 4:12. Teens and children alike can be used by God to reach others. We want to train them up so they will know how to live a Godly life and serve him with all of their heart. I encourage you to take a look at our links and our events. Everyone is welcome at our church and is welcome to come to our youth events. May you know the Joy of the Lord this day!
