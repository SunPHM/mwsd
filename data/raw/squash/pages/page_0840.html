Postgraduate 5-a-side

 

Match Rules

The game is played under the normal rules for Association Football except for the adjustments for the indoor game as detailed below.

Each game is of one 20-minute period or until the end of the time allowed for the match to be played – whichever is the sooner.

Goals can be scored from any part of the pitch.

Teams may use as many substitutes as they wish.

Substitutions can be made only when play has been stopped. i.e. the goalkeeper has the ball or there is a free kick to be taken.

A player substituted may return to the game as a substitute himself.

All four walls may be used but if the ball travels behind the goal it is the goalkeeper’s ball

Players may be ‘Sin Binned’ for up to five minutes if necessary.

The ball must be kept below head height. If not, an indirect free kick to the opposite team from the place where the ball was kicked shall be awarded.

If the ball comes off the ‘keeper and goes high it is “goalkeeper’s ball”.

Players must not use their hands on the wall for support or as an aid to shield the ball.

Goalkeepers can only bowl the ball out under arm. They must not kick it out.

Goalkeepers are not allowed outside their area. If a goalkeeper deliberately comes out of his area a penalty shall be awarded to the opposite side.

No other players may enter the area:-

If an attacker deliberately enters the area, it is the goalkeeper’s ball;

If a defender deliberately enters the area it is a penalty.

Opposition must stand 2 metres away from the ball at free kicks.

A player may take only a 2-step approach to kick a penalty.

No slide tackles are allowed.

Restart after a goal with a throw-out by the beaten goalkeeper.

If the ball goes out of bounds, the ball is given to the 'keeper in the nearest goal to where the ball went out of play, regardless of who kicked it out.

One player can only play for one team.

All matters of dispute should be raised with the League Administrator and his decision is final.
