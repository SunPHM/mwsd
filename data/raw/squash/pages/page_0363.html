Squash Courts
We have six international squash courts for your convenience. Full Members may book a one-hour court for $4 plus GST from 7:00 am (8:30 am on weekends) to last booking at 9:30 pm. Bookings are mandatory for all courts for all members and Squash-Only members.
Note: In addition to the squash court fees day guests must also pay the day user fee of $9 Adults and $6 non-Carleton Student.


When a Squash-Only Member shares a court with a person who is not a Squash-Only Member, a half court fee will be charged.
Courts may be booked up to seven days in advance either in person or by phone (520-5655).


Note: The Squash-Only membership entitles you to unlimited free court time.
