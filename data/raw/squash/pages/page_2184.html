Description:
Sabi River Sun - 4 Star Premium Hotel
Situated along the banks of the Sabi River, the Sabi River Sun is surrounded by magnificent scenery. In close proximity is the world-famous Kruger National Park.

The guestrooms offer luxurious accommodation, with private bathrooms, air conditioning and satellite TV. The resort boasts unrivalled sporting facilities, including an 18-hole golf course, 5 swimming pools, 3 floodlit tennis courts, a bowling green, squash court, volleyball, mountain biking and river rafting (during summer).

Room Types:
Delight in the cosiness of your very own thatched chalet equipped with a superb kitchen, a private courtyard and braai (barbecue) area, fireplace, television, and air-conditioning. Each beautifully decorated chalet sleeps up to six people, with double bedrooms and bathrooms, as well as a private loft.

Tasteful furnishings create a haven of every room. Each of the 60 rooms has a private bathroom, telephone, radio, colour TV with satellite, air-conditioning and electric shaver plug (110/ 220V). Same day laundry service is also provided.
