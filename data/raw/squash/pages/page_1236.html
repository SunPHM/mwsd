Welcome to my website, which offers you access to my catalogues that will make gardening decisions easier than ever.

Ginny's Herbs supplies plants of herbs, old, tried & true non-hybrid vegetables and flowers by mail order throughout New Zealand- also seed potatoes of old & new varieties.Companion planting is also very popular, and these plants are included in all my catalogues.Growing organically is greatly enhanced by using these wonderful plants.

In my catalogues you will find information about the plants that I find so exciting and fulfilling to work with. Herbs have been with us for hundreds of years and there is a herb for every purpose.Many people are now turning to the natural way of eating, gardening and treating illness and I am humbled to be able to help in this small way by making these gems of plants available.

The old, tried and true non-hybrid vegetables and flowers are all strong GE-free types. Many have not been seen for years and I am delighted to be able to help bring them back for you.

Ginny's Herbs also supplies a limited selection of seed grown out by us under controlled conditions to get the truest seed possible-this selection is growing year by year as we build our collection. August is a great time to sort out what you want to plant this season.

See Seed catalogue under Catalogues.

 

Summer is a good time to organise your herb garden and purchase your plants, as all the winter dormant ones are available, and also the summer annuals.

Over the years I have been delighted at the popularity of my herb baskets as presents- not just for Christmas gifts, but for retirements, birthdays, housewarmings, etc! Being a living gift, and with the different types there is a basket for everyone- check them out by going to 'gift baskets' on the above menu.

 

Also available to you is my herb/potager garden design package: I look forward to helping you bring these wonderful plants to your place, along with the herbs to enhance your cooking and health.

$10 and $20 gift vouchers are proving very popular. They can be used towards seed or plants, garden design or gift baskets.

Give a personalised gift voucher as a gift- ideal for a wedding present, new home gift, or for a  gardeners' birthday! Email Ginny for this service.

I have been delighted at the way my business has built up over the last few years, but there is a down side to it as well. I am now so committed to all facets of my business that I am unable to be open to the public at this stage. I am, however, at the Whangarei Growers Market (behind Forum North from 6.00 am to 10.00 am,) every Saturday morning, and would love to meet personally any of you who are up this way. You would really enjoy seeing the Market as well- it is unique in N.Z, in that it is for growers of produce, fruit, flowers and plants only, and it is wonderful to be able to talk directly to the  growers themselves.
