Squashes, we feel, are one of the most under-rated vegetables, easy to grow, very few pests attack the plants, and what is the most important factor, the taste. The flesh is very solid with a sweet nutty flavour, you can use them in soups, stews or can be roasted whole and stuffed with many combinations of savoury fillings and meats.

Squashes may be used when the skins are tender, but when mature can be stored for up to three months in a cool dry area.
