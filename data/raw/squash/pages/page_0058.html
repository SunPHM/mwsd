Where can I play?

There are a number of clubs in the county that provide opportunities for adults and juniors from beginners through to advanced. For more information, visit: www.intosport.org.uk.

Junior Development

Junior Player Development Squads sessions:
  • are usually held at three venues around the county
  • take place from autumn until spring
  • are funded by Nottinghamshire County Council
  • provide additional coaching to young people to help them progress to County standard.
Players are encouraged to take part in graded tournaments, which provide opportunities to experience match play in a friendly environment.
There are a number of county squads for both boys and girls.
Squad members receive advanced coaching and often represent the county in tournaments.
