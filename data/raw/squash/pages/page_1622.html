Welcome to Waldvogel's Farm Online

Located just minutes from the world famous Horicon Marsh in Dodge County -- Open May through October Waldvogel’s has been offering homegrown fun for over 25 years. What started as a small stand is a thriving farm market (Dodge County’s largest) offering the freshest in produce and farm fun, starting in May with Asparagus and continuing on with strawberries, fresh garden peas and our famous super sweet corn picked daily from our fertile fields. Waldvogel’s also offers an excellent line of jams, jellies, salad dressings, and other fine products. We also have an on-farm bakery featuring homemade pies and turnovers. The mini golf opens in July and is open every day through the end of October. Waldvogel’s also offers picnic facilities and campsites for companies and groups. Our fall activities begin on September 24 and continue through October 31. Our hours are Monday through Friday 9 a.m. - 6 p.m.; Saturdays 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.; Sundays 11 a.m. - 5 p.m. Weekend hayrides begin at 11 a.m. Weekdays there is a hayride at 5 p.m. weather permitting. Other fun activities at Waldvogel's include a large Corn Maze, the Lost Mine, the Haunted Granary, the Hay Barn, Mini Golf, the Trike Track, Barnyard Buddies including the Bunnyville, Halloween displays, Pumpkin Lookout and the Giant Mine Shaft Slide, and the Beaver Creek Gemstone Mining. Pan for real gemstones you get to keep!

Each fall visitors will be able to take a hayride out to the pumpkin patch, navigate their way through a large corn maze, visit with farm animals, jump in the hay barn, and check out the Halloween displays in The Back Forty. For the very brave, a walk through the Haunted Granary awaits. You can explore the Lost Mine, and children 8 yrs old and younger can race around the Trike Track. You can try your skills at flinging apples at our target at our Apple Launch, and climb Pumpkin Lookout to survey the area and see how many geese you can see, and slide down the Giant Mine Shaft Slide. Don’t forget to play a round of mini golf. Try your luck at panning for real gemstones at Beaver Creek Mining. Stop by the Old Chuck Wagon Grill for fresh grilled sandwiches and other goodies and join us for the "Original Tailgate Party". Then make your way to the Apple Kitchen for Waldvogel’s famous caramel apples and apple split supremes made to your specifications, or a slice of homemade apple pie & ice cream. Dad can keep track of the big game on our large screen TV.

Before heading home, don’t miss the chance to fill the car with fresh fall produce, including Wisconsin apples, squash, and potatoes; homemade bakery and other goodies, and fall decorations and crafts.

Waldvogel’s is also a favorite choice for school and bus tours.

Waldvogel’s is located between Beaver Dam & Horicon on County Road I, 1.25 miles north of Hwy 33. Call 920-885-9590 or 1-800-589-9590.
