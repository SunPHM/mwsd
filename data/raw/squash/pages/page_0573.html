Our Club is conveniently located just a short
drive from Canberra or Batemans Bay and
the South Coast of NSW. The relaxed
atmosphere and country hospitality offers
members and their invited guests the
chance to escape from the hustle and
bustle of every day life.

 

Enjoy a game of golf on our 9 hole course

Whether you are looking for a round of golf,
a delicous meal, or drink with friends, the
Braidwood Servicemens Club boasts a
wide range of recreational and sporting
facilities to just about cater for every taste.

Inside the clubhouse we have 7 beers on
tap, 19 television channels, including Austar &
Sky Racing Club Keno, ATM
and EFTPOS.
