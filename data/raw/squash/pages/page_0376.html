Delashe Investments, LLC, managed by long time Placitas residents, Tom Ashe and Steve Gudelj, is proud to announce its newest community, Anasazi Trails featuring the best land and the best views in Placitas! Their previous projects include Placitas Trails, Tierra Madre, La Mesa, Sundance Mesa, Sky Mountain and Desert Mountain.

These communities are shining examples of how homes can blend into the landscape rather than dominate it. Tom and Steve have earned the reputation over the past three decades as developers with an ecological conscience. Stringent covenants and restrictions require that as little vegetation and land as possible would be disturbed beyond the building site, and builders are required to revegetate areas disturbed during the construction process. Roads meander through the neighborhoods; homes are offset from each other, thoughtfully sited to preserve precious views and privacy.

Anasazi Trails, located north of Placitas Trails and west of Tierra Madre, featuring 102 lots ranging in size from 3/4 of an acre to over 2 acres, is the jewel in the crown for the developers, a culmination of the creativity and experience that has been a driving force in making Placitas what it is today.

Many lots have coveted mountain and mesa views, and are among the most gorgeous lots offered for sale in Placitas. Pricing will be competitive and consistent with lots in the Northeast Heights neighborhoods of Albuquerque. The difference in lifestyle — priceless.

A network of walking trails emphasizes the extent to which this subdivision has been designed and will be built on a "human scale" that encompasses why so many people are attracted to Placitas - the ability to enjoy outdoor life starting at your front door. A tennis court is planned for the residents of Anasazi Trails. In addition, this community will have paved roads, well thought out restrictive covenants, and underground utilities that include high-speed Internet access and digital cable television.

The subdivision has met all requirements and has been approved by Sandoval County. Delashe Investments, LLC has constructed a water cooperative to serve the subdivision along with a planned fire hydrant system.

Recorded copies of the covenants, disclosure statement, and subdivision plat map are available at La Puerta Real Estate Services, LLC. Please call 867-3388 to arrange to pick up a set or have them mailed to you.

We believe that Anasazi Trails will be one of the most beautiful and prestigious communities in all of Placitas, and a wonderful place to hang your hat for many years to come.
