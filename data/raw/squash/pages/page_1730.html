Skiing

I also like skiing a lot, and I try to go skiing as much as I can. I've been skiing in Austria (Axamer Litzum and Kaltenbach, Zillertal), France (Tignes/Val d'Isere and Val Thorens/Les Trois Vallees), the US (Georgia, North Carolina, West Virginia, and Summit County, Colorado), and Canada (Blackcomb mountain).

In 1999 I went skiing in Serre Chevalier in France, and in Haute Nendaz near Verbier in Switserland.

2000 was a French year exclusively, and also a year I only skied in March. Nicer weather than January, but also lesser snow quality. I ended up skiing in Les Deux Alpes and La Plagne.
