Squash Courts

Squash is a game of skill, co-ordination, fitness, and fun, and at the centre we have four championship-standard squash courts where you can enjoy the game.

There are a number of different leagues that run in the centre throughout the year. These include the Sports Union club, inter mural, summer leagues, and the squash ladder.

For booking information, contact reception.

For clubs, contact Sport Union.
