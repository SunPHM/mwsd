A versatile artist, Bucquet creates a limited number of large scale cast and carved glass sculptural pieces.

He achieves a color palette that ranges from subtle to bold, using various formulations of metallic oxides and rare earth elements such as gold, cobalt, neodymium, copper and others blended into the molten glass. When completed, each piece is individually signed and dated.
