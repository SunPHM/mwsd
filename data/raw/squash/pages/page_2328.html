2nd Anual summer Doubles Squash Tournament

Over 50 participants competed in the 2nd Annual Summer Doubles Squash Tournament, July 13th to 18th. The play was surprisingly good for summer (apparently Tim Kehler trained and peaked for this event!) and everyone had a great time off the courts enjoying the around-the-clock barbeque and basking in the sun with their souvenir lawn chair.

A few highlights on the courts including the mens open finals as winners, Winston Cabell and Bob Smart, beating Scott Kidd and Tim Kehler after being down 4 match points to beat them 15/14 in the fifth! Another great effort was first timers from out of no where, Ben Block and Stephano Walker of Hollyburn C.C. winning the Men's Div 1. Also, the new team of Penny Wedd and Mark Heaney making their debut by winning the mixed event. A new dynasty in the making? You'll have to ask Penny's husband Bob Smart for the answer to that one. The women's draw was small but probably the fiercest competition in the event as all were fairly evenly matched and anyone could have won. It was also great to see Ruth Castilino back on the courts! Anyway, I would like to thank the following for their help: Keith Jolly / Manuever Services and Events for providing prizes for the winners and the silent auction; Don Smerch / Freybe Sausages for providing food for the barbeque and wine the and cheese party and getting the keg of bear from Bowen Island Brewery; Marv Mizinski for helping with the draw; and Cia Van Horne for making up the entry forms. Also a special thanks for Holyburn Country Club for the use of their court and the BC Doubles Squash Association for providing free entries for "first timers".

The results are as follows:

Men's Open - Winston Cabell/Bob Smart def. Scott Kidd/Tim Kehler (3/2)

Women's Open - Nicola Martin/Frances Frost def. Karen Levine/Penny Wedd (3/0)

Men's Division 1 - Ben Block/Stephano Walker def. Greg Desaulinier/Don Smerch (3/2)

Men's 40+ - Dennis Hoffman/Lyn Broman def. Rob Forest/Iain Saville (3/0)

Mixed - Hark Heany/Penny Wedd def. Scott Kidd/Karen Levine (3/0)

Thanks to all participants and we'll see you next year.

Paul Marley and Dave Adams to World Masters

Paul Marley and Dave Adams will be participating in the World Masters Squash Championships in Sheffield, England in August 1999. David, who has participated in the same event in Edingburh on 1993 and Melburne in 1995 where he reached the last eight, will be playing in the 55 plus category. Paul who reached the quarter finals of the US National Championships in 5 stage group will be competing in the 60 plus category.

Best of luck, Paul and David.
