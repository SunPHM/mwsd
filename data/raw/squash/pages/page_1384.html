CLEAN UP NEW ZEALAND 2003 

AN UPDATE FROM LAKE PUPUKE 

For Clean Up NZ Week this year Neptunes Underwater Club joined forces with the Dolphin Underwater & Adventure Club for their annual lake and quarry cleanup. Some of the Dolphins dived the lake while the rest did a kayak foreshore cleanup. 


Neptunes and Dolphins

The amount collected this year was  a little down compared to previous years but still included the usual tyres, polystyrene, golf balls, squash balls, tennis balls, plastic bags, drink containers etc. 


The Rubbish Starts To Grow

However, something is sure to be found that has not before, this year a couple of items. An outrigger canoe outrigger (a fairly valuable piece, wonder if they tipped out when they lost it), and a TV screen with baseboard. A successful day was finished off with a BBQ and the usual refreshments.
