Green Hotel
Kinross, by Loch Leven, Perthshire, Scotland


General Details

 It is well over two hundred years since colourful horse drawn coaches were seen and heard bustling in the forecourt of The Green Hotel, yet today's visitor will still find much to commend this former coaching inn.

The same air of expectancy and excitement felt by those early travellers can be experienced by current guests.  The rich comfort and fine amenities of this family owned hotel are waiting to be discovered.

There can be few privately owned hotels in Scotland able to compete with the variety of sporting and leisure facilities offered at The Green.

Accommodation
The hotel offers double or twin bedded rooms, along with family suites and four-poster rooms.

The comfortable, traditionally furnished and spacious bedrooms are all well appointed, with modern facilities.

All bedrooms have:

  • private en-suite facilities
  • direct dial telephone
  • colour television with satellite
  • radio
  • hairdryer and trouser press
  • tea and coffee making facilities

    Ground floor rooms, suitable for use by disabled guests, are in the new 'traditionally styled' wing which surrounds a courtyard garden.


    • Food and Drink

    		 The Defiance Bar is open all day and serves a wide range of hot and cold meals in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    A children's menu is available for younger guests.

    The Cocktail Bar overlooks the fountain in the courtyard garden and is just the place to enjoy that pre-dinner drink, whilst choosing from the restaurant menus.


    On these you will find a choice of continental dishes and traditional Scottish fayre.

    A comprehensive wine list is available, with something to appeal to everyone.

    Location

    		 The Green Hotel is just 5 minutes away from junction 6 of the M90 motorway, 35 minutes north of Edinburgh.

    The small market town of Kinross is well connected to the capital, and this hotel makes a fine rural base from which to explore the surrounding countryside.

    Trains from the nearby station run direct to Edinburgh and Express coaches linking all the main centres stop at the hotel door.

    Leisure

    		 The Green Hotel has an enviable reputation when it comes to leisure.  The leisure complex features a large heated indoor swimming pool with attractive pool-side lounge and sauna.

    There is also a fitness area, squash courts and a games room, with a pool table and table tennis table.

    In season, you can even have a go at curling in the hotels 4 sheet curling rink!
    You can wander through 3 acres of landscaped garden with herbaceous borders, putt on the putting green, play croquet on the lawn, or have a game of tennis on the hotel's 2 'all-weather' courts.
    A five minute drive away is Loch Leven, famous world-wide for its trout fishing.  Here the hotel has 48 boats for anglers to fish on this beautiful loch, which also has historic connections with Mary Queen of Scots.

    Just a short iron from the front door you will find the first tees of the hotel's two scenic 18 hole golf courses.

    Both offer an enjoyable and stimulating challenge whether you're a holiday golfer or a low handicap player.  Preferential rates are offered to residents.

    Many other well known and respected courses including, St Andrews, Gleneagles, Carnoustie, Rosemount and Ladybank are within 50 miles.

    This area is steeped in history and there are many sites of particular interest to the visitor, including the fine landscaped gardens of Kinross House.

    A short a boat trip takes you to Loch Leven Castle, where Mary Queen of Scots was held prisoner in 1567.
