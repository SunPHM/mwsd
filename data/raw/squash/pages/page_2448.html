Troy Waterfront Farmers Market

I cant say enough about the Troy Waterfront Farmers Market. I am a huge supporter of locally grown produce/products and this is the place to get them! 

The Market is open Saturdays from 9am - 1pm in the Hedley parking lot on River Street. This time of year they offer some produce, apples, cider, honey, meats, cheeses, bread, spices and much more.  As we enter into the summer months, more vendors appear with fresh cut wild flowers and a wider range of produce from tomatoes to squash. 


On May 31st and July 19th, I will be doing a cooking demo at the market using the products available. On May 31st I will be tossing up a terrific salad using the fresh greens, herbs and other exciting ingredients as well as a market fresh dressing.


On July 19th, bring your sombreros to the market, its salsa time! Ill be making a super fresh salsa that will make you crave more!



For more information on the market or to see their Calendar of Events, click on the link below!

Hope to see you at the Market!

Http://www.troymarket.org/calendar.asp
