Laguna Beach Resort, nestled between a tropical lagoon and the sparkling Andaman Sea, features a stunning array of sports & leisure facilities including a four-acre water park.

   

From relaxing and enjoying the Thai hospitality to trying your hand at any number of leisure and sporting activities, Laguna Beach Resort, Phuket offers an unforgettable experience that will leave you feeling refreshed and relaxed.

   

However please note that the use of other Laguna Phuket hotels sports and leisure facilities may be restricted or subject to a charge.

You will be welcomed at Laguna Beach Resort, Phuket: a dream resort on the beautiful island of Phuket, where nothing has been spared to bring you the finest in accommodation, facilities, dining and relaxation.

   

Laguna Beach Resort, Phuket, offers a new concept - sheer quality without formality, in an exotic tropical setting with an almost unlimited choice of leisure activities at no additional cost.

   

Located on a prime land at Bang Tao Bay and only a 20 minute drive from Phuket International Airport. This low-rise Resort spreads out over 20 acres, embracing typical Thai architecture and blending with the local landscape and culture.

The perfect location for business conferences as well as holiday breaks.

   

We believe you deserve the height of luxury, and Laguna Beach Resort offers you full 5-star style accommodation; it’s the perfect ending to each day of fun, relaxation and pleasure.

We are making every effort to conserve scarce resources on the Island and your help is appreciated. Water is scarce, please use it conservatively. There is energy saving key system in each room.     Angsana Spa Laguna Beach Resort is nestled in the quieter part of Laguna Beach Resort, located on Bang Tao Bay in Phuket, Thailand. It focuses on the use of aromatherapy and the sense of touch to rejuvenate the body, mind and spirit.

    It has 28 exclusive treatments featured on its spa menu ranging from Angsana Massage, Natural Thai Herbal Wrap and Fresh Coconut Rub to Peace and Spirit packages. These rejuvenating remedies are derived from natural ingredients such as native flowers, fruits, spices and plants. Drawing inspiration from traditional Asian healing therapy, these treatments are further infused with a modern twist to suit the contemporary lifestyle of the Angsana spa-goers.

    There is a total of eight private outdoor spa pavilions at Angsana Spa Laguna Beach Resort. Scattered amidst an eclectic mix of breathtaking scenery, these pavilions feature a mesmerizing lagoon view, two landscaped private gardens and one exquisite lotus pond. The pavilions are named after indigenous herbs and spices, ranging from Basil and Mint to Plai and Turmeric.

    From the moment you enter the Angsana Spa, your senses will be awakened by the vibrant décor complemented with refreshing hues of lime green, yellow, tangerine and violet. The artistic interior design coupled with inspiring colours, reflect the Angsana Spa’s core philosophy that emphasizes on a vivacious outlook on life.

    If you wish to replicate the Angsana Spa experience in the privacy of your own home, specially designed spa accessories can be purchased at the Angsana Gallery, which is located within the Spa's reception area.

    Another unique feature of Angsana Spa Laguna Beach Resort is the al fresco spa cuisine restaurant, christened Nutmegs. The spa cuisine menu has a selection of appetizing food renowned for their health and medicinal properties; which completes the spiritual and physical dimension of the Angsana Spa experience.
