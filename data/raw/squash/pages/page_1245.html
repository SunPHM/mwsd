Squash - English Vegetable Marrow

A source of vitamins A and C, Calcium and Iron. 6-8 seeds / gram.
Hints:
  • Sow when soil is warm.
  • Sow 4 to 6 seeds 2 cm [1"] deep in hills 1.5 to 2 m [4-6'] apart.
  • Thin the best two or three plants.

    • English Vegetable Marrow - 52 days. Bush type. A favorite variety. Fruits are oblong and creamy white. Start seeds out-of doors after danger of frost, in a warm sunny location with enriched soil. Flesh is pale green, mild and sweet.
