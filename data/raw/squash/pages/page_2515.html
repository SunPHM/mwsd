Amla Juice
Amla is the richest natural source of Vitamin C and necessary for blood, bones, skin and brain. It is helpful in acidity and keeps blood, liver and kidney clean and also helps in controlling high cholesterol. As an antioxidant, amla juice prevents damages caused by pollution, smoking, alcohol etc. It keeps one healthy and energetic while offering the stamina to work longer and reduces wrinkles and makes the skin glow.

Amla Panchamrut
Panchamrut brings to you the cumulative strength of five herbal ingredients amla, tulsi, pudina, ambehaldi and ginger. Besides strengthening the immune system, it is effective in joint pains, arthritis, asthmatic conditions and respiratory disorders. It is also an excellent tonic that rejuvenates the body.
