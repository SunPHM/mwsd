Jackson School Community Garden

  • Contact name: Mr. Jackson
  • Contact Number: 583-8436
  • Garden Location: Jackson School, 1798 Summit Ave.
  • Number of Plots: 1
  • Average Plot Size: 50' x 70'
  • Number of Gardeners: 30 per class

What do gardeners grow: amaranth, tomatoes, peppers, collard greens, squash, beans...

The Jackson School Community Garden is planted by Jackson School students in grades five througth eight. The lot is located right across the street from the school and was acquired throught the Adopt-A-Lot program. Mr. Waley, a science teacher, taught his students about gardening in the class room, and then let them practice what they learned in the garden. The students decided which plants to grow, and also harvested and ate what they grew. Last year was the first year, and they are looking forward to continuing next year.
