Three indoor Plexicushion courts and two of the best maintained red clay courts in Northern New Hampshire

Kids' After-School Clinics

Youth lessons, organized by age. Help your child develop confidence and skills while meeting new friends. Weekly after-school lessons.

    $14/hour
    Five weekly lessons: $63 members & $90 non-members
    Summer Camp: full-day & half-day camps

Private/Semi-Private


Private lessons. Go one-on-one with a tennis pro. Work on any aspect of your game — serve, ground strokes, net play, strategy. You decide which part of your game you'd like to improve.

    $50/hour members
    $60/hour non-members

Semi-private lessons. Share an hour with a friend. Practice your strokes and play out points under the watchful eye of the instructor.

    $25/hour members
    $40/hour non-members

Round Robins

Mixed doubles. Round robins organized by skill level of players. Weekends and evenings. Call for scheduled times.

Men's Doubles

Sunday mornings, 9:30-11:00 a.m. Call the front desk or sign up at Four Seasons bulletin board.

Men's Singles Ladder

Matches arranged at your convenience. A and B levels. Call to sign up for ladder.

Seasonal Tournaments & Leagues

Challenge the best players in the area, or just sign up to have some fun. Call for dates and details.
