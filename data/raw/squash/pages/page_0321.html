Facilities

Squash

The University has three squash courts. These are situated on Princess Road East opposite the School of Education Car Park.

The courts are built to S.R.A. specification and have changing facilities for both men and women attached to them. There is a small balcony upstairs to allow viewing of the courts. The courts and associated facilities were fully refurbished in 2004.

The student squash club meet at the courts every Tuesday and Thursday evening for mix-in play, coaching and play their matches.

In addition to the three University courts there is another squash court within the Student Union, Percy Gee building.

Opening Times:

The squash courts are open for use at the following times throughout the year except during general University closure periods.

Term time & Vacation

Monday to Sunday 9a.m. to 9p.m.

Access to the courts is by swipe card. A personal swipe card for you to keep is available on production of a valid Sportscard at the Charles Wilson and Manor Road Sports Halls, The Sports Association Office within the Percy Gee Building and greenhouse 2 Health & Fitness Club. A £2 deposit is required if you have a student sportscard.

Bookings:

All of our indoor facilities are booked on a rolling one week in advance basis, that is the booking sheet for this day next week opens today.

Bookings for the squash courts must be made in person at the Squash Courts on Princess Road. There is no facility for telephone booking of squash courts.

Bookings last for 40 minutes and £1 is needed to turn on the lights for the session. The light meters are situated adjacent to the courts. Please note that if there are people booked and waiting to come onto court at the start of their session then you are asked to vacate the court and not wait until your light money has run out!

In vacation the booking cards for the forthcoming weekends are put up the Monday before.
Due to the limited number of courts available at the University, squash is only available to those who have a sportscard. Pay-as-you-play is not available for this activity.
