Lynfield Recreation Centre - YMCA

Address: Griffen Park Road, Mt Roskill
Phone: (09) 627 1642
Fax: (09) 627 1254
Email: lynfield@ymca.org.nz
Opening hours:
Monday to Thursday   6am - 10pm
Friday 6am - 8.30pm
Saturday 7am - 6pm
Sunday 9am - 9pm

The Lynfield Recreation Centre has an indoor sports stadium and four squash courts which enable opportunities for all types of indoor sporting needs including soccer, bowls, badminton, and volleyball.

The facility also has a fitness centre with top quality equipment and professional instructors offering quality exercise and aerobics programmes.

There are heaps of kids programmes available - Y's pre-school and after-school gymnastics, junior basketball, karate, squash, tennis and badminton clubs, and of course school holiday programmes

The centre is ideal for celebrations and meetings with our special birthday party stadium hire, function room and attached courtyard available.

A creche is available on site for facility users.

The facility is run in partnership with YMCA.
