To the Courts!
The Pickering Recreation Complex offers a variety of Squash activities and is proud of its team of dedicated Squash professionals who are always available to answer your questions and help you to improve your game.

Lessons
Both private and semi-private lessons are available for both adults and juniors.

Ladies Round Robin
Women of all levels are invited to participate in the Thursday morning fun.

Women's Pre-School Program
While you learn the game from a pro, your child can be steps away participating in a special, professionally supervised, arts and crafts program.

Women's Intermediate Clinic
Developed for those who have some Squash experience, this Tuesday morning clinic allows individuals to further develop their skills in a group setting.

Adult Beginner Clinics
This program provides both the Racquet and protective eyewear needed for those just starting out.

Junior Beginner Clinic (age 12 and under)
Intended for children interested in learning the basic rules and techniques of Squash.

Junior Intermediate Clinic (age 11 +)
Intended for children who show strong hand-eye co-ordination, and who may have played Squash before.
