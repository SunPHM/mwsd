Visions of Vegetables

Not that anyone cares, but I thought I would keep a running record of the growth of my summer vegetables here on the website. I was inspired by the website of the National Zoo, which has a web camera in with the lions. I often watch it at work, but I've never seen a lion (camera shy). Hopefully you will see some vegetables here later in the summer! Maybe we can have a feast after the crops come in.
