U-Pick Vegetables and Fruits

Available as Mother Nature allows. Listed is a sampling of our fruits and vegetables, which are also available at the fruit stand for purchase if you are unable to enjoy the generosity of time to pick them yourself.


Our fruit stand also stocks special and unusual fruits and vegetables.

U-Pick

Vegetables – Tomatoes, Peppers, Cucumbers, Pickles, Egg Plant, Summer Squash (assorted varieties).

Fruits - Apples, Peaches, Pears, Plums, Cherries, Strawberries, Raspberries, Blackberries, Grapes, Currant, Gooseberries

WE-PICK

These items will be harvested by our staff and made available for your purchase at the fruit stand:
Asparagus, Rhubarb, Onions, Beets, Green Beans, Dill, Super Sweet Corn.

BEE-HEALTHY

The Fruit Stand is delighted to be able to offer you healthy fresh honey, manufactured by our staff of busy honeybees, who work tirelessly to produce pure home raised honey. You’ll feel as energetic as they do.
