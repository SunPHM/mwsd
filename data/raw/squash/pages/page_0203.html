Barnton CC Squash Section

Members of the

The Squash Section, formed in 1985, boasts two private, now centrally-heated squash courts that are considered by many to be the best in the area. The section fields four competitive teams: three mens teams in the SRA Winter Leagues and one team in Summer Leagues. For our Junior Section, one of the best supported in Mid-Cheshire, coaching and practice sessions take place on Friday evenings and Saturday mornings throughout the year, conducted by SRA-qualified coaches Carl Adamson and Harry Hardy.
