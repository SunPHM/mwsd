Bookings

Booking a squash court: 020-6267883
Squash courts can be booked on a daily basis by telephone or at our reception desk. A playing period at Squash City is 45 minutes. Because of our booking system, bookings can be made up to 7 days in advance; 2 players can book a maximum of 1 period per day. Cancellation of a squash court booking must be done no later than 12 hours before the booking. See squash rules for the other conditions and for instructions about how the game is played.

Booking a regular court
We offer you the possibility to book a court for 1 period per day in advance over a longer time. For more information on bookings, please contact our reception: 020-6267883.

Bringing a guest player
When you, as a member, wish to bring a guest player you are required to first check in with reception before you enter the squash court. The guest player will be charged the court fee that applies at that moment.

Booking cancellations: 020-6267883
Cancellation of a squash court booking must be done at reception no later than 12 hours before the booking. Failure to cancel will result in a late booking fine of € 10,00 per period, to be paid by the person whose name is on the court booking. This applies to both members and non-members. Failure to cancel courts on time and/or failure to pay the relevant fine may lead to your right to book a court being revoked.

Memberships
Squash City offers different memberships and prices during daytime (off-peak hours) and during the evening (peak hours). For students we have a special rate during daytime. For the complete price list and the different applicable rates see memberships.
