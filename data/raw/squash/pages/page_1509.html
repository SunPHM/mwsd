Philosophy
The Junior Varsity squash team invites players that are looking to continue to improve their current level of play. Players work on fundamentals and strategies of the game, with the overall goal of progressing to the Varsity level. Through a variety of drills and game scenarios, players learn the mental aspects of the game in addition to stroke mechanics. The focus of the season is to set and accomplish goals that the individual player and the team as a whole create.

Coach's History
Karen McKenzie is in her second year coaching the Junior Varsity Squash team.

Facilities
Junior Varsity Squash plays on three American Squash courts located in the Crisp Athletic Center.

Playing Season
The season begins with try-outs on November 15th and runs until the end of February. We play approximately 14 matches throughout the season.

Main Competitors
Deerfield, Choate, and Westover.
