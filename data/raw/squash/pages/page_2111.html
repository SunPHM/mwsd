all year round  


Usedom isn´t just a holidayparadise in summer. In the other 9 month of the year we have the best possibilities for you to relax, too.                                                                                  Whenever you want to visit Usedom - You will find peculiarities everytime of the year. 

Usedom is with 1906 hours of sunshine the place with the most sunshine of the year in Germany. Usedom has a lot more to offer apart from Sun, Sea and 40 km of fine white sand, Dunes, forests, moors, wide meadows, fields, rare plants and animals, different types of coast as well as interresting architekture, seabridges, promenades and much, much more.....  . The speciality of the Island of Usedom is the great variety of landscape and the change from salt- to freshwater between Baltic Sea and Peenestrom. Since December 1999 Island of Usedom is a nature reserve

There isn´t just nature for our guests. Those who come to Usedom can expect History, Culture, Sport and more than 2500 events in a year. Just to name a few: "herring-week" in spring, Vikingcamp on Whitsun as well as fashion events in seaside resort Heringsdorf. And there is something for everybodies taste in the wide range of cultural events like VINETA, shows in Theatre-tent "Chapeau Rouge", "Blechbüchse, the yellow theatre in Zinnowitz, in the halls of Heringsdorf, Zinnowitz and on all stages along the coast. The traditon of the Usedom Musicfestival and the Tüften- Tage ( patatoes days) in September stay for the cultural variety in fall.

Everybody can do some sport like riding a bike, hiking, sailing, windsurfing, golfing and tennis. Usedom is perfect for hiking, walks or a visit in many museums or churches. You still can be flexible and move around even though you don´t have a car. Next to 50km well made cycling tracks you can use the "Usedomer Bäder Bahn", which is going from Züssow straight onto the island and to the border to Poland in Ahlbeck. This will make your arrival a lot more easier. This enables you to use nighttrains. The flight-connections to Augsburg will be working from May till September.

You will find an accomadation in hotels and boarding houses with a special kind of architecture, called " Bäderarchitektur", in compfortable appartements, cosy private rooms and on nice campinggrounds.

Infomation, a travel-guide called " Handicapped Reisen" and some more material to plan your holiday on Usedom is available at Tourismusverband Insel Usedom e. V. servicenumber 24/7:   01805 / 873366 (12 ct. / minute )

 

Summer  


... that means in the first place Sun, Beach and sailing.               To the cultural enrichment there are a lot of open air-events on the stages all over Usedom, summertheatre in Blechbüchse and in Chapeau Rouge, beach parties, summerfestivals and so on.

spring / fall  

Seabridges and promenades are inviting you for a walk not only in summer. Hikes along the beach or through the forest, along the coast through the attractiv landscape, horserides or shipcruises- all this has its peculiar charm in the off-peak season. 

Take a walk on one of our hiking tracks or cycle along 150 km cyclingtracks along the coast or in the inland.


Visits to one of the wonderful churches or to one of the interresting museums are always worth its time. We have all kinds of museums, from " Historisch-technischen Informationszentrum " ( historical-technical information center ) to a 2 wheel- museum in Dargen.

winter  

Even though it isn´t possible to do ship cruises or cycling-tours because of snow and ice, you will find out about the peculiar charm of the Island of Usedom in winter. For example you are able to do some  foresthikes, beachwalks or tours with the sleigh, with a warming picnic in between. 


Visits to one of the wonderful churches or to one of the interresting museums are always worth its time. We have all kinds of museums, from " Historisch-technischen Informationszentrum " ( historical-technical information center ) to a 2 wheel- museum in Dargen.

Many hotels on Usedom are offering rooms at an surprisingly low price. A double bedroom with breakfast for 49 Euro.

Culture is a thing which is offered to the guests all year. For example the theatre with its funny name " Blechbüchse " ( can ) has a very interresting and big winterprogram.  In the forum " Villa Irmgard " in Heringsdorf and in many other hotels there are many more events waiting to be visited.

Nice food and drinks are availabel the whole year. Especially in the cold seasons our chefs have new ideas and creations. How would you like a  " Goeth-menu " or a meal out of  the 20´s? 

There is no lack of possibilities to do some sport. There are Ostseetherme (indoor swimming pool) with sauna and a great and beautiful rest area in the seaside resort Ahlbeck as well as small swimming pools in Lütow, Koserow and Zinnowitz to go swimming. The beauty and wellness offers of many hotels are usually in winter the best. In the sportcenter " Sportpoint" in Trassenheide you can play tennis, squash or badminton. Also availebal is a big fitness center and bowling.


The 18- hole gulf course in Balm is of course also useable in winter. Friends of riding will be faszinated by the possibility to do long rides along the beach.

If you think you are lucky in playing games you can try yourself in the casino Heringsdorf.
