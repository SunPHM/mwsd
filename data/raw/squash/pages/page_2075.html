  8th Annual
 FALL HARVEST FESTIVAL

This year's festival will be bigger and better than ever featuring all of the activities from past years plus many, many more.  For the first year ever, Underwood Family Farms joins the Newbury Park Rotary
for Oktoberfest on October 1st & 2nd.

  Back by popular demand, is our 2nd annual Cowboy Weekend! There will be gunfighters, stunt ropers, country music, cowboy poetry, a medicine show and much more! 

  As always, we will carry numerous varieties of gourds, winter squash, Indian corn, and other fall decorating accessories available for purchase, as well as a large selection of pumpkin carving accessories.  And it wouldn't be a Fall Festival without acres and acres of pumpkins! 

As in past years, the 2005 festival will be open from 9:00 am until 6:00pm October 1st through October 29th, and from 9:00 am until 5:00 pm starting on the 30th. Admission to the festival is  $5.00 per person the first 2 weekends & $7.50 per person the last 3 weekends.   Monday through Friday, admission is $3.00 per person. Children under 2 are free. On the weekends, each person will receive a tractor-drawn wagon ride with their paid admission. Plus- Parking is FREE.  We are proud contributors to our local non-profit organization.  A portion of all proceeds helps support 4-H, Pleasant Valley Lions Club, Boy Scouts of America, and several other non-profit organizations. We look forward to seeing you
this year at the festival!
