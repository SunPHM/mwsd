Uptown Boys - Change of Venue

The Uptown Boys will now be playing all of their home fixtures at the Christie Miller Leisure Centre, which is located on the Bowerhill Industrial Estate in Melksham. There may be a few rearrangements needed as their Thursday home nights clash with a Ladies team which also play there. Their captain (Jason Vickers) will confirm details with opposing captains in due course.
