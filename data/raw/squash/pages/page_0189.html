Squash at Squash City

Besides 13 squash courts, Squash City offers several other possibilities to get everything out of the game. These possibilities include; coaching (squash lessons), competition ladders, tournaments, beginners club and our Single Players Club. General squash news and information is posted outside court 5 and on our monitor above reception. We like to keep you informed!

Note: All visitors to Squash City, whether you have a membership or not, are required to first check-in at reception!

Squash Courts

Squash City offers 13 professional glass-backed squash courts; 10 located on the ground floor and 3 on the 2nd floor. On the ground floor we have several lounge areas with comfortable couches. An excellent spot to see others play while you take a rest and relax with a drink and a magazine! Squash City is a busy club and we strongly recommend you book courts in advance.

Find a squash partner

Would you like to know more about our national squash competition, tournaments or club events? See our staff at reception for more information, they are glad to be at your service! Also, check the monitor above reception desk at Squash City to keep up-to-date with upcoming events. Are you looking for a squash partner to play squash with regularly? Look for one on our website and make an appointment with find a squash partner!

Squash competition: not just for professionals

At Squash City we also offer you the opportunity to join the national squash competition. This competition runs from September to March and is open for all year-members. If you prefer not to play, you are always welcome to come and watch one of these exciting matches played by the teams of Squash City.
