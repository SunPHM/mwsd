The Shopwise Book of Records, another first from Shopwise Supercenters, is one of the blockbuster regular promotions which aims to add more fun and excitement to our customers' shopping experience. Special contests are held to complement our product fairs, in the attempt to set record breaking feats. Monthly winners are declared the official entries to the Shopwise Book of Records.

Here they are:
 
 
Longest Receipt Contest
The first record holder of the year, Reynaldo Tamayo was declared the winner in the Longest Receipt contest where he won a cool P50,000 during Shopwise's Home and Household Fair held last January. The longest accumulated tape receipts measured 638 meters or 2,093 feet. Altogether, the receipts measured twice the height of the 320-meter Empire State Building in New York and the 300.51-meter Eiffel Tower in France. In the local scene, it is even taller than Makati's 200-meter PBCom Tower, which is considered to be the country's tallest building.

Longest Hair Contest

Gloria Tuason of San Antonio Village, Makati City just gave Rapunzel a run for her money. Gloria is this year's Longest Hair winner in Shopwise's Haba - haba Doo Contest during the Health and Beauty Fair held from February 15 to March 14, 2003. Her winning tresses measures 208 centimeters or more than six feet long which takes her more than two hours to wash and only five minutes to roll up in a bun. Her locks which was last cut ten years ago earned for herself a cash prize of P50,000

Fresh State Fair

The State Fair, a brainchild of Shopwise Supercenters was held at the Filinvest Corporate City in Alabang, Muntinlupa City last March 15, the first-ever State Fair to be held in the country.

One of the highlights of the event was the " Whattababe" pageant, a unique beauty pageant which featured piglets competing for the "Prettiest Pig" title. Ms. Dealco, clad in a pink floral ensemble matched with a cap, pink sunglasses and knitted pink booties, bagged the title. Her undisputable cuteness made her virtually the crowd favorite.

Other events held on that day were the Heaviest Pig competition, which was handily won by Dealco's Pride, an entry from Dealco Farms, who weighed in at 330 kilograms or a whopping 726 pounds. There was also the search for the Tallest Chicken with the winning entry standing at 68 centimeters.

The Largest Squash competition was won by Jairo Bati from Trinidad province with his entry measuring 47 inches in circumference. Meanwhile, Leandro Bautista was awarded the Largest Watermelon whose entry measured 32.5 inches in circumference. The winners in each category received P30,000 in cash.

Watch out for other record breaking feats as Shopwise brings other unusual accomplishments to the fore in its Book of Records promotion.
