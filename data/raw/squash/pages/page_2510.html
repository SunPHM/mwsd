Demske Sports Complex
The Rev. James M. Demske, S.J. Sports Complex was built in 1989, and provides a home field for Canisius College athletics. It has been hailed by the New York Times, USA Today, and the NCAA News as a model for urban campuses. 

The facility is unique for its space utilization in a compact urban setting, and has set the standard for design for other schools with limited land. Built on a 14-acre parcel of land located behind the Koessler Athletic Center, the multi-use, all-purpose, all-weather astroturf field is used for baseball, football, lacrosse, soccer and softball. The turf cushioned by an E-layer, features inlaid lines, a crushed red rubber track, a portable baseball pitcher's mound and breakaway bases. The field, is fully lit for evening activities, and features grandstand seating for 1,000 with additional portable bleachers available to accommodate an extra 150.

Koessler Athletic Center


The KAC consists of a multi-purpose gymnasium, swimming pool, racquetball and squash courts, training room, rehabilitation room, a newly renovated weight room, and several classrooms.  As a part of Canisius College's rebuilding the gymnasium was recently renovated.  The ceiling was removed and an air exchange system installed, the lighting and wiring was upgraded to allow for televised games, and the facility's locker rooms were given a facelift.  The bleachers were replaced with arena chairs, and the floor was refinished.  The capacity of the gym is now 2,570.

Patrick Lee Student Athletic Center
The Patrick Lee Center was built in 1998.  The facility has two full, overlapping basketball courts, three volleyball courts and an indoor batting cage.

Patrick Lee Student Athletic Center panorama

Weight Room
 
The KAC weight room is split into a fitness room and a free weight room. Each has rubber floors and a sound system, and the fitness room has a television.  The cardiovascular room contains 10 treadmills, five stationary bicycles, seven stair climbers, four rowing machines and six elliptical climbers. The free weight room has 15 hammer strength machines and five squat racks with benches.
