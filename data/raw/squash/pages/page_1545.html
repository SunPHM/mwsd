Rambagh Palace Hotel (5 Star Deluxe) Jaipur

Conference and Banquet facilities

The Polo Bar: Adorned with pictures, trophies and memorabilia, the Polo Bar is the perfect blend of the choicest spirits and liqueurs and a magical ambience.

Panghat: For special theme parties and banquets, this amphitheatre is set like a traditional Rajasthani village. Can be booked on request.

Recreation/Health and fitness center

Start with a few laps around our scenic jogging track. Enjoy a game of tennis, squash, badminton or table tennis. Catch up with your routine at the fitness center. Golf and horse riding can be arranged on request.

Laze in our large indoor swimming pool or rejuvenate yourself; courtesy our Jacuzzi, sauna, steam and massages. Leaving matters of destiny to our in-house astrologer. You may even enjoy the Puppet show
