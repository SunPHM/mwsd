Curtin Recreation Centre

The Curtin Recreation Centre (Building 108) is located at the northern end of campus opposite the Bankwest.

The Recreation Centre is equipped with World Class Squash Facilities, including 4 Standard Courts, 4 Glass-backed Courts and WA's ONLY International Exhibition Court.

The Curtin Classic (International Squash Tournament) is held here annually and can be viewed by an audience of up to 250 people in the seated viewing area. The Curtin Recreation Centre also host a variety of in-house sporting competitions, including:

  • King of the Court Squash Competition
  • Team Squash Competition - Squash Wars
  • Intervarsity Squash Competition
  • Intervarsity Table Tennis Competition
  • In-house Table Tennis Competition
  • In-house Racket Sports Competition
  • In-house 3 on 3 Basketball Competition
  • International Rec Games

For more information please phone 9266-7052 or email R.B.Glossop@curtin.edu.au

The Curtin Recreation Centre is open to staff, students and the general public.
It is the home of the Bentley West Squash Club which caters for both social and pennant players alike, with it's members enjoying one of the lowest court hire rates in Perth.

Salary Packaging is available for Curtin staff members.

In-house Squash Competitions

The Curtin Recreation Centre currently organise 2 In-house Squash Competitions. King of the Court Timed Squash, which is a fun exciting competition that involves a good work out and a friendly and fun game of squash. Squash Wars is for players who want to play in a team environment. This competition is for beginners and average players. For more information please email us or phone 9266-7052.

The Curtin Classic presented by Smoke Free WA

The Curtin Classic presented by Smoke Free WA is a World Squash Tournament that is held once a year at Curtin Recreation Centre. The tournament enables Curtin Staff and Students the opportunity to see some of the best players in the world compete. There is also a graded tournament if you were looking at competing. CASH PRIZES!!

Squash Coaching Course

Coaching courses take place on a regular basis - we are proud to have at our centre Chris Lark professional Squash Coach and player. To find out when our next Coaching Course is on please email us or phone 9266-7052. We can also organise smaller group sessions to teach people the rules of squash and teach the basic skills to squash for those who are looking to start playing squash.

Table Tennis

The Curtin Recreation Centre hosts a Table Tennis Competition each semester. The competition is a great way to have some fun and enjoy a game of Table Tennis in a competitive environment. A round robin competition run over 4 weeks during student free time.
