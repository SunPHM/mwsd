Eden Roc Resort & Spa 
.4525 Collins Avenue, Miami Beach, Florida  33140
.
Located in glamorous Miami Beach near the intersection of 45th Street and Collins Avenue, the prestigious Eden Roc Resort & Spa reigns regally in the mostly residential Millionaire's Row.  No place on earth evokes an image of international glamour like Miami Beach.  Here the palm trees sway to the invigorating beat of tropical breezes.  Historic Art Deco buildings, with sweeping curves and bright pastel lights, stand poised against the romantic evening sky ablaze with the oranges and reds of the world famous Florida sunset.  High fashion and immutable style dominate the Miami scene.  Admist this fabulously picturesque skyline lies the oceanfront Eden Roc Resort & spa whose style and status has made it a mecca for the world's rich and famous for years.
Standard Room:  Each spacious, elegantly decorated room reflects the hotel's glamour and eye toward comfort.  Each stylish room boasts two Queen size beds (one King bed upon request if available), private Italian marble bath and individual controlled climate.  Other thoughtful features include a direct dial dataport phone with voicemail, alarm clock, coffee/tea maker with complimentary coffee and tea, hair dryer, iron, ironing board, in-room safe, walk-in closet, mini bar and a remote controlled color cable TV. Guests may request a microwave for an additional fee of $5 per night, a refrigerator for an additional fee of $5 per night and a roll-away bed for an extra fee of $20 per night. (Sleeps max. 4 People)
  The Eden Roc Resort & Spa pampers guests with amenities found only in the best hotels.  During their stay, guests enjoy such plentiful amenities as two heated oceanfront outdoor pools, state-of-the-art glass enclosed fitness center with a cardio theater and weight facilities that overlooks the pools and the ocean, full service Spa of Eden, Swiss showers, daily aerobics classes, sauna, steam room, game room, gift and sundry shop, regulation basketball court, rock climbing wall, squash, racquetball, beach volleyball courts, beauty salon/barber shop, full service concierge, car rental desk, room service, valet laundry service, fax and copy service at the front desk, a business center with fax, copier, computer, printer, internet access, data ports and audio/visual equipment, express check-out, 24 hour security, baby-sitting services (see hotel staff for cost), foreign currency exchange and Scuba instruction.  The hotel also offers private poolside cabanas with TV and phone for rent, an 18 slip private marina and the Victory.  The Victory is the hotel's private yacht which is available for special occasion rentals.  For guests who enjoy the beach, the Eden Roc Resort & Spa boasts its own private beach and offers water sport rentals for Wave Runners, kayaks, Hobi Cat sailboats, aqua bikes and parasailing.
Fine dining is only steps away at the Eden Roc Resort & Spa. Harry's Bar and Grille offers fine dining in a relaxed atmosphere.  This pool side steakhouse features Black Angus steaks, veal, pork, pasta and local fresh seafood in a full service atmosphere for dinner. Harry's also serves a bountiful American breakfast.  When guests are seeking a more laid back Florida casual dining experience there is Jimmy Johnson's Sports Bar and Grille.  This glass enclosed restaurant overlooks the ocean and serves outstanding steaks for lunch and dinner.  The Outerdeck Barat Jimmy's is located on the spacious deck overlooking the ocean and serves light snacks and drinks.  The Huddle Bar is located inside Jimmy's and offers breathtaking views of the ocean as well as menu ordering from Jimmy's menu.  Each Sunday Jimmy's offers a sports brunch.  The Grand Lobby Bar specializes in martinis and boasts over 20 varieties!
