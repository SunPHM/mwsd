Return to Groton for Alumnae and Alumni Games Day 2004!


Sunday, January 18

You and your family are invited to visit the Athletic Center on January 18 for a game of basketball or hockey, or for a squash match. Play with or against your former coaches, current students, and alumnae and alumni.
Childcare is available from 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. (Pre-registration is required.) Activities for your children include swimming, arts and crafts, and caricature drawings.

We look forward to seeing you on January 18.

Please contact Joshua Adams '96 by January 9 if you wish to attend.


Schedule

11:30 a.m. - 1:00 p.m. Lunch in the Dining Hall
1:00 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Alumnae/i Games: Basketball, Hockey, and Squash
1:00 p.m. - 3:00 p.m. Wallace Natatorium and fitness center open
3:00 p.m. - 4:00 p.m. Free Skate
3:30 p.m. – 4:30 p.m. Refreshments in the lobby of the Athletic Center
