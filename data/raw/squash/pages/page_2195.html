Monday, May 19, 2003

Conclusion: I'm a good cook

I just lack "presentation." Tonight I made this really awesome chicken in wine sauce with mushrooms. Now, mind you, if I cook, I do it once a week so I have leftovers. I was quite impressed with my skills (okay, I followed a recipe, ya happy?) So what did I do tonight? I slopped a helping onto my plate and called it dinner. My meal would have been more attractive had I defrosted a friggin' lame-ass Lean Cuisine. Ironically, whenever it's a choice between ordering out or cooking for Whitey, he always orders out.

Mind you, I am an awful housekeeper. Whitey is the one person I let into my apartment when I tell him, "It's a mess!" He gets it. My apartment is just cluttered. And there's a whole bunch of kitty furballs. ( gratuitously cute picture of a kitten.)

Anyhoo, I'd like to make Whitey dinner. I need ideas. He hates brocolli. He likes summer squash and zucchini. And red pepper. I'm not good at cooking meat, with the exception of chicken. No fish! He likes the Mexican and the Italian.

I'm expecting you people to help me! I got a romance goin' on!
