Sunday, 13 October, 2002, 11:12 GMT 12:12 UK
Kemp through to first final
England's Jonathan Kemp, seeded five, beat top seed Tommy Berden in the semi-finals of the Betteridge Squash Trophy in Mamaroneck, USA.

Kemp, a former British under-16 national champion, won 15-11 9-15 15-9 15-6 in 51 minutes to reach his first ever PSA Tour final.

The 21-year-old will play second-seeded Frenchman Gregory Gaultier in the final.

Gaultier beat third-seeded Canadian Viktor Berg 15-6 15-7 15-13 to reach his second final in a month.
