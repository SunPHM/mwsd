Leithead
Field
House
The former home of the Saint indoor track team, Leithead Field House will continue to serve the Saint athletic program as a practice site for teams during inclement weather or the off season. Baseball, softball and lacrosse teams can use its artificial turf for winter practices and intramurals stages indoor soccer tournaments in the building. Its 1/10 mile track is also popular for walking and jogging.

Robie
Squash
Center

The Robie Squash court complex includes six new international squash courts and is home for the Saint men's and women's squash teams. Squash is also a popular recreational sport among St. Lawrence students, faculty and staff, and coach Vijay Chitnis is available for lessons.

Future plans call for the addition of two more courts to the Robie Squash center, giving the Saints one of the finest squash facilities in the Northeast.
