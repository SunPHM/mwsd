PUMPKINS!

 Pumpkins come in all shapes and sizes here at Bishop's.  For the biggest selection you could imagine, board the free hayride and enjoy a pleasant ride to the fields. We are one of just a few places around where you can actually pick your own pumpkin right off the vine.

 You can stay out there as long as you like and board any wagon for the ride back.  If you're one of those people that just have to have the biggest of everything, then head out to the jumbo fields near the train tracks. 

 Our tractor drivers will help you get it to your car (or semi truck) and you can be the envy of all your neighbors when you get that most beautiful pumpkin home.  Come early in the month and you will find a few out there that will weigh as much as 200 pounds.

 Bishop's is also the source for all of your fall decorating items.  Our open air market has pumpkins of all sizes, gourds of every imaginable color, indian corn, popcorn, and winter squash that is just as tasty as it is decorative.  We can even send you home with a bundle of cornstalks for your decorating needs.
