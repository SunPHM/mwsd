Old Actonians Squash Club

The Club was formed in 1976, has a current membership of around 180 and is always on the lookout for new members. The courts are of a high quality and are given a thorough annual maintenance. The squash year starts on 1 October. The subscriptions are very competitive and shown on page 8. Squash members must also be members of the Old Actonians Sports and Social Club.

The Club runs domestic leagues throughout the year and in the winter months run around 15 leagues. The leagues are changed approximately every 6 weeks and provide new members an opportunity to be quickly integrated into the Club.

There are several Club competitions held throughout the season, including a handicap tournament and the Club Championships. The finals are held one night in April followed by the presentation of the various trophies and a social evening.

See Fixtures List for Teams and the divisions that they play in.

This winter we shall be reinstating Club night which will be held on Friday night at least once every month.

New members can join by collecting a membership form from behind the bar.
