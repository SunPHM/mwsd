Stuffed Summer Squash With Ground Meat (Etli Kabak Dolmasý)

Ingredients Measure Amount
Summer squash 6 large size 1250 gr
Salt 2 teaspoon 12 gr
Rice 2/5 cup 75 gr
Onion 2 medium size 120 gr
Parsley 1/2 bunch 30 gr
Dill 1/2 bunch 20 gr
Tomatoe 2 small size 200 gr
Ground Meat (medium fat) 1 3/4 cup 300 gr
Black Pepper 1/2 teaspoon 1 gr
Mint 2 tablespoon 2.6 gr
Water 1 1/4 cup 250 gr
Margarine 1 tablespoon 10 gr

Preparation

Instructions : Remove stem and blossom ends of squash. Cut into half crosswise. Scoop out pulp forming 3-4 mm thick shels. Set aside. Combine ground meat, rice, 1 minced tomato, chopped onions, parsley and dill, mint, salt and pepper. Add 1/4 cup of water mixing thoroughly. Fill squash shells with fýlling. Cut remaining tomato into 6 slices. Press 1 slice of tomato over fýlling, covering completely. Arrange in a shallow saucepan side by side. Heat remaining water. Add to dolmas. Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until squash and rice are tender. Remove from heat. Serve hot.

Servings: 6
