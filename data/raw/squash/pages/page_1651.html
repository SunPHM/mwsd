Fuzion Frenzy

 
 

 

 

Developed by Blitz Games, the company that brought us Walt Disney's the Little Mermaid 2 and published by Microsoft, Fuzion Frenzy is a fast pace action game that takes place in a fantastic city where you and your friend can see who is best at outbashing, outracing and outmaneuvering each other.

You can play Fuzion Frenzy with up to 4 players. First you will need to pick a character to represent you in the game. There is 6 characters to choose from since it is a fast paced game each of them is represented by a specific color so you can identified yourself quickly while you play like Dub is green, Samson is orange, Geena is blue, Zak is yellow, Jet is pink and Naomi is red.

There is 2 game mode Tournament where you will experience the game fully or the Minigame Frenzy where you select one specific minigame to play.

The city is divided in 6 zones. Each zone has a different type of minigames associated with it and each type uses specific controls. In total over 45 minigames to play.

In the Coliseum zone which is dominated by a stadium you will find yourself encased in a giant rolling ball - that is what you see on the cover of the box game - or sitting on a big slab of ice trying to bump and bash your opponent out of the game.

In the Downtown zone they celebrate everyday so will need to create the best fireworks and be the Music DJ and match what the other is doing.

In the Outlands zone you will use construction tools like explosives to blow your opponent.

In the Military zone, you maneuver a tank or pilot a rocket ship to control territory, shoot other players, grab pods and race pass bombs.

In the Power Station zone you need to avoid giant bolts of electricity and battery acid as you jump and duck for points or splat your way to victory as you squash all the bugs streaming up out of the city's sewers.

In the Waterfront, you go down to the docks to race jet boats or grab things without falling into the water.

Depending which game you are playing they will be power-ups that will appear randomly in different locations to help you like the Lightning Bolt, Hammer, Shield and a Question Mark.

although the character are very litlle when you play the minigames are well design.

It is also a game where you can play with young gamers since the gameplay is easy to understand and full of fast pace action that will capture their interest

Fusion Frenzy is a game that focus of fun and fast action that you can enjoy with your family and friend for long time.
