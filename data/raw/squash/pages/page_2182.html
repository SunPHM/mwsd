Costa del Sol weather

Golf, water sports, ski (Sierra Nevada), trekking

The Costa del Sol offers a complete range of sports. Here you will find absolutely everything you may desire: all types of water sports, golf, tennis, volleyball and beach volleyball, football, basketball and squash, while both the beaches and the interior invite you to take long walks.

Costa del Golf

Given the number of golf courses on the Costa del Sol, already more than fifty, the area has received the name of Costa del Golf (Golf Coast). Here you will find a wide variety of well-kept golf courses in idyllic surroundings and a beautiful weather almost the whole year round. During the summer months, some courses have special green fee offers. Taking into account their popularity among tourists, many golf courses also employ English and German speaking personnel.

During the winter you are only 1.5 to 2 hours away from one of Spain's most beautiful skiing areas, near Granada in the Sierra Nevada. This ski resort, the highest in Europe, has already organised two world ski championships. Ski runs are open from December till April. Enjoy the complete range of après-ski offers or return to the beach after skiing to enjoy yourself during the night without cap or scarf.
