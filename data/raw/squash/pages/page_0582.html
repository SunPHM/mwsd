As a student or racquet centre member you are entitled to join the Squash Club. The club is a group of players from beginner to advanced who get together to improve their skills, meet new players, and have fun. Your membership includes drop-in times, Interclub, tournaments, ladders, and training sessions. Drop-in times are Wednesdays from 6:00pm - 9:00pm and Saturdays from 10:00am - 12:00pm. The club starts in September and runs until the end of April. The cost is $32.00/year.

Men's Squash
The University of Calgary Squash Club is for campus recreation Racquet Centre Members and active students only. Join the box leagues or become an interclub player (interclub fees included). Your membership card is valid for 10% off of the regular price of any squash racquet at the Sports Shop.
