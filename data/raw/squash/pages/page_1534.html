ROYAL HAATHI MAHAL RESORT
Cavelossim, South Goa

Location : Just 45 minutes from the airport and 25 minutes from the railway station at Margao. It stands proud between the River Sal and the Arabian Sea, which is just a 10-minute walk away. Cavelossim beach is widely regarded as the most beautiful in South Goa.

-----

The latest jewel to be added to the Royal Resort crown is the marvelous Haathi Mahal. It is the fifth development on the beautiful coast of Goa – where Royal has its roots and where ‘Royal Goan Beach Club’ is a synonym for holidaying in luxury.

This outstanding hotel and condominium development is indeed a palace with something catering for every taste. Walk into the entry foyer and you immediately get the feeling of luxury. Look over the swimming pool and you get the feeling of excitement. 

But this is only the beginning of an outstanding holiday experience..

Water World

Haathi Mahal offers you a whole range of aquatic facilities ranging from the Water slide for kids to the open air Jacuzzi.You can enjoy a swim in the choice of 3 pools, the main pool, the canal or the wading pool by the restaurant while your kids frolic in the kiddies' pool near the landscaped waterfall

The Sunshine Spa

The complete health, beauty & fitness center where you will encounter total revitalization as you indulge in the finest urative & invigorating treatments & applications.Experience some Regal pampering at Royal Resorts at Haathi Mahal. Explore the vast array of features set to take you to the highest levels of rejuvenation and health care. Luxuriate in the lavish attention of our professional staff eager to cater to your every desire in an ambiance that makes your visit comfortable and an experience to come back to.From a simple haircut to a complete beauty package and from a relaxing massage to a stimulating game of squash the Spa boasts of top of the line services that will definitely make your day. We have imported state of the art equipment in our Gym for those who are health conscious and an air-conditioned squash court for those who enjoy vigorous sport. Work out, tone up your body or just have fun. Our extensive range of facilities has something for everyone.For the rest of the family, the games Pavilion is sure to keep you occupied with its host of activities like live demonstrations, competitions, games, puzzles, library, cycles for hire and even a kiddy's play area

Gardens

Heath Mahal has landscaped gardens all through the resort, offering an Indian, English and Japanese gardens where you can relax, maybe indulge in afternoon tea or a romantic candle light dinner

Shops

No holiday would be complete without shopping. Haathi Mahal offers you an exclusive range of Indian crafts and gifts. We don't forget the essentials.You will find everything you need at this air conditioned supermarket.

An ethnic multi cuisine air-conditioned restaurant serving excellent fare

Enjoy your choice of indoor or outdoor dining, open buffet or a la carte. The a la carte menu offers a wide variety of excellent dishes from International, authenthic Goan & Indian cuisines. Ask for the day's special …a special creation by our Chef, authentic and exquisite.Apart from extensive food and beverage services we have live entertainment daily and two theme evenings at Tuskers to ensure that you have a true holiday experience

A one of its kind in Goa….You got to see it to believe it

An authentic English Pub called the Pirates Tavern is designed to take you back to the 18th century ….an excellent place to spend your evening …cocktails,snacks,music,pool tables you name it and we have it.The electrifying & scintilating sound of the discotheque attached to the pub is sure to get you on your feet and dancing every night.

Nestled admist most soothing surroundings by the Poolside is a swim up bar

An exotic location to enjoy a lazy afternoon basking in the sun. The swim up bar serves excellent cocktails and snacks. On site cooking of authentic,exotic and mouth watering creations by our Chef. A casual set up with deck beds spread around is an ideal spot to have a great time

Lose yourself in the quaint natural surroundings...

Situated at lobby level, the English Garden, the Japanese Garden & the Indian garden provide a private retreat in the resort. Spend a day here in the soothing & rejuvenating atmosphere if it catches your fancy. To refresh yourself order what you like by the phone provided at the lobby bar.It is a great place for a quite romantic evening amidst nature.You can arrange to have a private dinner for two or a small party here.

We are at your beck and call

Our room service menu is available in your room and you can just place an order sit back, relax and enjoy your meal in comforts of your room. A daily bread delivery service is also available. All our breads, cakes, croissants and pastries are baked on site, so all our produce is home baked and fresh.
