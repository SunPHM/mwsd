  • Welcome to Sheraton Abu Dhabi Resort & Towers


    • Picture an imposing modern building set between the sparkling waters of the Gulf and the thriving business centre of a busy city. We also offer our Sayyidaty (Arabic for 'My Lady') services which aim to make the travelling businesswomen's stay more comfortable and secure. Whether you're travelling for business or pleasure - and why not combine both? - this magnificent hotel has all you need to make your stay a successful and contented experience - the Sheraton experience.
