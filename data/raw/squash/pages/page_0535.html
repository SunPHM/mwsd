  Mini-League Rules- To help in the smooth running of the Mini Leagues, here are the rules. 
  

1.

 THE MINI LEAGUE STRUCTURE.

The Mini-Leagues will consist of a number of leagues arranged into groups of 5 players (leagues 1 to 8) or 10 players in two sections (leagues 9 and below), playing a series of matches over a period of approximately one month (six weeks during the summer). At the end of each month there will be promotion and relegation. 
The main season runs from September through to the following April, and for the other months, Summer Leagues will be run.

 

  

2.

 ENTRY TO LEAGUES

All players who have played in the March and August leagues will be entered automatically for the new season. A list will be posted on the noticeboard for any other players wishing to join the leagues at this time. During the course of the season, there will be a list on the noticeboard for entry (or re-entry of suspended players) to the leagues. After October, however, entry (or re-entry) to the leagues cannot be guaranteed, and will be dependent upon spaces being available in a suitable league.
   

3.

 ARRANGING MATCHES
Each player will contact two opponents (as shown below) in his/her league to arrange a suitable date for their match. At least 3 'dates (one of which should be on a weekend) should be offered, with a reasonable amount of notice given; if a suitable date cannot be found, the challenger shall be entitled to claim the match. If a challenger fails to contact an opponent in reasonable time before the end of the month, the opponent shall be entitled to claim the match.

4. 

 INACTIVITY
Any player who, without notifying the league convener of the reason, does not play any matches in one month, or over a two month period does not play at least three matches, may be suspended from the leagues for at least one month. Suspended players will be required to apply for re-admission to the leagues as per Rule 2 above.
   

5.

 SCORING
Each match will be the best of five games. Points are awarded as follows :-
Result -  3-0 3-1   3-2  w/o  Unfinished
Points:- 6-1 5-2 4-3 3-0 3-3
   

6.

 ARRIVING LATE
If player is 10 - 15 minutes late on court the first game shall be forfeited, if 15 - 20 minutes late, the first two games shall be forfeited, and if more than 20 minutes late, the match shall be forfeited.
   

7.

 PROMOTION & RELEGATION
a. Leagues 1 to 8 - at the end of each month the top two players in each league will be promoted one league, and the bottom two in each league will be relegated one league.
b. Leagues 9 to 20 - at the end of each month, the top four over both leagues will be promoted one league and the bottom four over both leagues will be relegated by one league.
c. Players who total 23 or 24 points in the month, may be promoted two leagues should there be space available.
d. A player will not be promoted if he or she has fewer points and has played two or more matches less than either of the bottom two players in the league above.
     
8. INTERPRETATION OF RULES
All decisions in respect of the foregoing rules, or any other matters relating to the Mini-Leagues, will be at the discretion of the League Convener.
