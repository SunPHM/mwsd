H.O.P.E. Squash Fest!

H.O.P.E. Squash Fest

Students in the Wallace HOPE nutrition program got an opportunity to expand their culinary horizons last month. James Station donated two big boxes of winter squash to the program, including acorn squash, Delicata squash, Kabocha squash, butternut squash – even spaghetti squash!

 Nutrition educator Debi Griswold said that it was the first time many of the students had ever tried winter squash. Delicata squash won the group’s approval for the tastiest squash, with an honorable mention going to Spaghetti squash - for being the most fun to eat.

Winter squash is also good for you. One ½ cup serving of winter squash provides up to 73% of the Recommended Daily Amount of Vitamin A, and it’s also a great source of Vitamin C and fiber. 

Thanks to the generosity of James Station, Wallace H.O.P.E. students have discovered another tasty way to get their 5-A-Day!
