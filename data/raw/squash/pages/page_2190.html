Description:
Eight Bells Mountain Inn - 3 Star Guest House
Set amidst the magnificent surroundings of the picturesque Outeniqua Mountains, with an atmosphere of friendly hospitality. Relax in the elegant lounge, cosy pub or the historical billiard room before enjoying a tasty ? la carte dinner complemented by a superb wine list. Guests may enjoy a host of leisure activities on the 400 acre estate including swimming, tennis, squash, bowls, horse riding and walking trails.

Room Types:
1 timber-log suite, 7 thatched rondavels, 12 bedrooms and 5 timber- log cabins, all en-suite, are set in enchanting gardens with an abundance of birdlife. Room facilities include television, safe, radio, telephone and under carpet heating.
