Run #513 – 22/9/98 – Voyeur’s Paradise
A hash associated with a Gym?!? What kind of heresy is this? Is NH4 starting to become a ‘serious hash’? I mean, choosing Gold’s Gym is bound to be fraught with dangers isn’t it. Visions of drunk hashers staggering around slinging beer over the grunting athletes were… Oooo errr missus! As it happens, it turned out to be a fascinating time as the hashers gathered at the corner of Skopa Street gawping at the rows and rows of well oiled bodies using the equipment, muscles rippling, faces contorted with the strain of it all…. And that was just the women! Mind you they were all in there looking out at the assembled hashers – probably thinking “That’s it, if that lot are coming in I’m not coming back no more”.
The off was delayed slightly longer than usual due not only to the aforementioned distractions but also the free drinks being plied by a couple of extremely attractive young ladies (down boy). Eventually the pack was off, snapping and yapping through the streets of Acropolis. Given that this was almost completely tarmac, it made ingenious use of the terrain, making sure most of the major routes in that area were covered. Fairly soon the short runners, ably led by that star navigator ‘Fraid Not were engaged in a live hare situation – when in doubt, improvise. The long run proved to be pretty long at that (or so it was reported) anyway, it took them a while to find their way back.
So it was that the circle was treated to a rare sight of the hares taking their downer on their knees for setting an illegal hash. Alledgedly. Makes the mind boggle. Heid Banger was singled out for strange associations with a BFBS2 program (see plug elsewhere in this rag) which progressed from a downer, through to a disgraced “On your back” when the sore subject of DeInformer (or lack thereof) came up.
Itchy Balls made a very rare appearance. Clearly too long an absence as he was discovered to be wearing “NEW SHOES” [Shock Horror], only escaping the total tea bag experience through the unusual generosity of the R.A.
Loads of virgins this week – Linda who was shivering in the unaccustomed Cypriot chill after Malaya. She will henceforth be known as Monica – I wonder why? Maria, Johanne now known as Best Bits, Fotis Economides and Carl Russel who shall be known as Chubby Chicks.
Maria was too busy trying to avoid the wet spot and so she had a downer as she was the last one down during the “No Problem” session.

‘FraidNot brought the delightful Grace along to the circle to see what the Hash is all about before she disappears off to Canada for the winter. Well she got an excellent view as they both got an up-downer. Any rumours that this had anything to do with a recent squash match between FN and the R.A. are just that.
You may have heard of the delightful American sport of wildlife spotting known as “road-kill”. Well the R.A. is starting his own trend by explaining the distinctive Pssst Pat Pssst Pat sound that the Horny backed Strumfaki is heard to make as he creeps up behind most of the pack. Actually this may give the wrong idea as to Strumfaki’s proclivities. What is meant is that it happens as he habitually overtakes most of the pack.
At last an award! OO-Sex managed to land the Zimmer frame in the forlorn hope that it may slow him down a bit.
So on to the lavish spread laid out in Gold’s Gym. I mean picture the scene if you will. Looking through the huge plate glass window of the gym you can see the rows of bicycles and treadmills along the top and the masses of weird weight machines to the right. In the middle is the reception area and to the left the cafeteria.
So we have athletic activities (reeeeaaaally) to the top and the right, order and discipline to the middle and to the left, a bunch of hashers slobbering down free beer, eating cholesterol friendly sheftalia and mixed, smoking, using mobile phones and generally breaking all the rules. It is a great tribute to the generosity of the chaps at the Gym and Aris in particular who not only put up with us with good humour but was also so spectacularly hospitable. Thank you and we’ll be starting the 10 o’clock housewives session OK?
Hares: Sprinkle & ‘FraidNot
