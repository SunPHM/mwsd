Squash

 

FACILITIES:

  • There are six courts (including 2 with a glass back) which can be booked for ½ hour or 1 hour periods. Three courts operate on the hour and ½ hour and three on the ¼ and ¾ hour.


CONCESSIONS:

  • Court fee is $3.00 per person per half hour but the following concession applies: Members: $1.00/person/½ hour

BOOKINGS:

  • Bookings may be made no more than 7 days in advance by phone (ext. 6433 or (03) 364-2433) or at Reception.
  • A court may be booked for no more than 1 hour at a time.
  • At least 1 hour's notice of cancellation must be given or a court fee may be charged.
  • Players must vacate the courts quickly at the end of the game.


EQUIPMENT:

  • Locker keys are available. Only "NO MARK" balls may be used. These are available for hire or purchase from the Receptionist.
  • Rackets are available for hire. A charge will be made for a broken racket.
  • Towels are available for hire.

SQUASH LEAGUE:

  • This league allows you to have regular games against players of your own ability. Your opponents are arranged for you. All you have to do is make the court booking, play the game and record the score.
  • The league is open to all students and Recreation Centre Members irrespective of playing standard.
  • You will be matched against players of your own ability.
  • The competition will commence about the end of March and continue until about the end of September.
  • Entry forms are available from the Reception Office. Squash court notice boards will provide further details or ask at Reception.

INJURY:

  • Please report all injuries. Protective glasses are available for use free of charge. Injuries will be reduced if players change into appropriate footwear upon arriving for their game. This will reduce the amount of dust/dirt walked onto the courts. Excessive dust/dirt makes playing surfaces exceptionally slippery and dangerous.


LEARNING AND COACHING:

  • Why not enrol for a series of beginners sessions? The first series should start during the 2nd week of term. Fee payable. Enrol by calling at Reception. A new series will begin every fourth week making it possible to enrol at any stage throughout the year.
  • It is recommended that those who have attended a beginners' series return at a future date for a more advanced session. These sessions will be held as the need arises.
  • Check out the Recreation Classes page for this term's programmes.


BLACK SOLED SHOES:

  • Black soled shoes and other improper footwear are not permitted in the courts. Anyone found to be wearing black soled shoes may be asked to contribute to the cleaning of the black marks off the court.
  • Shirts must be worn at all times to prevent sweat staining the floor.
