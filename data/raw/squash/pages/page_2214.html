Yellow Crookneck (Heirloom)
Summer Squash

A summer squash favorite and one of the most interestingly shaped veggies, not round or long but thin curved necks with football shaped bodies, yellow and golden and a bit bumpy on the surface. Pick the young squash from 4-8 inches before the outside skin becomes hard. Among the best tasting and visually pleasing of the early summer squash. If you like to make fall or holiday displays, the old, dry, hard fruit (seed pods) are up to 12 inches or more and will keep through the new year in most situations.(Pepo) (3 grams = 26 seeds)
