Golf Link Travel: Colorado: Lodging

Cheyenne Mountain Resort

The Pinnacle of
Accommodations & Service

The resort's 316 guest rooms and suites are conveniently clustered in eight residential lodges, each with a private balcony and breathtaking views of Cheyenne Mountain and the Country Club of Colorado. With 310 sunny days annually, the direct access to balconies and decks is a delightful way to experience the crisp mountain air. For maximum convenience, most guest rooms feature two queen beds and double closets. Separate study areas provide two desks equipped for a computer work station. The Resort is also networked to provide wireless broadband Internet capability.


Whether you choose a leisurely round of golf, an intense game of squash or handball, laps in an outdoor pool, a set of tennis or an individual workout in the fitness center, the Cheyenne Mountain Resort provides it all.
Guests will discover the ideal retreat for a relaxing weekend getaway or an active family vacation. This undiscovered gem, nestled in the shadows of the Rocky Mountains, serves up breathtaking natural beauty and a host of recreational opportunities, both on the resort and in the surrounding region. Hiking, biking and white water rafting are just a few of the popular activities within minutes of The Resort. Even fishing, sailing and windsurfing are close at hand on our private lake. It all combines to assure a unique and memorable vacation experience.
