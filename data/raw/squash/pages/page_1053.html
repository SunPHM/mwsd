FMCC Cooking School

Pasta and Sauces (2 3-hour classes)

Let’s prepare fresh pasta – linguine, ravioli, manicotti, and any other pasta you have experienced. Learn how to create infused pasta (pasta with flavors – spinach, etc.) and seven different sauces, from tomato to cream, that can go with them.  Limited enrollment. Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$69.00

Feast from Our Oceans

For seafood lovers – the bounty of the ocean awaits you! 3 of the most popular seafood dishes – lobster ravioli, baked stuffed flounder, shrimp scampi – will be prepared in this class. Need we say more????!!! Bring an apron! Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$39.00

Italian Again!

Stuffed breads, cheeses, soups, pasta, and a special dessert! Learn the secrets of the many types of breads, different kinds of cheeses and what they are best used for, and those ingredients which make special dishes supreme. To top off the day, you will learn how to make that delicious dessert, tira misu (“pick me up”). Bring an apron! Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$49.00

Amazing Appetizers

You’ll do more than impress your friends as they walk in the door – you’ll astound them with any one of these incredibly delicious appetizers! Learn how to prepare chicken satay with Thai peanut sauce; pumpernickel toasts with dill cream cheese topped with smoked salmon; spanokopita (traditional Greek pastry filled with spinach, black olives, and feta cheese); Aunt Rosie’s pepperoni rolled torta; Prosciutto-wrapped jumbo shrimp; and bruschetta on crostini. Bring an apron!  Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$39.00

A Holiday to Impress

Don’t wait to sign up for this “best of the best” culinary feast! Ready for an absolutely fantastic alternative to the traditional turkey or ham holiday meals???! Here’s the menu – crab-stuffed Portabello Napoleans; Shrimp Louie; roasted rosemary au gratin potatoes infused with sweet potato; and…..Beef Wellington! Bring an apron! Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$49.00

Super Soups!

Just in time for the cooler weather! You will become a chef of fall soups and impress all with mastering these 4 soup recipes – lobster bisque; maple butternut squash soup; chicken tarragon and wild mushroom soup with variations (rice, pasta, etc.); and caramelized French onion soup with applejack broth and gruyere croutons. Bring an apron!  Additional supply fee of $15 to be paid to instructor at the start of class.

$39.00
