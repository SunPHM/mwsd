CONFERENCING Facilities


St. Francis Bay presents conference groups with ideal surroundings for both work and play. The secluded facilities ensure undisturbed discussion, isolating you in a productive environment, without the distraction of holidaymakers and noise pollution associated with large towns. All this while still being accessible enough to be serviced by friendly staff and timely Cordon Bleu Chef. 

When business comes to conclusion, the area also offers numerous outside activities such as golf, squash, bowling and more - all of which are situated no more than a few minutes walk from the conference centre. The self contained facility and friendly surroundings will make you feel right at home… come and experience our unique atmosphere in laid back luxury!
