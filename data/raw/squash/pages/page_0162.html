Sports

Squash

Downe House has two squash coaches, one full time and one part-time. There is an U15 team and an under 19 team. This year, Downe House came 3rd in the Under 15 and U19 National Schools Squash Competition.

Downe House plays squash matches against Cheltenham Ladies College, Wycombe Abbey, Queen Anne’s and various other schools.

Each year there is held a Removes Cup Competition and a L4 and U4 competition. Last year, we also ran a Father / Daughter competition.
