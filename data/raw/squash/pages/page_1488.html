Leisure Facilities

Our leisure facilities are, we promise, truly extensive. Enjoy the benefits of the swimming pool be it serious lengths or just a gentle wallow. The spa bath is a real pleasure which offers therapeutic advantages too.

Indulge yourself at Bodyworks, our beauty salon or simply melt away your worries in the sauna and soothe away stress in the spa bath. You can even book your tan on a sun bed.

Build your health and stay on top with a serious workout in the gymnasium, or perhaps a game of squash?

Afterwards relax in the sports bar with a choice of refreshments.

Trained staff are on hand to ensure you get the most from your leisure time.
