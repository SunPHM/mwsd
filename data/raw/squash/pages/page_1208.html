Jerk & Tings™

Adjust quantities in shopping cart to get the number of desired servings.

 

All our "Jerk and Tings" entrees are 100% cooked in our jerk pit or on our grill over wood and charcoal for the ultimate in flavor and tenderness.  Our flavor is more than "skin" deep. Upon special request, most of our jerk entrees are available as extra hot jerk, mild jerk, Southern BBQ, and herb-roasted.  There is a 5% surcharge for extra hot jerk.
