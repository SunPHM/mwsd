Residents of the Town of Peace River, as well as those of surrounding communities, enjoy a large number of community recreation facilities and activities.

Indoor facilities available in Peace River include: an arena, a curling rink, an indoor pool with a water slide, seven gymnasiums, a bowling alley, three community halls, and an auditorium. Two sport and recreation clubs are also located in Town. The publicly operated Peace Country Sports Club offers racquetball and squash courts as well as a weight room and a gymnasium. These facilities provide residents with the option of indoor recreation year round.

Peace River's Misery Mountain is a downhill ski facility enjoyable during the winter months. As well, there are many trails available throughout the valley suitable for cross country ski use providing options for all ski enthusiasts. A lighted toboggan hill and ten outdoor skating rinks also help to provide variety for outdoor recreation activities. All these facilities receive significant use during the winter months.

During the summer months, the beautiful Peace River setting entices the Town's residents to enjoy the natural environment by walking, hiking and cycling in and around the natural valley hills.

For racing enthusiasts, the North Peace Bracket Racing Association is an organization which hold a number of races each year. Races are usually held at the Three Creeks Air Strip just northeast of Peace River.

Outdoor recreation facilities in the Town that contribute to summer recreation are: a water spray park; three sports fields; eight tennis courts; eight groomed ball diamonds; and two golf courses in the immediate proximity to town. The Mighty Peace Golf Course is an 18 hole grass green course approximately 10 kilometres west of the town and the Peace View Course is a nine hole facility with sand greens located 4 kilometres north of Peace River along Weberville Road.

Boat launches are located in the Lower West Peace area of Town and along River Road to provide recreation access to residents who seek to venture onto the river, while recreational riverboat cruises are offered on long warm summer days. Water-skiing and rowing are popular events given the excellent access to the Peace River.

The Town also has an excellent library facility. The Peace River Municipal Library is a member of the Peace Library System and offers a wide selection of information services.

Many community groups also organize special events throughout the year. For more information on the facilities located in town contact the Town of Peace River's Community Services Department. (624-1000)
