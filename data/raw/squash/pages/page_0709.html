Summer Squash

POSTED: 11:11 a.m. CDT July 10, 2002
UPDATED: 11:56 a.m. CDT July 10, 2002

Ingredients:
6 strips of bacon, cut into small pieces
3 Tbls. olive oil
1 large onion, thinly sliced
2 lbs. small, yellow and green summer squash, washed, trimmed and thinly sliced
3 medium tomatoes, peeled, seeded and coarsely chopped
Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
1 Tbls. chopped, fresh basil leaves
1 cup shredded mild cheddar cheese

Directions:
In a large saute pan, cook the bacon pieces over medium heat, stirring until crisp. Remove with a slotted spoon. Pour off all of the bacon fat.

Add oil to the skillet and heat. Saute onion in the oil until translucent. Add the squash and cook, stirring often, about 10 minutes or until tender crisp.

Stir in the tomatoes, salt and pepper, and basil. Cook 5 minutes more. Sprinkle cheese evenly over the top of the vegetables.

Cover the skillet for 2-3 minutes or until cheese melts. Serve immediately.

Serves 8
