Some interesting writings about squash:
  • Jonathon Power shakes up squash world. Reuters interviewed Jonathon Power at the Super Series Finals, for a look at the personality of the world # 1.

  • 'Getting Squashed' (II) article on Citysports website. This is a good article about starting out with squash.
