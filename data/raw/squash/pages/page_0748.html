Updated: Thu, Nov 8, 2001

Portland Square - 2nd November 2001.
These photographs were taken late afternoon - hence the long shadows and 'autumnal glow'.
Buildings A and B:

This picture shows the current state of Building A (background) and B (foreground). The core of the steel frame of each building has been completed to roof level; the lower four floor slabs are also in place.
Buildings B and C:

This view shows the 'bridges' which join Buildings B and C together. Building B's stair and lift tower (in the foreground) is taking shape; the inter-building links will enable users of Building C to access this lift.
Building C:

The main structural elements of Building C are now all in place, up to main roof level. All that remains to be added is the steel frame for the atrium's sloping roof, and the 8m high plant room.
Squash Courts Building:

The roof of the Squash Courts building has been finished; some of the exterior wall cladding has also been installed.
