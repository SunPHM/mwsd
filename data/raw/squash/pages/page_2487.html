Spring Break Camp 2006 @ HFAC!
March 27-31, 2006 Look for more info coming in April.

Children will play organized games, play inside our obstacle course Kiddiepillar, jump in our bouncy Castle and free play in the morning. A healthy lunch is provided and served mid-day. Afternoons consist of swimming (with lifeguard) and/or rock climbing on our 30' indoor rock wall!

Our youth programs are growing at HFAC and kids of all ages can enjoy the majority of our camps! Hawthorn Farm Athletic Club is looking forward to a great season and we are anxious to get started! We have made a few changes in our camps along with a few additions, so we hope that you find something that you are interested in! All of our camps are on a first come-first served basis, and we do have a cap for the majority of our camps, so please get your registration form in to the front desk as soon as you can!

Our refund policy is as follows:
You get a 100% refund if you cancel within a week of the start date. A 50% refund will be given within three days of the start date, and no refunds will be given after the session has started.

Our staff strive to make your child’s camp experience a positive one. Unwanted behavior will be addressed immediately. Please be aware that if there are any problems with the children, our staff will talk to the children and also call the parents if the problems persist. We want our camps to be as fun and safe as they can!

To register for camps, please fill out a separate form for each child and hand in forms to the front desk. You can also register on the web! Sorry, no phone registrations accepted.

Please feel free to give us a call or email if you have any questions regarding camps!

Dawn Rudolph 503-640-6404 ext. 144 or email youth@hfac.com
