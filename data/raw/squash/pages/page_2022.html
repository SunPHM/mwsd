The Doyle's Unami Farm, a fifth generation farm family, would like you to enjoy a fun and educational taste of farm life.  You'll learn the history of the early settlers and Indians who once roamed and the hunted these fields.

You'll share in planting or harvesting crops (depending on the season), get hands-on demonstrations of farm machinery and care for various farm animals.

Enjoy scenic hayrides while stopping at points of intrest around the farm.  In the Spring, the focus is on planting with a project for each child.  During the Fall, harvest your own pumpkin, popcorn, Indian corn and winter squash.  Throughout the season, enjoy historical demonstrations, pony rides and farm animals.

Our goal is to acquaint children with the vanishing hertiage of farm living.  Additionally, the peaceful farm setting and change of pace from today's busy life-style will rekindle fond memories for adults.
