Market Deeping Leisure Centre

Park Road, Deeping St. James, Market Deeping, Lincolnshire

Telephone (01778) 344072

Squash Courts

The centre has two squash courts available for use by the public and each can be hired in advance for individuals or clubs/meetings. However, they must be booked a minimum of three days in advance.

The centre is home to a couple of squash clubs - there is a noticeboard on the first floor giving details of meetings and matches - the courts are therefore unavailable for hire on these nights. The squash courts are also used by a local ballet school and also aerobic classes have use of the courts on some nights.

For those accompanying people who are playing squash there is a viewing area located on the first floor (just outside the activity room) which overlooks both courts.

Maurice Mordell (the man in charge of the centre shop) is a keen squash player and is often available for a game or demonstration of a squash racket. He is also available for general advice on the game and its equipment.

The floor is kept in good condition by regular washing, cleaning and re-surfacing and all users respect the floor.

Equipment to play is available for hire (squash balls are available for purchase) from the reception and the centre shop also has demonstration rackets available for evaluation.
