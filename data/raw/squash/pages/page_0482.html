Sports Facilities

The Hall is very fortunate to have a number of excellent facilities. These facilities are well maintained and are available to all members of the hall. Indeed, we find they are well used and very popular amongst the students who live here.

We are lucky enough to have our own squash court,  and during the year a squash ladder and knockout cup are played between hall members. We also use it for inter-hall competitions, in which we are always able to field strong teams.  Then there is the table-tennis table; one table may not be anything to shout about, but on a wet Sunday afternoon when you have an essay to write, it can be a godsend.

 

In contrast, the recently student-renovated snooker room is a fine asset to the Hall. It contains a full size table, with it's own rests and cues (though many people have their own), and has a proper lighting rig above.  It is accessible at all times, and is well used. There is also an annual snooker competition that draws a lot of entries.

The gym has also recently been student-renovated. It is now an up-to-date modern facility with free weights, a punch bag, machines, other miscellaneous items of kit, and most importantly its own stereo. The gym is open all the time, and again is well used, whether for serious training or a more relaxed exercise session away from the books.

We are lucky in Slems to have the facilities that we do, and consequently we try to look after them well: the two recent refurbishments bear testament to that.  The facilities are here for our students to take advantage of, and large numbers of them do.
