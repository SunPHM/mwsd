 Location


Welcome to Clark Hatch Legend Hotel which is located right across the Putra World Trade Center, Kuala Lumpur.

Exercising at the Legend center is a great experience with a cheerful and relaxing atmosphere partly due to the great view of our outdoor swimming pool and jacuzzi, which overlook KL's many skyscrapers.  To keep everyone on the move, our committed, friendly instructors are always near-by giving fitness evaluations, personal workout programs and assistance.

 Facilities & Services


We provide exercise attire, lockers, showers, steam room,  sauna, outdoor swimming pool and jacuzzi.  The gym  is fully equipped with various types of cardiovascular  / aerobic training machines  and for strength conditioning we use one of the safest brand of machines. We have sufficient weight stations to exercise every muscle group in the body and for those who prefer a more intense workout, we have a separate free weights area.

For those who enjoy a game of squash, we have two squash courts available. Aerobics classes range from hi-lo and advanced aerobics, to kick-boxing and step.  Classes are conducted daily and an addition to your cardiovascular exercises.

At Clark Hatch Legend, we have the tools and motivation factors to keep your daily exercise routine fun and enjoyable.  We strive to help one another to make fitness a part of our lifestyle.A part from location and facilities Clark Hatch members are also entitled to 2 hours of complementary parking.

The different types of membership offered are

  • Premier Membership 
  • Corporate Membership
  • Gold Membership.
