Squash is one of the Club's focal points. Three international soft ball exhibition courts are occupied with players throughout the day. Over 300 members use these newly renovated courts, as well as the over 200 racquet lockers. The courts are the perfect place for well-seasoned players, as well as those who are new to the game. Head Squash Pro Servando Velez teaches as many as five lessons a day. A native of Mexico, Servando learned the game in 1978 in a New York City raquet club. In only a few years, he had mastered all the skills of a pro.
