Beechdown
The first club venue
Before a proposal could be made to establish the Basingstoke IVC Group, a venue needed to be found for the weekly Wednesday evening club night meeting.  Through a recommendation by a hairdresser in the Edward James Salon, I found the Beechdown Club, which was about 3 miles west from the town centre, and arranged a first visit that afternoon.

The manager of the Beechdown Squash and Racquets Club at the time was as I recall a pleasant woman called Theo Johnson. Theo showed me around the club facilities, which I was very impressed with, and we were able to agree outline terms for IVC to use the club bar once a week.

Now all I needed was for someone to provide me with the money to put this deal together.  To achieve this I would need to put together a business case for Reading IVC Committee and present it at one of their meetings.
