There are many opinions why the Novotel Pacific Bay Resort heralds a new era in resort-style living. Some say it is the rich green surroundings with white sandy beaches tucked away, waiting to be discovered. Some say it is the warm and friendly staff who make you feel so special. Others say it is the perfect balance between fun, relaxation and sophistication. But we believe it is a combination of all this and more which makes Pacific Bay Resort a landmark in excellence.

Set in its own sub-tropical valley, Pacific Bay is the place to relax and recharge. 

Facilities: 

• Two restaurants (seasonal) and a cocktail bar 
• Extensive conference facilities 
• 9 hole par-3 golf course 
• Wallabies football oval & training centre 
• Four floodlit tennis courts
• Volleyball 
• Two squash courts 
• Games Room
• Bayside Spa Centre & fitness centre
• Three heated swimming pools
• International standard golf courses nearby
• Walking and jogging trails
• Direct beach access
• Club Dolfi Kids Club (2-15 years)
• Convenience Store 

The Novotel Pacific Bay Resort features 180 beautifully appointed rooms, including standard hotel rooms and luxurious spa suites with kitchen and laundry facilities. Each room has an outlook of either the lagoon, gardens or deep blue ocean, and is designed with your comfort and relaxation in mind. 

You will notice an extraordinary attention to detail in every aspect of your stay, but it is no more than you would expect from such a unique and stylish resort. 

Pacific Bay Resort caters for families, singles and couples. It provides a host of activities of you are so inclined, or secluded areas of you want to get away from it all. 

Here you can do as much or as little as you like. 

The resort is perfect for your conferences, meetings and functions, and best of all, delegates can combine business with pleasure by taking their families. 

Various conference, seminar and function rooms cater for corporate groups or make special occasions even more special. 

Whether you are planning to hold a conference, stage a product launch or sales promotion, or provide team building or motivation, experienced conference organisers will be there to assist.


With squash courts, 5 tennis courts and a 9 hole golf course, fun and relaxation are the name of the game.

 The biggest decision you’ll have to make is whether to relax in your own private Spa Suite, lie beside one of the three pools, or walk to the beach just a few minutes away.
