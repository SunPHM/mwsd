This is the official qualifying draws and results information page for Jericho Tennis Club squash events, to be updated daily during the course of hosted squash tournaments.

Come place yourself in the heart of the action and see the action of the squash stars ignite. Jericho Tennis Club has earned a reputation as one of Vancouver’s premier sporting and social event locations, with state-of the-art facilities located on the shores of Jericho Beach.

The squash event schedule continues to attract an outstanding array of competitive players from local, as well as from international locations, all eager to battle it out for prestigious tournament titles. We hope you can join us for these exceptional squash events and see the present stars of squash and those of the future emerge.
