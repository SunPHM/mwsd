Our Services

Aphids Communications specializes in the design and development of web sites and dynamic, data-driven web-enabled software applications.

Web Site Development

Aphids Communications offers a comprehensive range of services for developing the web site that is right for your organization or business. Our services include everything necessary to create and maintain full-featured web sites. We specialize in web sites that load quickly and are easy (and inexpensive) to maintain. Our range of web site development services includes:

  • Information architecture, web site layout and navigation planning
  • Custom programming, including database components, and online ordering.
  • Graphic design
  • Web site promotion and registration with search engines and listing services

The cost of developing a web site depends on many factors, including the number of pages on the site, the types of features that you want in your site, and the level of the client's involvement in the process. We customize our proposals to our clients based on process, not product. For example, if you come to us with a well developed idea of how your site should be organized and you can provide us with the page content in a form that's ready to be processed, then you have already done much of the work. Our time can be spent making your ideas a reality rather than figuring out how your information should be organized and working on various proposed design schemes.

For an estimate of the cost to develop your site, please contact us via the information on our contact page.
