The Panels of A.S.B. walls are manufactured in the company's factory and brought to the site as whole units with the playing surfaces ready for use. The systems for an A.S.B. court include the following possibilities:

  1. System Walls -

    The front wall and side walls are manufactured in the factory in panels of up to 10meters in length and are brought to the site in a 40 foot container. The walls are manufactured by joining two panels by means of a series of aluminium profiles and this wall is then installed in an existing building with the gap between the two panels being filled with a special granulated quartz.

  2. ½ System Walls -

    This system is used mainly for existing courts that are in need of repairs or if the building is not sufficiently big for the conventional court.


  3. Multi-Purpose Sports Halls -

    Building of the courts as detailed in 1 and 2 above, but when building two or more courts, one alongside the other, the wall separating the courts is suspended on two rails, one in front and the other at the back, so as to allow the wall to be moved sideways by means of an electrical pushbutton system. By being able to thus open the court walls separating two, three, four or more courts the area becomes a multi- purpose facility.
