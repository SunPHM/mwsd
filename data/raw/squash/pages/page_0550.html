Canadian Health & Squash Club

SQUASH
Put your game face on! Let the Canadian foster your love for the game of squash. A fabulous aerobic workout that combines skill, dexterity and the thrill of athletic competition. We offer 3 international sized courts and a squash instructor who can help you to develop your skills. Whether you are new to the game or have been playing for years, John Larabie is available to share his years of experience with you to help you improve your game.

Our club offers a house league, supports two city league teams and has an informal seniors and women’s league. We are in the process of developing a junior program as well. Stay tuned.
