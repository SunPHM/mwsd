Sports and Leisure

First and foremost La Manga Club is renowned for its sports facilities. It has three international standard golf courses, a tennis complex with 28 courts, a squash court, a climbing wall, and a pristine bowling green, to name but a few of its many amenities. A health and beauty centre also recently opened at La Manga Club. It also has an excellent footballing centre that frequently hosts top teams from throughout Europe.

In addition to these, there are also water sports available nearby, as well as great opportunities for mountain biking, hiking, horse riding and jogging trails. We have split the activities into two sections, those available at La Manga Club, and those that can be done in the surrounding area.

In La Manga Club

Boules

A petanque court is located at the Tennis Centre. Play the game enjoyed by the French for centuries.

Bowls

The bowling green is situated at the tennis centre, and woods can be hired. It is one of the few in Spain.

Climbing

It comes as something of a surprise to learn that La Manga Club has its own climbing wall at the Tennis Centre, and that regular supervised sessions are held there.

Cricket

Not a sport normally associated with Spain, but La Manga Club has opened up an all weather cricket facility at the football centre, and was used as a venue for a cricket tournament in November, 2003.

Football

La Manga Club is becoming increasingly famous for its football complex which is frequently used as a training facility by some of Europe’s leading club sides including Chelsea, Tottenham, Grasshoppers Zurich, Real Madrid and Dinamo Kiev in recent years. It has also been home to the England international squad on numerous occasions.

A footballing academy operates for youngsters at certain times of the year with daily training sessions from FA qualified coaches. It is highly recommended that you pre-book the Football Academy, as it is usually very well attended. Advance bookings can be made through the Tennis Centre on 0034 968 175000 extn 1666.

Golf

La Manga Club’s golfing facilities encompass so many amenities, it is difficult to know where to start. The three courses, North (par 71) South (par 72) and West (par 73) offer different challenges for golfers of all abilities. Golf can be booked via Golf Administration (0034 968 17500 ext 8081) and it is always advisable to pre-book tee times, as the courses are very busy at certain times of the years.

All three courses have been designed to offer different challenges, and are likely to test the talents of even the most gifted golfers. The best way to experience the different challenges the courses offer is to play each one, but if you are limited in the amount of time you have available, then our handy guide below may help you choose which course you play.

North Course

The Par 71 North Course was completely remodelled in 2000-2001, and is more a test of accuracy than power. The course is quite hilly, and is noted for its tight fairways and large greens. It is dotted with water hazards and ravines and presents a real challenge to even the best golfers.

South Course

An interesting Par 72 course with a number of challenging holes. It is undergoing some remodelling, but is considered to be the most suitable course for golfers of all standards. Lakes, bunkers and gullies are located throughout the course, and help provide a stiff challenge for golfers of all abilities.

West Course

Although shorter than the South Course, the fact that it is a par 73 indicates the level of difficulty the course presents. It is almost a course of two halves, with the front nine littered with “barrancas” (storm ravines). The front nine are very tight, demanding precision play rather than power, whilst the back nine is more open, but its hilly terrain adds to the level of difficulty. The course, which is located amidst beautiful scenery is considered the most challenging of the three located at La Manga Club and is sure to provide a test of all facets of your game.

Please note, there is a dress code for all courses, and soft spikes must be worn.

Golf Academy

If you are frustrated by your golfing skills (or lack of them) then the Academy of Golf located on the North Course may be the place to help you improve. Its facilities include:

  • A team of golf professionals to help you improve your game
  • Indoor teaching studios complete with video analysis of your swing
  • A 60 bay driving range
  • 21 Outdoor tees
  • Ping Fitness Centre

La Manga Club also has an additional range of practice facilities, including floodlit driving ranges and target greens, practice bunkers, chipping greens and a large putting green.

Par 47 Pitch and Putt

Located near the Tennis Centre, the manicured 18 hole pitch and putt course is the perfect place to perfect your short game. The course has a full compliment of bunkers and water hazards making it ideal for practising your approach play and putting.

Mini Golf

There are two mini golf courses, one located at Las Lomas, the other next to the Mini Club Fiesta in the heart of La Manga Club. Great fun, especially if you play the courses with the kids.

Tennis

The Tennis Centre is located at the heart of La Manga Club, and offers a vast array of facilities and services. Its facilities include:

Tennis Courts

There are 28 tennis courts, including 13 that are floodlit. The Tennis Centre consists of 20 clay courts, 4 hard courts, and 4 artificial grass courts. Most of the courts have seating areas for spectators. The Tennis Centre is one of the best in Europe, and is the used by the Lawn Tennis Centre as its winter training base.

Tennis Academy

If you are a budding Serena Williams or Andre Agassi, but feel that your game is lacking the finishing touches, then why not enrol at the Tennis Academy? It includes:

On Court coaching sessions with a team of professionals

State of the Art digital tennis coaching system, including video analysis of tennis shots and footwork

Racket Service Centre

Forgot your racket, then don’t worry. The Tennis Centre has a selection of rackets available for hire. Additionally, a racket stringing and grip service is also available on request.

Paddle Tennis

An increasingly popular form of the game, and a great way to learn how to play, the Tennis Centre has two glass walled paddle tennis courts, making it possible to play at any time of the year.

Squash

If running around a tennis court is not enough to tire you out, why not indulge in a game of squash. There is a court located within the Tennis Centre complex.

Swimming

Most communities at La Manga Club have their own swimming pools, but for a change of scenery, the Oasis pool located at the Tennis Centre is the perfect alternative. It has three separate depth sections, and a bar serving drinks and light refreshments which opens during the summer months. It also has sun beds and sun umbrellas for hire.

Resort Gym and Spa

There is currently a small spa and fitness centre located at the Tennis Centre. However, a state of the art Spa and Health and Beauty facility opened at La Manga Club in March, 2004, and has an extensive range of facilities.

Cycling

Hiring a bicycle is a great way of exploring La Manga Club and the surrounding area. They can be rented through Biking La Manga which is part of the Activity Booking Centre www.abclamanga.com close to La Plaza in the heart of the resort. La Manga Club is situated amidst the Murcian foothills and these offer some exciting and challenging bike trails.

The Activity Booking Centre has a wide range of mountain and road bikes available, and also offers guided rides throughout the year to nearby Los Belones, or to Calblanque Regional park or the famous Portman guns.

Biking La Manga has a wide selection of bikes that are suitable for both beginners, and more experienced cyclists, and also has trailer bikes, child seats and child trailers available for hire. They also provide helmets, maps, locks and can be pre-booked in advance by calling 0034 968 338260.

Diving

Scuba diving courses are available at the Adventure Divers Scuba Centre at Las Lomas. Courses are designed to cater for all levels and abilities and include PADI Programmes. Extensive diving opportunities are also available in the Mar Menor and also at the Islas Hormigas Marine Reserve located just off the coast at Cabo de Palos.
