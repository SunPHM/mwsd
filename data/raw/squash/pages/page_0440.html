New River Organic Growers was started to help local organic farmers compete in today's market. Farmers must transport their crops to the public quickly and efficiently. The organization joins farmers in different parts of the county and assists with crucial programs such as marketing and delivery.

By building a local market base the group hopes to expand the availability of fresh produce. Having organic food that was grown in this community in more stores is good for the local economy as well as the environment. Obvious goals include reducing the amount of agricultural pollutants in our water supply and avoiding the high energy use and traffic problems associated with shipping. This type of venture can also help keep family farms intact by providing a viable alternative to tobacco farming.

New River Organic Growers meet on Monday nights at 7:00 PM in the Agricultural Extension Building off Poplar Grove Road.
