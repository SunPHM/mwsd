Tullamore Squash Club is a thriving sports club based in Tullamore, Co. Offaly in the midlands of Ireland. We welcome members of all ages and host regular competitions that cater for players of all ability.

 

This website was designed as a resource for those with an interest in Squash in the midland region and as a constant source of club information for our members who wish to keep updated with news, competition fixtures and latest results from Tullamore Squash Club.

Non-members of the club can learn more about this exciting sport by reading the ‘About Us’ section and they can get information on becoming a member by clicking on the ‘membership’ button. Those seeking more knowledge about the sport in general can discover more in the Squash Rules’ section.

 

Comments on this site or questions about Tullamore Squash Club are always welcome and we encourage visitors to email us or write a short message in our ‘Contact Us’ section. We hope you enjoy this site!
