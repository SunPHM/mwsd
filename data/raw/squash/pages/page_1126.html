The Vegetable Garden

First plant 4 rows of peas:
Patience
Promptness
Preparation
Perseverance
Next plant 3 rows of squash:
Squash gossip
Squash criticism
Squash indifference
Then plant 5 rows of lettuce:
Let-Us be faithful
Let-Us be unselfish
Let-Us be loyal
Let-Us be true to obligations
Let-Us love one another
And no garden is complete without turnips:
Turn-Up for important meetings
Turn-Up with a smile
Turn-Up with determination to make everything positive and worthwhile

Best of all, you do not have to have a GREEN thumb to grow this garden. "Happy New Year!"
