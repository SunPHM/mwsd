The Prince Edward Island Association of Chefs & Cooks is proud to announce that a junior team  from the Culinary Institute of Canada has won the right to represent Canada in the upcoming 2004 Culinary Olympics in Germany.

The team of two Students  Rebecca Hutchins and Kreg Graham and team coaches and Culinary Instructors Hans Anderegg and Richard Braunauer, had the challenge to compete against  teams from colleges across the country. Team PEI got top honors for their efforts in a competition recently held in Toronto. Other colleges competing for this honor were, Vancouver Community College, George Brown Toronto, Northern Alberta Institute of Technologie NAIT, Liaison College Toronto, Humber College Toronto, Algonequin College Ottawa, and Nova Scotia Community College NSCC.
