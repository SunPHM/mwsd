I hope this site can offer people that already play ways for them to improve their game, if they play competitively, or for them to get more enjoyment out of squash as recreation or excercise.

Websites take a lot of time so I'm afraid this one will develop slowly, but it will grow and with that I would like to ask your help. If you have any comments or suggestions, please feel free to send them to me here.

I hope to hear from you and I'll see you on the courts as well!

Robin MacAlpine

As I became more and more involved in the sport, I gradually began looking for information such as official rules, where to play, when tournaments are coming up etc. It didn't take long for me to realize that there is actually quite a bit of information available, but it is in lots of different places.

I am building squashnews.com to give English-speaking squash players in Hungary a single place to look for items of interest concerning squash. My other intent is simply to help increase the interest in and knowledge of squash as a sport here in Hungary.

Thank you for visiting!

I started playing squash 3 years ago and it has really turned into a passion of sorts. I now play 3 or 4 times a week, take private lessons from the top coach in the country, started a club (and a website) and spend most of my free time reading, writing, thinking or talking about the amazing sport, game, pastime (and lifetime) of squash.
