The "St." in the name indicates the connection to a saint and Catholicism. Many pilgrims came in past time to honor "Waldvater Englmar" and to ask for his blessing and help.
According to my knowledge, pilgrims are no longer coming, but every year on Whit Monday a play with religious background is performed.

St. Englmar in summer

I think St. Englmar is an attraction at any season. However, I am a summer person and I never have been here in winter.

St. Englmar in winter

St. Englmar is perfect for visitors who want to be active or just relax. I would prefer a mixture of both. I love biking better than walking, would go swimming and certainly you could find us in a sauna. Eventually we would try "summer sledding". We would participate in the local events - and naturally read and watch some German TV.
No don't be afraid - we will not forget to try the local food and some beer.
You may prefer cross country skiing, sledding (in winter), squash etc. Everything is offered, including rental of the needed equipment.
