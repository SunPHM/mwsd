If you are a complete beginner or you are looking to improve your game, join with a group of three to five other people at a similar level to yourself for six one hour lessons.

Kevin Wright and his team of qualified and professional resident coaches will provide you with expert tuition to improve your game irrespective of your level or experience.

Facilities
Dunholme Tennis Club, Remuera.

How To Book
Group lessons run once a week for six weeks usually in conjunction with school terms.

Group size is usually 4 to 6 people.

You Need
Comfortable sports gear and shoes, the racquets and wonderful facilities are provided.
