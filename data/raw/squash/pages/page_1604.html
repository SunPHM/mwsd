Hotel Mitre

Check avaibility and rates for your dates and book it

Stars: 3
Address: BERTRAN 9-15 - Barcelona, ES 08023

The Hotel Mitre is a first class hotel located 19 kilometers from the Barcelona-Prat Airport. Excellently situated on quiet Calle Bertran, this hotel is next to the Ronda de Dalt. The area is served by several modes of public transport, providing hotel guests with easy access to many of Barcelona's attractions. The Hotel Mitre, totally refurbished in 2001, exudes warmth and elegance. This hotel now boasts some of the best facilities and services that will satisfy even the most discerning traveler.

With only 57 guest rooms, the hotel radiates coziness and intimacy. Luxuriate in the full marble bathrooms accentuated by a whirlpool bath, magnifying mirror, hair dryer and slippers. Other amenities for your convenience include a safety deposit box, free coffee and mini-bar. For fitness or fun, guests can work out in the well-equipped fitness center or play a game of tennis and squash nearby. The Hotel Mitre is convenient to dining, shopping and entertainment venues.
