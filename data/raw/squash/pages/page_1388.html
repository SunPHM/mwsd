Canyon De Chelly - Tsegi Overlook

Tsegi overlook is one of the most beautiful overlooks at Canyon De Chelly. From here you can see the Chinle Wash - water that is diverted to grow crops for the Navajo people who live at the bottom of the canyon.

From the Tsegi Overlook you can also see Navajo farms and dwellings. The Navajo people grow corn, beans and squash. We are standing on cliffs that rise 600 feet from the canyon floor. As we progress to the east, the cliffs become higher until the Spider Rock overlook, which is at 900 feet above the floor of Canyon De Chelly.

Here's yours truly "on the edge". Unlike the Grand Canyon, there are very few fences to prevent careless people from falling over the edge. However, Canyon De Chelly is much farther north and on sacred Navajo land, so it is thankfully less travelled.
