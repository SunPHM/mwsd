Phil Corley has been involved in the Produce industry his entire life. The Corley Family began farming in Southern California in 1929.  Through the 50s and 60s were one of the largest growers and shippers of watermelons, winter squash and mixed melons in California.

Phil has been a grower, shipper, distributor, jobber and broker at various times in his life as well as a Director or manager for some of the largest produce companies in the US.  His commitment to Organic production began in 1989 and Phil has worked exclusively with organic product since 1995.  Agri-Organic Sales, Inc was conceived with the mission of bring good wholesome food to good people while ensuring the profitability of the dedicated certified organic growers they work with year round.
