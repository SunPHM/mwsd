Squashes & Sharbat 
The squashes are made from juice concentrates with very minor added features . They are drunk by dilution in a glass with the ratio 1: 6 ( squash to water). Another form, Sharbat, is the mixture of nine types of fruit juice and a little flavor enhancer to give a special taste. You can make drinks or add them in other drinks to get a cocktail feature. It is a non-alcoholic beverage.

Our types are
1. Orange Squash- from the freshest orange juice 
2. Lemon Squash – rich lemon juice are preserved in concentrated form.
3. Sharbat Naurus- a blend of nine types of fruit juice with a touch of
traditional syrup, rich in glucose that freshens life.
White Vinegar
The white vinegar is nothing special, just our edible form of acetic acid. Our white vinegar is much stronger and better than even many Chinese produces (who are good at it). It is added in any meal during or after cooking for their preservation and some like taste. It’s also added in salads as lemon juice substitutes.
