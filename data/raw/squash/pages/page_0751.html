Updated: Tue, Oct 9, 2001

Portland Square - 18th and 19th September 2001.
Building C:

Here's the next two sections of the portal frame for Building C - these were delivered to site on the afternoon of 18th September and were stored overnight.

The following day a mobile crane was on site to lift the portal frame sections into position - here's a close-up view of the first section being bolted down.

This view shows how Building C is progressing. You can see that the precast concrete floor planks for the first floor are in position and that the next two floors of steel frame are in the process of being erected.

Overview:

This picture shows how construction on the three buildings is progressing. Building A is still just a steel frame, while Building B has two floors decked with concrete beams. The mobile crane in the foreground is parked in what will eventually be the atrium of Building C.
Squash Courts:

The Squash Court building now has a roof - this deeply profiled sheeting will eventually be the inside of the roof. You can see that scaffolding is being erected to provide safe working conditions.
