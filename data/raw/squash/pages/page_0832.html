Score Reporting Form - Individual Sports

Individual participants can register on-line or stop by the Campus Sports office.
**Entries will not be held or registered until fees are paid within (24) hour of on-line registration.

How to Enter an Intramural Team
Fall Quarter: Volleyball, Flag Football, 3 on 3 Basketball, Wiffleball (no fee)
Winter Quarter: Basketball, Indoor Soccer, Broomball
Spring Quarter: Soccer, Volleyball, Softball 12", (no fee) Softball 16" and Ultimate Frisbee

1. Pick up an Official Entry Form and Complete Name of the Sport, Select League, Division Classification, Name of your team, Team Manager’s name, e-mail, phone number. Teams will be required to list a name and phone number of a team member that will be designated to be a game official. This person WILL be paid and required to officiate games each week, if necessary.
2. Team Roster. List of the names, identification numbers, and e-mail of all the members of your team. Six (6) players names must be on the roster in order to compete for your team or maybe added to the game sheet on site. Roster changes will be closed after the last regular schedule season game before the tournament.
3. Remit $30.00 CASH only, refundable if non-forfeiture season. $15.00 forfeit fee per game. If a team forfeits twice, they are dropped from the schedule and forfeit all monies.
4. Attend Mandatory Manager and Officials Meeting. The team manager or alternate must attend the mandatory manager’s meetings to acquire rules and schedules. Failure to attend MANDATORY meetings will assessed a $5 fine from the entry fee.

How to Enter an Individual/Dual Sport
Fall Quarter: Tennis, Squash, Badminton, Racquetball, Croquet, Chess, Spades and Hearts
Winter Quarter: Table Tennis, Racquetball, Euchre Tourney, Archery, Billiards, Backgammon
Spring Quarter: Tennis (Single, Double, Coed)

1. Pick up Entry Forms. Complete the following information: Participant’s Name, Identification number, Phone, (if appropriate) Partner’s Name, EMAIL, phone, and signature Same procedure as for major teams sports registration.
2. A $2.00 entry fee will be assessed per person. Pick-up Schedules from the Campus Recreation Office on the Friday after entries close. Schedules will not be given over the phone.
