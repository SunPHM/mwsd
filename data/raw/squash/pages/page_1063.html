Gourd Crafts

By The Artist Formerly Known as The F. Gourdmother   Articles in this Topic Discussions in this Topic

Hello again from Your Nameless Gourdmother.

Got squash? Ready to make this demonic skeleton and ghoulish ghost out of a plain old butternut and acorn squash?

That reminds me, do you know why demons and ghouls hang out together? Because demons are a ghoul's best friend! hehe

Okay, my mirth is over and my gourd rage is returning. It's not nice to fool your Fairy Gourdwhatchamajigger! Do you still have those jackass-o-lanterns on your porches? Are they soup yet? Don't make me come down there -- I'll take out every one of those festuring punkules in a drive-by gourding spree!

I simply cannot believe that after all the gourducation I've bestowed upon you, you persist in persecuting me with those gangrenous gourd wannabes. Have you no shame? Have I no credibility? Well, don't take my word for it, then. Here's visual proof of what those pukey pumpkins do:

Allrighty then, let's put away our Pepto Bismol and move on to something more pleasant: my new name. Which I don't have yet. And time is running out, my gourd friends. The contest to give me a new name ends October 21, so you must enter now! (Right after you get that pumpkin off your porch.)

Now, on with the show! Print out the ghost pattern and the skeleton pattern, then the instructions, and let's spiff up your Halloween with a splendiferous squash skeleton and ghost!

Gourdially Yours,
Your Fairy Gourd-yet-to-be-announced
