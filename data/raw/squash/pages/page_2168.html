Field Trips

Stowes Pumpkin Patch

Mrs. Buffington, Mrs. Powell and Mrs. Eckerley's class took a field trip to Stowe's Pumpkin Patch.  Students learned about harvesting, and different types of squash.  After taking a hayride and eating an apple, each child picked their own pumpkin from the patch.

 

Doud's Apple Orchard

Mrs. Gorseline's Kindergarten AM and PM class took a trip to Doud's Apple Orchard.  The children were able to walk through the orchard,  pick an apple, and select their own pumpkin.  They also went through the Corn Maze and took a wagon ride.

Little One's Visit Seasescapes Nursery

Mrs. Gorseline's AM/PM Kindergarten, Mrs. Donaldson's Extended Kindergarten and Mrs. Fodge's Preschool visited Seasescapes Nursery for a fieldtrip.

They learned the process of growing seedlings into beautiful plants and the machines that are used.

Each child was given a Sensitive Plant to take home and enjoy.
