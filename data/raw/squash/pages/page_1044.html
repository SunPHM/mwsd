"Experiment with Taste"

The Chef: Daniel McCarthy
The Recipe: Sweet Potato and Ham Soufflé

When it comes to experimenting with various ingredients, Daniel McCarthy, co-owner of Two Chefs Are Better Than One, says it's always been an important part of creating his next successful menu item.

McCarthy and Steven Beauvais, co-owners of Two Chefs Are Better Than One, strive to provide customers with harmonious flavors — constantly experimenting with new and exciting recipes. "We put a lot of care into our food. Both of us really love giving people good food that they can enjoy. It's fun and it becomes both a challenge and a reward to do," McCarthy says.

McCarthy developed his passion for cooking as an apprentice in Europe and has assisted at various venues in Spain and France, including as executive chef at some of Boston's best catering companies. His philosophy is pure and simple, "Play with it." He insists, "If you want to try something, try it, and if it doesn't work out, play with it again." This philosophy helped McCarthy and Beauvais create CorningWare's featured recipe, Sweet Potato and Ham Soufflé, a delicious dish perfect for holidays or anytime of the year.

"The combination of sweet potatoes, ham and cheese works well — all of the ingredients complement one another. And the addition of sour cream and chives, plus the maple syrup, really bring out the true flavor of this dish," McCarthy says. If you want to avoid sweet potatoes, McCarthy suggests substituting hubbard or butternut squash. "It's about finding your individual taste, so vary the ingredients and play with it."

In preparation for this recipe, McCarthy uses CorningWare's 1 ½-quart FRENCH WHITE® Round Dish. He prefers using CorningWare cookware because of the quality, convenience and durability. "The fact that you can easily cook, clean and store them makes CorningWare most versatile to work with," he says. McCarthy also prefers to use CorningWare dishes because of their white appearance and styled edges. "The white plate brings out the natural colors of the food and the styled edges add a nice presentation to the dish," he says. McCarthy loves to prepare individual dishes for parties of 2-4 using CorningWare's 4 and 7-ounce ramekins. "You can use them to make chocolate soufflés, appetizers or entrees such as individual lasagna, or pretty much anything."

Two Chefs Are Better Than One is an upscale diner serving healthy breakfast items ranging from fresh fruit and yogurt to more interesting dishes, including a variety of delicious omelets, pancakes, waffles and French toasts. McCarthy and Beauvais also cater events such as black tie galas, weddings, cocktail parties and intimate dinners for two.
