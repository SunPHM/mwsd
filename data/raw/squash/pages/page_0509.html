Squash Cake
(Squashkage) 		Denne side på dansk

Late summer is the time where the main squash harvesting takes place, and squash is available in abundance. This lovely cake provides a way to use this affluence. The advantage of using squash this way is a firm and moist texture.

Print recipe

Ingredients:

200 ml oil
3 eggs
250 g sugar
350 g white flour
2 tsp vanilla sugar
2 tsp powdered cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
50 g chopped walnuts
100 g raisins
lemon zest
4 squash
icing:
icing sugar
lemon juice
water
coarsely chopped lemon zest

Method:

Beat eggs and oil together. Stir sugar, flour, vanilla, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, salt, walnuts and raisins together. Blend in the egg mixture and add the coarsely grated squash.

Pour the dough in a baking tin and bake at 175°C (347°F) for 1 to 1½ hour. Let the cake cool, remove from tin and decorate with icing and coarsely grated lemon zest.
