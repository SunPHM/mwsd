Club information

Squash City organizes club events, competition ladders and demo's at international level. If you want to improve your skills, you may need some coaching, available from any of our internationally recognised squash coaches. Year-members of Squash City are also offered the opportunity to take part in the national squash competition.

National competition
Squash City offers its year-members the opportunity to join the national squash competition. This competition runs from September to March and is open for all year-memberships. If you prefer not to play, you are always welcome to come and watch one of these exciting matches played by the teams of Squash City.

Squash City competition ladder
See if you can match your fellow squash players and challenge them by joining our competition ladder. You can work your way up on the ladder in two ways: by putting your name on the waiting list which means starting at the bottom of the ladder or by challenging someone above you. Its best only to do this if you consider yourself a somewhat higher-level player. The competition ladder is played during both day and night time. See the board located at court 5 for more information.

Single Players Club
Don't have a regular squash partner? Would you like to play more often than your squash partner or maybe you would like to match your skills with other players once in a while? We have introduced the "Squash City Single Players Club" especially for these people. On the board located at court 5 you can fill in your name, telephone number and hours and days you would like to play. In this way fellow squash players who are looking for a squash partner can respond to it. You can also look for a squash partner on this website with find a squashpartner.

Tournaments & club events
Squash City organizes great club events, tournaments and 'the beginners club' on a regular basis. These events often end with a delicious buffet and a drink. Watch the monitor above reception at Squash City to be sure of an early entry. Coming soon... Beginners Squash Clinics Learn the basic skills to introduce you to the game in a 5 week course. Sessions will include: warm-ups, stretching, skill developments, fitness for squash, rules and a whole lot more
