The new swimming pool and health spa at Martlesham Leisure have added a new dimension to the already extensive health and fitness facilities on offer at the Club.

The spa and pool area provides leisure swimming, steam room and spa tub in attractive surroundings.  The perfect conclusion to a work-out, exercise class or competitive game of squash.

The relaxation area by the pool leads out on to the sun terrace, an ideal location during fine weather to relax after an invigorating exercise programme.
