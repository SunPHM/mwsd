PARADIS
Hotel Paradise - Paradis

"The Greatest Leisure Resort in Mauritius and the Indian Ocean"

Ideally positioned on a peninsula of 125 acres with 7km of beach at the foot of Le Morne mountain

Full sports and health facilities including an 18-hole championship golf course & BIG Game Fishing (experts)

The widest range of luxury accommodation on the island, perfect for the chic individual


Your own private peninsula - Just Perfect !

 Situated in the South West of Mauritius on a peninsula of more than 125 acres is the Paradis Resort. At the foot of Le Morne Mountain, the location of this premier resort complex is possibly the most beautiful site in Mauritius
little wonder they call it 'hotel paradise'.

With 7km of sandy white beaches slope to the turquoise waters of the vast coral-protected lagoon - Paradis is truly Paradise.

Paradis has the widest range of luxury accommodation on the island, ideal for the chic and worldly. The accommodation consists of both garden and sea view double rooms, superior and deluxe rooms which are all sea facing, as are the suites.


Senior Suite Accommodation

All the rooms are located in elegant two or three-storey buildings, with their own sitting area and terrace.


Junior Suite Accommodation

For those seeking an active break the Paradis will be their Garden of Eden with everything surpassing expectations. This is the golfing resort of Mauritius.

The Paradis is situated 42 miles from the airport which involves a transfer time of 1 hour 10 minutes and is 19 miles from the capital Port Louis.


Dining Room - within the Beach Villas

Dining

There is a choice of dining in 4 superb restaurants.
Le Paradis is the main restaurant and offers a tropical American buffet style breakfasts, à la carte lunches and a themed buffet every evening. Either as an alternative to lunch, or for those feeling peckish mid afternoon, there is afternoon tea where delicious pancakes are on offer.


Blue Marlin Restaurant - adjacent to beach

The delicate and varied cuisine of the Paradis
will meet the appreciation of the finest gourmet.

Le Blue Marlin - adorned with momentoes of deep sea fishing, a beach restaurant of wood and thatch is open for breakfast, à la carte lunch, and dinner where you can choose from the à la carte or table d'hôte menu. It is very popular in the evenings with the honeymooners who can enjoy the candlelit dining and romantic atmosphere.

La Palma is an Italian restaurant and is open for dinner with an à la carte menu. Whatever restaurant you choose, you will find that your evening meal is an occasion.

La Ravanne offers guests the chance to sample the many flavours of local Creole cooking. This restaurant is set under the stars and can be approached by sea on a pirogue, which departs at regular intervals in the evening from the Blue Marlin Restaurant. A traditional Mauritian atmosphere is created, and is further enhanced by the fine cuisine.

Entertainment

In keeping with its reputation as "The Greatest leisure resort in the Indian Ocean", the Paradis has two fresh water pools for those people who do not want to bathe in the sea.

There are numerous other daytime activities to enjoy. In addition the Paradis also has squash courts, and its own fleet of BIG Game fishing boats - the Paradis holds many records for their 'catch' of the fabulous - Blue Marlin.

In the evening there is entertainment, a casino, cabarets and the vibrant and exciting Sega dance once a week. If this isn't enough to keep you busy there are beautiful gardens bursting with tropical trees and flowers for you to explore. For the more energetic, there are regular aerobics classes in the gymnasium, mountain bikes for hire and several organised excursions.


     18 hole Championship Golf Course

One of the most sought after spa resorts on the island
the Paradis - ( hotel paradise as it nick-named ), just has that little bit of 'magic' that makes it 'special' - especially amongst sports enthusiasts and those seeking excellent golfing holidays abroad.
