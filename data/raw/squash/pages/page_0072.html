KINGFISHER, the World and the UK’s number one selling premium Indian lager, is the official beer sponsor of the PSA Super Series Squash finals (Monday 10th to Friday 14th May).

The Super Series Finals is one of the most eagerly anticipated dates on the world squash annual calendar and takes place in a specially-constructed, all-glass court at the Broadgate Arena, London.

Established in 1992, the tournament presents the top eight players from the five Super Series qualifying events staged in 2003, including The Tournament of Champions in New York, The PSA Masters, The Canadian Open, the Quatar Classic and the World Open in Pakistan, who will compete in the annual grand final.

Kingfisher Premium Indian Lager, which first established itself as the No.1 Indian Lager in Britain’s Indian Restaurants, is now also widely available in the retail drinks sector and almost all major supermarket stores.

A further success for parent company UBSN Limited was to have its newly launched Kingfisher Natural Spring Water as the sole, official water to the PSA Finals. Drawn from natural springs near the Foothills of the Cambrian Mountains in Wales, Kingfisher Natural Spring Water has been targeted for sale in top quality hotels and restaurants in the U.K and is sure to go down well with entrants.

Last year’s tournament saw a thrilling final between Jonathon Power and Peter Nicol, the sport’s greatest rivals. The event also boasted record ticket sales and unpredicted levels of media coverage.

As part of its sponsorship Kingfisher is offering spectators the chance to win crates of lager and mineral water in a prize draw that will take place on each day of the Finals. In addition, The ‘Kingfisher Player of the Day Award’ will be presented to the best player voted by the media.
