The Wolverine's Secret

By: Joanna

This legend is about the creation of the Northern lights. A young orphan girl who sets out to find the sun and moon which was stolen by the wolverine and taken to a secret location. She meets her aunt who knows where it is and tricks her into saying where it is. When she reaches the location, she has a snowball fight with the wolverine. The girl takes some sunlight and moonlight and mixes it with snow. She throws it in the air and a great light appears in the sky which today we call the Northern Lights. When the orphan returned to her village, a shaman, who is very jealous of her, realizes that the orphan is greater then he could ever be.

Why the Buffalo Has a Hump:

This legend comes from the natives of the plains. It is about a fierce buffalo that has no humps on its back. One day he is running with the herd when he runs off just to trample a flock of birds. The wizard hit sit over the back because of what he did to the flock of birds. The next daythen a day later the buffalo had a hump on its back. That is how the buffalo got its hump.

The Man Who Hated His Life

This story is about a man who hates his life and tries to become an animal, so he sets off to become an animal. He goes and asks a whole bunch of animal chiefs and he is expected by the eagle chief. The man/eagle is flying in the sky when a little boy shoots him with a arrow. The man/eagle falls down to the ground and tells the boy to rip off his feathers. The boy sees that the man is his father and and they go home holding hands.

The Three Sisters

 A very long time ago God saw that the native people were starving. He asked three beautiful girls to die so their people would not starve. The girls died and were buried and each one of them grew back as a vegetable, one as corn, the other as beans and the third as squash.

Basket Women and Clamshell Boy

The native tale is from the Pacific west coast. The main story line is about 4 kids who disobey their mothers by staying out at night, then Basket Women takes the kids away to her house to eat them. Then Clamshell Boy thinks of a plan to trick Basket Women.

How the Rabbit and the Owl Look the Way They Do

The the creator made the rabbit and the owl look the way they do when the owl made a mistake. The creator was busy making the rabbit when the owl interrupted the creator and said " I want the best of everything." The creator was angry with owl and gave owl big eyes and a small beak. He gave the rabbit long ears and short legs.

The characters in this legend were the creator, the owl, and the rabbit.
