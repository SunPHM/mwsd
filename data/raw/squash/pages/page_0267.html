Squash/Tennis Courts


Laurier’s Athletic Complex houses two International Squash Courts that are heavily used by members for no charge. Racquets and Balls can be rented from the Hawk Desk for a nominal fee.

In addition to the Squash Courts are two Tennis Courts located adjacent to Willison Field (across the parking lot from the Athletic Complex). 

Rental Option
 One Court: $25/hour
