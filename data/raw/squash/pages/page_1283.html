    • New Varieties: Vegetable and Melons

    Information on some of the latest vegetable and melon varieties suitable for Florida markets is presented on the following pages. Details about disease resistance/tolerance, product description and maturity dates were provided by the seed companies.

    Key for variety resistance/tolerance
    A = Alternaria ALS = Alternaria Leaf Spot
    ASC = Alternaria Stem Canker
    BLS = Bacterial Leaf Spot
    CMV = Cucumber Mosaic Virus
    F = Fusarium
    PM = Powdery Mildew
    PVY = Potato Virus Y
    TobaMo = Tobacco Mosaic Virus
    S = Sclerotinia
    V = Verticillium
    WMV = Watermelon Mosaic Virus

    Cantaloupe

    Odyssey (Formerly SXM 7119) ñ This large cantaloupe from Sunseeds has resistance/tolerance to Fusarium wilt race 2 and powdery mildew. Odyssey is an early Easternñtype melon with good netting and interior quality, and firm orange flesh. Fruit is uniform in shape and size. The firmness of the fruit provides good storage and shipping capabilities. PXC 221 ñ An alternative hybrid Easternñshipper type from Siegers Seed Company with better production of fruit per acre, and superior netting. For commercial melon growers who require a premium shipping cantaloupe. PCX 221 has minimal sutures, weighs in at about 4.5 to 6 pounds and has firm, sweet, aromatic flesh. Its plants boasts very strong vines that allow for an extended harvest period and is tolerant to Fusarium and powdery mildew. Limited seeds available. An exclusive of Siegers Seed Company.

    Gourmet F1 ñ A whole new category of melon. Gourmet offered by Hollar Seeds is a oneñofñañ kind fruit that will amaze you with its taste, texture, sweetness and aroma. Maturing in 70 to 75 days, the fruit weighs 3 to 4 pounds and is 7ñinches long and 6ñinches in diameter. The rind has a sparse, distinctive netting over green and orange skin. The interior color is creamy white with green towards the rind. It has a high sugar content, substantially higher, on average, than cantaloupe or honeydew. It has smooth, firm flesh, not crisp or too hard. The shelf life is better than muskmelons, not hard like a cantaloupe. Should be picked immediately upon ripening.

    Cucumbers

    Speedway ñ A gynoecious slicer from Seminis/Petoseed yields quality cucumbers in about 56 days. With a moderately vigorous vine, Speedway is resistant to ALS, Añ2 and Sc and has intermediate resistance to CMV and PM. Fruit are about 8ñinches long and hold their uniform, darkñgreen color better than many other American slicers.








    Lettuce

    BOS 9041 ñ Orsetti Seeds offers a somewhat larger Two Star type green leaf with improved color. Leaves are wavy, frilled, have heavy weight and a thick texture. BOS 9041's excellent pliability is beneficial to retaining head integrity during packing and shipping.

    Peppers

    ACX 209 ñ A superior main season bell pepper from Abbott & Cobb is consistent with high yields. This variety has strong plants with a full canopy, extrañlarge, highñquality fruit and excellent disease resistance. Fruits are blocky to deep blocky with thick walls and mature green in 76 days to red in 86 days. ACX 209 carries resistance to Bacterial Leaf Spot races 1, 2 and 3.




    ACX 400 ñ A Hungarian Hot Wax hybrid from Abbott & Cobb with extremely high yields. ACX 400 has very compact, vigorous plants that protect a concentrated set. Early maturing plants produce very attractive, smooth fruits. The fruits average 5 1/2 x 1 1/2ñinches and range in color from light yellow to orange to red.


    ACX 500 ñ An exciting new hybrid cubanelle from Abbott & Cobb. In 60 days the fruits of this variety are uniform, 7 x 2 1/2ñinches, 3 to 4 lobed and an attractive lightñgreen color. The plants are large with excellent foliage protection and are very productive.




    Agriset 4001 ñ This early maturing jalapeno from Agrisales Inc. is fast becoming the variety of choice by growers who demand high yields of uniform glossy fruits. Plants are compact and provide an excellent cover for this thick walled hot jalapeno. Fruits have shown no cracking or purpleing and are medium to large in size.



    Aristotle ñ This greenñtoñred, blocky bell from Seminis/Petoseed has tremendous yield potential. It has a heavyset, thick walls and has exhibited enormous fruit size. Wellñsuited to growing conditions in the Southeast, its very vigorous plant produces dark green, anthocyaninless fruit reistant to Bacterial Leaf Spot races 1,2 and 3 (X3R), PVY Po and TobaMo Po.
    Defiance ñ This greenñtoñred hybrid bell pepper from Seminis/Asgrow maintains an attractive blocky shape even under stress. The fruit have been mostly extrañlarge with shallow shoulders and good color. The plant is tall, sturdy, dense and resistant to bacterial Leaf Spot races 1,2 and 3 (X3R). This variety is wellñadapted for growing conditions in the Southeast.
    Legionnaire (RPP 6089ñVP)ñ Legionnaire's excellent disease package is well suited for Florida and the Midwest. This ROGERS® brand pepper delivers a concentrated set of large dark green, firm, thickñwalled fruit that maintains their shape despite temperature fluctuations. Legionnaire has demonstrated resistance to bacterial leaf spot races 1, 2, and 3, tobacco mosaic virus and tolerance to Stip.




    Orion ñ Great crops begin with great seeds. Orion, (BLSR I, II and III), from Enza Zaden is the latest offering to premium pepper growers. It has a large, blocky, 4ñlobe, thick walled midñmaturing variety with excellent plant cover. Orion is entering its second season with a long list of success stories.
    Peninsula F1 ñ This pepper from Harris Moran offers a unique combination of outstanding fruit yield and quality combined with disease resistance on a plant that will withstand continuous picks throughout the season. Peninsula has firm, smooth fruit with heavy walls that will withstand harvesting and shipping stress.
    Summer Heat #5000 ñ An outstanding hybrid jalapeno pepper from Abbott & Cobb. Summer Heat is a midñseason jalapeno pepper with strong vigorous plants. It has an excellent yield potential, with smooth, highñquality fruits and plants that provide excellent cover. The fruits are 3 1/2ñinches long, 1ñinch wide and a very darkñgreen color. This variety is a consistent performer, with high yields, that is adapted for all growing areas and is resistant/tolerant to TMV and PVY.
    Summer Sweet #830 ñ An outstanding bell pepper from Abbott & Cobb is especially well adapted to early fall and early spring planting in Florida. The plants are strong and vigorous with a resistance to Bacterial Leaf Spot races 1, 2 and 3. Fruits are early maturing, dark green turning to an attractive red, with a smooth, long, blocky shape. Excellent set and yield potential.

    Sweet Corn

    Envy ñ This main season 81day SuperSweet hybrid offered by SeedWay has excellent yield potential. Performs best for fall harvest. Grows and yields excellent at higher plant densities 25 to 28,000 population. Envy has a good dark green cover with excellent flags, ears are 7 to 7 1/2ñinches in length.
    Starship II ñ This main season sh2 from SeedWay has proven performance in all sweet corn growing areas. Vigorous, robust plants provide great yield potential. Ears average 8 inches in length with excellent tip fill and are packaged in attractive darkñgreen husks. Resistance to common rust and matures in 82 days.
    Saturn ñ Enjoy the full range of colors with yellow, bicolor and white versions of this 75 day sh2 sweet corn from SeedWay. Very uniform and consistent hybrid producing ears of ideal size and appearance for crating and shipping or for local sales. Easy snap, tight shank suits hand and machine harvest. Matures in 82 days.

    Squash

    CashFlowñ A combination of high yield potential and exceptional quality makes CashFlow an attractive new option. In 44 to 47 days, its vigorous plant has produced high yields of medium green fruit with highly uniform shape and color. Demonstrated tolerance to strains of zucchini yellow mosaic virus enhances CashFlow's profit potential.
    Cougar F1 ñ This yellow straightneck squash from Harris Moran has tolerance to Zuccini Yellow Mosaic Virus (ZYMV) and Papaya Ring Spot Virus (WMV1) you want plus the fruit quality and the yield you require. Cougar colors early and resists greening caused my Cucumber Mosaic Virus and WMV2.

    Tomatoes

    Agriset 8279 ñ This hybrid grape tomato variety from Agrisales Inc. has the quality, size and flavor that growers have been searching for. Strong vigorous plants have shown tolerance to many bacterial diseases. Uniformly grapeñshaped fruits are held tightly in clusters of 10 to 14 and have a Brix of 8 to 11 percent. For more information visit GrapeTomatoes.com.

    Best Boy ñ This hybrid tomato from W.A. Burpee & Company matures in 70 days. It's determinate and its compact plants produce heavy crops of large, round, red, 8ñounce fruits. Yields begin in the early part of the main season with shoulders having superb, uniform coloring. A Celebrityñtype tomato with excellent flavor has slightly larger fruits and better firmness with outstanding disease resistance (VFFNT, ASC, ST). Burpee variety.
    BHN 410 ñ A hybrid roma tomato from BHN Seeds produces excellent volume of large fruit with a classic deep blocky roma shape. This variety offers decreased labor costs through: fewer harvests from a very concentrated fruit set, no need for pruning and fewer tying of the meduim compact bush.
    BHN 444 F1ñThis tomato, offered by Johnny's Selected Seeds, is Tomato Spotted Wilt Virus (TSWV) resistant. Vigorous, determinate with large, red globeñshaped fruit. Produces good yields even under high disease pressure. Grow for your own crop or as pack sales to home gardeners in affected areas. (76 days.)
    BHN 543 ñ This hybrid tomato from BHN Seed provides high yields and excellent quality large to extra large fruit for South Florida's fall and winter production. BHN 543 has the quality the packinghouses expects and the high yield and pack out the grower needs.
    Florida 91 ñ A hotñset tomato for the eastern U.S. is offered by Seminis/Asgrow. Maturing in the main season, it has a highñyield potential and exhibits exceptionally firm and smooth fruit throughout the season. Mostly extrañlarge in size, the fruit are uniform green with jointed pedicels on a strong, healthy vine.
    Heatwave II ñ A hybrid tomato from W.A. Burpee & Company matures in 68 days. This determinate tomato was developed to excel in the heat of the South. This plant produces heavily when daytime temperatures range between 90 and 96 F. Fruits are red, round to deeply ovate and perfectly sized at 6 to 7 ounces each. Good disease resistance (VF 1 and 2, AS) makes this an ideal plant for any size grower. Our southern customers swear by it. Burpee variety.
    St. Nick ñ Available only from Siegers Seed Comapny, St. Nick is a beautiful hybrid, oblong grapeñshaped tomato weighing only 12 to 15 grams with 8 to 10 percent sugar content (Brix). Clusters of up to 50 green shouldered fruit are possible and hold firm on its indeterminate plant type. Brilliant red ad delicious, this variety is comparable to, if not better than, any other grapeñ shaped tomato you have ever grown or heard about. Limited seed availability.
    Sweet Olive F1ñ This determinate, Santañtype red grape tomato offered by Johnny's Selected Seeds, matures in 57 days. Outstanding in our extensive grape tomato trial for flavor, texture and preferred small size. Strong tolerance to heat, humidity and cracking. Plan for multiple plantings to endure abundant supply throughout the season.

    Watermelon

    Anthem F1     (Formerly HSR 2261)ñ A great watermelon from Hollar Seeds. The stripes stay bright green. Compare Anthem F1 to Dumara or Crimson Tide and you'll find that Hollar Seeds' new watermelon is worth singing about. Bright red flesh is set off by small dark seeds. Storage/shelf life is good due to crisp, fine flesh. Rind is tough enough to withstand shipping. Maturity is about the same as Crimson Sweet, but the stripes stay sharp; beautiful green on a light green background. Fruit weigh 17 1/2 to 27 pounds in most areas. It tolerates Fusarium wilt and anthracnose. Fruits are about 11.5ñinches long and 9 inches in diameter. This variety has been found to be well adapted to different climates.
    Margarita ñ An attractive Allsweet hybrid offered by Florida Seed. It has a deep red flesh with average fruit weight of 22 to 26 pounds. This highñyielding variety has uniform shape, is a good shipper. Fusaium wilt tolerant. Pinata ñ This classy Allsweet type melon is offered by Florida Seed. It has an extremely uniform shape with bright red flesh. A very productive variety with average fruit size of 20 to 25 pounds. Tolerant to Fusarium wilt and powdery mildew.
    Premiere ñ This Crimson Sweet type seedless watermelon offered by Southwestern Seed Company is well suited for all growing regions. This melon has red flesh and excellent fruit quality. Fruit weighs 17 to 20 pounds. Tolerant to Fusarium. All Southwestern Seed's seedless are grown in North America and greenhouse tested.
    Revolution ñ (Formerly SXW 4034) Triploid watermelon from Sunseeds is a blocky/elongated Allsweet type with wide darkñgreen stripes. This midñseason variety offers excellent quality, attractive red flesh and small seed traces. At 20 to 26 pounds, it is ideal for the fresh market and food service industry. It resists hollow heart and is resistant/tolerant to Fusarium wilt race 1.
    SeedWay 4502 ñ A crisp looking seedless melon with a distinct Crimson Sweet rind pattern. Offered by SeedWay, this variety has a thick rind great for shipping and flesh is crisp and bright red. Fruit weighs about 15 to 18 pounds matures in 85 days, has a low incidence of Hollow Heart and high yields. Seed quality is superior and university trials have shown high transplant yields.
    Slice N Serve 830 ñ This earlyñmaturing, Crimsonñtype seedless watermelon is offered by Southwestern Seed Company. Fruit weighs 15 to 18 pounds and has deep red flesh and tough rind for superior marketability. High yielding and uniform melon. Tolerant to Fusarium.
    Triple Crown ñ High yields and good interior qualities are what put Triple Crown above other varieties. This variety offered by SeedWay is among the top varieties in trials, expect melons in the 15ñ to 18ñpound range. Slightly oval in shape with a Crimson Sweet look and has a maturity of about 85 days.
