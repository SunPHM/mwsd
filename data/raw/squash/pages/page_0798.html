Coaches & Squash lessons

Once you know the basic rules, everyone should be able to enjoy a good game of squash. However, if you would like to get the most out of it, you may wish to consider squash lessons. Squash is a technical sports game and the right instruction and tips will make your squash game become better and smarter. By taking the right positions, improving your ball and racket skills and being able to think 'a few strokes ahead', you will learn how to outsmart your opponent and how to keep up your pace throughout the game.

Squash lessons
Squash City offers leading coaches from the international squash world. Squash lessons are given on a regular basis. To make an appointment contact our reception at: 020-6267883. Lessons are 25 minutes each and given in private, so 1-on-1. Should you still want to take lessons together with someone else, ask for the possibilities at reception first. You can also buy a 5-lessons card which works out to be less expensive. This card is valid for 6 months after the date of purchase.
