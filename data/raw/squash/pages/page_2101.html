Enthusiasm, skill, participation; we aim to combine all three in our sport at Edgeborough. Enjoyment is the key, from beginner to star. All boys and girls have a full sporting programme which revolves mostly round the team games - rugby, netball, soccer, hockey in the winter, and cricket, rounders and athletics in the summer - but a number of other sports are available.

 

Squash, riding and sailing take place off the site, but having our own Sports Hall, floodlit astro courts, Swimming Pool and Golf Course enables us to provide a number of other activities which include basketball, gymnastics and tennis. Those who compete must learn, to do so wholeheartedly with generosity of spirit; it is with that in mind that we regularly invite top class internationals to come to the School to give all the children a taste of what really constitutes a true sportsman.
