Playing arrangements......

The aim of the tennis section playing arrangements is to maximise the use of courts and give a fair allocation of time to beginners, intermediates, juniors, club and match players. Club "nights" are held on Monday, Tuesday and Thursday evenings and Sunday afternoon. During club play full members are expected to mix in and ensure that all players get a game with mixed doubles the priority. 

On Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday there are ladies' mornings. Junior squad coaching is on Monday and Wednesday afternoon and Friday evening. 

Wednesday and Friday evening are match practice and junior night respectively. 

See this page for full details of the playing arrangements. 

Teams

During winter the club runs two ladies', two men's and three veteran's teams which play in the Aylesbury league. In the summer three men's and four ladies' teams play in the Herts league. Aylesbury league teams also play through the summer (including 2 mixed doubles teams). 

Club matches take precedence over all other play, but are arranged to minimise the disruption to club play. They are usually held at weekends and some midweek evenings during summer. Two or three courts are required for home matches. 

Tournaments

The club holds its major annual tournament during June and July. Players of all standards are welcome. 

Leagues

Throughout the year a singles league operates for men and women of all abilities and juniors who have represented the club at senior level. There are currently about 100 participants playing in leagues of 5 on a 6-week cycle. Matches are won by the first player to reach ten games. 

Coaching

The club coach (Chris Stringer) provides one-to-one lessons at £10 per half-hour and conducts a variety of squad coaching sessions throughout the year. Chris has priority at all times on court 4 (day) and court 3 (evenings). 

Junior Tennis

The club actively promotes junior tennis. Teams are entered in the Herts Leagues (boys and girls) during the summer and winter. For younger players we currently enter three teams in the U12 Chiltern League. An annual tournament is open to all juniors with finals normally held in September. 

Juniors always have priority on courts 5 & 6, and Friday evening is junior club night.
