Third Grade Garden

The Third Grade Classes at Neutra recently planted a vegetable garden in the inner circle. Some of items planted included strawberries, squash, and tomatoes. All the students had an opportunity to set out a sprouting plant and will be enjoying the chance to water them and watch them grow. Hopefully, we will all have an opportunity to sample what we planted in the near future.
 
     
 
We would like to offer a special thanks to Mr. Valdez, Toby, and Condie for their help in this endeavor!
