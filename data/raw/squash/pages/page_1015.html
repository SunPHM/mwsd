Butternut Squash and Shiitake Mushroom Hash

This recipe was adapted from The Culinary Mustard, our handy recipe pamphlet of our favorite mustard recipes.
 

8 Tbsp. Butter
1 Butternut squash, peeled, seeded and diced
1 Russet potato, peeled and diced
1 lb. Shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and sliced very thin
1 Yellow onion, peeled and diced
2 Tbsp. Thyme (fresh)
1 Tbsp Pommery Moutarde de Meaux or other Whole Grain Dijon

 

Place the butter in a very large sauté pan over medium high heat. Add the squash, potato and mushrooms and without turning too often, allow to brown a little. Add the onions, thyme, mustard and continue to sauté  until well browned and very aromatic. Serve right away.

Note: Moutarde de Meaux is an exquisite French grained mustard that comes in an earthenware crock. It can be hard to find but the Mustard Museum usually has some in stock. Yes, we have some on hand right now!
