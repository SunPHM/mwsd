What We Do, Garden

We keep a small garden and orchard on the ranch to help supplement the food bank and the nutritional needs of visitors.  Come on in and be surprised by the fresh food and wonderful creations of Chef Charles.  If you're hungry and you and your loved ones can't afford to put food on the table, we provide 3 hot meals a day seven days a week to help get you back on your feet.  After enjoying the fine cuisine, please sit a spell with us and perhaps we can help nourish your spiritual needs as well.
