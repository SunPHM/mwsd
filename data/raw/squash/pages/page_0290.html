Squash Club

The Naden Squash Club is your direct line to fun and fitness. Squash is at the top of the list in terms of sports that provide the most aerobic benefit. Whether you are a seasoned professional or just want to try it out, the club is for you. Club members also get a discount at selected sports stores downtown.

The competitive and playdowns for the City League Squash Team, meet on Thursdays; with future competitions between the YMCA, Panorama, Royal Rhodes and UVic. Twice a year the City League Teams hosts a tournament night at Naden Athletic Centre. The club also offers clinics and lessons on Saturday mornings at 09:15 for those who wish to improve their game or to start learning. The program is offered to the kids also, through our Junior Squash Program, available to dependents of authorized gym users.
