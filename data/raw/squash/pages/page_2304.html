Squash Beetle Morris

Lexington-in-the-Bluegrass

Squash Beetle Morris came into existence in the Summer of 1994. The name Squash Beetle Morris was inspired both by the famous Fieldtown caper known as a beetle squasher, and by all the Japanese beetles that were eating Judy and Andy's raspberry plants.

Our Fore is Andy Klapper.

Our Squire is Judy Goldsmith.

Our Bag is Bill Penn (fiddle).

Our musicians are Bill Penn (fiddle), Chris Newberry (recorder) and Shirley Carlberg (recorder).

Our current traditions are Lexington-in-the-Bluegrass (a work in progress), Bledington, and Border Morris.

Practices are 7-9 PM, Tuesday evenings at Arts Place, 161 North Mill St., Lexington. Please join us. Beginners are welcome.
