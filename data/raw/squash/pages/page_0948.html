Kira, 5 Months, February 2004

Kira is 13.8 lbs, and 25  inches long as of 2/5/04.

This month Kira had her first bite of solid food, which was rice cereal (enthusiastically displayed below!) She moved on to oatmeal and butternut squash this month, also. (She also "moved" her first "real" poop, the stinky kind! She flips and rolls all over and almost crawled this month. She "helps" me in the office with my paperwork.

She attended her first SuperBowl party and got her first pair of jeans! She celebrated our 4th Anniversary at Ruth Chris Steak House in her Valentine's Day outfit (shown here)

She also slept through the night a couple of times this month, but has lately taken to waking us up every two hours again. We're trying to figure out how to get her to to the former more consistently!! She had a pediatrician appointment around her 5 month birthday. He said she's a bit bowlegged and her eye is still a little lazy. If they don't clear up on their own we'll get her a brace and an eye patch and she'll be just fine.

Her new activities are her rolling around the floor, crumpling paper, playing in her new saucer, sitting up by herself, standing with a little help and support, and of course, EATING!

...and her first SUPPER Bowl!!

 

Happy Valentines Day!
