 Tuesday, September 25, 2001
Theme: ATA2001 

The Anti-Terrorism Act of 2001 could land you life in prison if you don't have a firewall. Suppose someone launches an attack from your machine and you can't prove it was not you? Zip...you're in the slammer.

And you thought ATA100 was fast.

I actually called my congressman on this one. Well, the Iowa congressman on the committee, I mean.

India, US differ on terrorist groups list :

President Bush himself spoke publicly on Monday about the need to ensure the stability of the Musharraf regime in Pakistan, in apparent contradiction of the US pressure till some weeks back of the need for that country to return to democracy.
Check out the last line in the article.

I had to chuckle when I was browsing through the latest Federal Computer Week and saw the news section entitled, A new kind of combat. I chuckled because I initially had read it as A new kind of wombat.

We harvested some squash over the weekend. It's good with butter and brown sugar, and maybe even a few marshmallows on top.
