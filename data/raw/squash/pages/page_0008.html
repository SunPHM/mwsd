More than just golf

  
As well as golf, Los Arqueros Golf & Country Club offers table tennis and has 2 all-weather tennis courts. It's a great way to have fun and keep fit and with on average 330 days of sunshine a year, get a tan!.



 


 

The Country Club Squash courts are available to all guests - ideal for the really fit or a gentle, friendly game out of the sun.


Tennis & Squash equipment is available for hire. Ask at the Club House shop.

Guests can also use the air-conditioned fitness gym based in the Club House. It is well equipped with running, rowing and hill climbing machines to name a few.

Instructors are on hand to demonstrate any of the equipment or work out a fitness plan for you. And afterwards, you can indulge in a relaxing sauna.
