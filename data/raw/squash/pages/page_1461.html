It's All Happening at Hatfield Leisure Centre


Hatfield Leisure Centre provides a wide variety of excellent sport and leisure activities.
With friendly staff and a welcoming environment, it's the ideal place to exercise, play sports and socialise.
The centre caters for all ages and standards of abilities, offering superb facilities and a comprehensive programme of activities and clubs for everyone to enjoy.


Squash


 
Enjoy a game of squash in one of our glass backed courts. Don't worry if you're new to the game - you can hire the racquets and staff are on hand to give you advice to get started. Regular players are invited to join our in-house league.

Badminton


A great game for people of all ages and abilities - easy to pick up and fun to play. We have six courts, all bookable in advance.

Family Badminton - Saturday 5pm to 7pm


This special session is for families to spend some quality time together. Enjoy a game of badminton with your children or maybe even challenge other parents!
Badminton Improvers Session Sunday 7pm - 9pm
Enjoy a game of badminton and develop your skills. This special practice session is for adults of all abilities and is ideal for players that don't have a partner. It includes free play and mixed friendly competitions.

Table Tennis


Table tennis for all - check out availability


Aerobics, gymnastics, fencing and martial arts

With its wooden sprung floor and mirrored walls the Activity Studio is just the place for our aerobics, gymnastics, fencing and martial arts classes.

New - New - New - Hatfield SKA Karate Club New - New - New

A new club starting in Hatfield practising traditional Shotokan Karate. Suitable for Men and Women, helps to get fit, develop self confidence and teaches practical self defence techniques.
Taught by a highly experienced instructor with over 23 years experience

Dance

The Susan Ford School of Dancing offer a wide range of Childrens and Adults classes in Classical Ballet, Tiny Tots Ballet, Tap and Modern Jazz Dance for Complete Beginners to Advanced Movers.

Five-a-Side Football, Netball

The Main Sports Hall
The ideal indoor venue for football, netball and any other indoor games requiring a large space and a spectator area - perfect for our indoor 5-a-side league and cup competitions.

Outdoor All Weather Courts
 
Outside we have two floodlit 5-a-side football pitches, with the best synthetic surfaces in the District. Come and try them for yourselves.
Netball Improvers Session Sundays 11am - 12 noon
Enjoy a game of netball and develop your skills. This special practice session is for ladies of all abilities. It is ideal for those wanting to keep fit in a fun, friendly environment.
