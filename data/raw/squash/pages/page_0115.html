Jack Adakai-Navajo






Jack Adakai (Deceased) well known for his high quality Squash sets and concha belts. The necklace has a total drop of 15 inches, the bracelet has an overall width of 2.75 inches. Both pieces are set with best quality natural Blue Diamond turquoise. This gives a very high polish denoting the hardness of the material. Note: Jack Adakai was the father-in-law of the well known Gallup trader John Hornbek (Red Shell Jewelry).
