Squash Balls

Squash Sporting Goods Exporters Sporting Goods Catalogs Sporting Goods Trade Leads

Squash balls are made of an extremely durable high quality rubber compound that provides the ultimate performance squash ball for professional tournament and serious players. The balls may be made from specially developed PU foam which is softer, lighter and larger than a conventional squash ball.
The ball doesn't bounce until it is warmed up by hitting it hard 50-100 times. After that the ball reaches an equilibrium temperature and is quite bouncy. The good quality squash balls have professional hang time.

These are the four balls offered by leading squash goods manufacturers like Dunlop:
  • Pro-double yellow dot.
  • Competition -single yellow dot (for club players and/or cool courts).
  • Progress (for intermediate players).
  • Beginner - blue color and slightly oversized.
