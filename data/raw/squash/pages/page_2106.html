A TRIPLE CROWN WINNER:
The stunning beauty of Montreux
The exceptional educational center of Surval Mont-Fleuri

Who says you shouldn’t mix educational endeavor with pleasure? Or that you can’t have it all? Well, you should and you can!

For the discerning modern young woman, who would like to focus on an intensive academic program while enjoying a breathtaking setting, Montreux is the perfect choice.

Montreux: First of all, it is located on the shores of beautiful Lake Geneva in the canton of Vaud and is surrounded by pristine pre-alpine mountains. The city boasts a unique microclimate in which palm trees blossom in the temperate weather all year round. This well-known tourist resort offers an exceptional view of the French Alps on the other side of the lake.

Montreux: It is also conveniently situated in the heart of Europe with easy access by car, train or plane. It is only a one hour drive from Geneva’s International Airport.

Montreux: In addition, it is a cultural center hosting a wide variety of events such as: The International Jazz Festival, September Classical Music Festival, The Montreux Choral Festival, The Art Forum, and The Christmas Market. Chillon castle and a number of museums are just a few of the other attractions found close to the city.

All the unique features mentioned above mean that Montreux creates a special atmosphere which encourages and nurtures the learning experience far from the unavoidable problems of everyday urban life. Mont-Fleuri is a haven of peace and calm, perched upon a cliff overlooking the spectacular beauty below. Surval Mont-Fleuri was established on the cliff in an old hotel which was built in 1871. It retains all the original old world charm although it has been renovated and is constantly updating to keep pace with the latest modern trends.

The students enjoy a wide variety of conveniences. The school has forty rooms: either singles, doubles or triples. They are all equipped with private bathrooms including showers and toilets, as well as telephones, satellite televisions and wireless internet connection.

The Icing on the Cake; Academic standards of the highest quality and an “à la carte” program. In 2004, Surval Mont-Fleuri and The Institute Villa Pierrefeu, the two finishing schools with the best reputations on the continent, joined forces to offer the young women of the 21st century the “best of the very best”:
• Surval Mont-Fleuri (SMF) has intensive language courses, French and English, and classes in general knowledge and culture.
• The finishing program branches are taught by Villa Pierrefeu.

Two other attractive academic programs complete the educational curriculum:
• Our solid American High School Program (Grades 9 through 12) offers all the core subjects necessary to enter the best universities. Surval Mont-Fleuri High School is fully accredited by The Commission on International and Trans-Regional Accreditation (C.I.T.A.).
• The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program is run by The Mont-Fleuri Women’s College in association with the European University.

Student curriculums can be tailored to individual needs as it is possible to combine courses from the High School, BBA and Finishing programs. For example, a student enrolled in the High School program, can choose elective courses like etiquette, marketing or public relations.

Surval and Villa Pierrefeu – two educational assets in one - the one and only European boarding school with a Finishing program during the school year. And all of this under the enchanting spell of Montreux: Voilà! Our Triple Crown Winner!
