Bottle (Calabash) Gourd

The bottle, or calabash, gourd is cultivated for its hard-walled fruit, which is used for decorative purposes and as a container for liquids. This species is one of the oldest cultivated plants in the world, grown for its nutritional and practical value for thousands of years.
