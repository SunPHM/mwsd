Central Squash Microcourts Available to Primary Schools

5+ A Day and Bonita Bananas sponsored Squash Microcourts are available to any primary school in the Central Districts region. The Central region covers Taranaki, Wanganui, Ruapehu, Manawatu, Horowhenua and Southern Hawke's Bay areas.

Run by the Central Squash Association the portable perspex microcourts are part of the Association's development plan to introduce the game to children at a primary school level. Supported and sponsored by 5+ A Day, Bonita Bananas and the Hillary Commission, the courts and programme are a perfect mix, "promoting health and exercise".

The schools can access and book the perspex courts easily, have fun with hand/eye coordination games and skill drills using the latest equipment. Plus every booking gets a FREE box of Bonita Bananas.
