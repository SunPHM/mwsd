Burk the Blue Lump of Tack!
After many weeks of playing with a certain piece of blu tack i had lying around my room, I got quite attached to it, and so, decided to give the little sucker a name, and make a dodgy website about him

So I asked a few chaps off msn to suggest names for a lovely lump of blue tack, and a vote was held, it was a fearcly fought event (see news), and it turned out that my lump of tack would forever be known as Burk!

Nehu, have fun on the site, have a look at the pictures of the wee chap, he's quite friendly, and remember to visit often, Burk does like lotsa friends to play with :)
