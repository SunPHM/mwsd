Spaghetti Squash Timbale, Apple Herb Sauerkraut, Braised Pork Shank


Spaghetti Squash Timbale

  • 1 spaghetti squash, split and seeded
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup Swiss cheese, grated
  • 1 Tbsp. Chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 1/2 tsp. Nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp. Ground white pepper

Cook squash, cut side down, in a water bath (1 inch deep) in a 350 degree oven until tender (45 minutes to an hour.) Let cool. With a fork, scrape out squash. Combine eggs, heavy cream with remaining ingredients. Add reserved squash to egg mixture. Spray molds with non-stick spray and fill 2/3 full. Bake in a water bath at 350 degrees until just set. Let cool slightly, invert and serve. Makes 10 timbales.

Apple Herb Sauerkraut

  • 2 Tbsp. Bacon fat
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 Granny Smith apples, unpeeled and sliced
  • 2 lbs. Sauerkraut
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Caraway seeds
  • 4 Juniper berries
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 whole clove
  • Black pepper to taste

Sauté onions and apples in hot bacon fat in a sauté pan or skillet. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer until most of the liquid evaporates. Remove the Juniper berries, clove and bay leaf. Serve warm or cold. Serves 6.

Braised Pork Shank

  • 4 pork shanks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup Dijon mustard
  • Flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves of whole garlic
  • 1 large carrot, peeled and cut into large chunks
  • 2 ribs celery
  • 1 large onion, peeled and quartered
  • 1 bunch leeks, cut
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 2 oranges, zest and juice
  • Veal stock
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 Tbsp. Fresh sage
  • 1 Tbsp. Thyme
  • 3 sprigs rosemary
  • 1 Tbsp. Oregano

Heat oil in a thick-bottomed pan. Salt and pepper pork shanks and spread Dijon mustard on each side of shanks. Dredge in flour and brown on all sides in hot oil. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Turn flame down to a simmer and cover. Cook until tender (approximately 1 – 1 1/2 hours). Serves 4.
