Racquetball & Squash Courts
 

 

The Berry Center racquetball and squash court area consists of 5 international squash courts (1 of which is a glass enclosed exhibition court), 2 North American squash courts, and 6 racquetball courts. The squash courts are home to the Dartmouth Men's and Women's Squash teams. The courts were dedicated in November of 1987. The exhibition court is one of only three permanent exhibition courts in North America with three glass walls. A distinct feature of the squash and racquetball court area is the large overhead atrium that provides natural light to the area.
