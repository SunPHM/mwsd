 My Instruments  

The current production includes 4 different models of double-basses:

  • Model "for Orchestra";
  • Model "for Solo";
  • Model "Guitar Bass".
  • Model "for Jazz"

Label placed inside the top.
The woods used for the construction are: white poplar from Emilia Romagna, flamed poplar, maple from the former Yugoslavia, red spruce from Val di Fiemme and red spruce from Valcanale. All of them are naturally seasoned even for fifty years.
