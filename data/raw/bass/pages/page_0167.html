When largemouth or smallmouth bass are feeding on shiners,
shad or other fin-baits, tie on one of these proven Acme spoons.


Kastmaster 3/8 oz., pattern CH (chrome).
The top choice when bass are eating shad.
Cast to surface fish or jig for suspended bass.

 


Side-Winder 1/2 oz., pattern NCS.
If the bass are chasing minnows,
here's your lure.Cast near cover,
let it sink, then retrieve.



Little Cleo 2/5 oz., pattern FT. Perfect
for big smallmouths on lakes and rivers.
Cast and retrieve or jig around rocky
cover and sunken islands.
