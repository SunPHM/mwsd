Sega Bass Fishing  
Dreamcast
Choose your lure, cast your line, then reel 'em in! You're out to land some large-mouth black bass, and you'll have ample angling opportunites in this game's Practice, Arcade, or Original modes. Create your own character and fish your way through four tournament levels. It'll require a thorough knowledge of lures, water temperatures, and fish behavior to hook the big bass, not to mention precise reflexes to reel them in. The game supports the optional Dreamcast fishing controller for more realistic angling action.
