FISHING

The fishing is truly amazing all year around on our own 125 acres of water.

We have stocked these pits with Florida Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Cat, Redear, Hybrid Bluegill, and a lot of Fathead Minnows to fish for. Our main body of water is approximately 85 acres! We have built a concrete boat ramp for ease of loading and unloading boats. This pit consists of around 7-8 miles of shoreline. Various depths consist of 1 ft. deep flats to 30 ft. deep water for your fishing pleasure.
