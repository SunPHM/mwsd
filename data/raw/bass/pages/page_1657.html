Vintage Style Tone Control. Sadowsky Guitars is pleased to announce that the "Vintage Tone Control" will be standard equipment on all Sadowsky NYC Basses.

The Vintage Tone Control is available as a retrofit kit for older Sadowsky NYC Basses and for Sadowsky Metro Basses.

The Vintage Tone Control (formerly referred to as the "passive tone control") is a treble roll-off that makes the bass warmer and darker sounding. It functions in both active and passive modes. The treble and bass preamp controls are placed on a stacked pot with our new matching stacked knob. This feature can be "de-selected" when placing build-to-order NYC basses.

Weight Reduced Alder Bodies! Due to a gradual increase in the weight of alder over the last few years, we are now offering "weight reduced" alder bodies.

Bodies are chambered and then receive an alder top. This has resulted in an average weight reduction of one pound per body. We can guarantee that all of our alder bass bodies will weigh less than 4 pounds. Average body weight is about 3.75 pounds.

We are very pleased to offer our new humcancelling 5-string J-bass pickups. These pickups were designed to be the 5-string equivalent of our popular 4-string pickup. They are totally quiet and sound so good that many players have told us they prefer them to our single coils.

They are now the standard pickup on all Sadowsky J-bass style 5-string basses. The single coils remain available as an option.

We're very excited to present our newest models: The Ultra-Vintage J-Bass and The Ultra-Vintage P-Bass. Designed to combine the nicest features of an early '60s instrument with everything you've come to expect from a Sadowsky.

These basses feature full-sized bodies and deeper neck pockets so the strings sit closer to the body. Necks feature a 9" radius, 20 frets, spokewheel truss rod adjuster and rare Brazilian Rosewood fingerboards. Passive tone controls are standard on these instruments, along with our treble/bass boost preamp and active/passive switch. They're not just vintage ... they're Ultra-Vintage!

We're very pleased to introduce our newest 5-string model, the Vintage P/J 5-string. This new bass is essentially the same as our Vintage 5, but with a P/J pickup configuration.

We offer this bass in two pickup configurations: a traditional configuration for classic "P" tones, and the reverse P, which offers a "tighter B-string" and also improves the "pop" on the G-string when the bass is in the "combination pickup setting."

We now offer all of our maple top guitars (Strat and Tele style) with bent maple drop tops and a scraped "faux" binding on the top edge. The top edge is not rounded over and is taped off during the color coat to provide a binding "effect." Very handsome!

We now offer a greater selection of fingerboard choices in addition to our standard maple and morado boards.

Available woods include:

  • Ebony
  • Flamed (curly) Maple
  • Birdseye Maple
  • Brazilian Rosewood

Our standard fingerboard radius for basses and guitars is 12". For those who prefer more of a vintage Fender feel, we now offer a 9" radius (more curve).

The 9" radius is available on 4-string basses and guitars. It is not available on 5-string basses.

Our 4-string bass neck has always been 1+1/2" inches (standard J-bass specification) wide at the nut. We now offer a full-width P-bass style neck with a nut width of 1+3/4" (vintage P-bass width).

Our guitar neck has always been 1+5/8" wide (standard Fender specification) at the nut. We now offer a wider neck with a nut width of 1+11/16".
