About Us

Come land a Large Bass with Burkes Guide Service!

I have been a fishing guide for over 10 years in Heber Springs, on Greers Ferry Lake.  Helping others to land that large bass, trout or other exciting fish that are in the lake and river that is what I enjoy best about guided fishing.


I remember as a 10 year old boy that the lake was fun and exciting.  Three of my school buddies and I would rent a boat with no motor and a hand full of paddles.  We would row out into the lake, and throw our lures behind the boat and start paddling.  I dont know how in the world we caught as many fish as we did, but when we would return to the Marina the men there would frantically ask us where and how we caught those big bass!

I will never forget the thrill of catching those first fish, and my goal is to see that you leave with the same memories.

Let me show you how to catch some fish!
