About Me

I have been a practicing Internist  in central Texas since 1991, initially board certified in 1991 and have recertified in Internal Medicine in May 2001.  I am affiliated with Providence Health Center, the Providence Clinics and Providence Health Alliance which serve the health needs of Waco, McLennan County, and surrounding area.  I grew up in the Waco, TX. area and went to Baylor University in Waco.  I received my B.S degree in Biology in 1974.  My interests were in Medical Technology and I worked as a Medical Technologist in the Laboratory Service  for the V.A. Medical Center in Waco, TX.  for 10 years. During that time, I also received my MS degree in Health Professions with a management major and education minor through Southwest Texas State University in San Marcos, Tx.   I subsequently decided on a medical career and chose to go abroad to AUC School of Medicine, Montserrat, West Indies, and England.  Additional training was done in Wisconsin and Michigan and I received my Medical Degree in 1988.  From there, I did a 3 year Internal Medicine Residency at East Tennessee State University, Quillen College of Medicine,  Johnson City, Tn.   It was very hard to leave east Tennessee after three years of residency.  The mountains are beautiful, and the people are wonderful.  'Family and friends' brought me home.  My travel and experiences abroad will always be memorable ones and have surely played an impact on my personal style of medical practice.

Interests

 My personal interests are computers (my son built me a real mean machine  in 2001) and Bass fishing(my favorite is Lake Fork, east of Dallas). I do a lot of fun things on my computer such as Flight simulators, voice recognition and just surfing the Web.  It is a great source of information and I use it extensively in medical research.  There are many health/medical related Web sites and I have provided a few links above. 

 A couple of years ago I did fish at one of the many Bass Lakes in Mexico, but my brother-in-law, Dr. Tim Bray in Rowlett, Tx.. (also an Internist) beat me so bad, I have never gone back (too ashamed).  Also I didn't appreciate the bee sting, snake in the boat and then Montezuma's revenge I endured for 3 days.  Just kidding, I would probably go back.  I'm sure there is a 14 pounder with my name on it just waiting to be caught.

 I love music, especially the 70s and 80s, however, have learned to appreciate Country Western (through my wife).  I love ‘rock and roll’ and especially enjoy Elton John.  Wish I could play piano like him.

My most recently interest has been in radio controlled gas powered model airplanes.  I received instruction through the Heart of Texas Miniature Aircraft Club in Waco, TX.  I soloed August 8, 2005 and have accumulated  several airplanes since then (check these out on my photogallery).  I think this is a great hobby and find it very rewarding.

They deserve more than I can ever pay back.  I would like to thank them here; my lovely wife Kim, my children Amanda, (in memoriam Melissa), Robert and Vanessa, my parents Dr.and Mrs. O.H.
