The Maine Dish Seafood Market has the freshest fish that you'll find. We have what is in season, what is in vogue and most importantly, what is preferred by our customers. Our staff members care about you and your good health. We guide you through the seafood buying process and then even give you suggestions on how to cook what you have chosen.

In the old fashion way, as Rob demonstrates here, we cut your fish to order. You point to how thick you want your tuna steaks and we cut them that way. Our fresh fish is not pre-packaged. You can buy just what you need, and no more or less.

 

 

Salmon, tuna, halibut, haddock, cod, swordfish, tilapia, yellow perch, walleye, red fish, trout, catfish, Chilean Sea Bass and grouper are but a few of the fish in our showcase. There are clams, mussels, shucked and unshucked oysters and scallops to add more treasures of the sea to your dining table.

 

 

 

Often live crawfish and heads-on shrimp are flown in from Louisiana and we get a big steamer pot brewing up some Crawfish Gumbo. Other times we get a huge pot of Lobster Bisque or Shrimp & Roasted Corn Bisque going. Of course there is always New England Clam Chowder to take home.

 

 

 

The freezers are brimming with various sizes of uncooked shrimp for pasta dishes, and cooked shrimp for salads and shrimp cocktail. Crab is a favorite of Ohioans so we stock a large supply of Jumbo King Crab legs and Snow Crab clusters.

Our Crab Cakes are our biggest seller and for good reason - they are easy to cook and very tasty.

Frozen breaded items such as stuffed shrimp, butterflied shrimp, cod, walleye and perch fill out other parts of our large freezers with 1 lb. and 7-8 oz. lobster tails, alligator meat, frog legs, scampies and more.

 

The lobster tank is filled with active Maine lobsters from 1 1/2 pounds to more than 15 pounds. The state-of-the-art lobster tank is one of the largest in Ohio. Whether you want a lobster for two or lobsters for 200, we can satisfy your order.

We like what we do and it shows. Customers come back because our products are the freshest and the best around -- and shopping with us is never boring! Check it out for yourself!
