Bass Fishing Charters Florida Bass fishing charters freshwater fishing charters, on the central Florida Chain Of Lakes. Go Fish Charters and guide service offers world class light tackle largemouth bass fishing on the calm pristine waters of Lake Walking Water and the surrounding lakes. Professional fishing charters, bass fishing with Bass Fishing guides .... Capt Phil Hartman & Capt Andy Boyette
 
 
Capt Phil & Capt Andy specializes in Big Bass Fishing Charters and crappie fishing charters. Live bait and artificials bring the thrill of these adversaries alive. There is nothing like seeing a school of hungry Bass chase down a top water plug. Or the excitement of the elusive Large Mouth Bass taking line on a run toward the bullrush lined shore. Except maybe picking a fight with a trophy Bass and having him bend you rod to the breaking point. These largemouth bass are plentiful early winter, and bed up in late winter through early spring in Lake Walking Water, and the Orlando, Kissimmee, and Disney World area Lakes. These bass can be fished throughout the Central Florida Chain of Lakes the entire year.

Go Fish Charters offers many other angling opportunities in Florida. A short drive form Orlando and the Disney World area to the west coast and go Tarpon fishing on the beautiful Peace River Basin known as Charlotte Harbor and Boca Grande Pass, or Snook and Redfish fishing on the saltwater flats of Pine Island, Captiva, Gasparilla, Punta Gorda, Port Charlotte, Ft. Myers, and Sanibel . There are miles of back bays, rivers, and grass flats in our area all connecting Boca Grande, Charlotte Harbor and the Gulf Of Mexico. With so many opportunities for a saltwater fishing experience from back country, flats and near shore fishing our area is as good as it gets. Go Fish Charters can arrange charters to accommodate the novice to expert fisherman; "Southwest Florida has an angling experience for everyone".
