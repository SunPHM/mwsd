Florida Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
8 1/2 lb. 4 1/2 lb. Trophy Bass
June 27, 2004

Florida bass fishing anglers Lester & his son Josh Hilts (Port Charlotte, FL - Ph: 941-697-6211 - e-mail: jwhilts@yahoo.com) caught and released 13 bass up to 8 1/2 lbs during 1 full day of wild shiner fishing on June 27, 2004 with Ron's Guide Service. Josh caught the 8 1/2 lb'er. This bass had hit 2 lines (Josh's & his Dad's) that had a shiner on each line. We discovered this after netting the fish and finding both hooks in it's mouth! Lester caught a 4 1/2 lb'er. Trophy bass was caught on the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

6 lb.& 5 lb. Trophy Bass
June 24, 2004

Florida bass fishing anglers Doug Ferris, his wife Kelli and 9 year-old daughter Tori (Kalamazoo, MI - ph: 269-345-2920 - e-mail: kellidjtrading@yahoo.com) caught and released about 35 bass that included a 6 lb. & 5 lb.trophy bass during 1 day of wild shiner fishing on June 24, 2004 with Ron's Guide Service.
