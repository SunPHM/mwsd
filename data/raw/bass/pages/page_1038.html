Bassist Review - November 1999
Ever since we heard Overwater was building an Anniversary bass as a prize in our comp, the Bassist team has been eagerly awaiting its arrival. It's been well worth it - this really is something very special indeed from Chris May and the team at Overwater.
 
A highly experienced luthier, this bass is a fine example of Chris's abilities and quite simply a pure joy to behold. What's more this is the very first of a limited edition of 20 intstruments and the signed headstock reveals with that alluring 01 serial number.
 
When it comes to producing something rather special, Overwater are certainly no novices, but their recent redesign has played a major part in making this Anniversary bass go way beyond any past glories to providing something extraordinary.

The careful selection and combination of exotic woods are visually complementary as well as being sonically effcient and just plain down to earth, practical. The body facing is AAA quality, figured American Walnut over a redwood core and the almost unadorned fretboard is Indian ebony.
