Free Guitar Lessons And Music Theory For Guitar Or Bass Players !

Welcome to Theory Lessons.com, online guitar lessons in music theory. These free guitar lessons are designed for both guitar and bass players. You can access scales, chords, and music theory instruction for guitar or bass without leaving your home. I will be offering what I feel are some of the best guitar lessons and bass lessons anywhere on the internet, and my music theory lessons are taught using plain english for both the beginner and advanced player. These lessons are designed to help you understand the music and improvise freely within it to find your own playing style.

My name is Dave Allen and I have been playing guitar for 25 years, and have been earning a living as a professional guitarist for 14 years. I put this online music theory course together to provide musicians with the quickest and easiest way to learn music and modal theory. At Theory Lessons Online you can learn scales, chords, and music theory at your own pace, and whenever you have the time. Learn from a professional guitar player and instructor to become the guitarist you have always wanted to be, playing your own style of music and improvising. Try my course today, and see how quickly learning music theory can transform your guitar or bass playing. Your playing will improve rapidly, you'll learn to improvise, solo, and play many styles of music.
