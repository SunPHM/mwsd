About Me

I was born October 1965 in Bristol Connecticut but moved back to Allentown with my parents before my first birthday and have lived there ever since. I spent a couple of years in State Clowntown — I mean State College during my first run at a college degree but I don't really count that as living.

I like to do outdoor activities, hiking, biking, Frisbee golf, music shows, that sort of thing. I like any kind of music which of course doesn't include rap or country because they are music. I played bass in a band for a while and I like to jam every now and again, here's an old photo from the days we were just learning how to really play.

I really miss that jam room we had, it had lots of character.

I'm a big ice hockey and Philadelphia Flyer fan, and I follow the Eagles and Phillies when there's no hockey on. I played for a while too but it got expensive and I was going to school too which cut into available time.

I've been working in the IT world since 1995 but when I got laid off for the second time on 7 December 2001 (how poetic) I went back to school to finally finish a B.S. degree from Penn State. Working in IT has some perks, I got to go to NYC for computer shows and see many wonderful things.

I have other stuff available on my web site at www.cygnusx-1.com, but most of it is unfinished and not very interesting. The Tix section has some promise but I'm just getting started on that.
