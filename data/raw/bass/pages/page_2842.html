Lake Huites bass fishing Mexico with Bassmex

Wild Bill
Lake Huites, Mexico

MEXICO BASS FISHING ACCOMMODATION:  Lake Huites, Mexico has been called by some, "The next Baccarac"!  Now that's a tough act to follow, but in my opinion, Huites could very well become the best trophy lake in the world in a few short years!  The Lake already has huge Bass and a great new lodge to enjoy and relax in.  Trophy Bass Lodge is owned and operated by American Bass Fishermen, making this wonderful new Lodge almost unique in all of Mexico!  We think this fresh new approach has produced a lodge where fishermen and their friends and family feel right at home.

Click Here for a peek at some Pics of Trophy Bass Lodge.

At Trophy Bass Lodge, the fisherman create their own agenda.  For example, if a fisherman doesn't want to come in at lunch and enjoy a fine, hot meal, but would rather have the chef fix some great sandwiches to take along in their onboard cooler, then that's what they get to do!  This Lodge at Huites even accommodates anglers who enjoy seasonal night fishing and supplies black lights and spotlights (seasonal) for each boat!  It's innovations like these that make Trophy Bass Lodge my choice in all of Mexico!  Now if you like that kind of service and freedom, then Trophy Bass Lodge is your lodge and Huites is your Lake.


Trophy Bass Lodge Lake Huites
It has truly been my pleasure to work on this innovative, fresh new approach for a fishing lodge that even the most discriminating Bass Fisherman will Love!  The appointments show insight and concern for the American Bass Fisherman and for giving us all a little bit of home in Mexico, while you enjoy the freedom to be yourselves!  Our Bass Fishing Friends will also come to realize that the great service and food at Trophy Bass Lodge is a touch of class in a relaxed atmosphere.

Lake Huites is accessible from either Los Mochis, via a paved highway for two hours and an exciting forty-five minute drive on a unpaved road that winds its way through the foot hills of the Sierra Madre Mountains.  (See the Getting There page to find out why!)  Another option is being driven from El Fuerte.  You will be met at the airport in Los Mochis or El Fuerte, by Trophy Bass Lodge staff, for your departure to the lodge at Lake Huites.  Once you arrive at the lodge, the Trophy Bass staff will greet you with a Margarita, cold beer or your favorite soft drink, then help you get your gear stored in your spacious air-conditioned room.  While you get unpacked and settled in, the friendly staff will be serving some "real" Mexican appetizers and your favorite beverage.  In the meantime, the chef, whom I believe is among the very best at any lodge, will be preparing a great dinner for everyone!

The Lodge is set on a flat at the water's edge, with a fantastic view.  Bring your Dad... at this lodge there is no hill to climb from the water's edge - even elderly friends will find it a breeze.  It has been custom designed with your comfort in mind.

Cool Surrounds

How good does it get? After a comfortable nights sleep, you will be greeted by the smell of hot, Fresh Coffee will remind you how good it really is.  Fresh Squeezed Orange Juice, Sizzling Bacon and Fresh Farm Eggs or Pancakes are enjoyed as a hint of Sun turns the sky into indigo blue.

After breakfast, a short walk or ride will take you to your boat and guide. Now it's time to get with the program of catching bass and enjoying the unparalleled scenery this beautiful Lake offers each angler!  After a great morning of fishing and exploring the Lake you will be taken to the lodge for a hot lunch or continue fishing and enjoy sandwiches prepared for you that morning.


Comfortable Lodgings

For those of you who opt to return to the lodge for a terrific lunch, expect fresh cooked meals consisting of wonderful home made Soups, Fried Chicken, Enchiladas or the Catch of the Day, fried fish, complete with condiments. Enjoy your lunch with Margaritas, Beer, Juices, Soft Drinks or my favorite, Fresh Iced Tea.  After lunch you can take a short siesta if you like or simply go out after 'em, again, You're the boss at Trophy Bass Lodge!

Returning at sundown, the Lodge is a welcome site after enjoying the tropical weather and fighting big bass all afternoon.  You will be greeted by the lodge staff with tasty Margaritas or beer and some great conversation with your fellow fishermen. After a nice warm shower in your large private bathroom and some appetizers, it's time for some delicious food!

Enjoy a fantastic dinner overlooking the Lake.  The bar is always open at this fine restaurant - Beer, Margaritas, soft drinks, juices and bottled spring water are included in the package.  If you desire other beverages (e.g. bourbon, scotch, etc.) please bring your own. 


Wall of Fame

The menu will feature impressive entrees consisting of Authentic Mexican Plates, Stuffed Jumbo Shrimp and Grilled Angus Beef imported from Hermisillo and all the fixin's!

As our Bassmex clients have experienced in the past, meals are delicious and memorable and the weather is always pleasant.  Pleasant enough that after your meal in the evening, perhaps you'll re-arrange your fishing tackle and share that day's fishing highlights with the other guests, while enjoying the star studded skies on the big spacious patio overlooking the Lake.

Your room beckons after a hard day of Trophy Bass Fishing.  You will be surprised just how nice and comfortable these rooms are. We have suites
that can accommodate 2,3,or 4 depending on the group size  Trophy Bass Lodge offers spacious bedrooms, each of which has a private shower and toilet facility with fine American fixtures.  Each suite has two beds, electricity, air-conditioning, bottled spring water, large vanity area, hot showers, soap, shampoo, and nice soft towels.  You also have maid service twice daily and a laundry service for your convenience.
