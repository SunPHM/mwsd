Permanent Fleshhoop
On special request, natural skins for Ø28", Ø30", Ø32", Ø36" and Ø40" drums can be delivered with the very durable Permanent-Fleshhoops. The light, low-torsion aeronautical aluminum takes the head bulge and also allows the use of thicker heads and facilitates mounting.
