JUST FOR YOU: JEFF & MIKE
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
October 30, 2003
16 bass up to 3 lbs

Bass fishing anglers Jeff Trez and Mike Osiecki (Shippensburg, PA - ph: 215-872-6545 caught and released 16 bass up to 3 lbs during a half day morning of shiner and artificial plastic worm fishing with Ron's Guide Service on October 30, 2003. JUST FOR YOU: ED & GEORGE
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
October 22, 23 & 24, 2003
50 bass 5 1/4 lb, 5 lb & 35 crappie

 

 

 

 

 

 



Bass fishing anglers Ed Holloway (Port Charlotte, FL - ph: 941-629-0224 - e-mail: eddiehjr@comcast.net) and George Grant (Punta Gorda, FL - ph: 941-637-7684) caught 35 crappie and they caught and released 50 bass that included a 5 1/4 lb.and a 5 lb'er during 3 days of shiner, artificial plastic worm and crappie fishing with Ron's Guide Service on October 22, 23 & 24, 2003.
