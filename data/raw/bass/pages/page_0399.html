Dr. Don Bass

Don Bass completed his bachelors, masters and doctoral degrees in Pure Mathematics in the UK. He joined the department of Mathematics at Memorial in 1969 after teaching High School for a couple of years in the UK. In 1983 he obtained a joint appointment with the Faculty of Engineering in the discipline of Naval Architecture and Ocean Engineering because of his work on Ship Stability and Capsizing. He has worked extensively in the area of Ship Dynamics and Marine Hydrodynamics, focusing on the Fishing Vessels of Newfoundland and Labrador for a number of his studies. One of his more useful contributions has been in the development of anti-roll tanks for small fishing vessels.
