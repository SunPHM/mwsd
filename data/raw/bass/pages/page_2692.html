CONSERVATION POLICY

Crow Rock Lodge has encouraged catch and release fishing and conservation for many years. To ensure the quality of fishing for future years, we have adopted a 10% catch and release discount policy off our rates

The fisheries' biologists are now certain that, genetically, there are only so many large fish in a given lake. By removing any of these large fish, you can destroy the prime breeding stock and decrease the chances of catching a trophy fish in the future. Quality fishing, even here in the wilderness, is not limitless.

We encourage the use of replicas for mounting trophy fish.
