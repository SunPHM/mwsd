  Suzuki  
  Striped Sea Bass
(45 calories per sushi slice)

Suzuki is the largest variety of Sea Bass. It is found in inland areas and swims back and forth between the shallow and deeper waters. Suzuki is most delicious during the summer months and is one of the most expensive sushi tane even in peak season. Suzuki is high in vitamin D which helps absorb calcium.
