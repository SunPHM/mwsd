Year-Round Vacations on the 
Salmon "River of No Return"

Riggins is located on the legendary Salmon River, just east of famous Hells Canyon of the Snake River.  A unique mix of white sandy beaches, high mountain lakes, rugged canyons, alpine meadows, and lush forests provide an abundance of outdoor recreation for any visitor.  Just outside of Riggins rises the Seven Devils, part of the Hells Canyon National Recreation Area.

Whitewater Rafting & Jetboating

Experience whitewater thrills, view wildlife, hike historic mining sites and old homesteads, and enjoy nature at its finest.  Visit sites on the Salmon, Snake, Hells Canyon, and other locations.  This is an experience you will talk about forever.

Fishing

Steelhead fishing September-March.   Bass, Sturgeon, and trout year-round.  Enjoy world class fishing in one of the most beautiful settings anywhere.  Fishing is on the Salmon, Snake and local streams.

Hunting

Hunt with a bow, rifle, or camera for trophy elk, deer, black bear, cougar, turkey, pheasant, bighorn sheep, and chukar.  Some outfitters allow you to bring your own dogs.  A hunting experience not soon forgotten.
