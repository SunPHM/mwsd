Florida bass fishing references from the state of Kansas. I have put together a Lake Okeechobee bass fishing report of trophy bass caught on wild shiners by Kansas residents. Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's July 25 & 26, 2000
Florida Bass Fishing Trip

This Cormorant which was hooked and released unharmed while it was diving and searching for a meal caught and ate one of our wild shiner baited lines. It put up a good fight and had us believing we had a trophy bass on the line until it surfaced!

This dad and son Florida bass fishing team did however manage to catch a good amount of Florida bass as the below Florida bass fishing report notes:

Florida bass fishing dad Ken McAlister and 15 year-old son Andrew (Andover, KS - ph: 316-218-0340 - e-mail: mcalisterkg@veracom.net) caught 34 Florida bass that included a 6 1/2 lb., 5 1/2 lb. and 5 1/4 lb. bass during 2 half days (Jul. 25 & 26, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Andews dad caught the 6 1/2 lb. bass during the same time Andrew was fighting the 5 1/2 lb'er. Andrew also caught the 5 1/4 lb. bass. We fished the Harney Pond Canal cuts of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
