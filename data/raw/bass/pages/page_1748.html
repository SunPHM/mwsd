Comments:

     This particular F bass may be visually stunning (especially in person, the photos do not really do it justice), but more importantly it sounds flat out amazing. Most people who frequent our site already know how much we like George Furlanetto's F basses - we have played dozens of them, and we've found them to consistently be some of the most versatile, great sounding and playing basses available to working musicians. While most builders seem to be lost in trying to build ever more 'fancy' instruments (no matter what they come out sounding like - yes, we come across a LOT of fancy-wood basses that sound somewhere between exceedingly ordinary to painfully bad and beyond), George F. has always remained firmly rooted in building real musical tools (thank goodness there are still people who do this). But even among his wonderful instruments this one is one or two levels above the rest, for some reason this one came out especially nice and we can only describe it as sublime.
     There are hundreds or even thousands of luthiers 'out there', but really only a very small handful that we would consider to be true masters - builders who have over time honed their unique vision into an authentic identity, to the point where they are now producing mature, fully-conceived instrument designs. So of course it is always very exciting for us to encounter a very special instrument from one of these rare and extraordinary builders, an instrument such as this one gives the very uncommon feeling that we have here an exceedingly precious gem.
