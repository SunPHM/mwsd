Charlotte rocks with the Fallen Angel

12/04/2004

Irish indie-rockers Ash seem to have been on the verge of greatness for a while, but we think 2004 is really going be their year. The new album Meltdown, due out in May, is produced by Nick Raskulinecz (Foo Fighters), and if the download single Clones is anything to go by, it’s gonna be massive.

Guitarist Charlotte Hatherley sounded fantastic with her Fallen Angel 180H head and Peacemaker 4x12 cab on a recent warm-up tour of English clubs, and we can’t wait to see Charlotte and the band in action at the upcoming Reading and Leeds Festivals in August.

You can also catch Ash playing more gigs around the UK in May and June, with international dates planned later this year and into 2005.
