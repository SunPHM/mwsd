Florida Bass Fishing
Brag'n Board: May 2000

I have put together some May 2000 Florida bass fishing trophy bass pictures. These Lake Okeechobee trophy Florida bass were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Florida bass fishing pictures are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught on Lake Okeechobee and other information is listed with each Florida trophy bass fishing picture. To ensure validity the angler's name, address, phone number and e-mail address is also listed.

Check out our Florida Bass Fishing Special Rate listed below.

Florida Bass Fishing 8 lb Trophy Florida Bass

Florida bass angler Larry Daughtry (Orlando, FL - ph: 407-695-3570) caught 18 bass up to 8 lbs during a half day (May 26, 2000) of bass fishing with artificial lures with Ron's Guide Service. The 8 lb'er was caught on a black blue tail worm. Bass were caught on the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.

Florida Bass Fishing 7 1/2 & 6 lb Florida Bass

Bass fishermen Marty Weisbart (Manhasset, NY - e-mail: marty@pfile.com) and Howard Lambert (Chicago, IL - e-mail: hjl3525@aol.com) caught 53 bass that included a 7 1/2 lb. and a 6 lb'er during 3 and the half days (May 21 - 24, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. Marty caught the 7 1/2 lb'er. Howard got the 6 lb'er. Bass were caught on the North Shore of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.

Florida Bass Fishing 8 3/4 lb. & 6 lb. Florida Bass

Florida bass fishing couple John Vargosko (West Palm Beach, FL - ph: 561-640-4809 - e-mail: John.Vargosko@icsltd.net) and Sarah Stabley caught 6 bass that included a 8 3/4 lb. and a 6 3/4 lb'er during a half day (May 21, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. John caught the 8 3/4 lb'er and the 6 3/4 lb'er. Sarah caught a bass that was almost 5 lbs. Bass were caught in fisheating creek area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.

Florida Bass Fishing 8 lb. & 7 1/4 lb. Florida Bass

Florida bass fishing anglers Jim Noel (Revloc, PA - ph: 814-472-6123 - e-mail: LGillin@aol.com) and Brad Bopp (Annapolis, MD ph: 410-643-8736 - e-mail: sonex@erols.com) caught 71 bass that included an 8 lb, 7 1/4 lb and 5 3/4 lb'er during 3 days (May 5 - 7, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Jim caught the 8 lb'er even though it took both Jim and Brad's shiner and had both of their hooks stuck in it's mouth. This was the third time in 15 years that I have guided on Lake Okeechobee where I have witnessed such an event. Brad however caught the 7 1/4 lb'er. Bass were caught in fisheating creek area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass except the 8 lb'er were released.


Florida Bass Fishing 7 1/2 lb. Florida Bass

Florida bass fisherman Rich Balogh (Windham, CT - ph: 860423-4829 - e-mail: rikabal@snet.net) caught 12 bass up to 7 1/2 lbs during a half day (May 1, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Bass were caught in fisheating creek area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
