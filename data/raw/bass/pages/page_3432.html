High School Art Lesson Plans

Level 2 

Below are the lesson plans that I created for my high school art level 2 students during the 2003-2004 semester.  Other teachers --- feel free to use them as a resource.

Click on each link to view the document in Microsoft Word.  Some documents are in jpeg format because parts were created by hand.  You can also see larger views of the student artwork by clicking on each thumbnail.  All lessons are presented in the order in which they were taught.  Not all lessons of each unit are present on these pages.

Unit 1:  Printmaking and Line

Students were required to carve a linoleum block printing plate in which at least three types of lines and two variances of lines were used.  Their best print was also turned in as part of the final project.  I will have pictures of prints up soon.

Unit 3:  Geometric Constructions and the Creation of a Personal Mandala in acrylics on canvas, featuring artist John DeMarco

Students acquired the knowledge of basic geometric constructions as well as how to create symmetrical designs using only a compass and straight edge.  I began designing this unit while pursuing teacher certification in art and revised it when I was finally able to teach it to high school art students during the school year of 2003-2004.  I was currently teaching geometry, so this was an excellent way to integrate art into a math curriculum or vice versa.
