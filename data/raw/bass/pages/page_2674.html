LAKE OKEECHOBEE BASS FISHING REPORTS
2000


Lake Okeechobee women anglers bass fishing reports and pictures from the calendar years of 1999 - 1997. This Florida freshwater lake over the 20 years I have operated a trophy bass fishing guide service on has produced on a person to person (male to female) ratio "more trophy bass to women anglers" It seems the girls are able to put more Okeechobee trophy bass in the boat far more offen then the experienced men anglers!

Florida Bass Fishing 8 3/4 lb. Florida Trophy Bass
July 27 & 28, 2000

Florida bass anglers Tom and Judy Dandridge (St. Pete., FL - ph: 727-898-5030 - e-mail: judy.dandridge@gte.net) caught 10 bass up to 8 3/4 lbs during 2 half days (Jun. 27 & 28, 2000) of florida wild shiner bass fishing with Cap'n Ron. Judy caught and released the 8 3/4 lb. trophy bass. We fished the Fisheating Creek area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass including this beauty were released.


Florida Bass Fishing 6 lb Trophy Florida Bass
June 26 & 27, 2000

Buzz and Lana Broughton (Corpus Christi, TX - ph: 361-937-4431 - e-mail: HLBROUGHTO@aol.com) caught 35 bass up to 6 lbs during 2 half days (Jun. 26 & 27, 2000) of florida wild shiner bass fishing with Ron's Guide Service. Lana caught and released the 6 lb. bass. They fished the Harney Pond cuts of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.

Florida Bass Fishing 6 3/4 and 6 1/2 lb. Trophy Florida Bass
April 16 - 18, 2000

Lisa Sharp (Skiatook, OK - ph: 918-396-7515) caught a 6 3/4 and 6 1/2 lb bass. Her husband Mark also landed a 6 1/4 lb'er. They caught a total of 50 bass during 3 days (Apr. 16 - 18, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. Bass were caught on the North Shore and Monkey Box areas of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
