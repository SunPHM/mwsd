October 2002 -
April 2003

  

Comments:
An experimental time for the band. We went under the name Lucephonic Chamber Ensemble and during this time several different people came and went. The sound was more of an orchestral, all instrumental songs. No complete songs were made, just more jams and demos. Pat Sanders provided drums and guitar on some of these tracks.

January 2001 - October 2002

  

Comments:
This is the time period when Matt and Jonathan first got together and began writing music. The only releases during this time were more demos. Most of the demos are just jams and incomplete songs. They went under the names "We're only doing this till were 30," "Field Test," and "Face In A Crowd." Sean Ibanez provided drums on some of these tracks.
