Some info:
He used the Line 6 POD and the Zachary exclusively. The rest was programmed and played with a Roland Arranger Workstation. The first, strat-like sound is the Zachary with the neck single-coil pickup selected through a Fender Twin program with a little overdrive. The Second tone is a full-tilt rectified tone with the guitar using double coil at the bridge pickup. The last tone is a crunchy Les Paul tone with the same pickup and an Old Marshall setting. I think it sounds quite impressive. Rob is a good player. Hope you enjoy it.
