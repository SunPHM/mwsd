Did You
Know?

Most anglers are surprised to learn that the black bass family are not bass at all; this clan is a member of the sunfish family. True North American bass species include: the striper, white bass, yellow bass and white perch. The sunshine bass or wiper is a hybrid of striper and white bass and has become an exceptionally popular sports fisherman quarry.
