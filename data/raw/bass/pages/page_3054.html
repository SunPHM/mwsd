Cabo San Lucas on Mexico's Baja peninsula, Puerto Vallarta, and Mazatlan, located on Mexico's west coast, offers some of the world's best billfishing. Sailfish, Marlin and more await your arrival, promising to fill your day with great fishing action. And fishing is not all they offer, both of these cities offer incredible sightseeing, sun and fun activities and azure blue seas. Because of the great diversity of activities, Cabo San Lucas, Puerto Vallarta, and Mazatlan offer the sportsmen and their families an opportunity to share a vacation. While the angler enjoys the some of the world's best bill fishing, the rest of the family can enjoy sightseeing, shopping and sun and fun activities at the beach.

The Sinaloa Region offers great Bass fishing and wing shooting. With Lake Huites just to the north of Los Mochis and this newly opened Lake offers bass anglers many choices. Lakes Baccarac is known for its world class "trophy" fish. Yet Huites is respected for its quality and incredible numbers of rip-roaring feisty Bass and potential trophy bass. At any of the Mexican lakes, both fly and spin anglers have an equal opportunity to catch great numbers of largemouth bass and maybe even get that one "trophy" fish you've been looking for your whole career.

Mexico offers bass anglers additional opportunities at Lakes Guerrero and Aqua Milpa. Both of these lakes, along with most bass lakes in Mexico, provide great fly fishing along with wonderful spin casting.
