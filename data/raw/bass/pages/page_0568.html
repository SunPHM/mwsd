BASS
 		  

 

Smallmouth Bass
Brown or bronze color on the sides. Jawbone does not extend behind rear margin of eye. Connected first and second dorsal fin. Up to 10 lbs., 1 to 3 lbs typical.



Spotted Bass
Evenly arranged black spots in alternate rows below the dark lateral line. Small rough patch of teeth on tongue. Connected first and second dorsal fin. Up to 8 lbs., 1 to 4 lbs typical.

 

Largemouth Bass
The jawbone extends beyond the back of the eye, the two dorsal fins are nearly separated or deeply notched. Up to 20 lbs., 1 to 6 lbs. typical
