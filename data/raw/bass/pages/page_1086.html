Martin began playing drums in country western bands in the local nightclubs of Salida, Colorado in 1974 at the ripe old age of 14. Since then, he has almost always been in a group. While the styles have ranged from country to funk to heavy metal, his passion has always been rock n' roll, fuelled by his love of The Beatles from growing up in the 1960's and having their music as a virtual soundtrack to his life.

Although he has no professional training or sight-reading ability, Martin is proficient at bass, guitar, drums, keyboards and vocals. He has performed regularly in nightclubs and concert venues, opening for the likes of Foghat, Steelheart, Pat Travers, and Sleezebees to name a few. Martin is currently the bassist, vocalist, and songwriter for his all original group, Dogman.
