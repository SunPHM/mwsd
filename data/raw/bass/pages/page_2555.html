JUST FOR YOU: TODD & DANE
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
April 12 & 13, 2004
25 bass up to 4 lbs



Florida bass fishing anglers Todd Neldberg and his son Dane (Miami, FL - e-mail: tneldberg@aol.com) caught and released about 25 bass up to 4 lbs during 2 half days of wild shiner fishing on April 12 & 13, 2004 with Ron's Guide Service. They fished the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.
