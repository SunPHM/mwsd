Sasha's Favourite
10:59 - June 25, 2004 
by bhaskar 

Oh yes, i:vibes is first with the info's! Sasha's much championed artists of the year Dirty Bass have today released very limited promo copies of their first album "Darkness from Light" after an explosive year for the band they have topped it off with a sublime mix of techno and progressive grooves to rattle the soul. The album is available now in only one store in the world - yep www.ivibesstore.com

He has conquered the record boxes of the worlds best dj's in just one year, Dirty Bass have moved from zeros to mega heroes, the first release on the label sold an astonishing 3500 copies. For an unknown techno release this is enormous.

If you want to be one of the first to hear and purchase the album go to www.ivibesstore.com.
