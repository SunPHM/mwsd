Largemouth bass are super game fish that lover cover.....

From ponds to reservoirs the largemouth bass exist and devour what ever it pleases. Largemouth bass will take a variety of lures and bite all year long. With an explosive burst of speed and a love for thick cover, these are the muggers of the freshwater world and nothing is safe. Available to most anglers and an appetite that will not quit there is no question why largemouth bass are the most sought after game fish in North America.
