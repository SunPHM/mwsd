Research contracts awarded

The following is a list of some members of the university community who have received research contracts. The Office of research has supplied information about the contract recipient, funding source, contract value and the research topic. This list is not meant to reflect all of the research happening at Memorial now, but rather who received funding for the months of January, February, March and April 2000.

Photo by Stephanie Fleming

Engineering professor Dr. Donald Bass has received several research contracts to design anti-roll tanks. The tanks reduce roll of fishing vessels by 70 per cent.
