Fish-Rich Waters…

Our main lake is home for Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Perch and Whitefish. Lake, Speckled, Brook and Rainbow Trout are all available within a short drive of the camp. For the more adventurous, a trip to one of the many nearby lakes and rivers may be in order.

Use Our Boats and Motors

We have 14' Sea Nymph boats with Yamaha 9.9 & 15 HP motors waiting for you in Gowganda Lake to rent. Our 8 HP motors are ready to go to the outlaying rivers and lakes where boats await you. We have boats available on six additional lakes for your use. If you prefer, bring your own boating equipment, there is no extra charge for launching from our concrete ramp or for dockage.

You Can Keep Busy…or Just Relax!

The verandahs on our cottages are great for taking life easy, barbecuing or just spending time "watching the clouds roll by". If you prefer more activity, you can go for a swim at the beach, walk down to the docks and cast for smallmouth bass or feed the wild ducks, take a quiet canoe paddle or boat ride along some of Gowganda Lake's 35 miles of rugged shoreline, or just go for a leisurely stroll.
