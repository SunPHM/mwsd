LP Bass Drum Cowbell Mounting Bracket

The LP Bass Drum Cowbell Mounting Bracket securely clamps to the edges of bass drum hoops. The 6" long 3/8" diameter knurled mounting rod allows for convenient mounting of LP Cowbells and can also be used to hold one or more LP Jam Blocks, LP Blast Blocks or LP Cyclops Tambourines. The clamp is lined with rubber to protect bass drum hoops.
