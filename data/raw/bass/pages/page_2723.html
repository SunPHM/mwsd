Florida Bass Fishing Trip June 27, 2003
35 bass up to 3 lbs

Bass fishing anglers Adam Runsdorf (Boca Raton, FL - e-mail: ARUNSDORF@aol.com) and 3 friends caught and released 35 bass up to 3 lbs during a half day of artificial plastic worm fishing with Cap'n Ron on the morning of June 27, 2003. JUST FOR YOU:
TERRY & BERNIE

Florida Bass Fishing Trip - June 6, 2003
Snake and a 9 lb'er

Bass fishing anglers Bernie Knouse (Lake Placid, FL - ph: 863-465-7873) and Terry Huff (Lake Placid, FL ph: 483-465-3878) caught only 6 bass up to 9 lbs during a day of shiner and artificial lure fishing with Cap'n Ron on June 6, 2003. The 9 lb'er was caught on a small shiner by Terry. Terry also managed to hook and catch a snake (see photo below) on a plastic worm which was foul cast by Terry and just happened to land just where the snake was sitting. Bass and snake which were released were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.


Look closely (left side) and you will be able to see the plastic
worm hanging out of the snake's mouth
