G&L:

The Comanche is an incredibly well-made instrument. The detail work is superb, the nut and neck joint are airtight, and the finish is mirror perfect. The sleek, shallow neck is exquisite, and the jumbo frets are immaculately set and rounded. The feel is creamy and consistent. The non-locking arrived superbly set up with two springs. The trem arm pushes snugly into its socket without screwing or locking, and stays put until you tough it. The entire assembly is light, accurate, and super-responsive.

 

Hamer:

Each Hamer guitar is truly hand-built in a manner unparalleled anywhere else. Call them the biggest of custom shops or the smallest of production plants; either way, Hamer knows how to put together some fine guitars. Even their most affordable instruments reveal an attention to detail you might expect to find only in high-priced guitars.

 

Fernandes:

This instrument is at home in any playing situation.

The Cort CL-1400 has great looks, great feel, great sound. Mahogany body with flamed maple top and CNC-routed chambers with F-holes. Set mahogany neck. Mightymite pickups. Full binding. Large block inlays.

Easy playability and an impressive array of tones.

Schecter:



The Schecter C-1 Artist is a limited edition electric guitar with a elegant look and feel. It is all black body that incorporates the schecter zero heel ultra-access neck joint that gives the guitar more contact with the body for improved tone and sustain. It also features a 5-way pickup selector.

The Body is made of mahogany with a supurb looking carved top. They've also added a pearloid binding that follows the body shape that is really eye catching. The neck has a rosewood fretboard with the schecter vector pearloid inlays. The grover tuners and string through tail design also help to maintain the high sustain and excellent tone. The Pickups are Seymour Duncan USA pickups. It has a SD '59 pickup at the neck, and a SD JB at the bridge. They've also included a 5-way pickup selector that allows for the Humbuckers to be split into single coil pickups so you can get the single coil pickup sound.
