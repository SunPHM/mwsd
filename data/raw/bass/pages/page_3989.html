  Sea Fishing Trips

Many declare the fishing around Anglesey to be amongst the best in Wales, with an abundant variety of species.

As always, beginners are welcome, with expert help and friendly advice always on hand from the skippers, hot tea and coffee is free and there are facilities for onboard cooking.
Rod hire is available and there is free mackeral bait on hand at all times. Other baits for use are: squid, lugworm, ragworm, sandeel and crab. These are only available subject to prior arrangement.

We have two boats with powerful turbo charged engines to get you there quickly. Both vessels are fully equipped to the latest D.T.I, regulations and Code of Practice implemented on the 1st April 1994 for 60 miles offshore with life jackets, life rafts and the latest hi-tech electronic aids. (Fish finders, satellite navigators, VHF radios, auto pilot and radar, etc.)
