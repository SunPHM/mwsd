My Dad: "Charles Giannetta"
with his Prize Winning "24 Inch Small Mouth Bass"
The year was "1958"


My dad did win a prize that year for the largest "Small Mouth Bass" caught in Northeast Pennsylvania.

He won a "Heddon Fly Rod & Reel" after his passing I received all his fishing gear.

I still have most of my dad's fishing gear and I have given some to my son Charles.
My Dad passed away in 1967.



My Dad had the fish mounted and it now hangs on the wall in my home.
Some day I will pass it onto my son Charles.
