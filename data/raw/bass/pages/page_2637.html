Welcome to Lake Fork Outdoors with professional, licensed guide, Danny Silcox.

Danny is a full-time, licensed, resident guide and dedicated professional who has been fishing Lake Fork Reservoir since 1982.

Whether you are a beginner, or seasoned Angler, Danny offers a personalized service geared to your goals and fishing abilities.  Book a trip today with Lake Fork Outdoors professional guide, Danny Silcox for an outdoor adventure that is fun and educational! 

Maybe, you too will experience the thrill of catching that bass of a lifetime!!!

 Mission

Provide a safe and pleasurable fishing environment, while sharing my knowledge and enthusiasm for the sport of fishing.  Committed to providing my clients with a memorable and informative outdoor experience.

Philosophy

Protect the lake's resources, and promote the future of the sport  of fishing by encouraging "Catch & Release" and the use of artificial lures only.
