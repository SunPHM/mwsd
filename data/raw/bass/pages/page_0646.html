Song List

    For all you "gear heads" out there, here's a list of the instruments and amplification The Beatless use to re-create the sound of The Fabs. The Beatless have added several vintage instruments to their collection just in the last year or so. And the list continues to grow...

    Gene Brigham plays a Rickenbacker 340/12 Jetglo, two Fender Stratocasters, and a Takamine acoustic/electric. He uses a Crate GFX-212T amplifier, and several effect pedals to imitate the many sounds that The Beatles and George Martin conjured up for their recordings.

    David Butler plays a Univox "copy" of the Hofner bass that Paul McCartney made famous, as well as a Fender Stratocaster, and an Ovation acoustic/electric. He uses a Fender Bassman 100 amplifier. David also plays a Yamaha S08 and a Kawai K-1 synthesizer on several songs.

    Ralph Peters plays a Rickenbacker 330/6 Mapleglo and an Alvarez acoustic/electric. He uses a Fender Princeton Chorus amplifier.

    John Schroeder plays an exact replica of the 4-piece Ludwig drum kit used by Ringo Starr for most of The Beatles' career.
