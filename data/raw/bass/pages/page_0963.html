Justin Brehm

Keyboard

Vocals

Justin is a senior at Selinsgrove Area High School. He sings, plays the piano and french horn, and has participated in several band and chorus festivals. He acts and sings in school plays and musicals. In his free time, he enjoys impersonating the Backstreet Boys; he is, after all, the "hottest guy on Earth." You can e-mail him at jbrehm@cnnsimail.com.
