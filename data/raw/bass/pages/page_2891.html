JUST FOR YOU: Karen!
Florida Bass Fishing November 16-18,1998
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
Florida bass fishing couple Karen and Kenny Myers (Maryville, TN - ph: 423-379-5222 / bus. ph: 423-216-3811) caught 33 Florida bass up to 5 1/2 lbs. during 2 1/2 days (Nov. 16 - 18, 1998) of wild shiner fishing with Cap’n Ron. Karen caught the 5 1/2 lb’er and a mud fish that was at least 10 lbs. In fact Karen caught just about all the Florida bass put in the boat. She also told everybody around town that Ken got skunked! For some reason Ken, who had numerous bass hits just could not hook them. He did manage to bird nest his reel which requirer bringing in a bass he had on his line by hand. On the other hand Karen had no trouble hooking and landing any fish that hit her shiner. Of course Ken would not take any advise from Karen on how to set the hook. After such a whipp’n I hope Karen will be taken on the next fishing trip.

Karen Kanipe (Knoxville, TN - e-mail: kkanipe@cellular.uscc.com)

JUST FOR YOU: Tracy!
Florida Bass Fishing August 8 & 9, 1998
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
Florida bass fishing angler Tracy caught 16 bass up to 4 1/2 lbs. on wild shiners during 2 half days (Aug. 8 & 9, 1998) of fishing on Lake Okeechobee with Cap'n Ron. All the bass which were released were caught in the Monkey Box area of Okeechobee. The fish during the first session of fishing put a show on for us buy playfully blasting the wild shiners out of the water. These tactics which are a sight to see have a way of keeping you on your toes. During the second session of fishing the bass were more aggressive without the topwater action that is delightful to watch. Tracy who I must say is an exceptional good lady bass fishing angler and a Arlinton Texas school counselor wanted me to say hello to all of her school students in this report. Well here's a big "HELLO" from a very special counselor who's thinking of all her students even during her vacation.

Tracy Teaff (Arlinton, TX - e-mail: TTeaff@aol.com)


JUST FOR YOU: Bob & Joyce!

Florida Bass Fishing couple Bob & Joyce
This Florida bass fishing senior citizen couple Bob and Joyce Napier caught about 30 bass up to 5 lbs. during 3 days (Jan. 25-27, 1998) of cold weather fishing on Lake Okeechobee. Joyce caught and released the 5 lb'er which is in fact considered a trophy bass in Indiana. This couple who fished with wild shiners during their stay with us also released all the fish they landed. Joyce somehow managed to out fish her husband Bob during each day of fishing. The smile on Joyce's face holding that 5 lb'er is beyond words. Hope to see you guys someday again - real soon.
