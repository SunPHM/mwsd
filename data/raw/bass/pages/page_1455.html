Hagstrom DeLuxe Bass

Just like the other Hagstrom basses with short scale necks, their DeLuxe model is a happy kick in the pants to play. If you wear a slippery guitar strap you had better maintain a death grip, this is the most neck heavy of the Hag basses I've played.

The tones have a nice edge to them and make the DeLuxe a natural for rock music. Pat's bass pictured above has been repainted and sports a non original brass bridge. He prefers the plastic topped Hagstrom I bass to this one. They sound similar, but the DeLuxe has a more luxurious warm tone. The DeLuxe is also quite a bit heavier. There must be a nice chunk of wood under that blue paint.

Hagstrom Canada
One of the features at Hagstrom Canada is their database of photos depicting an example of each model from every production run. You can ID any old Hag here. Great site.
