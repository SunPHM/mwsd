Aylesbury Community Concert Band

How the Band helped Aylesbury celebrate Christmas…

Aylesbury Community Concert Band is an amateur wind band which draws players from the town of Aylesbury in Buckinghamshire, England, and the surrounding area. Our aim is to provide an opportunity for members of the community to pursue their musical interests and develop their instrumental skills, to provide a musical resource for the people of Aylesbury, and to promote the town wherever and whenever we perform. The band is a 'Concert Band' comprising brass and woodwind players with a percussion section.
