The Charter Boat Fleet



"Island Girl" Charters - Captain Tracy Campbell - A 50' Sportfisher, she carries up to six passengers. Tackle & bait are provided, and live bait fishing is available. In-shore, offshore, Gulf Stream, trolling or bottom fishing is available. Offering full day, half day and 3/4 day trips. Half day & 3/4 day - Spanish & Kings, full day - Wahoo, King Mackerel, Tuna, Sailfish, Marlin, Dolphin.


The "Sudden Impact" is a 40' Custom built Sportfisherman. The twin 350 H.P. Cat engines provide 22 knot cruise for a fast, comfortable and safe ride. Equipped with full electronics, 48 mile Furuno radar, VHF radio, Loran color fish finder, tackle supplied includes Penn International 50 to 130 W and light tackle Shimano TLD's and custom built rods. Boat has air conditioning and heat. U.S. Coast Guard licensed for up to 6 passengers. Captain Jerry Elliott provides experienced and caring service to make your trip enjoyable.


"Jo Le" is a 55 foot vessel loaded with features like, 3 State Rooms, 2 Heads, Microwave, Ice Maker, VCR, TV, All the Comforts! Giant Bluefin Tuna(Seasonal), Marlin, Wahoo, Dolphin, and Billfish trips available using Penn International Tournament tackel! Captain Ed Braxton has over 18 years of charter boat experience.




The "Sea Mint", Captained by Curly Williams, is a 34 foot vessel with a cruise speed around 22 - 23 knots. The boat specializes in bottom fishing.




The "Rainbow" is a 35 foot motor vessel captained by Danny Harrison. She cruises around 19 knots providing a fast, comfortable ride. The boat specializes in bottom fishing for popular species such as grouper and sea bass using electric reels.






The "Mary Lee" is 36 feet in length and specializes in bottom fishing. With a cruising speed of 18 to 19 knots, she is captained by Larry Ingram.



The "Diver Down" is a 42 foot long fully U.S. Coast Guard certified crew boat capable of carrying up to 18 divers to the various shipwrecks off Morehead City. There is a large swim platform on the stern of the boat with a large easy to get to stainless steel fins-on ladder and hand rails for easy access back onto the boat after your dives.

As far as electronics goes....the Diver Down has it all: Loran, Radar, GPS, VHF, Hi-Band, EPRIB. She is fully capable handling any sudden rough ocean conditions. You can feel right at home on the Diver Down while diving The Graveyard of the Atlantic Ocean.
