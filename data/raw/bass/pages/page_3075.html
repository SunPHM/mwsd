Daily Day & Evening Fishing Trips
Supplies Included - Bay Tours - Lodging

Enjoy some of the best Sportfishing on the Chesapeake Bay off Maryland's scenic Eastern Shore with "Sawyer Fishing Charters". Hoopers Island, a quaint waterman's village is located in Southern Dorchester County, approximately a 2 hr. drive from Washington DC/Baltimore, and 2 1/2 hrs. from Philadelphia, PA.

The Middle to Lower Bay area offers the best fishing, with trophy striped bass in record size, from a minimum length of 18" up to 40". The season for striped bass runs from April through November. Size and quantity limits vary during the season. Trout and hardhead (croakers) are extremely plentiful as well as blues and an occasional flounder. In the Spring & Fall seasons, we troll for Trophy striped bass and chum and bottom fish during the Summer with great results.

We offer full day trips at $560 for up to 8 people which last 6-8 hrs. and leaved dock at either 6 a.m. or 2 p.m.; Half-day trips are $440 for up to 8 people. Our popular Summer Evening fishing trips which leave dock at 5 p.m. are $50 per person for groups of 8 or more. These are excellent for taking the wife and kids! Prices include license, bait, tackle and ice.

Also offered are scenic cruises to areas such as Smith Island (access by water only, where you may explore the small island via golf cart or bicycle, visit the museum and dine at a waterfront restaurant); or visit the shops and museums of Solomons Island on the western shore; and other points of interest along the Bay.

FISHING SEASONS:

Late April to Early June: Trolling for Trophy Striped Bass (to approx. 40 lbs.) June and July: Light tackle. Great fishing for croakers, trout and striped bass. August to October: Light tackle fishing for bluefish, striped bass, mackerel, trout, flounder. November: Trolling for Striped Bass.

VESSEL AND CAPTAIN:

"Sawyer" whose logo is derived from fictional character "Tom Sawyer" is a new 48ft.x 17ft. Chesapeake-built vessel that is USCG inspected & certified to accommodate up to 49 people. The boat is equipped with the latest electronics, features a spacious air-conditioned & heated cabin with 2 dinettes and restroom.

"Sawyer" is piloted by experienced USCG licensed Capt. David Schauber, an Eastern Shore native with a lifetime of knowledge of the Chesapeake Bay and its' tributaries."

LODGING PACKAGES:

Fishing/Lodging packages are available at a special rate. Lodging is available at area motels and hotels OR the rental of a private home on the Chesapeake Bay.

NEARBY ATTRACTIONS:

Visit nearby Blackwater Wildlife National Refuge, home to native Sika deer, Fox, Beaver, Delmarva Fox Squirrel, Snow & Canada Geese, Bald Eagles, and other waterfowl. Tour historic Old Trinity Church and churchyard, America's oldest continous operating church.

LIGHTHOUSE TOURS AND CRUISES

Offering our new "Chesapeake Beacons of Light" Lighthouse Tours & custom cruises.

DIRECTIONS

Take Rt. 50 East to Cambridge, MD turning right onto Rt. 16, then left onto Rt. 335 and follow signs to Hoopers Island. The 48ft. "Sawyer" is docked at Rippons Marina on Hoopers Island.
