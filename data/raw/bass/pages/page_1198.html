Michael McKenzie, flutist and composer, is a native Californian with a degree in music performance from SF State University. Michael plays as a soloist and with a wide variety of instruments to form small ensembles. These ensembles play either classical or jazz or even both for your event.

Michael and his ensembles have performed at hundreds of weddings and special events throughout the Bay Area.

Michael's ensembles include:

  • Flute and Guitar Duo
  • Flute, Violin and Cello Trio
  • Flute, Guitar and Cello Trio

He can also provide flute accompanied by a harp or other instruments upon request.
