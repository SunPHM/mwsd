bass fishing

Americas #1 Freshwater Sport

Experience bass fishing at its finest when you visit Southwest Louisiana's Grosse Savanne Lodge.

The experienced bass fishing guides at Grosse Savanne know firsthand which channels and habitats are most heavily populated.

Blessed with a maze of inland marshes and temperate weather, Southwest Louisiana is a vast nursery ground for crawfish, finfish, shrimp, crabs, oysters - an array of food sources for predatory fish. Crisscrossed with bayous, canals, and freshwater and brackish lakes, the waters attract numbers of largemouth bass ranging up to eight-pounds and panfish, such as bluegill, white perch, redear and sun perch, as well as redfish, flounder and speckled trout.

In addition to fabulous fishing, Grosse Savanne offers first class accommodations, fine southern and Cajun cuisine, and unsurpassed service.

We hope that you will join us for your next bass fishing expedition!
