Lake Fork is one of the best bass fishing lakes in the USA. Bass fishing is enjoyed year round on Lake Fork. The lake holds title to the largest bass caught in Texas. Lance Vick invites you to come fish the lake everyone is talking about...Lake Fork. Let BASSMASTER touring Pro and professional guide Lance Vick teach you bass catching techniques learned on the BASSMASTER tournament trail. Lance Vick will guide you to the best bass fishing in America, offering all the personal instruction you'll need to make your trip to Lake Fork fun and successful. Lance is a licensed full time resident guide on Lake Fork. For Lance fishing is not just his job... it's his way of life!
Let us guide you on this one!

Mission:
Be it fishing or hunting, our goal is to provide our customers with all of the tools and personal instruction necessary to make for a fun and successful trip.
While we want your repeat business and referrals, we also want each customer to take with Him/Her information that may enhance his/her next Lake Fork trip without a guide!
