Striped Bass

Latin name: Morone saxatilis
Size: generally 15 - 40 lbs, but can reach over 80 lbs.
Season: April - December
Recommended charter: 1/2 day, evening (sunset trolling or night drifting with eels), full day with fluke combo or extended day offshore combo
Location: Inshore

Montauk striped bass fishing is hotter than ever. We fish for bass in a variety of ways, depending on wind/tide, time of day, water temps, abundance of bait, type of bait fish, etc.

The preferred method of fishing for stripers in Montauk is trolling with jigs, long tubes or umbrella rigs. Each of these lures is customized to work best for the style most charter boats use in Montauk.

While trolling is the most productive, we also fish for stripers while chunking, diamond jigging, with bucktails and other light tackle methods. However, based on the construction of our boat type, we do not do fly fishing. If that is your interest, we can put you in touch with the right captain. Let us know.

The Montauk Friends of Erin hold a great fishing tournament towards the end of the summer. Each boat fishes for 4 types of fish: Fluke, Striped Bass, Seabass and Bluefish. The fish are given different points per pound. It's a great tournament and well worth entering. We do charter for this event.
