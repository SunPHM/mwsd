Drum Fish

Drum fishing in 2003 was the best we have seen in many years. We had many trips where we caught 3 drumfish weighing almost 100 pounds, and many more trips where we caught up to 21 drumfish in an hour and a half. Many of the drumfish  weighed close to 60 pounds and there were a number of trips when we caught  25-35 stripers as well as drumfish. We look forward to repeating our success in the upcoming season. 

Drum fishing starts in early May and runs until the end of June.
