Drum & Bass Wars 1998

Presented By :

 



 

Top of Page ^

Seminole Music & Gators


Musicians from all over the country competed in July for prizes totaling more than $6,000.  The competition was proudly held at Gators Cafe and Saloon in Treasure Island, FL.  Seminole Music & Sound organized the contest with sponsorship from ESP Guitars, Ampeg Bass Amps, Mapex Drums, Sabian Cymbals, D'Addario Strings, EMG Pickups, Premier Drums and Evans Drum Heads. 

The Kingpin Band hosted the activities, and Gators offered food and drink specials all night. All ages were welcome to come out and enjoy the contest. 

The semi-finals for the competition were held on the following dates: 
  

  • Monday, July 6th, 1998
  • Monday, July 13st, 1998
  • Monday, July 20th, 1998

Grand prizes were given away on the final evening of competition - Monday, July 27th, 1998. 

Congratulations to our grand prize Bass War winner for 1998 Xavier Chisholm !
