My Job:

I'm a Senior Design Engineer and supervisor of The Low Temperature Facility. My office is in room 2144 and my shop is in room 2138 of Smith Lab. I design and fabricate low temperature apparatus from Dilution Refrigerators ( that will go down very close to absolute zero ) to simple dip probes that will work from 4k up to room temperature. I also design and build gas handling systems, pumping systems, and etc. needed to run these apparatus for low temperature research. I do all my design and machine drawings on the computer using the Acadr12 program. I started at this job on September 3, 1963.
