Musicvox (Tom Petersson Special) Space Ranger 1 of 1 
Submitted by Matt Eichen of Musicvox on September 24, 2001

"What do you think of the famed mirror Space Ranger 12-String Bass Guitar? Can you make out some features in the photos?  I had it in my possession for only 6 hours between it arriving from Philip Ralph in Hollywood and me "SAME DAYING " it out to Tom Petersson by FedEx. The Space Ranger's got locking Sperzel tuners, no tone controls, just two volume controls. It's a 32" scale. Pretty lightweight and the neck is just the sweetest most finely crafted piece of handwork I've seen.  It's a set neck which also might be pretty unique in 12 string bass circles. The body is a standard Space Ranger sized mahogany body with royal blue Aniline stain on the sides and headstock."
