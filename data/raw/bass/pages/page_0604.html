Peavey Announces G-V(TM) Bass

July 10, 1998 -- Peavey Electronics Corporation is pleased to announce release of the G-V(TM) bass, a 34" scale, expertly-crafted bass with a sculpted, lightweight body. The G-V bass features an adjustable graphite composite neck, Pau Ferro fingerboard, and 2 VFL internally-active pickups and three-band active equalizer. Built for maximum playability, the G-V has 21 frets, a 15" fingerboard radius, and a lightweight, ABM fully-adjustable bridge.

FEATURES:

  • Solid alder body
  • 35" scale graphite composite neck
  • 21-fret Pau Ferro fingerboard with 15" radius
  • ABM fully-adjustable bridge
  • 2 VFL pickups with master volume, pickup blend control
  • 3-band active EQ with bass, mid and treble controls, boost and cut

For more information, visit their web site at www.peavey.com.
