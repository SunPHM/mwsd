The Weapon of choice!



Make: Aria

Model: Pro II (Magna Series)

Amplification: Laney BC30

I have always liked the sound of the bass guitar and have always promised myself that I would buy one...

Well, it took me until June 2000 but I eventually got round to it! This was £200 (I'd only just gotten my finances in any kind of order and then along comes this!) 2nd hand from a guy at work but it was worth it!

Since buying it, I've had the action lowered to that of a six-string, and I've re-strung it with medium gauge Rotasound strings which gives it a nice almost acoustic sound. I have tried lessons but at the moment, I am quite happy to improve in my own time and at my own speed!

Two useful sources of tabulature for the bass are available at: The Online Guitar Archive, and The Bass Tab Archive.
