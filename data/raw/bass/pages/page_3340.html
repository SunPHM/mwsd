Dinner at the Sonoda house. Frank's famous Chilean Sea Bass (ok, we KNOW it's on the endangered species list but we only eat it as a treat = $15.99 a pound!). Frank pan seared it so there was a light crust on the outside, and then steamed it. Then it was topped off with his delicious asian dressing. Sooooooo good!

This has got to be one of my most favorite things to eat in the whole world! This is why i have a whole page dedicated to the meal.

We all licked our plates clean! Frank and Stu like to eat their chilean sea bass first, but I like to save it to the end, and eat the other stuff first.


Stu served a chardonnay with the fish. I usually don't care for chardonnay but this one was wonderful. My description of it to Stu was : "like drinking a sara lee butter pound cake drizzled with extra warm butter with a hint of almond extract". Yum yum yum!
