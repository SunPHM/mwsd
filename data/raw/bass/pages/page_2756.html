Warmwater Fish Species
Info on the most popular Manitoba warm water game fish

Fishing in Manitoba, Canada. There are 50 fish species which are found in Manitoba. The following covers some of the most popular species. Manitoba Warm water Fish Species. Catfish, Walleye, Northern Pike, Perch, Smallmouth bass, Carp, Freshwater Drum, White Bass

Small Mouth Bass
Discover the best in Smallmouth Fishing across Manitoba. Try taking a smallmouth bass on light tackle, and you'll see why anglers rate this Manitoba fish the best freshwater fighter pound-for-pound. The Bass is probably one of the most fun fish to catch. They will readily take lures, topwater baits, live bait, and are eager fish to catch for the avid fly fisherman. Found throughout the Winnipeg River System, the Whiteshell and some Northwestern Manitoba waterways.


Carp
Found in good numbers in the Red and Assiniboine rivers, including Lake of the Prairies. The southern basins of lakes Winnipeg, Manitoba and Winnipegosis also offer impressive trophy carp fishing. Carp Fishing has grown in popularity and sees many anglers traveling from overseas to carp fish in Manitoba's waters. Still not overly fussy due to lack of fishing pressure, worms, corn, and dough baits work well in catching these fish.

Freshwater Drum (Silver Bass)
The Drum can be fished for using the techniques you use for other sport fish. The Red, Assiniboine and Winnipeg rivers are ideal spots within the province of Manitoba. Within city limits they can be taken from Sturgeon Creek and the Seine River. Will take most natural baits, lures and jigs.

White Bass
The white bass is an excellent fish for the angler. It fights well, takes a hook readily, travels in schools. Find one fish and you could find many to catch one after another. Not a native species here, the fish was found in Manitoba waters back in the 60's but is a fish many anglers discover is fun fishing for.
