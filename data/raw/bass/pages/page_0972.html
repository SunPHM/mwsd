Electro-Harmonix Bass Micro Synth
[Electro-Harmonix Bass Micro-Synth Website]

The Electro-Harmonix Bass Micro Synth is a bass guitar effect that processes your bass in such a way to to create a more synthesizer type of sound. The sounds range form fairly pure tone like to distorted square waves.

You use the ten sliders to shape the attack tone release and other parameters. There are four voices that can be mixed and processed by sweepable filters.

This bass version is very similar to the guitar version but is tailored for the bass guitar.

Features

  • Ten sliders shape your sound
  • Four independent mixable voices
  • Sweepable resonant filter
  • specifically tailored for bass
