(below right) Here we see Ken playing Bass for The Johnny Johnny Boom Boom Fitowski Attack!  (Note the full grown beard!).  Ken was spending most of his time in the studio those days.  This was shortly before he started working on his solo album, Zensational.  Ken used some of the musicians from The Fitowski Attack! on the Zensational album.  (ex. Gary Zicaro (Guitar), Chuck Jacabacci (Keyboards))

(below left) These are photos taken at Dare Studios in New York during pre-production for the Zensational album.  

(above right) This is Ken with Guitarist Gary Zicaro from the above mentioned "Fitowski Attack" and "The Lost".   (below left) Ken and Gary again at a New York beach during a live broadcast with WBAB 102.3 FM New York radio.

(below right) Here we see Ken in Don Casale Recording Studios (NY) with none other than Don Casale himself and George Snow, recording George Snow's album "Words Revolve Forever".  You can catch Ken playing drums and percussion on many of George's albums.

(below left) This is Ken in the recording studio with the renowned photographer Bob James having double the fun! (never before seen photos!)

(Below right) As you know, this is Ken with ICE.  These are some rare photos taken from a photo session before John James, (Bass) joined the band.  Here we see Spunk, Kerri, and Flint (as he was known with ICE).

We asked Ken about this next shot....."Ken, how did you get it to come out that way?"  He said, "Lot's of prayer.........."

 

Ken says, "One of the many things I miss about ICE is the stage show we had.  And as big as it was, we had so many ideas to make it bigger and better.   I remember winning over an audience that was so blown away when we first got onstage that no one out there knew what to do.  By the third song, they were going nuts!"
