David Smith of Blacksmith / Bad Karma


David a New York City native is a longtime Capital land favorite and musical survivor. Having wore many musical hats throughout his career, guitarist, artist, singer, songwriter, producer, band member and all around musical bonvivant, David comes full circle, leading his own band with their own unique standard of exciting, hard edged, heart pumping music. As guitarist and songwriter for nationally and internationally known groups Blacksmith and Freaknation. David brings many years of experience to the stage. Touring and promotions throughout the U. S. and abroad, along with a number of CD/Record releases America and Europe over the past decade prove his professionalism and dedication to music. A number of David's original songs have appeared in several Fox syndicated television shows over the past few years. David's fiery and imaginative guitar work, along with his powerful and soulful lead vocals, lay down an exciting framework for his Bad Karma partners. David has always loved the look and sound of Dean Guitars and has been a long time Dean Player, his weapons of choice are the mighty Dean USA Time Capsule Z, USA Time Capsule Cadillac and 25th Anniv. Cadillac.
