Our History

For the past 15 years I've been associated with one of the Worlds Best Anglers - "Wild Bill Skinner".

Wild Bill
Wild Bill has probably caught more bass over 10lbs than any other angler in the world.
In1996 Wild Bill and I fished the "Grand Slam" of Mexico for Bass. We pounded them at the four best lakes in Mexico Agua Milpa, Baccarac, El Salto and last but not least Lake Huites. (As far as I know we are the only two people to ever fish the Grand Slam - that is all four lakes consecutively at one period of time)

Huites Sunrise
These lakes are teaming with Bass and each year they are getting better and better. I hauled in a "Toad" weighing 10lb 3oz at Lake Huites. Fishing these lakes rekindled fond memories of the past, since then I've helped hundreds of Anglers Men and Women Realize their goal of the "Fishing Trip of their Dreams".

 

Our Goal

Trophy Bass Lodge

For Example: I just returned from Mexico, Fishing Lake Huites, I stayed at the New Trophy Bass Lodge. I had a great time and we hammered the Bass. While there I overheard one Angler say,
"This was the best fishing he had ever experienced." Another said he caught at least 100 bass a day.
Bass fishing in Mexico at Lakes Huites and El Salto is the best Bass fishing in the world. The Florida Strain Largemouth Bass are lurking in these Mexican lakes.
Trophy Bass over 19 pounds have been boated in Mexico. It is the opinion of many that the World Record will be caught here. Lake El Salto (record 18 lbs 8 oz) and Lake Huites (record 15 lbs 3 oz caught by Wild Bill Skinner) continue to excite Anglers with the large numbers and size of bass. It's the place to go for heavy action and bass up to and over 12 lbs.
