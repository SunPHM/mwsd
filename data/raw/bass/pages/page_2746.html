Lake Baccarac bass fishing Mexico with Bassmex

Steve Quinn Fishing Editor
IN-FISHERMAN Magazine
Lake Baccarac offers HUGE Trophy Bass. The Ultimate Wish of any Bass fisherman is to lay into a Largemouth Bass over fifteen pounds. The World Record Bass fishing is thought to be lurking in the waters of Lake Baccarac. The Lake Baccarac Record (19.10 lb) was caught in January 1993. In November of 1994 another angler came close with a Giant Bass weighing 19.6 lbs! Many anglers claim to have hooked and lost fish well over the 20 lb mark. Netters say they have caught and released fish over 22 lbs. Literally thousands of big Bass over ten pounds have been caught and released at this Super Trophy lake!


Ken Kross Chemist
Owner of Kick'n Bass

Bill Chapman Sr. stocked Lake Baccarac was with the Florida strain largemouth bass in 1978. The fingerlings that were used to stock Baccarac came from Lake Dominguez, the very first lake to be stocked in Mexico with Florida Strain Bass. Like Lake Huites, Baccarac started as an incredible numbers lake. Since then the natural life cycle of this lake has produced some real giants.  

The Lake Baccarac bass fishing record was caught by Bruce Knutson in 1993 with at least one more fish over 19 lb. caught by Ray Hill of Phoenix a few years later. Baccarac is more then 25 miles long and 5 miles wide in certain areas. At full pool Baccarac is between 25,000 and 35,000 surface acres depending on the water level. There are 200 islands, hundreds of points, and many coves to keep any fisherman busy. You can expect to catch 10-30 fish per day with at least a couple of Trophy Bass every trip. Big Bass over 10 pounds are caught daily and fish much larger are hooked but lost to the brush or a broken line! Recent rains in the past few seasons have given Baccarac a new lease on life and the higher water levels and freshly submerged cover should make for an excellent spawn, keeping this incredible bass fishing Trophy Lake strong for years to come. The lake is nestled among green-forested mountain peaks.

Karen Savik Professional Bass Fisherman

The season is short, at Lake Baccarac compared to all of the other lakes.  The lake is open for fishing from October until April.  Early booking is the key to fishing this great reservoir.  My favorite months to fish at Baccarac is late October, November, December, early January and the whole month of March for some great post spawn Bassin'!  Accommodations are limited so make your reservations early!  No matter what time of the season you decide to fish Lake Baccarac, it will be a trip that will remain in your memory for a long, long time!  Anglers at Lake Baccarac enjoy fishing from 18' to 21' fiberglass boats with 45 to 65 hp. outboards.  They come equipped for traditional Bait Casting or Fly fishing and all boats have Coast Guard approved Life jackets, swivel seats, nets and an onboard ice chest full of your choice of Beer, bottled water and Soft Drinks!  There is also a small doctor's office located right across the street from the lodge.

Your seasoned professional local guide sits in the rear of the boat out of the way so that you will have plenty of room to "Pound those Pigs" in this world class bass factory.  The guides know Lake Baccarac like their own back yard, and I will assure you that they will do work hard to put you on the big fish.  They also untangle your bird nests, and show you what lures and presentations work best for that day and in the area you are fishing.
