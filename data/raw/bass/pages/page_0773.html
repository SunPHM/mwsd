Captain Vernon C. Bryan III


 Captain Vernon C. Bryan III knows a thing or two about fishing - after all he grew up in the family seafood business and fishing was his way of life. A native of the Jersey Shore, Captain "V" has been fishing these waters for over 25 years and has an extensive knowledge of their sea life. As the holder of a 100 Ton Masters License, the Captain is fully experienced in both sport and commercial fishing. Join Captain Vernon for a memorable day at sea and a fishing experience you'll never forget!



Captain Scott Kulaszewski

  


Captain Scott Kulaszewski has been fishing and boating at the Jersey Shore his entire life. He is an experienced guide in fluke, striped bass, offshore tuna and shark. He gained knowledge of the local fishery while in the seafood business over the last 15 years. Captain Scott’s dedication to fishing and his passion for the sport are evident every time he sets sail.
