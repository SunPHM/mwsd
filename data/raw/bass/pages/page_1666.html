Comments:

     Definitely a very special instrument. The first impression is its striking appearance: elegant, with clean, sweeping lines and woods that possess a kind of quiet, reserved beauty. Then you pick it up and it is unbelievably slight - a little over 7 pounds for a full sized 5 string bass. Held on a strap the bass almost melts into nothingness because of the light weight, perfect balance, and effortless playability (the action is so low that many people may even choose to raise it). Then the sound: a very detailed, woody sound with a raspy low-mid growl that is both unique and musical. This bass has a complex voice that in some ways reminds us more of a fine, acoustic instrument than of a solidbody electric bass. Props to Carey Nordstrand for having the courage to take some risks and create something unusual and different, as well as the skills to pull it off and make it a real musical instrument.
