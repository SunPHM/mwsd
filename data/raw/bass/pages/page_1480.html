Michelle Fiore of Classic Contrabass Rentals has repaired, played & taught in Chicagoland for many years, and is acutely aware of the great need for quality double bass rentals for bass students of all ages. Classic Contrabass offers just this ~ basses of fine quality in good condition, with a professional set-up job!!

Properly set-up string instruments are easier to play, allowing a student to focus on the technical challenges of the instrument.  This, in turn, helps maintain student interest!!!

 

 
 
 

 

The bass pictured here is just one of the many basses in our rental pool.  As with all of our rental basses, it has:

Adjustable Bridge -- All of our 1/4, 1/2, and 3/4 rental instruments have this!!

Steel cable tailgut, properly tuned for the instrument.  Many student basses have stiff coat-hanger style tailgut, which impede the instrument's vibration.

Properly dressed fingerboards.  (Like most of our rental basses, this model has an ebony fingerboard and saddle.)

Helicore, Spirocore, or other quality strings.

Stable, sturdy endpins.

 

Rental outfits include bass, case & bow.

Classic Contrabass has 1/10,  1/8,  1/4,  1/2, and 3/4  basses for rent.

Call for availability and rental prices.
