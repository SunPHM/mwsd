Tansi, and Welcome!

Doig River First Nations, Campfire Festival

Winston entertained in both the morning and afternoon as featured entertainment with longtime friend, Ed Peekeetkoot at the Campfire Music Festival.

Winston showcased his talents with guitar, violin, drum, bass guitar, clarinet, mandolin, show stealing puppet, Joe and great comedy.

Despite the extreme heat, Winston kept everyone entertained and laughing as he interacted with the audience. A good time was had by all attendees at the Festival.
