JUST FOR YOU: Phil & Loni

Florida Bass Fishing September 7 & 8, 2000
Florida Bass Fishing Trip

This Florida bass fishing couple Phil and Loni Janik (Torrington, CT - ph: 860-482-1547 - e-mail: pjanik@javanet.com) caught and released 12 bass up to 4 1/4 lbs on wild shiners during 2 half days (Sept. 7 & 8, 2000) of Florida bass fishing with Cap'n Ron. Phil caught the 4 1/4 lb. Florida bass. His wife Loni also caught and released a nice 3 1/2 lb. bass.

Their best catch of the day however was a ugly 10 lb. mudfish that Phil is struggling to hold.

JUST FOR YOU: Mike & Ian!

Florida Bass Fishing August 3-7, 2000
Florida Bass Fishing Trip

This 3 foot alligator which tried to eat our float and hooked it self in the tail was the only trophy this father and son team boated and released unharmed.

This father and son Florida bass fishing team did however manage to catch a good amount of Florida bass as the below Florida bass fishing report notes:

Mike Harris (Newark, DE - 302-368-6441 - e-mail: IAceOne@aol.com) and his 19 year-old son Ian caught about 80 Florida bass up to 4 1/2 lbs. during 3 1/2 days (Aug. 3-7, 2000) of wild shiner fishing. Bass were caught in the Harney Pond Canal and Fisheating creek areas of Lake Okeechobee.


JUST FOR YOU: Chae Myong Chi!

Florida Bass Fishing & Wild Boar Hunting August 2-5, 2000
Florida Bass Fishing & Hunting Trip

Chae Myong Chi and his son-in-law Chuck Etheridge (Taegu, Korea - ph: 011-82534734777 - e-mail: EtheridgeC@usfk.korea.army.mil) caught 117 Florida bass up to 5 lbs during 3 days (Aug. 2-4, 2000) of Florida wild shiner bass fishing with Cap'n Ron. Mr. Chi caught the 5 lb. Florida bass and had a larger one throw the hook just as it was being netted. Chuck and his father-in-law on their last morning (Aug. 5, 2000) with me also bagged 2 wild boar hogs. We Florida bass fished in the Harney Pond Canal and Fisheating Creek areas of Lake Okeechobee. They released all (116) Florida bass and kept the 5 lb'er for mounting.

JUST FOR YOU: Andrew!

Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's July 25 & 26, 2000
Florida Bass Fishing Trip

This Cormorant which was hooked and released unharmed while it was diving and searching for a meal caught and ate one of our wild shiner baited lines. It put up a good fight and had us believing we had a trophy bass on the line until it surfaced!

This dad and son Florida bass fishing team did however manage to catch a good amount of Florida bass as the below Florida bass fishing report notes:


Florida bass fishing dad Ken McAlister and 15 year-old son Andrew (Andover, KS - ph: 316-218-0340 - e-mail: mcalisterkg@veracom.net) caught 34 Florida bass that included a 6 1/2 lb., 5 1/2 lb. and 5 1/4 lb. bass during 2 half days (Jul. 25 & 26, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Andews dad caught the 6 1/2 lb. bass during the same time Andrew was fighting the 5 1/2 lb'er. Andrew also caught the 5 1/4 lb. bass. We fished the Harney Pond Canal cuts of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
