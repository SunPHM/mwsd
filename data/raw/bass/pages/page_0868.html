Bass Guitar

Yamaha RBX260 (The only bass I've used for all of my recordings. This is the low-end Yamaha P-bass. Nothing fancy, except the 24-fret neck. Otherwise, it just does its job very well. Sounds good and stays in tune. It's pretty light-weight as well and is only slightly neck-heavy. At some point, I would like to also get a Jazz bass.)

Every bass guitar player should have one of these things. It takes up the same space that 2 standard guitar stands consume, but it hold 5 guitars. I think it also comes in 3-guitar and 7-guitar versions. I don't know why I didn't buy this sooner.
