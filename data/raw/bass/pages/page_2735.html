Bass Baby Boat

The Bass Baby is a two man fishing boat that offers fishermen and women a durable and long lasting two person boat. Anglers who want to fish small lakes and/or ponds, but don't want to invest in an expensive fishing boat, will be glad to discover the Bass Baby. This unique 8' fishing boat has wheels to get you into those secluded fishing holes. Just put the Bass Baby in your pickup bed and head to your favorite fishing spot.

The Bass Baby® will get you to the fish.


New - Bass Baby Video

It's never been easier than with the Bass Baby!

The Bass Baby's seamless hull is molded of environmentally safe and UV-protected polyethylene plastic, making it extremely durable, yet lightweight. The puncture-resistant shell is coated with a unique foam interior specially formulated for additional buoyancy and rigidity under foot. Complete with chairs and wheels for easier access to secluded areas, the Bass Baby weighs a slight 143 lbs and is rated to carry a 475 lb load, while meeting the strict safety regulations of the U.S. Coast Guard.
