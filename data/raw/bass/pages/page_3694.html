Aerial photography is our way of life. Welcome to the Cape Cod Bay Bass & Bluefish Tournament 2003 page of the website of the Aerials Only Gallery. This page displays "thumbnails" of the aerial photographs. Click on the thumbnail or the description below it to enlarge the picture to full-screen size. You can click on the description to view the picture even if the thumbnail has not loaded yet. With a 56K modem, the thumbnails will load completely in less than 2 minutes.

You may need to scroll down the image to see the entire aerial picture. Serial numbers of each photo are displayed on the image. Then use the back button on your browser to return to this page.

All our aerial photographs are taken with 2 x 3 inch and 2.25 and 1.75 inch negatives for superb clarity and sharpness. Your monitor shows the pictures at about 75 dpi while the actual aerial photographs are 3000 dpi. Enlargements are hand printed and color corrected in our own lab. We sell aerial photographs in sizes ranging from 11 x 14 inches to 30 x 40 inches. Here is a pricing and order form. You may also telephone your order to the Aerials Only Gallery at 508-295-5551 or toll free at 800-867-1240. We ship throughout the free world.

We would like to thank the Sesuit Boat Owner's Association, Oliver's Eating & Drinking Establishment and the Northside Marina for hosting such a terrific Bass & Bluefish Tournament. Thanks to all the Captians and Crew, you guys look GREAT!

All proceeds from this tournament are to benefit Dream Day on Cape Cod. Dream Day on Cape Cod helps children facing life threatening illnesses. They provide a fun and peaceful haven for these special children and their families where friends are made and memories cherished.

Captians, be advised that these are JPEG scans for faster viewing. The Prints that you will recieve from your order will be brighter, sharper, more colorful and have extreme detail.
