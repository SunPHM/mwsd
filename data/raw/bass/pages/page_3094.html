Striper Fishing at Elephant Butte

Striper fishing for striped bass is one of the greatest adventures you can have on Elephant Butte lake in Elephant Butte, New Mexico. The striped bass is one of North America's most unpredictable fish, and expert guide Frank Vilorio guides striped bass fishing tours for Land of Enchantment Fishing Adventures to ensure that your striper fishing trip is fulfilling and that you are able to take home your fill of striped bass.

 

Elephant Butte Lake is Rated one of the top premiere Striper Bass Lakes in The United States.  It's New Mexico’s Best Game Fishing!  Come join me and experience the best fishing adventure New Mexico has to offer.
