If you don't belong to a b.a.s.s. affiliated club, you are able to join nys's club n.o.n.e. for the cost of the national federation dues ($15), required by national b.a.s.s. and the regular state membership dues ($20).

Club memberships are $35.00 and clubs must be b.a.s.s. affiliated and consist of 6 or more federation members, one of which is the club president.

All memberships expire on december 31st. make all checks payable to the nys b.a.s.s. federation and send to pamala bliss along with mailing address, phone number and b.a.s.s. number, etc.

Further details can be found at the new york b.a.s.s. chapter federation web site
