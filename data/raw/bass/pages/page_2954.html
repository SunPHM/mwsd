LAKE EL SALTO - MEXICO BASS FISHING


The most popular Mexico bass fishing lake.


THE FISHING

Lake El Salto has thousands of hungry bass that are easily caught all year on a variety of of lures, but topwaters, crankbaits, and plastic worms are best. El Salto offers the amateur and the novice angler the opportunity to easily catch lots of fish most days without a lot of effort.

Lake El Salto has an excellent supply of trophy size bass, and an even more excellent supply of quality size bass. When the lake first opened for public fishing, it was a true bass fishing bonanza! 85% of everyone who fished during the opening, hooked the biggest fish of their lifetime.

Lake El Salto is full of excellent open-water structure that's prime for early summer fishing. But during fall, spring and winter most anglers choose to bang the banks and other shallow areas with top waters, spinner baits, crank baits and Texas-rig soft plastics.

There is plenty of cover to work your baits close to and in. Among the variety of cover available are: lay downs, standing timber, fields of water hyacinth and a prickly aquatic grass that resembles hydrilla .

While El Salto looks promising all over, we have found that the upper half is usually where the best bass fishing occurs. That's where our clients have found hundreds of brush-cluttered points, ridges and humps, not to mention the flooded ruins of two towns, a pair of ancient cemeteries and numerous shallow flats.

FLY FISHING: Lake El Salto provides a great opportunity for fly fishermen of all skill levels to enjoy their sport. From October until Fevruary, the bass are more concentrated on the shallow waters and are quite aggressive. This is a great time for explosive topwater action, which is even more of a thrill when Fly Fishing. We suggest surfaces flies and an 8 weight rod for your fly fishing. The Lake El Salto fly rod record is 9 pounds and 4 ounces!

THE BOATS: While fishing at Club El Salto, you will enjoy fishing out of FISHER BOATS, that are equipped to make sure you enjoy your fishing and the most productive possible.
