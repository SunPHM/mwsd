Travel November 4, 1999

DFW Lakes Hilton, Convenience, Comfort and Much More

Story By: Richard Thomas/ Photos By: Sol Tucker
Pointed Magazine Staff

Grapevine, TX   

Are you planning a trip to Dallas or Ft. Worth? Well if you are, we have found the ideal place for you to stay and have some fun.

The DFW Lakes Hilton, as we found out, offers convenience, comfort and much more. The much more we are referring to are the restaurants, athletic club and swimming pool and of course the rooms and the conference rooms for meetings, weddings or what ever a large group would require.

As we do in all of the properties that we review, will look at the different aspects that will make you want to stay at the DFW Lakes Hilton.

We will look at the overall hotel including the restaurant and the menu items served, the amenities including the meeting facilities, bar and the athletic facilities. Lastly, we will rate the overall hotel experience.

For starters, we will start with some background information that will make this property well worth checking into when considering staying in the Dallas/Fort Worth area.

Hotel

The hotel, which is located two and one half miles from DFW International Airport, offers the ease of a short drive or a hotel shuttle can be provided depending on what your stay requires while in the area. By car, the hotel can be reached from I 635 or Texas 121.

The DFW Lakes Hilton, which sits lake side in the town of Grapevine, has all a traveler will need when coming to the area. The very modern Bass Pro Shops are located directly across the street and as we found out is well worth the trip across the street.

If the Bass Pro Shop is not enough for you, then we suggest taking another walk to the Grapevine Mills mall, which is right to the left of the hotel and is sure to offer anything a traveler, could possibly want. So whether you are staying for business or pleasure, the DFW Lakes Hilton has everything you need within walking distance.

Now that we have described what a traveler should expect around the hotel we will look at the amenities.

Amenities

Next, we looked at the amenities of the hotel, which included the meeting space and the recreational facilities.

We would have to say the Hilton goes above and beyond many properties in the Dallas area to please the guest with both incredible meeting and recreational facilities.

The meeting areas which are located on three different floors in the hotel are perfect for anything such as weddings or week long conventions, which are very frequent at the hotel. If the hotel meeting space become full they can even use the 14,000 square feet indoor tennis courts for additional space.

If you are the kind of person that likes to work out then we have found a place that is second to none in the hotel industry. The DFW Lakes Hilton offers a full fitness center complete with indoor and outdoor swimming pools, tennis and racquetball and courts and a full pro-shop which even offers racquet stringing if you need it.

Rooms

After a guest has had the chance to walk around the hotel it might be a good time to retire to the spacious rooms and enjoy relaxing on one of the Hilton’s king sized beds. Many of the spacious rooms are also available in suites on private floors accessed only by room keys.

Other amenities of the rooms include hair dryer, coffee makers, Jacuzzi’s and bathrobes in the suites and premium Neutrogena bath and body products.

All of the essentials along with the designed folding of towels and wash clothes give the rooms a homely setting that any traveler is going to admire.

The mini bar and the data ports for a laptop use are all premium items that many business travelers enjoy and Hilton provides standard in many of their hotels.

Restaurant

Lastly, we could not forget about the delicious food offered in one of the three restaurants inside of the hotel. The Miratage Grill, which offers steaks, seafood and traditional southwest cuisine, is highly recommended and once we sampled some of the items we could easily see why any one would should try their menu items.

For starters we decided to try what Texas is known for, great steak. A great steak is exactly what we received. The 10-ounce filet mignon is a healthy portion and is cooked to order and is mouth watering.

According to Assistant Food and Beverage Director Arthur English, the filet is one of most ordered items from the menu. From what we tasted we see why people continue to order this time after time.

All of the seafood items especially the sea bass are highly recommended. The buttered shrimp as an appetizer is also highly recommended.

After we sampled the fine items in Miratage, we decided to step over to the Boardroom, which offers the complete lounge setting with big screen television and a full service bar with many beers on tap including beers exclusive to the State of Texas.

Rating

Overall, we give the DFW Lakes Hilton high marks and a total of four out of five stars. The staff is very courteous and will help you with any needs. The bell staff is attentive and will help any guest with bags and anything to get a guest situated in checking in. The only flaw we saw was the hours of the athletic club and the fact The Vineyard offers limited hours at night, sort of channeling all guests into the other two establishments.
