Last Update: January 9, 2006

2006 Recreational Marine Fisheries Regulations

Effective: August 1, 2005

Winter Flounder 
 Minimum length:  12 inches
 Daily creel limit:  10 fish per angler
Season:  Open April 1 - May 30

Summer Flounder (Fluke)
Minimum length:  17 1/2 inches
Daily creel limit:  6 fish per angler
Season:  Open April 30 - December 31

Striped Bass 
ALL ANGLERS
Minimum length:  28 inches
Daily creel limit:  2 fish per angler
Season:  Open Year Round

Bluefish
Minimum length:   None
Daily creel limit: 10 fish per angler
    (Including "snappers")
Season:  Open Year Round

Hickory Shad / American Shad
Minimum length:  None
Daily creel limit:  6 fish per angler
  (Both species in aggregate)
Season:  Open Year Round


Blackfish (Tautog)
Minimum length:   14 inches
Daily creel limit:  4 fish per angler
Season:  Open  January 1 - April 30,
               June 15 - September 7 and
               September 22 - December 13

Weakfish (Sea Trout)  
Minimum length:   16 inches
Daily creel limit:  10 fish
Season:  Open Year Round

Scup (Porgy) 
Minimum length:   10 1/2 inches
Daily creel limit:  25 fish per angler
   Party/Charter creel limit:  
           25 fish per angler Jul 1 - Aug 31
           60 fish per angler Sept 1 - Oct 31
Season:  Open July 1 - October 31

White Perch 
Minimum length:   7 inches
Daily creel limit:  30 fish per angler
Season:  Open Year Round

Black Sea Bass
Minimum length:   12 inches
Daily creel limit:  25 fish per angler
Season:  Open January 1 - November 30

American Eel
Minimum length:   6 inches
Daily creel limit:  50 fish per angler
Season:  Open Year Round

Atlantic Cod
Minimum length: 22 inches 
Daily creel limit: 10 per angler
    No creel limit on Party/Charter Vessels
Season:  Open Year Round

Haddock
Minimum length: 19 inches 
Daily creel limit:  None
Season:  Open Year Round

Pollock
Minimum length:  19 inches
Daily creel limit:  None
Season:  Open Year Round

Blue Crab
Minimum shell width (Spike tip to spike tip):
  5 inches - Hard shell
  3 1/2 inches - Soft shell
Daily creel limit:  None
Season:  May 1 - November 30
