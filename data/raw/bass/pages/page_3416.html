Biography

Born in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in 1975, Simon Wong grew up in Coffs Harbour on Australia's East Coast prior to relocating to Brisbane. He is currently performing in rural Australia as an understudy to internationally renowned Australian artist; Dale Frank.
