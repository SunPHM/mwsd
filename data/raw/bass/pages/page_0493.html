About Bass Buster Guide Services

Hello Friends, I'm Captain Randy Camps and I'll be your guide for one heck of a darn good bass fishing trip. Whether we're out trying to bust that Lunker of a lifetime or just plain catching plenty of mean Florida black bass. I'll do everything in my power to ensure you have a successful and enjoyable trip. I've been bass bust-n for most of my life and I'm a full time local guide. No bull and no hype, and when it comes to knowing how to catch bass in these parts I'm the real deal.

Realistically fish don't slam the baits everyday, and knowing where, when, and about how many will bite helps ensure a great trip. Big Bad "TROPHY BASS" are possible everyday!
I'll use every advantage to make that Florida dream of a trophy fish come true. My goal is to meet your needs and deliver that lunker of a lifetime. Providing you with a safe and enjoyable fishing trip is a top priority. Check out the lunker gallery and picture yourself catching that trophy fish you've dreamed about. When the lunkers won't bite there is plenty of ACTION catching high quality bass in the 3 to 8 pound range.

Live wild golden shiners produce the biggest fish; artificial bait action tends to be the most challenging.
When I'm not out chasing Trophy fish, I enjoy teaching beginners how to use artificial bait's to catch bass. You will learn the basics from top to bottom and catch your first bass using artificial baits. No hassle and no pressure. Just using basic techniques that every beginner can apply and have success with. Check out "our daily rates page" for an affordable rate for a day out on the water learning to catch Bass. Ladies, Gentleman, Young and old everyone can learn to enjoy the great sport of bass fishing.

You can expect to fish with top of the line spinning gear or bait casters if you prefer. The big bad lean mean fishing machine is always kept clean and in perfect running order. Fishing with 2 guests and a guide is very comfortable. A third guest will usually get in the way if you are using artificial bait and it is not recommended. Live wild shiner fishing with 3 guests is permissible yet it can get crowded. A single guest gets pampered and is the preferred way to fish.

Our Gear

Top quality freshwater fishing rods and reels are used. The Spinning outfits are lightweight yet strong and work well for most anglers. Bait casters are available and in general are for the more experienced angler. Shimano, St. Croix, and ALLSTAR rods and reels make for extremely reliable and trouble free operation. Super Pro Monofilament, Fire line, triple fish, and Gorilla braid make for extra tough lines that can turn those lunkers your way.

30 pound SEAGUAR Leader keeps them hooked up and coming to the boat. Artificial baits are provided on all artificial fishing trips at no extra charge. Rods and reels are pre-spooled with the correct line for proper presentation and quick clips are used for easy bait selection. You will have an excellent selection of baits to choose from. We fish out of 3 tackle boxes marked Rattletraps, Top water, and crank baits. All Spinner baits, worms and flukes are separated into separate containers.

Things You Need To Bring

It's Florida and the sun will fry you into a crispy critter, cloudy or not, if you are not prepared. At the Top of the list are Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat. Next you need a current fishing license. Finally rain gear. Rain can come at anytime! Generally during the winter months lighting is not associated with these storm cells and it is possible to fish through each cell. A nice pair of soft sole shoes will fill out the list and make for a comfortable day. Everyone who plans on fishing should come with a great attitude and be prepared to have a Marvelous time relaxing and Catching BASS!
