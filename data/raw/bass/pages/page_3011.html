Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
6 lb'er - May 20 - 22, 2002

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing honeymoon couple Travis and Corney Casteel (Maryville, MO - ph: 660-562-3399 - e-mail: tcasteel@asde.net) caught and released about 30 bass that included a 6 lb'er bass during 3 days of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron on May 20 - 22, 2002. Poor weather conditions slowed down the fishing. Corney caught and released the 6 lb'er. Bass were caught in Harney Pond Canal and the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

Red Dog knows where to get a good rub down!



Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
6 1/2 lb'er - May 19, 2002

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Paul Allen and his son Cody (Inkom, ID - ph: 208-775-3522 - e-mail: alcrane@dcdi.net) caught and released 11 bass that included a 6 1/2 lb. bass during a half day morning of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron on May 19, 2002. Paul caught the 6 1/2 lb'er. All bass were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee. Paul and his son both bagged a wild sow during a combined wild boar hunt during the afternoon.

Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
7 lb., 6 1/2 lb, and two 6 lb'ers - May 7 & 8, 2002

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Mike Wehner (Headland, AL - ph: 334-693-9100 - e-mail: mwehner@graceba.net and Karry Godwin (Grovetown, GA - ph: 706-210-8206 - e-mail: kpgodwin@aol.com) caught and released 17 bass that included a 7 lb., 6 1/2 lb. and two 6 lb'ers during 2 half day mornings of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron on May 7 & 8, 2002. Karry caught the 7 lb'er. Mike caught the 6 1/2 and two 6 lb'ers. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

Karry top left with a 7 lb'er. Mike top right with a 6 1/2 lb'er and below with a nice pair of 6 lb'ers.

These guys racked up an impressive catch of bass in just 2 half days of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron!
