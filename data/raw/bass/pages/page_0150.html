  Trombones have various different roles in the orchestra.  In Early times, trombones were used to play along with voices in choral music.  This meant they had lots of playing to do.

However in most orchestral music, especially in Romantic times, the Trombones are reserved for the big impressive parts of the music.  This means we have less to play, but it's exciting when it happens! The sound of trombones playing quietly can be very striking too. 

 We sit next to the Tuba, either beside or behind the Trumpets.
