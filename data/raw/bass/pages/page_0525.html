Night Fishing - Story

I was meeting some buddies for a little night catfishing, so I decided to go a little early and get a little bass fishing in out of my float tube. I had had no luck for about two hours and was heading in to meet my buddies. I let out about 50 yards of line with a Texas rigged purple plastic worm on. I'm guessing the depth was about 25'. My worm was bouncing off the bottom as I paddle towards shore. I came up on a point to where the water got shallower. My worm was really hanging up. Then I thought I had really hung up on the bottom. then my line started moving for the surface. That's when the beast went airborne. I lost my breath for a minute. I set the hook one more good time to ensure a good hook set. He dragged me around for a good 3-4 minutes before I actually landed him. This is by far my best experience with bass fishing.

Travis Reed
