Andy has played professionally for 25 years. His style was developed by singing Motown, Classic Rock, Country, Rhythm and Blues, and Reggae. Some of Andy’s biggest influences are Marvin Gaye, Elton John, Neil Young, and Van Morrison. He has experience in TV, radio, commercials, as a Master of Ceremonies, and just about anything to do with entertainment you could imagine. Along with his duties as lead vocalist, Andy also dabbles in a bit of acoustic, electric, and bass guitar. When he's not playing music, which is rare, Andy enjoys a good round of sub-par Golf. Catch him at The Peninsula in Gulf Shores, AL.
