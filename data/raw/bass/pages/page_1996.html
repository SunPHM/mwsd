WHAT SETS FOLKCRAFT LEGEND HAMMERED DULCIMERS APART FROM THE OTHERS?

 

Our design arches the frame of the whole instrument stretching the strings over a much stronger shape. The benefits are threefold:

  1. String tension works for the Dulcimer and not against it;
  2. A stronger structure results in increased tuning stability;
  3. Positive soundboard tension, created by the arching, enhances tonal balance and volume throughout the instrument.
