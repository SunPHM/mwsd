Specialties of the House

  Try Santa Fe's Signature Frozen Margarita, Strawberry or Lime.
  Enjoy imaginative dishes such as Sweet Potato Crusted Sea Bass.
  Chipotle steamed clams a are summer favorite. Local little neck clams are prepared with peppers, green chilies,
garlic and white wine.
  Santa Fe's fajitas, served on a sizzling skillet, are a perennial favorite. You can choose from grilled vegetables, chicken, beef or seafood.
 

 

Even Mexican classics like Nachos take on a delicious new feel with Santa Fe's touch of garlic, cilantro, and black beans.

  Santa Fe's desserts are home made. Pictured here is our Chocolate Torte. It's a special treat prepared with raspberry sauce, caramel sauce, vanilla ice cream and fresh fruit. Our Banana Taco with ice cream and and Sopaipillas with honey are also big favorites.

Looking for great seafood with killer sunsets! Your in luck, Santa Fe also owns Bostwicks Seafood Grill and Oyster Bar on
