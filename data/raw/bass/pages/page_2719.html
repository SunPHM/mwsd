Capt. Bill Cannan runs a 25' Boston Whaler Outrage powered by Mercury and a 31' Contender for all of his saltwater fishing trips. Cannan takes advantage of the seasonal opportunities as they are presented throughout the year.


From October through June bayfishing is hot and he runs bay trips for speckled trout and redfish, June through October the tarpon move in and he runs tarpon trips. During the tarpon season from June through October Cannan will also run offshore trips for kingfish, dorado, ling, and red snapper.

Bass fishing trips are also available to Lake Conroe, Lake Livingston, and two private trophy bass lakes. Cannan runs these trips from a current year model Nitro 896 bass boat. Cannan's bass fishing trips are offered year 'round but are mainly available in the Sping and Fall. 

All tackle is provided when you are fishing with Captain Bill Cannan. He is on the Outdoor Technologies Group Guide Program and welcomes you and your party to use his Fenwick, Berkley, and Ambassadeur products. Both boats are well equipped with Lowrance sonar and GPS, insured, and meet all coast guard requirements. Accomodations and/or transportation can be arranged if you are flying in to fish with Capt. Bill. 

**All you need when fishing with Captain Bill is food and drinks for your party, a Texas fishing license with saltwater stamp, sunscreen, and white or gray soled shoes.
