N Syncs Lance Bass To Pick Nose In Outer Space

[Wednesday, September 25th, 2002]

Lance Bass
NSync's Lance Bass is to appear in a new musical film called "Nose Pickers From Outer Space," reports Variety

His production company has bought the rights to the book of the same name and are planning to turn it into a film using live actors as well as computer generated images.

The book is about an alien who attempts to save earth from destruction by impersonating a school student. Bass will play one of the Nose Pickers, an alien race that carry computers in their noses.

"Grease" director Randal Kleiser will direct.
