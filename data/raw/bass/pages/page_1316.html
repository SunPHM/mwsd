Authentic instruments for serious children
 The size and dimensions for these models have been downsized as closely as possible to the regular guitars and basses. Shimamura Music is proud to introduce the 5 models of "History Kids Musician Series".


  All of the mini guitars in the past had small bodies but regular sized neck, head and pickups. The design was out of proportion and looked a little awkward. With the same sized neck and the heavy weight, it was still a unconfortable instrument for children to play. We did not want to discourage their will...
The Shimamura Music's "History Kids Musician Series" is a closely downsized guitar for chidren at the age of 4 to 12 (Height 90 - 150 cm) (*). With these Kids Musician Series, children who is at the middle of their growth should be able to practice on their skills more confortably. With the thinner neck and a smaller pickup, we have improved the overall balance. These guitars not only sound great, but they are also very fasionable.

* The age and height is referential standard.
