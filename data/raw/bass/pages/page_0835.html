Smallmouth Bass

Smallmouth Bass commonly called jumpers because of their acrobatics when being brought in, are found close to the islands just about all season long.

They are such great fighters that many bass fisherman practice catch and release.

Danny Buoy Fishing Charters has no problem with anyone catching and releasing smallies.

In Ohio Waters bass can be taken using live bait such as softcraws, bass minnies and leeches, however they will hit on artifical bait, plastic craws, worms, tubes etc.

Ask about our Canadian Bass Trips starting the last Saturday in June. Canada has a season on Bass and you MUST have an Ontario license.
