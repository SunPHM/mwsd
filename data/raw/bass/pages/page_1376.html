Thom Krauss
Bass Guitar

Thom likes to rock and he does it well.  He also likes Elvis, horseback riding, long walks on the beach, candle-lit dinners, fine novels and $2 bottles of wine...if he thinks it will help him score.   This dude can play and has really added something to the rhythm section.  Thom is an all-around musician known most for being a guitar player. He can also sing, and play drums.  He has an album recorded, which he played all the instruments.  His quiet personality gets a kick in the crotch when he puts on the bass.  You should check him out.!!

Thom was one of the finalists in the WNY 2003 People's Choice awards for Best Rock Bass Player
