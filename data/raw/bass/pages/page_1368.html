This page contains some of the art work I have created through the years.
The landscape paintings reflect memorable places that I've been, and were
created with oils, acrylics, putty knives, brushes, my fingers, rags, and tape.

The sketches were created with graphite pencils, pastels, charcoal, India ink,
chalk, chamois cloth, and felt-tipped markers.

The sculptures were created with clay, plaster, wire, pipe cleaners, Gapoxio
epoxy putty, Dremel rotary tool, newspaper, sandpaper, gauze, metal files, knives,
glue, rocks, wood, fabric, sequins, snow, ice, sticks, styrofoam, pins, acrylic paint,
Pearl Ex pigments, and whatever else wasn't nailed down. : )

The digital images were created using a Minolta 35mm camera, Nikon digital
camera, Photoshop 7 software, flatbed scanner, negative scanner, and mouse.
(images created with graphics tablet coming soon)
