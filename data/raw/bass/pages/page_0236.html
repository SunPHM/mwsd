Boys and Girls Homes of North Carolina, Inc.

North Carolina Extension and Community Association

 

NC Extension and Community Association members and Boys and Girls Homes of NC  Extension and Community Association members have long supported the mission of Boys and Girls Homes to make a difference in the lives of North Carolina's children.  Individual members and clubs provide financial support.  Club members enhance the children's experiences by providing occasional off-campus trips.
