FISHING

Charlton Lake Camp has access to over 1700 acres of connecting waters, (60' deep) in the surrounding lakes that are home to tackle busting northern pike, feisty small & large mouth bass, whitefish and a variety of pan fish.  All of which provide the ultimate test for any angler.

 

 

We will point out the best spots, supply you with a map, pass on some tips, and even let you in on a secret lure or two.  Don't hesitate to ask!!

The waterfront has a rental fleet consisting of Lund aluminum boats, a pontoon boat and Kevlar & Royalex canoes.

Our motors are Yamaha 9.9hp (more than enough to fill all your fishing and exploring needs).

We have an exceptional launch ramp, sturdy docking with built-in boat protectors and gasoline.  Our facilities are both pleasing and practical.

Our large swimming area has a sandy beach, lounge chairs, a roped-off swim area for youngsters and a large diving (or sunning) raft.

We carry bait, tackle & fishing licenses.
