Bass Fishing Adventures of a Lifetime in Texas ,
Mexico, Peacock Bass Fishing in Venezuela and the Amazon

 

 

In 25 years of bass fishing, professional bass angler Pat McCarty, has acquired a wealth of knowledge on outstanding bass fishing locations.  Through Bass Adventures he shares this experience with you.

From tangling with Lake Fork Lunker Largemouth Bass, in his home state of Texas, to battling Ferocious Peacock Bass in the jungles of South America he has fished hundreds of lakes and rivers for Largemouth Bass and Peacock Bass.

You can enjoy the advantage of this experience by booking your "Ultimate Bass Fishing Adventure" with Bass Adventures.

From this website you can get up-to-date, accurate information on the hottest new bass fishing destinations in Texas, Mexico, Venezuela and Brazil.  You can see photos of our lodges and lakes, read bass fishing reports and book your next trip.
