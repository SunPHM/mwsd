How do I install my Bass Blockers?

Bass Blockers are wired directly in-line (in series) with the positive speaker lead. In other words, you can just disconnect the positive speaker wire that is attached to the speaker, attach the Bass Blocker onto the end of it, then plug the other wire that comes from the Bass Blocker back onto your speaker. That's all there is to it!
