This page will contain information, pictures, movies and music of and about Boomer Box.
Boomer Box is a Manitou Springs based Classic "Baby Boomer" Band playing
cover tunes and original music from the 60's, 70's, 80's and more!

This site will also contain a special link for local musicians.
We will be providing a free "book" for bands looking for
players, backups, stand-ins, etc. and those who are looking FOR work.
No charge, no special setup...
All WE ask is that you keep it clean, "friendly" (no flames, put-downs, etc.), and honest...
The local music groups have always "cross-pollinated" with each other, helping out when needed - we are encouraging that!
