The "SP" Chugger and "SP" Shad features state-of-the-art, computer designed bodies, ultra-sharp EWG hooks, 100# PowerProline, and 14" stainless steel springs. Upon entering the water, the lures begin their lifelike struggling action while emitting a loud clicking sound that calls fish to the surface. The lures can be continuously re-activated from even the most distant casting range with a simple popping or chugging retrieve, producing irresistible audible clicking and body-shaking action with each activation.

The computer design provides a perfect balance and performance. Each lure undergoes a series of exacting tests throughout production to insure top quality and reliability.

At the heart of the SP Shad and the SP Chugger is a 14 inch stainless steel spring that can be continously wound with a simple popping or chugging action of the rodtip fromeven the most distant casting range. Each lure is tested and re-tested to provide a pre-set pulling force that allows easy activation but, at the same time, provides maximum power for lifelike struggling action and sound.

These SP fishing lures utilize 100 pound test braided Spectra line that has been specially treated to bind the line fibers and to reduce friction. A special non-slip knot secures the line to the internal components and to the external barrel swivel. Approximately a quarter inch of line is intentially left beyond the barrel swivel and should not be removed since it provides additional knot security.

The result of this technology is a rugged LIVE ACTION lure that will give you hundreds of hours of fishing enjoyment!
The new Green Dragon "SP" Chugger and the "SP" Shad fishing lures are the clear leaders in lure technology for the 21st Century!
