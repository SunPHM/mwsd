New From Fender - Standard Jazz Bass® V

July 10, 1998 -- The new, moderately-priced five string version of Fender's high-performance Standard Jazz Bass is now available in a poplar body; a one piece maple neck; pao ferro fretboard with 20 frets; two Standard Jazz Bass V single-coil pickups; three-ply white/black/white pickguard; vintage style bridge; and standard hardware. Comes factory equipped with genuine Fender strings for optimum tone and performance.

For more information, visit their web site at www.fender.com.
