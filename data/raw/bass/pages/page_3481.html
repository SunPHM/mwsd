We Specialize in Nearshore/Offshore Bottom Fishing

Fish with us on a full day of Offshore Fishing! Trolling and Bottom Fishing trips are available in Fall, Winter and Spring.

We fish from fast, open boats to get you to the fish QUICK! Our group offers over 20 years in offshore fishing experience in this area.

King Mackerel Fishing (Pictured above) can be done in the Spring and Fall for best results and can be caught in good numbers. This is a VERY LARGE King Mackerel that tipped the scales at nearly 60 pounds caught while fishing nearshore south of our area with Capt. Richie Lott.

Snapper, Grouper, Sea Bass and Triggerfish and other bottom fishing trips are available to Anglers willing to make the run Offshore. These fish are willing to bite almost anytime and catches can exceed 50 fish during a trip on a regular basis. On occasion, we encounter Amberjack, Porgy, Dolphin, or Pompano while Bottom Fishing on this type of trip.
