Parker Fly Bass 4 string Bass

This bass is a U.S. handbuilt Parker Fly Bass 4 string model that features an ultra-lightweight Spruce body (just under 7 lbs.) with Quilted Maple top and back wood. The 34" scale 24 fret 2 octave bolt-on neck is a Carbon Fiber type that has a Composite fingerboard with Stainless Steel frets and Sperzel locking tuners. The pickups are the DiMarzio "Ultra-Jazz" magnetic type with a 2-band eq. system and the bass also has a Fishman active "Piezo" bridge pickup that allows you to get a more acoustic type of tone from the bass.
