New Jersey Marine Life

Sea Raven

Anemones


Most Common: Frilled , Striped, Ghost, Smooth & Burrowing.
Filled is pictured & grows to 2ft

Hydroids


Pictured: Tubularian Hydroid

Jellyfish


Numerous species, common are; Comb jelly, Lions Mane, Moon.
Lions Mane is considered highly toxic and is largest jellyfish in world grows to 8ft

Blue Claw Crab - Callinectes sapidus


One of the commercial species of crab.
Commonly found in the waters here up to 120 feet deep.
Spindle shaped and grows to 12 inches. Grayish or blue-green coloring.

Clear Nosed Skate - Raja eglanteria


Grows to over 3feet and is wider than long.
Sides of snout are translucent. Tail is thorny entire length.
Common in warm inshore waters.

Monk Fish - Lophius americanus


Sometimes called headfish, all-mouths or anglerfish or Goosefish.
Grows to 50 pounds. Powerful jaws with razor sharp teeth up to a 1/2 inch long.

Black Sea Bass - Centropristis striata


Range: Maine to Gulf of Mexico. Temp: 50? Grows to 8lbs
Color: Silvery-Black, Blue Scales - spiny dorsal fin.
Free swimming or inside wreck/rocks

Blackfish / Tautog - Tautoga onitis


Range: Canada - North Carolina Temp: 32?-70? Grows to 25lbs
Color: Varies to Black, Grey,brown, red/yellow brown.
Usually blunt head large teeth. Can be found free swimming or inside wreck/rocks

Fluke - Paralichthys dentatus - Summer Flounder or Flounder


Range: Maine to Florida Temp: 35? to 84? Grows to 25lbs

Ling - Urophycis chuss or Red Hake


Range: Canada to North Carolina Temp: 33? to 45?
Grows to 6lbs Color: yellowish-brown, soft fleshy.
Usually found on bottom-sheltered in wreck or rock

Ocean Pout - Macrozoarces americanus ( eelpout )


Range: Canada to Delaware Temp: 32? to 70? Grows to 5lbs
Color: reddish brown, yellow, mottled.
Usually found inside wreck/rocks like lobsters

Bergals - Tautogolabrus adspersus or Cunner


Range: Canada to Delaware Temp: 32?-70? Grows to 3lbs.
The most common fish found in local waters and are free swimmers

Lobster - Homarus americanus


Range: Canada to Virginia Temp: 38? to 70? Grows to 40lbs

Dolphin


Most Common: Atlantic White-sided, Atlantic Spotted, Atlantic Striped and Bottle-nosed Range: Canada to Caribbean Grows to 8ft

Ocean Sunfish - Mola Mola


Range: Open Ocean-Atlantic to Pacific Grows to 15ft and 3300lbs
Color: Grey-blue, silvery Shark-like dorsal fins, eats small jellyfish

Turtle


Most Common: Leatherback, Loggerhead, Green Sea, Hawksbill Turtles
Sx can be determined by tail length. Most can stay submerged for
30 minutes or more and grow to 100 lbs, 3-5ft at age of up to 200yrs

Shark

Sand Tiger - Odontaspis tarus
Sandbar or Brown Shark - Carcharhinus plumbeus


Both can be found in along East Coast to Caribbean.
Grow to 300lbs
