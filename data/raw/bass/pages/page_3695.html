Living in Italy helped chef gain appreciation of Italian cuisine

Jill Gianoli ought to know Italian food.
      Not only is she of Italian descent. She lived in Italy three years, speaks the language fluently and worked for a time in the kitchen of La Strada, an Italian restaurant in Reno that has gained national recognition. Her long-term goal as a chef is to one day open a restaurant devoted to Mediterranean flavors.

COLUMN: TASTE OF THE TOWN: Michael Paskevich

Most people would credit Wolfgang Puck as the prototype celebrity chef who started the still-surging wave of upscale restaurants in Las Vegas with Spago in the Forum Shops at Caesars in 1992.
      Since then, Puck, his spouse and partner Barbara Lazaroff; managing partner Thomas Kaplan; and chef and partner David Robins have opened Wolfgang Puck Cafe inside the MGM Grand, 3799 Las Vegas Blvd. South, and Chinois, his Asian fusion cuisine restaurant not far from Spago in the Forum Shops, 3500 Las Vegas Blvd. South.
