LAKE COMEDERO MEXICO BASS FISHING


Mexico Bass Fishing for TROPHY Bass


Lake Comedero is a beautiful mountain lake nestled in the Sierra Madre Mountains 100 miles notheast of the seaside resort of Mazatlan. Its clear, cool water is teeming with thousands of hungry black bass which will strike almost any type of lure all year. Without any doubt, Comedero is the world's best bass lake for numbers of bass from 8 lbs. to 12 lbs. with some bass ranging from 12 to 15 lbs. Daily catches average 40 to 60 fish per day per boat. It is totally a catch and release lake. Comedero Bass Club began stocking Florida strain bass in the lake 10 years ago, and some of these bass now weigh 12 to 15 lbs.

Comedero Bass Club offers you Mexico bass fishing and Mexico bass fishing lodges at their finest. The thrill of the strike, a trophy size catch, Mexican cuisine, and great times with friends in the beauty of Mexico's majestic Sierra Madre Mountains.
