Legend Pathfinder

12/11 Hammered Dulcimer

 

A standard size Hammered Dulcimer with twelve double strung treble courses and eleven double strung bass courses for a total of thirty-five notes. This instrument plays in the major keys of "A", "D", "G", "C", and "F". The outer rails and bridges are made of Walnut. The pin blocks and frame are made of rock Maple. The  12/11-S  is finished with clear flat lacquer allowing the true colors of the woods to show. Circle of Hearts tone holes are standard.  Celtic knot may be added for and additional charge of $40.00. Dimensions: 37" x l3" x 2 1/2". Weight: 10 1/2 lbs., string spacing 1" - Click here for details.
