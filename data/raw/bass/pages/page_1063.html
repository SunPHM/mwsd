BASS CLASSES
Summer 2000 (July 5th - September 12th)

Beginning Bass
Mon 8.15 – 10.15 p.m.
This introductory class covers basic bass technique, notation and bass reading concepts. Contemporary style patterns and chord progressions are also introduced and applied to the bass.
Prerequisites: None.

Bass Grooves 1: Rock/Funk/R&B
Sat 12.30 – 2.30 p.m.
To offer a proper treatment of the rhythm-section text Essential Styles, we have planned the 30 'chapters' of the book over three classes. The focus of the Grooves 1 class will be to instruct players on how the bassist helps define and accomplish rock/funk/R&B/reggae styles. Emphasis will be placed on playing 'in the pocket' and locking up with the drummer, using the play-along CD provided with the text.
Prerequisites: Completion of the Beginning Bass class, or playing & reading ability comparable to advanced beginner.

Bass Grooves 2: Latin/Brazilian/Afro-Cuban
Sat 3 – 5 p.m.
This continuation in the exploration of styles may be taken concurrently with Bass Grooves 1. An intensive look into Latin styles, broken out into the Bossa Nova, Samba, Cha-Cha, Mambo, Songo and other identifiable 'feels', again all demonstrated on the Essential Styles CD included with the text.
Prerequisites: Completion of the Beginning Bass class, or playing & reading ability comparable to advanced beginner.

Bass Grooves 3: Jazz/Fusion/Swing
Sat 10 a.m. – 12 noon
A more technically sophisticated class, this section will progress from traditional jazz swing, through contemporary fusion and odd-meter vamps. Class will be working with altered scales and more complex progressions, again using the Essential Styles text and play-along CD.
Prerequisites: Completion of the Bass Grooves 1 and/or 2 classes, or equivalent intermediate bass playing experience.

Bass Reading & Technique
Mon 6 – 8 p.m.
This advanced beginner/intermediate class covers reading positions and techniques, use of different scales, musical phrasing, and application of contemporary harmonic concepts to the bass fretboard.
Prerequisites: Completion of the Beginning Bass class, or playing & reading ability comparable to advanced beginner.
