Mollie Stone's Seafood Depts. sells Wild Salmon -   No color added ever!!

Our fish buyers have a clear responsibility: Find out what's in season, where it's from-and sell it while it's fresh. Although we buy fish from all over the world, Mollie Stone's specializes in local fish, such as rock cod, ling cod, petrale sole, salmon and halibut.  In some cases, as with local King Chinook salmon, we even hire our own fishermen. With a few rare and unavoidable exceptions, every piece of fish we sell is caught and delivered fresh six days a week.

Local King Salmon  
The king (chinook) salmon season is the best in years. Plentiful amounts have brought the price down, and we will keep it down as long as it lasts. Many believe that our local salmon is the best in the world, so don't let this season go by without a double or triple dose of this fine fish with the high omega 3's.

Kauai Shrimp        
From the Hawaiian island of Kauai come these farm raised shrimp, which are one of the best tasting shrimp you will ever try. Firm, sweet, and a deep flavor that explodes in your mouth, these shrimp are all natural and are raised without the aid of antibiotics. Taste the difference.

Regarding Chilean Seabass  
 All Patagonian Toothfish (Chilean Seabass) sold at Mollie Stone's are leagally caught by authorized fisherman under the new international fishing regulations relating to the catch of this fish. No pirated or illegally obtained fish will be sold at Mollie Stone's. For a copy of the "boat of origin" paperwork regarding this catch, just ask our seafood departments.
