 Sea Kayaking in south east Australia and beyond...

sea kayak tuition, treking,  adventure...

and sea kayaks..

Meridian Kayak Adventures is committed to sustained environmental / ecotourism experiences. Sea kayaking really does let you reach out and touch nature, take your memories, take your photos and not even leave any footprints...

NO previous kayaking experience is necessary as all our trips start with a refresher tuition session in techniques and safety practices. 
Our fully trained and qualified guides will take care of all the details while you paddle through and discover the attractions of some of Australia's finest Maritime National Parks and wilderness areas of world class heritage value.
