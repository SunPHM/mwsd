To the Captain Clarke Charters website. We Offer a wide variety of Charter fishing excursions on the Island of Martha's Vineyard in Massachusetts.

Martha's Vineyard offers the best in Striped Bass, Bluefish, Bonito, False Albacore, Fluke and Sea Bass fishing.

Step aboard our 30-foot center console charter vessle named "Femme Fatale" where Captain Jennifer Clarke will be your guide to all that the amazing waters off Martha's Vineyard have to offer.

Jennifer is a seasoned fisherwoman with many tournament victories both locally and abroad. And is proud to be sponsored by Contender, Yamaha, Simrad and C-Map. Please take the time to browse our site where you will find informative articles, tournament calendars and a fishing journal of Captain Jennifer Clarkes adventures throughout the year.
