Halibut

 

 

If you have ever dreamed of catching big bottom fish that can weight 200-400 pounds, the waters off of Alaska are the next destination for you. Our charters run out of Sitka, Prince of Wales, and the Cook Inlet near Homer, AK. These are all professional fishing guide services with a long reputation for producing world class halibut fishing excursions. In many cases the cost of the halibut trip can include salmon as well for no additional expense. We book these trips for June through August. Packages will vary from $500 to $2500 depending on the package booked. In some cases we work with high quality lodges and remote destinations, in other cases we provide daily guide services only and fishermen can stay in local hotels or lodging facilities not included in the price of their package. These trips are totally customized to fit your needs. Please call our office today for more information on how you can book your next Alaskan fishing adventure.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 

 

Sturgeon 

 

 

Our Columbia River sturgeon trips provide the sporting fisherman with a world class experience. The best time to fish is between late May and throughout June during the shad run when it is not uncommon to catch multiple fish between 7' and 10' long. 11'-13' foot sturgeon are caught every year. We also have some spring & fall sturgeon and salmon combination trips. All of our packages are three days and four nights, all inclusive of meals, lodging, guides, boats, and fish cleaning for $1195.00. Please call today for additional details on how to book your custom fishing trip today.
