Did you know your garden or school Shed has musical potential?

The 'Shedstuff' Range consists of sound producing items that you install onto your shed. The items are designed in order that your shed becomes an integral functioning part of the instrument.

These are mail order self-install items that come with all fixings and instructions. You should ensure that your shed is in good condition

Make your shed into a giant tunable bass guitar with the Diddley Bow. (installation note) The bridges pictured left are temporary installations.

Adjustable to meet your desired pitch, this is a fun instrument for your garden with a fat sound that is easy on the neighbours.

This sounds like a double bass, you can feel the air move when you stand next to it. Touch the string lightly and there are beautiful harmonics, slide a hard object along the string to change the pitch.

Children love to investigate the Diddley bow, the vibration in a string of this scale is clearly visible and tangible. Show children the the light touch for harmonics and watch them discover the bells in the string.

Made throughout from marine stainless steel yachting supplies, this instrument will easily outlast the two year guarantee and most likely your shed, you and your children.
