The Guitar Hangar offers a variety of services to help keep your gear in tip top condition. Some of the services we offer include the following: Amp Repair, Stereo Equipment Repair, Pro Audio Repair and an on site Luthier.

Our Custom Shop Luthier Paul Von Schmidtis the absolute best in the area for making your guitar play like you want it to, look like you want it to, or anything in between. Paul is the guy doing your fret job, restoration or whatever your need; and he does it in a timely fashion! He has done custom work for Jose Feliciano, Dave Matthews, Sam Rivers of Limp Bizkit, Ian Hunter, Warwick basses, Todd Rundgren, Stevie Ray Vaughn, T.M. Stevens of Billy Joel & Joe Cocker, Mark Andies of Heart, etc. Got an old valuable vintage guitar that needs some TLC? Give us a call at 203 740 8889 for a quote or email us at sales@guitarhangar.com and we will get your favorite guitar on the road to recovery and playing like new!
