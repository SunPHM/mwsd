This Could Be You

After having their last bass player explode on stage, Armed to the Teeth is in search of a bass player. We have been playing out as a three piece, but L.D. and Cliff are getting tired of doubling on bass. If you live in the Southeast Michigan area, have the drive, talent, equipment, attitude, don't do drugs, and are realitively inflamable, you should look into joining the most kick ass band in Detroit. Do you have what it takes to be part of the Teeth?
