Bluegill Fishing Lake Okeechobee Report: May 2, 2004

Florida Bluegill & Mayan Cichlid Fishing - May 2, 2004
Fishing Report

Florida bass fishing anglers Todd Neldberg and his son Dane (Miami, FL - e-mail: tneldberg@aol.com) caught caught a total of 40 bluegills & mayan cichlids on live crickets during 1 half day of panfishing with Ron's Guide Service on May 2, 2004. Bluegills & cichlids were caught in the Twin Pond Canal area of Lake Okeechobee.
