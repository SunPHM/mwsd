Island Sea Bass




Ingredients:
1 1/2 pound sea bass fillet (halibut is a good substitute)
1 tablespoon each lemon, lime, and orange juice
Splash of Riesling or Sauvignon Blanc
1 teaspoon peanut oil
Splash sesame oil
1 teaspoon chopped garlic
1 teaspoon chopped fresh ginger
2 roma tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup diced red onion
1/2 cup diced papaya
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
Salt to taste
Mixed greens, for serving

Directions:
Combine all the ingredients except fish and mix lightly. Add fish and marinate for 1/2 to 1 hour.

Preheat coals. Make a boat-like oval bowl out of aluminum. Place fish in the boat and cover with marinade. Cook over medium coals with the barbecue lid on until fish flakes easily, about 12 to 15 minutes.

Place fish on a serving platter covered with mixed greens. Spoon cooked sauce from the boat over fish.
