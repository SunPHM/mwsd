Florida Bass Fishing
Reports: December 2000

I have put together some December 2000 Florida bass fishing reports of Florida bass that were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Florida bass fishing reports are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught on Lake Okeechobee and other information is listed with each Florida bass fishing report. To ensure validity the angler's name, address, phone number and e-mail address is also listed with every fishing report.

Check out our Florida Bass Fishing Special Rate listed below.

Florida Bass Fishing Report: December 20, 2000

Florida bass fisherman Steve Carter (Denver, NC - ph: 704-483-2430) caught and released about 10 bass up to 5 lbs during 1 day of wild shiner fishing on December 20, 2000. Bass were caught in the Harney Pond canal area of Lake Okeechobee.


Florida Bass Fishing Report: December 18 & 19, 2000

Florida bass fishing angler Joe Jordan (Columbus, OH - ph: 614-299-7236 - e-mail: FishnLegend@aol.com) who has a tackle and bait store in Columbus Ohio (ph: 614-261-0049) caught and released about 55 bass up to 6 1/2 lbs during 2 days of fishing with artificial lures on December 18 & 19, 2000. Bass were caught in the Harney Pond canal area of Lake Okeechobee.
