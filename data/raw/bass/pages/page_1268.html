Bass player Doug Ryan is the newest member of the band, as well as the newest resident of Vermont. Growing up on the coast of Maine, Doug started playing bass, saxophone, guitar, and piano in his early teens. His musical background is widely varied, and includesa degree in jazz and a four year stint in the Marine Band. After one too many John Phillip Sousa marches, Doug new it was high time to get back to his rock and roll roots. His first 3 years in Vermont were spent playing keyboards and sax in "The Naturals", before becoming the bass player for Sturcrazie.
