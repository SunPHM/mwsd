The Star 100.7 Supper Club
with Chef Steff

       Chef Steff has teamed together with John, J.R., and Kate (the hosts of the Star 100.7 Morning Show) to give listeners the opportunity to join them for dinner at a great Pittsburgh restaurant each month.  Three lucky listeners are selected to go with the gang to one of Chef Steff's restaurant picks, along with a few celebrities and famous Pittsburghers.  The special guests can include Steelers, Pirates, Penguins, local news anchors, radio personalities – you get the picture.  Each listener brings a guest of course, which helps to bring the head count to a dozen or so, give or take a few.  Everyone is there to have a good time, so it’s a great crowd to enjoy a meal with.  Listen every month for your chance to win dinner with Chef Steff and the Star 100.7 Supper Club!
