Marco Nolli

Marco Nolli is a Cremonese Master and winner of the gold medal at the 1988 Cremona International Competition. His instrument, a double bass, was aquired by the City of Cremona and is on perpetual exhibition at the "Stradivari Museum" in Cremona.

I personally met Marco a number of times and find his work expressive and bold.

His violins are mellow, powerful, project well and are played by well known soloists, worldwide.
Marco is a Member of the Cremona Violin Makers Consortium.
