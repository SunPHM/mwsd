Hard working volunteers of 2002

The Halibut Derby runs thanks to the perseverance
of these fine dedicated people.

MDRA members and others show up each year and give of themselves in a lot of ways.  Some big and some small.  All of the help combines to produce a really great event and to do a lot of really great things for the community around us.

The Halibut derbies help support the many fishing and boating related businesses in Southern California area.  Bait Docks, Boat Repair Shops, Tackle Stores, and so much more.  The money raised has a strong and positive impact on the lives of young kids in the community who have an opportunity to go out on the ocean to fish that they otherwise may never have.  The environment benefits as we give back with more baby white sea bass and soon many baby halibut as well.

These people make it happen.
