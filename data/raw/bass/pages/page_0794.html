~ The Lazy Bass Lakehome Retreat, Rice Lake Wisconsin ~


The owners have taken great care to decorate and anticipate your every need! The furnishings are brand new and the decor exudes the rustic feel ~ perfect for a get-away...
          
The Lazy Bass is situated on an acre lot with an expansive deck leading to the boat lift on Red Cedar Lake. The lake is approximately 1841 acres boasting bass, northern, walleye, bluegills and more! After a hard day of fishing you can enjoy the 4 person hot tub overlooking the lake, shoot some pool, play some darts, play pinball, watch some movie or just simply relax!

 The master suite has a walk-in closet and private bath. The king-size log bed is hand carved with luxurious linens. The two queen size pull-out couches in the lower level are very comfortable. There is an additional private bedroom with a hand carved queen sized bed on the lower level as well as a spacious bathroom. 

 
Talk with the owners about renting their new Tri-toon for some great water fun! Take advantage of Wisconsin snowmobile trails, ice fish, cross country ski, or better yet, bring your 4-wheelers, water skis, hiking shoes!

 Take in the stock car races at  Rice Lake Speedway about a 20 minute drive. Do some hiking or mountain bike riding as well as skiing and snowboarding at Christie MountainSki Area and Mt. Hardscrabble which are nearby as well.  For all you golf lovers, head over to Tagalongs on the lake with the boat or drive to Turtleback Golf and Recreation Center.
Wild Rivers State Trail and Pipestone Quarry offer hiking, biking and ATV trails for all levels just 10 miles east! So much to do so come and play!
