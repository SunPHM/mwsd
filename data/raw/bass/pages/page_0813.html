BASS FLIES

  Bass Flies- flys for flyfishing for Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Striped Bass, Pike and Musky.

Whether you are fly fishing for  Bass, Stripers , Pike or Musky, Dan's Custom Flys has a fly to fit your needs. We
will be updating this  Bass Fly section with many new, proven bass flies. Try the new "Dan's Jerk Worm Fly". It has
the same great fish catching action of a plastic jerk worm. The Neerly Live Bluegill is a great baitfish pattern made with
new synthetic materials that don't absorb water making it easy to cast, and it is very durable. Dan's Bass Flies are tied
with durably being a major factor. Dan's saltwater flies have passed the test put on by mean and nasty species, and conditions, the sea can throw at you. These fly tying techniques and materials, along with many years of Bass fly
fishing experience, help Dan to tie the best Bass flies you can find. If you have a favorite Bass fly  that you like to use,
or want your fly to be made a special way, Dan can tie them up custom for you!
