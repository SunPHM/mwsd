Florida Bass Fishing
Report: February 2000

I have put together some February 2000 Florida bass fishing reports of Florida bass that were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Florida bass fishing reports are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught on Lake Okeechobee and other information is listed with each Florida bass fishing report. To ensure validity the angler's name, address, phone number and e-mail address is also listed with every fishing report.

Check out our Florida Bass Fishing Special Rate listed below.

Florida Bass Fishing - February 28 & 29, 2000 Fishing Report

John Trudel (Cliffwood Beach, NJ - ph: 732-290-0147) and Alex Szabo (West Keansburg, NJ - ph: 732-787-0058) caught 19 Florida bass that included a 7 lb and 6 1/2 lb'er during 2 days (Feb. 28 & 29, 2000) of Florida wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. They fished the Moonshine area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
