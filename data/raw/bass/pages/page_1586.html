Metallica's Trujillo Lands On Cover Of Bass Guitar Magazine
by: Blabbermouth.Net on 03/09/2004

Bass Guitar magazine has done a major feature on Robert Trujillo of METALLICA in the current issue. Robert was interviewed by columnist and well-known bassist Nick Beggs (who works with LED ZEPPELIN's John Paul Jones). Trujillo reportedly discusses his rise to the top via SUICIDAL TENDENCIES, OZZY OSBOURNE, and the fathers of thrash metal themselves, METALLICA. Click here to see the cover of the current issue.
