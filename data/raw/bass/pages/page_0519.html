Our Bass is hand carved spruce and maple with ebony fingerboard and tail piece. The Bass is made by a private maker in China to our specifications. Comes with D'addario medium strings and adjustable bridge. Also includes Bag, Bow, and Pops Rosin.

Each bass has a custom made endpin assembly and made in our shop by Greg Smithson our String Specialist.

This is an excellent Instrument for the serious student or professional musician.
