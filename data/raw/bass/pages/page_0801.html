In this Web site you're going to find all the information you need to make informative decisions on where you ought to be fishing. Nobody wants to waste the little precious free time we have these days, and by utilizing this web site you'll know where & when to go fishing and what baits or plugs to use.

This web site contains all the information you'll need to know on "The Fishing Line" radio & TV shows, my new MAGAZINE, our Kid's Corner, host information, Event Calendars, my guide and seminar services as well as the best ways to advertise your service or product, whether it's fishing related or not!

You'll soon find this web site the best way to stay tuned for all the info you need, and best of all...it's FREE! So now there's no more need to spend your money,   at the newsstand and there's no print ink on your hands when you're done!
