Readers will learn about the differences in the tributaries, why some are much better than others for peacock bass fishing, and even some facts about the flora and fauna in the Basin. Each chapter is based on a specific experience and tactics at a location in the Basin or the Operation that handled the trip. Readers will learn tactics for "fry balls", "piranha bursts", post-rain, shallow and clear waters, "dolphin encounters", large lagoons, sandbar drops, walk-ins, "teener" schoolers, sight fishing, laydowns, storms, channels, irregular shorelines, topwater trolling, mid-lagoon haunts, "team" fishing, river bars and pools, "fly-outs" and flood stage waters. Readers will also learn about lure modifications, lure cadence and rhythm to make them the most effective. Information is included on the top bait colors and patterns, size selection of the lures, and many other tackle tips to increase reader productivity on their next trip to any peacock bass location. Many detailed illustrations and numerous photos highlight the most productive patterns. PF1 - Peacock Bass Explosions!
by Larry Larsen (ISBN 0-936513-35-7)

     Learn how to catch more and larger fish using the valuable information from the author and expert angler, an eight-time peacock bass world-record holder. The book includes Detailed tips, trip planning and tactics for the colorful peacocks in South Florida, Venezuela, Brazil, Panama, Colombia, Puerto Rico, Hawaii and other destinations.  This comprehensive book explores the most effective tactics to take the wild and aggressive peacock bass.  Whether fishing South Florida waters or venturing to the exotic Amazon jungle rivers, this book will show you how to catch more and larger peacock bass each trip.  You'll find out where and when to go, as some of the world's best peacock hot spots are explored in depth.  In Part 1, each chapter focuses on geographic areas in the United States, South America, Caribbean and Hawaii.  In Part 2 (six chapters) readers will discover numerous proven fishing techniques to try the next time they are on the water.

PF2 - Peacock Bass & Other Fierce Exotics
by Larry Larsen (ISBN 0-936513-33-0)

     Book 2 in the series discusses where, when and how to catch Latin America's most exciting freshwater fish.  Learn the top tactics to prepare you for the greatest experience of your life.  With stops in Peru, Colombia, Venezuela, Brazil and other countries, the author provides comprehensive information about the colorful monsters such as peacock bass, saber-tooth payara, rainbow bass, piranha, giant catfish and other exotics.  It is improbable that readers would not learn something from Larry's knowledge and experience with the peacock bass.  No other writers have fished as many areas and caught as many giant peacocks as Larry. He has caught hundreds of "teeners" from waters mentioned in this and his other two peacock books.  This reference source is invaluable to all anglers with a sense of adventure or yearning to battle the explosive peacock bass!

PF3 - Peacock Bass Addiction
by Larry Larsen (ISBN 0-936513-49-7)

     Book 3 in the series focuses on catching America's greatest gamefish, the peacock bass. It offers the latest tips on where, when and how to catch this exciting fish, including range/seasonal movements, fly fishing tips, battle tips and newly-discovered top locations in the world. Comprehensive tackle/equipment recommendations, and Larsen's secrets to finding the most productive habitats in the most productive lagoons for giant peacock bass are presented. Don't waste your valuable time learning on the water!  This book and the other two have all the information you need.
