Rock Hard (ATA) Guitar Cases are truly signature models of our case line, as each one bears the unique stamp of our case craft. Form-fitted 2" foam padding covered with rich velveteen fabric protects your valuable instrument, and a handy accessory compartment completes the interior.


Velveteen fabric colors include navy blue, black, maroon and red. Mix or match your guitar color with the interior and exterior case colors. Many players choose the key-locking twist latch option for added security.
