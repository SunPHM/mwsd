About "me and my music"...

I began to practice the keyboard in 1987, when I saw a keyboard pictured in a magazine. My parents allowed me to buy it, because it was not too expensive. It wasn't a very technical keyboard, but it was ok to play on in the beginning!


Here is my first keyboard (Casio), which I still have.

I started on a music school to learn playing the keyboard. It was by a danish teacher named L. Frandsen. I think it was a great education to learn how to manage the tangents by him. I bought a more advanced keyboard after a couple of years. It had many more tangents and more sounds to choose.


Playing on my second keyboard.

In 1994 I bought a really expensive keyboard. It was a Technics KN 2000, which I still play on today. This keyboard has given me so much, and I will not buy a new one, because I like it so much.


Practicing on the Tecnics keyboard

On the youth school I met Lars-Erik who has become a very good mate. He started to play guitar, and we practiced a lot together. In 1999 we got interested in Shadow music and played a lot together that lovely music!!


Lars-Erik and me plracticing

I ended to practice keyboard in 1995. Since then I have practiced only on my own.


This is a photo from my only gig on the keyboard. I played from 9 PM to 3 AM with no break!!

The guitar interest came with The Shadows, and I used my grandfathers acoustic Hagstrom guitar to practice on.


With the Hagstrom guitar

Summer of 2001 I bought a bass guitar, because Lars-Erik played guitar. Our dream was to make a Shadows tribute band!


Here with my Mexican Fender Jazz Bass

In January 2002 I visited a 60's music festival in a city called Bodø (in northern Norway). I was lucky and won a Fender American Standard strat in the lottery!! That was a dream! Included was a free performance next year, to show that I really had learnt to play on the strat!


The first day with my lefthanded strat :-)

Me and Lars-Erik practiced a lot this year! I feel that a day without practicing the guitar is a bad day!!


Practicing outdoors - Summer of 2002

In September 2002 I travelled whole the way to England, to visit Shadowmania. I had my guitar debut on the Friday Social Evening. That was a great experience before the gig in Bodø. I played three tunes from The Saunters backing CD "You Be Grompen"; "Domino", "Teen Scene" and "Saunters/Bongo Blues".


Friday Social Evening - Shadowmania 2002

In January 2003 it was time to show the audience of nothern Norway what I had learnt on my strat! I think it did very well. I got some great musicians to play with me, and we did great! And we had only practiced once together!!


Finally - on stage to play Shadow music!!!
