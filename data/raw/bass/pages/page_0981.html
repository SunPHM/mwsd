I've been a musican all of my life. At one time or another I've played the piano, clarinet, saxophone, bassoon and guitar.

My true love is the bass guitar, though. All the other instruments were merely flings. With the bass guitar, I've found my true calling. I've played the bass since 1985.

To the left is my main baby: a Tobias Toby PRO-5.

The Toby has active electronics and Bartolini soapbar pickups. This model features neck-through construction. For you who aren't guitarists, that means the neck goes all the way through the body of the bass and is integral to the construction, rather than being bolted on.

The body of this bass is made of flame maple. The neck is a laminate of two different woods, in five layers: wenge-maple-wenge-maple-wenge. The fingerboard is rosewood.

I use Dean Markley Blue Steel strings on my Tobias.
