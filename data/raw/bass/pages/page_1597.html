The Melancon Forum Image Gallery

         The Image Gallery is a pictorial record of the wonderful guitars made by Gerard Melancon and owned by Forum members. If you would like your guitar to be included on the Gallery, please email Brian Scherzer with your image and your name. If you have a paper image, email and you will be given an address to send it to. Always include your name, year of manufacture/purchase, serial number, and any details you'd like listed. If you can, send the images as jpegs or gifs. We love our Melancons!
