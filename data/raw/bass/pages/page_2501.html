Bass Fishing!

Tuesday, 12 June  2001 

ABC Radio's Sheik of the Creek Jim Kennedy goes bass fishing at Monduran dam. Find out more about fishing in Queensland, ABC local radio Friday 5.45 pm.

Monduran Dam is close to Gin Gin, west of Bundaberg in Queensland. Barramundi and bass have been stocked here by a local group for some time, and the rewards are starting to come. While Jim Kennedy caught no barra, he did manage to catch a few of the local bass, with the help of local John Finlay.

Jim's Bass.

ABC Radio's Sheik of the Creek Jim Kennedy shows off one of Qld's most prized angling catches, a small Australian bass caught at Monduran Dam near Gin Gin. Anglers are reporting better catches of bigger fish at Monduran every week.



Beautiful Bass.

This is typical of the small but beautifully marked bass to be caught in numbers at Monduran Dam. Jim Kennedy talks about how to catch them on ABC local radio, 5.45 pm Friday 15/6/01



John Fishing

Gin Gin local John Finlay relaxes in his tinny while trolling lures for bass on Monduran Dam.
