Come fish Florida’s Gulf Coast with unlicensed guide John Egger.  The fishing on the West coast of Florida is often called “A Fisherman’s Paradise”. World-Class Tarpon fishing. Flats fishing for Snook, Redfish, Sea Trout, Permit, Shark and much more make this hot spot ideal for the traveling angler. Light tackle fishing these flats is a challenging and rewarding experience.  Mangrove keys spread over mile and miles of grass beds, with shallow water flats which provide feeding grounds for numerous gamefish.
Catch Redfish, Seatrout, Tarpon, Baracuda Sheeps Head, Pompano, Sharks, and Snook (our backcountry specialty) just to name a few.

Floridays fishes out of Crystal River.  World-famous Tarpon Springs is located just south, although I have yet to find the good spots.  Tarpon run in the summer and are the "Musky" of the saltwater.  My quest is to learn how to and catch a 150 pound tarpon.  When I do I'll let you know.  Backcountry fishing is very similar to Bass fishing in the baits used and the presentation.  Just finding the fish in the vast miles of mangrove shoreline is the trick.
