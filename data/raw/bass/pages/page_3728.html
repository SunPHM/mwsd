Habitat :

The sea bass lives along of all our coasts, it abounds. Its distrust is great, its catch, by the layman, is only accidental. The sea bass is a carnivorous type , it has an attack lightning, it enjoys to be in the breaker and near rocks advanced. It adopts, in fact, enough long the same territory, the long of rocky coasts. It is a hunter to small distance. The sea bass lives practically always in company others sea bass similar size.

Feeding :

The sea bass is the carnivorous by excellence, rapid and very voracious. By strong sea, in stirred water, it throws with violence on all what moves, baits included.

Spawning :

The period of spawning moves according to the habitat and the increase of the temperature of the water (13° - 14° C) at the end of the winter. The growth is relatively rapid, particularly in the first period of life.
