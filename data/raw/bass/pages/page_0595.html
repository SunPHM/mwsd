Progress Series III Anniversary


Originally introduced to celebrate our 20th anniversary in 1999, this model represents the definitive example of our craft. Because of its exclusive nature, the Anniversary is only built to order.

Essentially the laminations are the same as our Elite models, but only the most stunning pieces of timber are incorporated with solid silver inlays and logo, engraved trussrod cover and silver plated hardware. Every example is individually numbered and signed by Chris May.
