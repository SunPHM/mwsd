Team Honky Tonk



On Bass Guitar is David Mack. At the age of 4, David started singing in his church choir. He took up clarinet in the 3rd grade and attended the University of Albuquerque on a music scholarship. David has played with musicians such as Bo Diddely, Willie Nelson, Mose Alison, Herb Ellis and Carmine Appice. Besides his full-time position with THT, David also sings with the L.A. City Choral.



David Mack



And on Lead Guitar and Vocals we have Bob Heft. This is very easy to say, Bob Heft is one of the finest lead guitar players, not only in the L.A. area but anywhere. He has endorsements with many of the best known equipment manufacturers simply because he is so damn good. Bob does about 200 gigs a year with Team Honky Tonk, and all the guitar players want to talk to him. What a monster...



Robert Heft

 




Now about our secret weapon. On drums Milo Tedesco. We found Milo in the Recycler. Unbelievable. He never played country music before in his life. He had heard of Hank Williams, but had no idea who Garth, Clint or Waylon were. Milo’s background was in punk, alternative, and Latin music. After playing with THT for five years, he has become one of the most sought after country players in Los Angeles.
