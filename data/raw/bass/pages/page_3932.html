Grey and Blue Whales, Dolphin and Pilot Whale sightings

The Blue Whale is an awesome sight to see.  It is that time of year when they are on their annual migration.  They are the biggest mammals in the world.  There are also dolphin trips with sightings of hundreds in each pod, jumping, feeding and playing right next to the boat. We are also having pilot whale sightings.  This is the time of year to charter your boat with sailcatalina.com and see the annual migrations.

The 05 fishing season is off and swimming 

The fishing season is off to a good with local Catalina fishing for Sea Bass and, Calico and Halibut and the potential of Albacore out of san Diego and a few bluefin in the mix. Boats are fishing as close as the 425 and as far as 180 miles south. Boats out of Newport are making the trip to the 43, pyramid ridge and the mushroom but are not seeing the San Diego numbers. Some yellowtail on patties south. The usual trolling colors are black and purple whatevers and mex flags aren't bad either. Also in the mix are barracuda with some yellowtail being taken off of long point and east end and at moonstone. Mostly biting surface iron and live bait. Again jig fish are the big count. Contact sailcatalina.com for your fishing adventure. Good luck.
