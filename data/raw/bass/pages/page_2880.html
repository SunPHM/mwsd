We have many comforts of home including fully enclosed bathroom, Sun deck for mom if she wants to relax, swimming platform for the children, a large cabin to get out of the weather and take a break from the sun or cold. Of course we have all the sport fishing equipment to make your trip a rewarding experience and we even supply the bait , side planners, down riggers, different styles of line, as we fish for Salmon, Lake Trout, Pike and Bass.

Safety is assured while you fish since the boat is NYS inspected, U.S. Coast Guard approved with an Licensed captain. I also have a NYS Guide License. A VHF radio and Telephone are also on board.

I personally have 38 years experience of boating and fishing on Lake George. I was raised on the water since my family own and operate a marina in Bolton Landing. I have been doing fishing guides for many years now , taking many charters out from the Sagamore Hotel in Bolton Landing. Children are especially welcome and I can provide lessons in baiting, casting and safety. We can also break up the day with a scenic cruise, swimming and picnic at one of the beautiful islands.
