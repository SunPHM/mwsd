"Reel" fun year 'round...

Table Rock Lake is one of the finest bass fishing lakes in the nation. Largemouth, smallmouth and Kentucky bass are abundant, as well as crappie, bluegill and catfish. Fishing on Table Rock Lake in the fall and winter is great... and yes Artilla Cove Resort is open all year -- Table Rock Lake never freezes!!!

Whether you are a beginner or a pro, you will find that fishing Table Rock Lake is "reely" a memorable experience.
Bring your own boat and motor and moor it at our dock with plenty of parking space for your trailer - or ask about our rental boats and reserve one in advance.

Your MISSOURI FISHING LICENSE may be purchased right at our resort.

Not familiar with Table Rock Lake? Let us "hook" you up with our guide for a day. He will teach you the techniques and show you the hot spots -- and you will be ready to catch fish on your own for the rest of your stay.  Check out our Fishing Packages!
