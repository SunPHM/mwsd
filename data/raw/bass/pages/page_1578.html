Welcome to Folk of the Wood's QuickTime Video Samplers
Here you will find sample video clips from most of our instrument product line. The videos are in QuickTime format and vary in length from 30 seconds to 5 minutes. Download time will depend on the length of the video, your computers processing speed, your modem hookup speed, and which version of QuickTime you have installed. Please allow the page to completely load and the video will play automatically. Double-click the image for repeated playback.

We are continuously updating our video samplers, so be sure to bookmark this page and visit us often. If you would like to place a request for a video of an instrument, have any questions or comments, or would like to place an order, please e-mail us at info@folkofthewood.com
IMPORTANT - PLEASE READ BEFORE DOWNLOADING!
It is important that your computer has the latest version of QuickTime installed with the appropriate extensions and browser plug-ins. If you see a "broken" image before download, cancel the download and confirm that you have the latest update for QuickTime. Click the below link for the latest QuickTime Player. NOTE: Missing audio is usually caused by having an out-of-date QuickTime version installed.
For the latest QuickTime updates for both Macintosh and IBM/PC platforms, visit http://www.apple.com/quicktime/download/
