FLORIDA BASS FISHING BRAG'N BOARD: MARCH 1999

Florida bass fishing during March has really been excellent. The mild Florida weather produced a good largemouth bass spawn. Florida bass have been boated in good numbers by our wild shiner bass fishing guide parties throughtout the month. Large numbers of 3 - 5 pound bass have been caught and released on a daily basis. Big Florida trophy bass as the below pictures and reports note have really been caught in good numbers on wild shiners by our guide parties. Bass are bedding all over the lake. There are beds with bass on them in just about every open water area on the North Shore. Shooling bass are starting to bust shad in the open water. Seeing lots of bass chasing minnows along the weed edges just about everywhere on the lake. The lowering of the lake water now and throughtout the year will greatly improve the bass fishing this year spring and summer.
