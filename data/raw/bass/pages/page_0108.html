With over 28 acres of world class bass fishing at your doorstep the odds are definitely in your favor to catch that trophy bass of a lifetime here at Wright Outfitters. We along with Southeastern Pond Management have set our sights on having the ultimate lunker lake by doing everything possible to create the best environment for our aquatic friends to thrive and prosper.

We have a liming and fertilization program in place that we implemented shortly after acquiring the property in 1995. This includes monitoring pH levels to assure we are getting peak performance from fertilization to ensure that the food chain is started at an exceptional level. We also introduced threadfin shad to help compliment the bluegill and bream population to provide plenty of forage for those lunker bass. With the help and information collected by Southeastern Pond Management electrofishing evaluations we are always trying to improve the quality of our fishery in any way possible.

Structure and aquatic vegetation placement are only some of the ongoing projects we are doing to provide you with a world class fishing lake that is second to none.

All large (over 15") will be released after being weighed, measured and photographed. This will allow you to have a fiberglass replica made of your trophy while giving someone else the chance to experience the thrill again.

At times we will require all bass 15" and less be kept and taken out of the lake for management purposes. The persons that caught them can keep these fish. Fish cleaning and preparation for transporting can be obtained for a nominal fee.
