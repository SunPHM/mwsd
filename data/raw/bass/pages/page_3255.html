Steamed Sea Bass with Black Bean Garlic Sauce
August 05, 2003
Ingredients:
1 1/4 lbs (625g) sea bass fillets (about 1 1/2" or 4cm thick)
3 tbsp (45mL) black bean garlic sauce
1 small piece fresh ginger (about 1/2" or 1cm long) julienned
1 green onion, cut into 2" (5cm) pieces
1/2 tsp (2mL) peanut oil
fresh cilantro

Directions
  1. Combine black bean, ginger and peanut oil.
  2. On a rectangular piece of aluminum foil, place the fish on one half.
  3. Pour the black bean mixture over top and sprinkle the cilantro.
  4. Fold the other half of the foil over the fish and make a packet, firmly sealing the edges.
  5. Place on the BBQ and cook for 12 minutes or until fish flakes when tested with a fork.
Preparation time: 5 minutes
Cooking time: 12-15 minutes
Serves: 4
Low Fat and Extra fast recipe

Rating: 4.5

Notes:
Recipe from "Six O'Clock Solutions" said to place a steam rack in a wok or large frypan. Fill the wok with water to about 1" (2.5cm) below the rack; cover and bring water to a boil. You place the fish in a pie plate and spread the black bean garlic sauce over the top and sides. Top with ginger and green onion; drizzle with oil. Cover and steam on the rack in the wok for 12 minutes.

You can also cook it on the BBQ in a tinfoil dish with a second dish over top which is how my Mom does it.
