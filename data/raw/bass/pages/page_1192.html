ABOUT BIGfest 2000
The Ibanez contest was part of the celebrations surrounding International Guitar Month and the Brisbane International Guitar Festival or BIGfest as we like to call it. BigFest 2000 was a 100%, total guitar indulgence day. It inspired, excited and enthralled everyone who attended. It was an all day event with workshops, master classes, guitar displays (new, unusual, old and vintage), guitar teachers, guitar builders, the latest technology guitar software and music organisations. The whole day concluded with a huge guitar concert that tore the roof off the venue. It was well worth checking out!
