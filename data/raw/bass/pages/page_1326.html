About Mike Mann and the NightRiders

Black Country Western Music? Afraid not. Black Cowboy Music? Yeah, that's what the NightRiders are about. "Cowboy Soul"!

Known as "America's Premier Black Cowboy Band", Mike Mann and the NightRiders perform a unique new style of music called "Cowboy Soul", an exciting mixture of Country Western and Contemporary Pop, fused with Soulful harmonies. If you like The Eagles, Lionel Richie, Al Green and Clint Black, then "Cowboy Soul" will stir your soul.

The first response to The NightRiders is usually something positive like, "Hey, a Black Country Music band . . . that's different." The next response is always, "Hey, these guys are good! And the music is jamming!"

Cowboy Soul? What's Cowboy Soul?

Cowboy Soul is a totally new genre of music. How can we tell? Because people who really know Country Western say "that's not country"; but people who don't really know Country say... "Hey, that's Country music." Mike Mann and the NightRiders have performed at numerous events from live concerts, to private parties, historical events, festivals and rodeos (thanks to their new affiliation with StarWest International Talent Agency) even Las Vegas.

From L.A. to Sacramento, from young to old, people are rockin' to the Cowboy Soul of Mike Mann and the NightRiders.
