FLORIDA BASS FISHING GUIDE:
Lake Okeechobee USCG Approved Captain

Fishing with a Florida USCG approved licensed guide for trophy bass on Lake Okeechobee is an experience you will not forget.

This 8 lb. Florida trophy bass was caught by John Kennedy (Roslindale, MA - ph: 617-323-4294). John and fishing buddy Stan Felton (Canton, MA - ph: 781-828-1635) caught only 76 bass during 5 days of wild shiner fishing due to hurricane Mitch's high wind and rain conditions. Some nice bass however were caught and released which included an 8 lb., 7 1/2 lb., 6 lb. and eight bass that went from 5 - 5 1/2 lbs. during the 5 days (Nov. 2 - 6, 1998) they fished with Cap’n Ron. This trip was their tenth year of wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee with Cap’n Ron. Stan caught the 6 lb’er while John caught the 8 and 7 1/2 lb’er. All the bass were caught in the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee.
