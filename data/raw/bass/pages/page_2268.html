Bass Guitarist

Russell Carr is a versatile bassist with over ten years of experience to draw on. He has worked on projects in the UK and Europe covering most modern styles.

Russell has worked with musicians and producers whose credits include artists as diverse as Sugababes, Feeder, Skin and Nitin Sawhney.

He has worked at some of London's leading live venues including Shepherds Bush Empire and The Forum; studios worked at include Sphere Studios, Battersea and NG Productions, Paris. Russell has also backed guest artists for Line6.
