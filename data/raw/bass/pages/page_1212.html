:: About ::

Who?
Jazz Band Bookings is based in Leeds & Skipton, Yorkshire and is a project developed and run by Oliver Hammond and Oskar Smith of Orchestrand Limited. Aside from offering bookings for jazz bands, Orchestrand also deals with web site design and promotion, music and label management and businesss services.

Jazz Band Bookings also has a sister site, Function Band Bookings which deals with bookings for Function Bands for weddings, parties and corporate events.


The Bands
Bands that we book for events are all hand picked by us and we are confident that you will love them! We look for feedback from our events to make sure that our jazz bands are always providing the best music and service that they can.
