JUST FOR YOU: BILL & JANET!

Florida Bass Fishing Trip August 30 & 31, 2002
4 1/2 lb'er

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Bill Hart and Janet Durrum (Lexington, KY - e-mail: janetdurrum@hotmail.com) caught 41 bass that included a 4 1/2 lb., 4 1/4 lb., 4 lb. and 3 1/2 lb. during 2 days of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron on August 30 & 31, 2002. All bass were released. Bass were caught in the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee.

JUST FOR YOU: SCOTT & CHARLOTTE!

Florida Bass Fishing Trip June 11 & 12, 2002
5 1/2 lb. & 5 lb'er

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Scott and Charlotte Pitts (Greenwood, IN - ph: 317-535-3088 - e-mail: scott@greenwoodchristian.com) caught 10 bass that included a 5 1/2 lb, 5 lb. and 4 1/2 lb'er during 2 half days of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron on June 11 & 12, 2002. Scott caught the 5 1/2 lb'er. Charlotte got the 5 and 4 1/2 lb'er. All bass were released. Bass were caught in Harney Pond Canal and the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

Linda with a 10 lb. channel catfish
