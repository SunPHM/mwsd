LICENSED GUIDE

 

 

Greg Crafts has been bass fishing Toledo Bend for over twenty years and is a full-time guide.  

He operates Toledo Bend Guide Service and the Lake Cottage.

He lives on the north end of the lake and operates out of Huxley Bay Marina. Fishing for black bass, white bass, crappie, bream or catfish, he’ll accommodate you in a new 20ft. ProCraft boat and has all the necessary equipment you may need. Huxley Bay is located on Toledo Bend Reservoir, 65 miles south of Shreveport, Louisiana and 20 miles west of Center, Texas which has an airstrip for private jets.

Greg has complete fishing packages available for small to large groups, from moderate to luxury accommodations. Also available are guided duck hunts in season and guided quail hunts from September through May.

For more information on bass fishing at Toledo Bend, please contact Greg at gcrafts@bigfoot.com or call him at (936) 368-7151
