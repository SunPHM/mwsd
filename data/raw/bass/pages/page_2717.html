The Fishing Across America tv show concentrates on examining the latest techniques in both fresh and salt-water fishing. Our half hour television show which airs weekly on The Outdoor Channel is dedicated to bringing you great fishing throughout America and all over the world.

Join Dave Maynard each week when Fishing Across America brings you the best in fishing adventures from around the globe. The multi species format, latest techniques and newest locations keep even the most avid angler updated and informed. Watch this week for another exciting episode.
