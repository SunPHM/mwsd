Music

I have been involved in a number of musical projects over the years which have included excursions in the pop music culture resulting in Gangbyeon Gayo Jae '92, Shi-Shil Lee,Cho Yong Kwan, Page 2 Project and "Net"  available on CD. Visit "Net's" related homepages and links here and here and look for an Eng;ish album review here and here. My instrument of choice is the electric bass guitar.  I have also have ample experience in MIDI, synthesizer programming, sound design, synthesis, studio bass guitar playing, producing and arranging.  My current concentration is in the field of of electro-acoustic and computer music which has been heard in numerous concerts, public radio, internet broadcasts and various conferences, festivals and venues including SICMF 2001/2000 (Seoul), NWEAMO 2001(Oregon), ISMEAM 2001 (Hungary), Engine 27(NYC), SEAMUS 2002/2001/2000, CEAIT 2002/2000 (USA), MAXIS 2002 (England), MATA 2002, Princeton, Dartmouth and Wesleyan University.
