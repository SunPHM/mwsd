Fly Fishing America - South Florida
Fly Fishing America airs Saturday mornings at 10 a.m. (Eastern Time) on ESPN2. Here's what's on tap for the next show:

June 2 - South Florida

Some people go to south Florida to hang out at the beach or fish in the ocean but after this week's episode of Fly Fishing America, trends are sure to change.

Host Chad Foster finds an incredibly unique fishery in the canals that criss-cross the tip of Florida. It's the ultimate "urban angler" experience for Chad and the crew as they pursue Peacock Bass on a fly.

This acrobatic hard-hitting bass is a South American transplant that has taken quite well to the tropical climate in South Florida and now provides an exciting challenge for fly fishing anglers. But get ready, this is not your typical fishing hole!!

Guide - Alan Zaremba
