July 6, 2002
Smallies in PA
 What better way to spend the holiday weekend than with a trip to the river for some old school river fishin'.
River fishing the Juniata River is to fishing what Nascar is to racing.  I'm a fan of both so I can say that, right?
This was Kelly's first time to the river so she was happy.  Sean showed off his physique and his computer programmer's tan.  He always seems to catch the most and the biggest fishies for some reason.

 July 27, 2002
Stripers on the Cape
 Matt and I travel to the mouth of Old Harbor to rangle some schoolies.  No keepers this night but we lost count of the little ones.  God bless the falling tide.  This was Matt's first time going after the mighty, mighty sea bass so he was very happy.

 August 9, 2002
More stripers on the Cape
 Matt and I travel back to Springhill Beach to continue our war on dog-day schoolies.  Low tide is 6:15 PM this night and we learn again that one hour before low is still the best time for this locale.  No pix this trip but I did snag a shot of Matt and Kristen's child Scooter.
