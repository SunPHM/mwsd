George Westbay

Guitar, Bass

George Westbay is a Berklee College of Music Graduate with a degree in Music Composition. He studied with Jim Kelly and Brett Wilmott and received awards and scholarships for his compositions. Jeff Gottwald

Guitar, Bass

Jeff received his Bachelor and Master of Science degrees in Music Education from the Pennsylvania State University; his major instrument of study was guitar. He has taught guitar, as well as elementary and middle school band for more than sixteen years in the public schools and is a certified music teacher in the state of New Hampshire. Jeff has studied guitar with George Allen and Miguel Abloniz at Ithaca School of Music in New York. Performance includes professional rock and jazz bands playing lead and rhythm guitar. Jeff developed and implemented guitar classes at the middle school level to introduce the guitar to all students. In addition, he has taught guitar privately throughout his professional career.
