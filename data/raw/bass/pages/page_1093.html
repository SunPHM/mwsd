Fender 5 String Jazz Bass.
Case style: Extruded Case™.
Case specifications:
Exterior panels: 9mm plywood and black ABS laminate.
Extrusions: 45mm lid and 80mm base sections.
Corners: 45mm lid and 80mm base knuckle corners.
Catches: Small key lockable recessed catches.
Handles: Solid leather handle.
Hinges: External strut hinges and piano hinge.
Fasteners: Pop rivets (150mm centres).
Case linings: 30mm polyurethane foam in walls of case, 25mm polyurethane foam in lid, 15mm polyurethane foam in base and polyurethane foam neck block.
