March Bassin on Reelfoot

 They say a picture is worth a thousand words. So, to save myself some writing, here is a picture of some Bass caught last week.

 Guide Mark Pierce of Cypress Point Resort and a customer from Indiana caught these fish March 28th in the Upper Blue Basin area. They ranged from 2 to 4.75 pounds and were caught in 2-4 feet of water on Blue Crankbaits, June Bug lizards and Pumpkin Seed Jig and Pigs.

 The bass fishing on Reelfoot is really heating up!
