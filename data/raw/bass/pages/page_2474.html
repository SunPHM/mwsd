Name: Lyndsey Lewellen

Instrument: Lead Vocals


I was born in 1983, in the rainy town of Columbus, Ohio, I grew up singing. I was inspired by the powerful vocals of Carol King, Pat Benatar, Joan Jett, and of course my mother. In my sophmore year of high school, my family and I moved to Plano, Tx. On my first day, in my first period class, I met one of my best friends Lauren Scott. Lauren introduced me to a small church that was just starting to grow. This church was great! I could come as myself, and they were using electric guitars and durms to praise God. It was there that I met my fiance, John Lewellen. I became greatly moved by the power of God in Christian rock bands. My brother, Austin Scott (the previous drummer whom we all Love and miss), and I decided to start a band of our own. Later on John joined us, and Follow the Leader was born. My other great enjoyment in life is drawing for comic books.

Name: Eric Welsh

Instrument: Lead Guitar and Vocals

Ever since I was a lil’ kid I wanted to play the guitar. When my parents Finally bought me one I spent every day of ...the rest of my life… playing it. Right now, I spend a lot of time working hard with the band and trying to get to know God more closely. I enjoy playing computer games such as Counter-Strike and Starcraft. Lately, all my close friends outside of the band have been moving away. At first I was feeling lonely and depressed, but then God made me see new things in my life that I had never seen before. I’ve had  more time  to work with the band, and most importantly, I’ve had more time for my quiet time with God. I’ve finally found  where I think God wants me to be. I’m finally doing what something inside me has always told me I should do. I love the ministry that I believe God has called me to.
