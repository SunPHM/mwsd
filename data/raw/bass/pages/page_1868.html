Back in the late 70's, the term "Dive Bomb" became part of the electric guitarist's lingo, thanks to an invention by a man named Floyd Rose.
He developed a system that allowed you to secure your strings at the bridge and nut to prevent them from slipping out of tune.
The resulting effect? Seriously aggressive tremolo. Pull up or drop down, and you're still in tune!
Fender offers 3 versions of the Floyd Rose Tremolo Assembly, one of which is just right for you.
All mounting hardware and installation instructions are included.
Available in Chrome, Gold, and Black.
Note: Alterations to your instrument may be required. Locking nut sold separately due to the large variety of nut widths.
