1st annual Memorial Day Picnic


1st annual Memorial Day picnic held one week late due to bad weather. Menu included Brats, Wings, Ribs, Sea Bass, Potato Salad, Baked Beans, Chips and to top it all of Apple Turnovers. Due to events at the park we were unable to use our usual spot but that did not stop us. We moved to the West pavillion and set up camp there.
