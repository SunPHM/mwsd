PAUL MILLER

Paul Miller.. Cloud Nine's Bass guitarist, also plays guitar, keyboard, drums, zither and a few other devices that we can't mention here.He has worked most of the top venues around the UK including the world famous 'Bermuda Club' at Hemsby. Paul also has a keen nose for a curry and likes nothing better than a 'Canaries Pizza & Kebab' (Catton Grove Rd, Norwich)

"Great Musician & ladies man" Showman Weekly.
"A real tradesman if ever there was one" Tradesman Journal.
