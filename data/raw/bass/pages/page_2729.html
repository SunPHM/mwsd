West Valley Bassmasters
B.A.S.S. Federation Bass Fishing Club

The West Valley Bassmasters bass fishing club is a group of fun loving, adventure seeking bass fishermen covering all of southern California's bass fishing waters from L.A. county to Modesto. We fish many of the big bass waters such as Casitas, Castaic and Perris. In the winter months we fish central California for spotted and smallmouth bass taking advantage of their cool water feeding habits. We also plan at least one long range trip a year to beautiful Lake Mead or to fish for the wild bass swimming in the vast river maze called the Delta in Stockton. Each Club Fun Fish is a combination of a learning experience and mini-vacation.

West Valley Bassmasters is a fully sanctioned B.A.S.S. Federation Chapter Member as well as a member of the California B.A.S.S. Chapter Federation.

Our members range from current touring pros & amateurs, retired B.A.S.S. (Bass Anglers Sportsmen's Society) champions and aspiring new anglers looking for more information and techniques to improve their bass catching abilities.

Come Join Us at 7:00pm on the 2nd Wednesday of each month at Lamplighter Restaurant - 9110 De Soto Ave, Chatsworth, CA 91311. Each month we have a raffle for fishing tackle, lots of fish talk and plaques are given out to the top 3 anglers of the previous month's fun fish.

Don't be shy, drop on by, everyone is welcome. We are currently accepting applications for membership to our bass fishing club from both boaters and non boaters and you could possibly be bass fishing with us that third weekend of the same month you attend a meeting.

If this interests you and you would like more info on our bass club drop us an e-mail at ClubInfo@westvalleybassmasters.com or come by the Lamplighter Restaurant on the second Wednesday of the month as described above!!!
