Label Grading Scale

MT - Mint, clean pristine condition. Perfect

EX - Excellent only minor flaws

VG - Very good. Light stains, margin crease and or small tears

G - Multiple tears, watermarks, soiling etc.

Abbreviations:

ts - top sheet. A label piece of paper placed on top of cigars to protect them from the lid.

tw - top wrap. A piece of paper placed on cardboard box to give the appearance of wood.
