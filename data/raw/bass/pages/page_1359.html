BASS WEEKEND with Neil Harland

   email me if interested in a bass weekend on the 7th of April 2006

Neil Harland Biography

  
This weekend is about learning to play Bass Guitar, it's designed to improve your playing no matter what level you are currently at. 
It will show you the route to improving your bass playing ability by planned practice, 
How to construct and play good bass grooves and bass rhythms. 
Neil will also show you some great techniques in bass playing but the end product must be the ability to produce a good rhythm related to the music being played. 
Learn to Develop your own bass lines.

How to book a weekend 
We plan to get you playing the bass, although we will cover some theory. 
It will be what’s required to enable you to play through progressions, 
recognize key and time signatures and to make a start at reading the dots. 
But ultimately we want the Bass weekend to be fun.
