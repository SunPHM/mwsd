Missouri Deer & Turkey Bowhunting and Bass Fishing

Eveready Outdoors & Pro Line Outfitters offers private estate whitetail Deer, Turkey, rabbit and dove hunting.We have several hundred acres of managed land with many food plots and lakes and streams and timber. There is a lot of trophy potential with several bucks taken in past three years scoring up to 170's. It seems to get better every year. Extra doe tags available at my shop. Limit four hunters on each area. Semi guided, with free lunch. RV area, and camping, Motels can be arranged with notice.

Shore fishing from private lakes in between hunts filled with crappie and bluegill. Or can combine hunting and fishing trip for trophy bass (some over ten lbs.) catfish, crappie etc. Page two-- Private estate trophy bass fishing. 6-8lbs. common, some over ten. Crappie average 12 in., many up to 17 in., hybrid bluegill up to 2lbs.- We have many 100 plus fish days in a series of small lakes and old strip pits. They have been managed for years and cross stocked with Florida bass. All bass over 15' must be released except you may keep one per day with a trophy fee. That keeps it great for everyone that comes. Also Mo. river giant catfish trips avail. up to 100 lbs. which was caught and released. Motels available, RV sight, camping-- guided or unguided. This is without a doubt the best fishing I have seen anywhere, and I have fished a lot! References available.
