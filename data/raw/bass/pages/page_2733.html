Fish for Bass on 8 dams set amongst the cane fields and coastal bush. We have our own tackle shop, with boats and fishing guides available on request. There's nothing better than ending off the day at our traditional African boma set on the water's edge of one of our best fishing dams.

- Bass Fishing Report -

We had big rains right in the middle of the peak period  which made the waters very muddy and difficult to catch. As the waters have been clearing up so the fishing has been rapidly improving.

I think the main dam has been the dirtiest. (thereby giving it a rest) Vusi will be cooking in a few days time. The sun is up early these days and the best fishing has been early mornings and on cloudy days.

Some of the smaller fish have been right on the surface and shy away if you walk towards them. These have been successfully hunted by a customer on a float tube. He was having a great time on fly with some big poppers. Top water lures orange and yellow are working well in the evenings. Till next time........
