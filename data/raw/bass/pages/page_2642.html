JUST FOR YOU: MIKE & MELANIE
Florida Bass Fishing Trip August 31, 2003
14 bass up to 5 1/2 lbs

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 



Bass fishing anglers Mike & Melanie Wright (Alta Monte, FL - ph: 407-314-1068 - e-mail: michaels@cfl.rr.com) caught 14 bass up to 5 1/2 lbs during a half day afternoon of shiner and artificial plastic worm fishing with Cap'n Ron on August 31, 2003. Mike caught the 5 1/2 lber on a shiner. Melanie caught a 4 3/4 lb'er on a shiner. Bass were caught mostly on plastic worm in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

JUST FOR YOU: LUIS & JAMES
Florida Bass Fishing Trip
August 30 & 31, 2003
9 bass up to 3 lbs

Bass fishing anglers Luis Cruz and his 9 1/2 year-old son James (Coconut Creek, FL - e-mail: Ricoinsulationco@aol.com) caught only 9 bass up to 3 lbs during 2 half days of shiner and artificial plastic worm fishing with Cap'n Ron on August 30 & 31, 2003.
