Florida Bass Fishing
Brag'n Board: February 2000

Florida bass fishing for trophy bass during the month of February produced some nice sized Florida trophy bass. The Florida wild shiner as usual was the way to fish for and sucessfully land one of these Florida lunkers.

Enjoy viewing hundreds of pictures of Florida trophy bass that were caught while Florida wild shiner fishing on lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Brag'n Board trophy Florida bass fishing pictures are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2004, 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A Florida bass fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught and other information is listed with each Florida bass fishing trophy bass picture. To ensure validity the angler's name, address, phone number and e-mail address is also listed with every fishing report.

Florida Bass Fishing 7 and 6 1/2 lb. Florida Bass

John Trudel (Cliffwood Beach, NJ - ph: 732-290-0147) and Alex Szabo (West Keansburg, NJ - ph: 732-787-0058) caught 19 Florida bass that included a 7 lb and 6 1/2 lb'er during 2 days (Feb. 28 & 29, 2000) of Florida wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. John caught the 7 and 6 1/2 lb'er. They fished the Moonshine area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.
