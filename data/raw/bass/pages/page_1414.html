Guitar & Bass Lessons

      Stevie B's has a great team of instructors who are very good with beginners but also can show seasoned players some advanced techniques as well. We won't force you to memorize endless scales and modes (unless you want to!) or give formal recitals. No, we'll simply teach you what YOU want to learn, in a fun and relaxed atmosphere that will inspire you to be the best guitarist you can be.

      Lessons are $15 per half-hour session, scheduled once per week. Tuition is paid monthly on the first scheduled date of each month.


Clearwater Location:

Adam Santucci

      Adam is a prototypical prodigy, comfortable in virtually any style but specializing in advanced "shred" techniques of the masters such as Yngwie Malmsteen, Joe Satriani and Steve Vai. He has only been playing for a little over ten years but possesses an astonishing technique and a deep knowledge of musical theory. Adam teaches Monday through Saturday, however many slots are already filled so call for his current openings.



Chris Santucci

      Adam's cousin Chris is another wonderful guitarist, whose main specialties are Classical, Fingerstyle, and Jazz stylings, but he is equally at home in Rock and Pop. He also has an affinity for Bluegrass and Flamenco music. His influences are John McLaughlin, Jimi Hendrix, Christopher Parkening, Steve Howe and Chet Atkins. He performs with local favorites "The Onlies". Chris teaches site reading, theory, fingerstyle and pickstyle playing. He is available Monday through Saturday, however many slots are already filled so call for his current openings.

Matt LaPorte

      Matt is a seasoned veteran, having played with many of Tampa Bay's top bands, as well as on tours with National recording acts "Circle II Circle", "Keel" and more. A real shredder, Matt has a hard rock style similar to Joey Tafolla, Reb Beach, George Lynch, Tony Macalpine, Jeff Watson et al with an amazing lexicon of riffs and techniques. He can nail everyone from Jimi Hendrix through Van Halen and many more. Matt is available only on Saturdays.
