     Ridge Road - Members and Bio   
     Author:  Neil
     Dated:  Sunday, July 25 2004 @ 04:09 PM CDT  
    Ridge Road was a band composed of Travis and Brandon Collins (vocals and acoustic; keys and misc. percussion), Chris and Joe Pangallo (bass; lead guitar), among others. I went to high school and continue to be close friends with the Pangallos, and the Collins family leads a Bible Study I attend when I'm in Cincinnati.

    Ridge Road played both Christian-influenced rock and worship, although their self-titled disc is all rock. Ridge Road played multiple Christian conferences as the headlining worship band, and Joe Pangallo recently was a guitarist in the worship band for Crusade's 2003 Great Lakes Christmas Conference.

    Although Ridge Road has disbanded, Travis and Brandon Collins have moved out to the West Coast and formed a new band called Emersen. Chris and Joe Pangallo have joined the band Day of Fire.

    With the permission of the band members, I present their self-titled disc. Remix, Fade and Scarecrows are my personal favorite tracks. I have also posted (again, with permission) some additional miscellaneous Ridge Road material that I have.

    Anyone who wants to buy a Ridge Road disc can contact me and I'll see if I can scrounge up a copy (and see that the band members get the money).
