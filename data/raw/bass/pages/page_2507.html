Choosing the Right Fishing Guide for You

If you wanted to learn to play basketball you would not go to the golf pro shop. Make sure you chose a professional guide with the knowledge needed to do his job. One of the hardest jobs in planning a trip is finding the right guide to fit YOUR needs and expectations. Don't be afraid to ask your fishing guide questions - before you book a trip. Check their qualifications. Almost every fishing guide will say they are the best, so use your discretion, and remember, if is sounds too good... Lastly, if the guide won't speak to you on the phone personally, before you leave a deposit, beware.
