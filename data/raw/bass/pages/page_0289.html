Welcome to the Internet home of Mike Roser Lures and the Bass Anglers Supply Shop, your Super Store for lures by Lucky Craft and Roboworm! We are a small bass fishing lures manufacturer and online tackle shop, based in Las Vegas Nevada, who specializes in bass fishing tackle for the tournament and recreational fisherman. Our bait and tackle shops mission is to provide the internets best selection of many popular brands of bass fishing lures and accessories, including Lucky Craft lures, Roboworm, Zipper Worm baits, Blackjack Custom Jigs and of course Mike Roser Lures. We are one of the few places on the internet you can find a good selection of Zeta Bait Ding-A-Ling worms and carry a nice selection of Boze Sumo Frogs, a household name in the tournament bass fishing world due to the performance of Dean Rojas at the 2004 Citgo Bassmaster Classic. Our hooks and weights section includes a huge selection of Gamakatsu hooks, Roboworm's ReBarb hooks as well as weight systems by Mojo. We carry a full selection of accessories as well, from culling systems to Kick'n Bass, Smelly Jelly and other attractants.

Being tournament anglers ourselves we know the kind of things you need and the urgency with which you sometimes need them. We work hard to stock the most popular baits of the brands we carry and can special order anything else you may need. Just let us know.
