Ashdown Labs: The future of bass

13/04/2004

Ashdown Labs is a new division of Ashdown Engineering, dedicated to innovating sophisticated new bass guitar amplification systems. While Ashdown Engineering bass amps are admired the world over for their fat, warm, characterful tones and 'modern vintage' styling, Ashdown Labs takes a 'Hi-Fi' approach, creating amplifiers of exceptional clarity and dynamic range. Using only discrete analogue components and strictly no Ics in the signal path, Ashdown Labs bass amps are also packed with innovative facilities like pre and post EQ digital recording outputs for direct connection to digital mixers and workstations, low impedance inputs for the ultimate low noise connection between instrument and amplifier, and programmable on-board DSP, enabling players to set-up and store Eqs, crossover points and compression settings from a laptop PC.
