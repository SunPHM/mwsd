Ray Anderson
(Bass Guitar/ Lead Vocal & Back up Vocals)

Ray Anderson has been playing music since he was seven years old. Starting out with guitar and moved on to the bass guitar. He studied bass guitar under Mr. Adolph Merez, the former bass player for "The Buddy Rich Band". Ray has a range on the base guitar from the key of "G" to "F3". Ray's excellent singing style was first attributed to Dick Mayer from the Central City Opera House, who vocally trained Ray. He has since aquired a singing range from Tenor to Soprano in full vocals.

Ray has an authentic and passionate singing capability and noted for his rock solid bass rhythms while performing with many local bands around the Denver Metro area including the band Trespasser.
