Hints for taking better photos of your catch

 

July 11, 2000 - This 6 lb. bass was caught a couple of weeks ago from a farm pond on a
plastic worm in some submerged timber. sure would like to have a limit like of these in a tournament. Yes I did get rained on, but it was worth it since I caught several more about this size. Phil Fiene

 

 

 

June 7, 2000 - My 11 year old son and I caught 9 Bass drifting across the
weed beds west of Wolf Island. The weather was very overcast and rainy
which had the fish busting topwater baits at midday. We caught em on
spooks, buzz baits, flukes, and worms. It will clear your arteries
when one trashes a buzz bait right at the boat. We took pictures. See
them. Also see the Kingfish that we limited out on while offshore last week.

 

 

 

 

 

May 11, 2000 - My sons and I fished Lake Texoma Wednesday evening. One of them came up with a 4.5 pound Bass while chasing the stripers. We didn't catch any stripers but another boat under the same birds we were under did. Guess
we need to improve on our striper fishing skills. Any pointers would be greatly appreciated. Visit us

Submitted Feb. 14, 2000 by Terry Shadduck - I caught these two fish at Brown State Lake in Kansas on 3/20/99 . The bigger fish weighed 7.2 oz. the other weighed 5.7 oz. . I returned to the lake late that day and caught a 9.8 oz. bass that held the BBWC Kansas record until august of that year , and then was overtaken by 3 oz. . All of these bass where released in my 12 acre farm pond .
