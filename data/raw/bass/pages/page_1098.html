Fender Mustang Bass
Short Scale Electric Bass Guitar

As my teenage grandson says after trying out this new Fender Mustang Bass Guitar,
"Sweet!"

This is a limited reissue of the fender Mustang electric bass classic, available right here in Juneau, Alaska.  It definitely has that classic Fender bass sound, and if you have never played a short scale bass, it is a breath of fresh air, particularly if you frequently change on stage between a regular guitar and your bass. In size, it is almost like playing my fender Telecaster, light on the shoulders, and a great attention-grabber. Gramps found that the shorter scale makes for faster, easier playing.
