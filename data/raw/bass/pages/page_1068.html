Electric Guitar Lessons

Do you want to learn electric guitar and impress your friends?

Electric guitar lessons from a teacher can be expensive and inconvenient but now, with Guitar and Bass' interactive online lessons, you can save money and make your own schedule for learning guitar. You can work through the electric guitar lessons as slowly or as quickly as you like, in your free time, online or offline.

You learn by seeing an expert strum the song first. There's nothing more inspirational than watching someone else play the song or piece of music you are actually learning. Especially when they make it look so easy.

The lessons take you through the song note-by-note, chord-by-chord, one step at a time, speeding up as you become more comfortable with it, learning with our animated fretboard.

Then you can jam along to a full backing track.

Other features include an in-built tuner letting you tune your electric guitar by ear, and an in-built, multi-tempo drum machine to get your timing down pat.
