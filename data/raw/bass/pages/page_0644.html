Bleeding Black Bass Bunny

 

http://groups.msn.com/ScottiesCustomFlies

 

Hook: Gamakatsu B10S #4 - #1 Body & Neck: Black Rabbit Zonker Eyes: Hourglass Gold or colored in size to suit sink rate required Tail Flash: Purple Krystal flash and Gold holo tinsel Red throat: anything red Tying Instructions: Secure thread and lay bed for eyes, secure eyes with fig.8 Technique, drop of superglue. Tie in flash materials to shank of hook before bend. Tie in zonker at beginning of hook bend and leave 2-3cm free for movement. About 1/2 way along shank, start wraps of zonker to form dense collar and secure red material and collar behind eyes. taper thread & Whip finish. Soak thread with superglue, varnish or headset. Good dusk & dawn bass fly or heavy overcast day, lots of movement in tail & body.
