Florida Bass Fishing 5 3/4 lb. Florida Bass
Florida Bass Fishing Trip June 27 & 28, 1999

Honeymoon couple Paul and Hollie Hendricks (York, SC - ph: 803-684-7018) caught 12 bass that included a 5 3/4 lb'er that Paul caught on wild shiners during the 2 half days (June 27 & 28, 1999) they fished with Cap'n Ron. Florida Bass Fishing 9 3/4 lb. Florida Trophy Bass
Florida Bass Fishing Trip April 5, 1999

Honeymoon couple Roland and Cody Toupin (Ft. Loramie, OH - ph: 937-295-4209) caught 11 bass up to 9 3/4 lbs during a half day (Apr. 5, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with pro guide Cap'n Ron. Cody of course caught the 9 3/4 lb'er as well as 8 of the 11 other bass put in the boat. Roland did manage to land a 5 lb'er. Fished the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released except the 9 3/4 lb'er which is being mounted.

Florida Bass Fishing 8 & 7 1/2 lb. Florida Trophy Bass
Florida Bass Fishing Trip December 5 & 6, 1997

8 and lb. Okeechobee trophy bass. Guided a honeymoon couple for 2 days (Dec. 5th. & 6th. 1997) of wild shiner fishing on lake Okeechobee. Dave Simpson and his wife Lisa (Marshall, VA - home ph: 540-364-4546) caught only 11 bass fishing due to a cold front that slowed down the fishing. To compensate for the poor fishing Lisa did however hook and land a 7 1/2 lb'er. Her husband Dave also hooked and landed a 8 lb'er. Lisa also had on another "big" bass that threw the hook. We fished the Monkey Box and Moonshine Bay. All the fish were caught on wild shiners.
