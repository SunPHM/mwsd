Electric Bass Guitar



Roxy music has many selections of the electric bass guitar. The first electric bass guitar was actually invented by the Fender company. Their first electric bass guitar was the P-Bass. From that came many improvements as technology changed. Now you'll see all varieties of body styles but the predominate style even today is the P-bass and jazz bass.
