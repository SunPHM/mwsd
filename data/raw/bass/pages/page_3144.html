Fishing Lures Club of the Month offers the greatest Fishing gift idea since the fishing pole. If you are looking for an original Fishing gift idea for that special person who loves to fish, you’ve come to the right place. Forget about buying socks, ties and other items that are shoved to the back of the drawer only to be forgotten. This is guaranteed to put a smile on the recipient’s face month after month. We offers a unique gift for that special fisherman. We send the finest quality lures monthly in a nicely packaged gift box. Any one who is an avid or casual fisherman will love this unique gift.


When you join on a monthly basis , you will receive your Membership Certificate, a Fishing lure with a detailed description on different techniques for its use and a water proof bag via the US Postal Service. That’s right, no more rusty hooks, the bag is the perfect size for your new lure. As an added bonus, you will receive the "Fish of the Month". No, we really don't mail you a live fish; rather we choose a different fish each month with a detailed description of that particular fish. Each lure will arrive in a nicely packaged gift box. Check out our photo gallery for a first hand view of what you will receive; A picture is worth a thousand words.

We are based in Southwest Florida, the Fishing Capital of the World. We select only the finest quality lures that catch fish. We personally fish with each lure before deciding if it makes the cut to become a "Lure of the Month".

Our web site uses the latest security technology, and we will not solicit your information. We respect the privacy of our members.

Feel free to browse our site. If you have any question please feel free to Email us at luresClub@earthlink.net our customer service representative will reply to your email in a timely manner.
