Florida Bass Fishing
Report: April 2000

I have put together some April 2000 Florida bass fishing reports of Florida bass that were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Florida bass fishing reports are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught on Lake Okeechobee and other information is listed with each Florida bass fishing report. To ensure validity the angler's name, address, phone number and e-mail address is also listed with every fishing report.

Check out our Florida Bass Fishing Special Rate listed below.

Mike Ashcraft Jr., his dad Mike Sr. (Waterford, MI - ph: 248-673-5253 - e-mail: ashcraftmi@plunkettlaw.com) and 2 other fishermen in 2 guide boats caught about 80 Florida bass between them up to 6 1/4 lbs during 3 days (Apr. 29, 30, May 1, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. They fished the North Shore, Moonshine and Monkey Box areas of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.

Florida Bass Fishing - April 23 - 25, 2000 Fishing Report

Florida trophy bass fishing father and son team John Sr. and Jr. Skimelis (Shrewsbury, NJ - ph: 732-741-7271 - e-mail: Bass7015@aol.com) caught 78 bass that included one 7 1/2, two 7's, one 6 1/2 and two 6 lb'ers during 3 days (April 23 - 25, 2000) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. John Sr. caught one 6 lb'er. His son John Jr. caught all of the other trophy size bass as well as 75 of the 78 bass landed on this trip. This was their fifth trip to Lake Okeechobee in six years with Cap'n Ron. They in fact set a three day record with Ron's Guide Service by landing six bass from 6 - 7 1/2 lbs in three days! All bass that were landed were released except the 7 1/2 lb'er that was kept for mounting. All bass were caught in the Fisheating Creek area of Lake Okeechobee.
