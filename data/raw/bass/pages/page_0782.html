Hofner 500/2 Club 4 string Bass

This bass is a German handbuilt Hofner 500/2 Club Bass model. The bass features a lightweight fully bound single-cutaway Flame Maple body/German Spruce top design and inset neck with a Rosewood fingerboard. The pickups are the passive Hofner "staple" type and the tone of this bass is classic. The bass is strung up with flatwound strings to give it a real vintage 1960's type of tone.
