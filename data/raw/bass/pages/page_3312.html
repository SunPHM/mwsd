SEA ANGLER - The Video!
An exciting guide to beach casting and boat fishing with Sea Angler editor Mel Russ.
This specially-commissioned Sea Angler video, which is presented by ‘Go Fishing’ television personality John Wilson, features World Casting Champion Neil Mackellow, who explains what key tackle items you need to successfully catch fish from the beach.
With stunning slow motion pictures, Neil shows how to cast off-the-ground and pendulum style so that you can mirror his technique to improve your own casting ability. The second part of this unique video focuses on Mel Russ wreck fishing for double-figure pollock and cod in the English Channel. There's plenty of action and a lot to learn as fish after fish hit small pirks and jelly worm. ‘Sea Angler - The Video!’ brings Britain's most successful sea angling magazine alive in your own home.

We did it and we'll show you how...
The first Sea Angler video was such a soar-away success they produced another, ‘Sea Angler - The Sequel’, directed by television's John Wilson and supported by a star cast, including editor Mel Russ, the magazine's technical guru Neil Mackellow and our small-boat expert, Dave Lewis. SEA ANGLER - Mastercast!
“You have never seen casting like this before, I promise!” - Neil Mackellow.
If you only ever buy one casting video, make sure it's ‘Mastercast!’
The thousands of readers who bought the hugely successful ‘Sea Angler: The Video’ have pleaded with Sea Angler Magazine to make a more in depth casting video. So the best tutors have produced the best casting video and here it is.
Directed by John Wilson, it features Neil Mackellow demonstrating off-ground casting and all steps in between, right up to a full blooded tournament pendulum. With the camera mounted on a high vantage point, look down on Neil as he performs cast after cast in fantastic slow motion. From this unique angle you are able to see exactly how he handles the rod to produce those world-class casts. He also strips a popular beach multiplier, showing in unique detail how to re-tune it for optimum performance on the beach with a combination of oils and brakes. Sponsored by Zziplex, ‘Mastercast!’ includes exclusive footage shot inside the Zziplex factory where we talk to Terry Carroll about the material and equipment used to make some of the best fishing and casting rods in the world.

SEA ANGLER - Cod!
Television personality John Wilson introduces the fourth Sea Angler video, bringing you the very best in cod fishing.
In this hour-long special we show you how to catch the nation's favourite fish afloat and along the shore, using a variety of baits and methods that are guaranteed to bring you success.
The action covers inshore dinghy fishing with Dave Lewis, who shows viewers how to up and downtide in fast currents with big flowing baits.  Champion caster, Neil Mackellow, travels to Scotland to fish the Fifeness coast and explains what gear and bait you need to fish along the shore.
Back afloat, editor Mel Russ goes cod fishing with jelly worms and scores as 15lb-plus fish come up through 200ft of water. Finally, Dave Lewis is back in action out of Whitby as the pirks flash and the North Sea cod attack.

SEA ANGLER - BASS!
The explosion of bass around Britain's coastline triggered this video, which shows the tackle you need, the bait to fish and the methods that catch bass. Directed by TV's John Wilson, you will be enthralled by the action as bass to 10lb smash into baits and lures. The action takes place out of Lymington abourd Roger Bayzand's Sundance and further west on the deck of Chris Caine's Tiger Lily out of Weymouth.
We show you how to catch fast-running bass on live launce, joey mackerel and lures. Clive Gammon captures the golden days of bassing in the surf and dinghy anglers Dave Lewis and Stuart Smalley push the boat out off the Suffolk and South Wales coast in their quest to catch the fish.

THE COMPLETE GUIDE TO OFFSHORE BOAT FISHING 
with Bob Cox
