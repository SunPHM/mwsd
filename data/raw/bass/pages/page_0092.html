LAKE EL SALTO: The most popular Mexico bass fishing lake. Lake El Salto, is located 50 miles north of the seaside resort of Mazatlan. EL SALTO has a healthy population of trophy size bass. The aggressive bass of El Salto will strike a variety of lures all year, but topwaters and crankbaits are the best.

Lake El Salto is a home to some of the very best Largemouth Bass Fishing in the Mexico and the world. Lake El Salto is located 50 miles north of the beautiful seaside resort of Mazatlan. Thousands of bass over 18 pounds have been caught and released at this great Mexico bass fishing lake! Every year the lake record keeps being challenged by yet another trophy bass!

Lake El Salto has thousands of hungry bass that are easily caught all year on a variety of of lures, but topwaters, crankbaits, and plastic worms are best. El Salto offers the amateur and the novice angler the opportunity to easily catch lots of fish most days without a lot of effort.
