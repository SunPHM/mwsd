Royale 12-String Bass Current Specs

*List Price: $3999.00US w/Custom Hardshell White Tolex Case

Headstock Overlay of Italian Celluloid /White/Tortoise Shell or Black  w/ Chandler Guitar Tuners (for the octave strings) and Gotoh GB-7 Bass Tuners (for the fundamental strings)

*10-Piece Laminated Maple Neck w/ a Graphite Nut and a Brazilian Rosewood Fingerboard. Two Graphite Rod Stabilizers (on either side of a Single Truss Rod) are installed with the correct tensile strength to resist the tension of the 12 string bass, keeping the neck from bowing or twisting. In addition the extended neck design up to the middle pickup adds extra stability in a 34" Scale.

Swamp Ash Body                                                                                                          

Body Binding/ White Pearl/ Tortoise Shell/ Black Pearl

Pickguard/ White Pearl/ Tortoise Shell/ Black Pearl*

SUPER 60 Pickups x 3 (Passive)/ available in the following options; 

Separate volume, On/ Off Switch, and output jack for each pickup (like Tom Petersson's)

Stereo: Neck pickup has separate volume, On/ Off switch, and output jack; middle & bridge pickups have individual volumes and On/ Off switches and combined output jack.

Mono: Each pickup has its own volume and/ On/ Off Switch w/ combined output jack.
