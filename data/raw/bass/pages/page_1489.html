    KaaosMachine is my, one man, project featuring progressive song structure ranging from delicate melodies with heartfelt lyrics to full throttle thrash with buzz saw riffing and soaring leads.

     

    I have recorded close to 40 original songs at my residence with analog recorders. I recently have moved into the digital domain and now have CD producing technology. I have an album out now:

    (Voice of Sanity - Beyond Infinity) consisting of a few new tunes and an intimate selection of re-mastered earlier material.

     

    I compose, arrange and perform all the music by myself. "KaaosMachine" is a name I use for my music; which is somewhat dark, very deep with a touch of sweetener. A new CD is coming soon featuring all new music with dark concepts and a chilling storyline.
