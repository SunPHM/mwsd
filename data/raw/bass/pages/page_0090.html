Bass Pro Shops Coming to BA

For the City of Broken Arrow, it may have been a "record catch" when Bass Pro Shops announced it would be building an Outdoor World just north of the Broken Arrow Expressway, east of Elm Place.

The announcement was made by Bass Pro Shops on March 18. The following day a press conference was held at City Hall, with City officials joining Chamber of Commerce President Ted Allison and Phil Roland of Roland Investments, Ltd., in giving further details of the development. The Bass Pro Shop Outdoor World will be the anchor of a $500 million development by Roland -- Stone Wood Hills.
