Whitetail Deer Hunting, Turkey Hunting, Bass Fishing and Lodging in Illinois & Missouri

Turkey Creek Lodge was created for the sole purpose of providing a high quality and exclusive hunting experience for our clients. Including whitetail deer hunting, turkey hunting and bass fishing. The concept for our family owned and operated lodge was developed when we realized that there were few options for hunters who wanted the best all around experience. Being avid hunters ourselves, the owners of TCL have hunted through many different lodges and guide services. TCL is a combination of the best aspects of each of these facilities. The result? An operation devoted to providing our customers with the best possible hunting experience.


Whitetail Hunts:
South Central Illinois offers some of the best whitetail deer habitat in the country.All properties are ARCHERY ONLY. This combined with trophy genetics, food plots and a harvest restriction on young bucks, gives our clients a great opportunity at their trophy. 5 day hunts start at $1750.


Turkey Hunts:
Illinois and North Central Missouri
Very high successful turkey hunts in both Illinois and Missouri. Nearly 100% opportunity in Illinois and 80% opportunity in Missouri 2 years running. All private land. We are flexible with your hunting style. 3 day hunt $850.

 Upland Game: Looking for the thrill of watching the dogs go on point, then enjoying the excitement of the flush? We provide a variety of upland game packages where you can test your skills against quail, pheasant, chukar and Hungarian Partridge. We have 2 shooting preserves that allow you to hunt on a variety of situations including food plots, grasslands, fence rows, brush and agricultural acres. 1/2 day hunts starting at $120, 3 days 2 nights $550.


Fishing:
Newton Lake, located 15 minutes from the lodge, is a power plant lake that is known across the country for its large number of Bass ranging from 4-7 lbs. We also have several ponds that hold a good number of bass, crappie, bluegill and catfish. All ponds are available for our guests. We can also combine bass fishing with any hunting trip.
