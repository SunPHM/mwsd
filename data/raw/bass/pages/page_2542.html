LAKE OKEECHOBEE BASS FISHING

2003
Lake Okeechobee loves women anglers! This Florida freshwater lake over the 20 years I have operated a trophy bass fishing guide service on - has produced on a person to person (male to female) ratio "more trophy bass to women anglers". I hate to admit it - but the girls are able to put more Okeechobee trophy bass in the boat far more consistantly then the experienced men anglers!



Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Girls Brag'n Board
7 1/4 lb., 6 1/2 lb. & two 5 1/2 lb'ers
October 27, 28 & 29, 2003

Bass fishing anglers Ed Papin, his wife Kate and 15 year-old son Mike (St. Charles, MO - ph: 314-494-4315 - e-mail: epaoin@sbcglobal.net) caught and released 90 bass that included a 7 1/4 lb., 6 1/2 lb. and two 5 1/2 lb'ers during 3 days of shiner and artificial plastic worm fishing with Ron's Guide Service on October 27, 28 & 29, 2003. Kate caught the 7 1/4 lb. and the two 5 1/2 lb'ers. Mike got the 6 1/2 lb'er. all the trophy size bass where caught on a shiner. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Girls Brag'n Board
4 1/4 lb. & 4 lb'er
June 23 & 24, 2003

Bass fishing anglers Tom Snyder, his wife Deb and nephew Bryant (Mount Holly, NJ - ph: 609-265-8756 - e-mail: tlsnyder3@comcast.net caught and released 68 bass up to 4 1/4 lbs during 2 days of shiner and artificial plastic worm fishing with Cap'n Ron on June 23rd. and Captain Bob on June 24th., 2003. Tom's wife Deb caught the 4 1/4 lb'er that would of went at least 6 or more pounds if it had some body weight. Tom also caught a 4 lb'er. Tom's 8 year-old nephew Bryant caught about 30 of the 68 bass landed. Bass were caught mostly on plastic worm in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.
