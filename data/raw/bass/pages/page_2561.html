Lake Huites Mexico Bass Fishing FAQ

General Lake Huites Bass Fishing Information

Lake Huites is approximately 15,000 acres. It is located on the El Fuerte River, in the Mexican state of Sinaloa, approximately 50 miles east of the coastal city of Los Mochis.
We will meet your flight at the Los Mochis Airport and drive you to the Villa Del Pescador in El Fuerte where you'll be welcomed by our friendly staff and served snacks and refreshments.

Before dinner we will go over your trip itinerary and orient you on the area's points of interest and history.

After enjoying a delicious dinner, prepared by our chef, you can enjoy a comfortable nights rest.

We will wake you early the next morning with hot coffee and fresh juice. After a hearty breakfast, it's off to the lakeside lodge at Huites, an hour and a half away.

Upon arrival at the Huites lodge our staff will quickly move your equipment into your room at the lodge then without delay you're into the waiting boats and onto the lake where our professional guides will introduce you to your first taste of that exciting bass fishing Huites is famous for.

Around 11:30 we'll return to the lodge for a refreshing lunch and short break.
At 2:00 it's back onto the lake for 4 more hours of lure crushing bass fishing action. A cooler filled with cold beverages is provided with each boat.

Refreshments will be waiting upon your return to the lodge each evening. Margaritas, beer, Cokes, etc. as well as snacks to tide you over till dinner. You can relax and recount the days highlights while enjoying a beautiful view of the lake and surrounding mountains.

A delicious dinner of BBQ, steak, fish, shrimp, chicken and "fixins" or a more typical Mexican Combo will be served by our attentive staff at 8:00pm. All meals are prepared with our guests' health, safety and enjoyment in mind. Purified water is served and used for food preparation.

After a good nights rest we'll wake you just before dawn with hot coffee and juice, feed you another hearty breakfast and get you on the water for another exciting day of fishing.

On your final day of fishing we'll leave for El Fuerte at around 4:00pm where you can rest for your return home.

We will return you to the Los Mochis airport after breakfast in plenty of time to catch your return flight.

A birth certificate and photo I.D. is all you will need for your Visa.

Temperatures

October 13th to November 25th daytime temperatures average between 72 and 85 degrees F. Evenings 65 to 70 F.

December 25th to February 25th expect daytime highs from 70 to 75 F and evenings 60 to 65 F. Temperatures can go down to the 40's at night.

February through April days 70 to 80 nights 65 to 70.

Lake Huitesd Bass Fishing Clothing

Always bring a rain suit. Shorts are fine when the weather permits. Always bring long pants and long sleeve shirts in case the mosquitos are around. Polarized sun glasses, a good cap and sun block are a must.

Bring a light jacket for early mornings and late evenings November - February.

Lake Huites Bass Fishing Tackle

Baitcasters spooled with 20 pound test line on medium to heavy action 6 to 7 foot rods are recommended.

Chugger and Zara Spooks in clear and chrome are good when the fish are feeding on the surface.

Shallow through deep diving crankbaits in chrome, chartreuse and crawfish are a good idea to bring along.

Soft plastic worms are the most productive. 4 through 12 inch in shad, red shad, tequila sunrise and black are usually good colors.

Check prior to leaving for best colors and lure types.

Lake Huites Bass Fishing Guides and Boats

Guides are all native to the lake and speak enough English to make your fishing easy.

Your bass fishing guide will do his best every day to ensure you are on the biggest fish all the time.

Lake Huites Bass Fishing Guides usually like to fish some of the time out of the back of the boat.

Boats are 18 foot fiberglass with 30 horsepower engines. They are plenty fast and smooth riding.

Lake Huites Trophy Fish Policy

Lake Huites is a "Catch and Release Only" lake. We will photograph, weigh and measure your Trophy fish. We can recommend excellent replica specialists.

Fly In

Fly your aircraft into El Fuerte's 4,200 foot paved runway at an elevation of 246 feet. MSL and we'll pick you up at the El Fuerte airport. Prior to your departure to El Fuerte we'll forward you an information package on flying in Mexico, border crossing information, and required aircraft, pilot and passenger documents.
