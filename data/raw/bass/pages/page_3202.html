Bluegill Fishing Lake Okeechobee Report: May 31, 2003



HONEYMOON bluegill & bass fishing anglers Mark & Erin Maloy (Noblesville, IN - ph: 317-770-7200) caught about 60 bluegills on live crickets during a half day afternoon of bluegill fishing with Cap'n Ron on May 31, 2003. This couple also caught 6 bass up to 4 lbs during a half day morning of bass fishing with Cap'n Ron on June 1, 2003. Bluegills were caught in the Twin Pond Canal area of Lake Okeechobee. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of the lake.
