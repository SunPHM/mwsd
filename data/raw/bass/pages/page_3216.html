Fishing
   Fishermen of all ages enjoy "going for the big one". Kokanee, 3 varieties of trout, Largmouth bass, crappie, perch, catfish, are all easy prey for the avid fisherman.


   Spirit Lake is famous for it's population of Kokanee Salmon. There are also bass, perch, crappie, trout, bullhead. Whether fishing from the docks or from your boat, you're sure to enjoy the relaxation of our wonderful lake. Bait, tackle, ice, pop & candy are sold at the General Store in the RV Park. A Porta Pottie is available near the dock parking lot.
