"Bass Hunter"
Freshwater Bass Fishing

We have freshwater fishing here too. In fact, our bass fishing produces some of the largest average size fish that you will find anywhere in the U.S. The canals are full of largemouth bass and a good number of exotic peacock bass are here too. Our 20 foot bass boat will get you there in comfort and style.

The peacock bass are quickly becoming the fish of choice for the freshwater angler. Their beauty and fighting ability make them one of the true trophy catches in our waters. Freshwater fishing is best done with 1 or 2 anglers onboard. This can be as good as the bass fishing gets anywhere in the U.S. If you get out west far enough, keep your camera handy - the gators love to smile for the lens.
