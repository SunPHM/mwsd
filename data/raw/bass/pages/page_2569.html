Smallmouth Bass Paradise on Lake Ontario in Pulaski - Port Ontario, NY

...Amercian waters of Eastern Lake Ontario near Pulaski- Port Ontario, NY abound with trophy smallmouth bass. Lake Ontario's New York shoreline has gained national acclaim as North America's Premier smallmouth bass fishing destination.

 

Bass Fish U.S. Hotspots near Pulaski, NY

...Smallmouth Bass hotspots like Mexico Bay, Fair Haven, Henderson Harbor, Port Bay, Sandy Pond, Sodus Bay, Oswego Harbor, Port Ontario and a Thousand "Secret Spots" in between are our charter's ports of call.

Pure Bass Fishing Fun

...Lake Ontario near Pulaski - Port Ontario, NY where Smallmouth Bass fishing is pure fun where the "essence of angling" is not lost in an avalanche of high tech gear and complicated electronics. We specialize in "hands-on fishing" with continuous anticipation of a strike after every cast.

...We drift or still fish with live bait or work shoreline, shoals, and structure with tube jigs and crank baits, and you'll be totally involved with your own rod, reel, and lure as you immerse yourself in our "down to earth" fishing experience.

Trophy Class Smallmouth

...Limit Catches of chunky bronzebacks are commonplace here near Pulaski - Port Ontario on Eastern Lake Ontario. Our average bass weighs just about two pounds and we catch lots in the 3 pound class!! The occasional World Class Lunker could weigh 5 to 6 pounds!!

Fast paced Action near Pulaski, NY

...You'll catch smallmouth bass all day long and when the bite is on, double and triple header hookups are common. The action can be fast and furious as some parties land in excess of 100 bass in a day's fishing.
