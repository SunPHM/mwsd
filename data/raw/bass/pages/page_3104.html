+ = In Season

March is historically one of the best months for cod fishing. Lots of cod to almost 50lbs. can be caught now along with other Fish like haddock, Pollack and wolf fish as a bonus to the catch.

 

April brings bent rods, sore arms and lots of cod, Pollack and wolf fish.The fish are 10-20 pounds with some bigger on most trips. Haddock pleased these anglers last April.

May equals huge schools of sand eels and leering moving into our area and bringing lots of big cod, Pollack haddock, wolf fish and monk fish to our customers. Hump back whales and white-bellied dolphins point the location of the bait and big fish below it. We get cod to 48lbs. and lots of big wolf fish. Pollack show up in greater numbers and are always plenty of haddock in the deeper water.

June brings lots of big striped bass to Massachusetts Bay and Cape Cod Bay early in the month. Bass to 40lbs. were taken at Pace Pt. and at the H-Buoy. Cod fishing is always excellent with most Cod 10-20 lbs. and many 30lb and even 40lb - fish. Pollack, wolf fish and haddock are also about and make a nice mixed bay.

July starts the summer bite and lots of different fish begin to be caught. School tuna begin to bite and these fish are 50 to 200lbs. Sharks show up in good numbers and our customers target Makos, Threshers, Porbeagles and blue sharks.

August Gives both giant and School tuna. Tuna are everywhere with huge schools busting all over the surface from 3 to 30 miles out. Most school tuna caught are 80lbs. to 200lbs. Shark fishing is great as is the cod fishing. Cod up to and over 50lbs. are frequently landed this month with lots of giant Pollack, haddock and cusk.

In September Tuna fishing is always red hot with both giant and school fish. Most trips are combination 1/2 day cod or 1/2 day shark and 1/2 day striped bass and blues. Thresher, blue and mako sharks are caught and cod fishing is excellent with lots of big haddock wolf fish and Pollack also caught. Most school tuna are 50lbs. to 250lbs. while striped bass are taken up to over 40lbs.

October is one of the best months for huge giant tuna. Our biggest giant blue fin was 1,050lbs and caught last October. Many school tuna are taken between 100 and 250lbs. on 50lb. standings gear. The sharks are always biting and cod fishing continues to be excellent with fast action on cod, haddock, Pollack and wolf fish.
