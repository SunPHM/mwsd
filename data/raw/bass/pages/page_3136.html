Florida Wild Shiner Bass Fishing
Brag'n Board: February 2001

My guide fishing parties are catching good size bass dispite the severe cold fronts of January which shut down Okeechobee's bass fishing. Sunny days which are heating up the water has really helped to get the baitfish moving and the bass active once again. There are hundreds of bass beds on the lake and along the canals. We expect a large bass spawn on the new and full moons of March and April. The low water conditions on Lake Okeechobee will greatly help bass fishing anglers catch trophy bass this year moreso because the bass are all congregated in the deep water areas of the lake.

Enjoy viewing hundreds of pictures of Florida bass fishing trophy bass that were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States.
