I go trolling for rainbows in my 7'11'' 17 lb. Micro. 2 lb. test line usually. Note rod holder at front of cockpit. Sometimes hit into a 10 lb. squawfish and get towed around in little circles, which amuses the bank fishermen no end.   R. Bear   Eugene, OR
Nice 33 inch, 14 pound Halibut caught near the Cabrillo Beach breakwall. I caught it on 8lb line and a Cotee Pearl/Green swim bait, in about 20' of water. Fishing near a breakwall can be very productive. Live bait generally works very well, but Halibut are ambush feeders. It have on several occasions been using a grub or swim bait, and have had a small Halibut follow it up to the kayak before he sees what's going on.   Perry Crosbie
