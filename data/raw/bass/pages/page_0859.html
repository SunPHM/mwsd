Guitar / Bass Guitar Lessons

Have you dreams of playing in a rock band, playing folk songs and singing around the campfire? Private lessons are available to people of all ages on acoustic, electric or bass guitar. You can choose to study classical, rock, jazz or country music. All students will receive a strong fundamental background on guitar that will allow them to grow into strong musicians, whatever the final goal. 

Fee:  $144 per quarter

Faculty:  Greg Langlois, Shawn Smalley, Chad Gray
