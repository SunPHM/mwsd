Turnout even with a Lakers game on... Was pretty good.

 

Tom Raftican reviewed the CAST Tournament and helped to sign up a few more entrants.  Tom and Paul Simon both gave us a lot of hot information on the White Sea Bass fish population and the grow out program.

Did you know:

  • over the past 3 years 375,000 white sea bass in the 6-8 inch range have been released.
  • the program is mandated buy the state until the release is 350,000 fish per year for a sustained 3 year period.
  • the return of the big white sea bass is due to the removal of the gill nets from state waters (Prop. 132 - protects the first 3 miles from shore)
  • we have a 1 fish per angler limit as it is sponing season for the white sea bass.
  • the bag limit for white sea bass changes to 3 fish per angler on Friday June 16th.
