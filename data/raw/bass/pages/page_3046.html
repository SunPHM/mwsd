Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
7 lb'er - February 27, 28, March 1, 2002

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Eugene and Sandra Ceglinski (Paducah, KY, - ph: 270-554-8612 - e-mail: KyVet@msn.com) caught and released about 25 bass up to 7 lbs during 3 days (Feb. 27, 28, March 1, 2002) of wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. The fishing was very poor due to a severe cold front that hit Florida. Eugene caught the 7 lb'er. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee.

Lake Okeechobee Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
8 1/4 lb. & 5 lb'er - February 19 & 20, 2002

Florida wild shiner trophy bass fishing father and son team John Sr. and Jr. Skimelis (Shrewsbury, NJ - ph: 732-741-7271 - e-mail: Bass7015@aol.com) caught 6 bass that included a 5 lb. and 8 1/4 lb. bass during 2 half days (Feb. 19 & 20, 2002) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. John Jr. caught the 8 1/4 lb'er. His dad caught the 5 lb'er. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee. This has been their 7th. bass fishing trip on Lake Okeechobee with Cap'n Ron. On their last year trip of 3 days (April 9 -11, 2001) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron John Jr. caught a 8 3/4 lb. and 6 3/4 lb. bass. On their 3 days (April 23 - 25, 2000) trip of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron they in fact set a three day record by catching one 7 1/2, two 7's, one 6 1/2 and two 6 lb'ers out of the 78 bass landed on that trip. All bass that were landed on this trip were released except the 8 1/4 lb'er that was kept for mounting.
