SportsFebruary 9, 2006 

Williams catches 10-lb. bass

Jett Williams of Nashville, Tenn., country and western singer, recently caught this 10-lb bass at Lake Joe-Joe, at the home of Helen and Joe Skipper of Jackson. Williams was fishing in a boat on the lake with her best friend, Lenora Johnston of Mobile. Williams, the daughter of the late Hank Williams, was also the Alpha Delta Pi sorority big sister and roommate of Helen Skipper when they attended the University of Alabama.
