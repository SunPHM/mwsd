Got out there around 11AM, fished till after 3. Non-stop action. Threw back plenty of keeper sea bass and other legal but small fish. Kept some nice croakers and a couple sea trout below.

Squid, clam, and sand fleas all worked well. We did combos of all and could barely hit the bottom before the bite many drifts. Had a live spot out as well, and caught and released a four foot shark.

General trend I saw: 80+ feet = mostly croakers. <80 feet = mostly sea bass. Did 27 knots on the way out, but wind picked up and got a soaking on the way in. Great day!
