March 2004

 

We have been doing really well on most of our stand up trips this spring. We've been catching Yellow Fin Tuna and a few Wahoos. Below is a picture of the Yellow Fin Tuna and 2 Wahoos that were caught on March 22, a really rough day. The best is yet to come and the weather should steadily get better.

 

The bottom fishing has been great also. Big Sea Bass and Vermilion Snapper, plus a few Grouper have been caught on all offshore fishing trips.  It's time to get fishing!

 

Good Fishing!
Capt. Woo Woo
