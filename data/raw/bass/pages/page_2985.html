Explore the Edge of the Wilderness Minnesota Resorts Area!

The lure of Minnesota's Northwoods is unmistakable.  Generations of families take their Minnesota vacation here year after year, returning to bask in the ambience of the tall pines, the clean fresh air, cool, clear lakes and rivers, and warm hospitality.  Sportsmen are drawn to the area in search of the magnificent fishing and hunting opportunities offered within our thousands of acres of state and federal public lands.
 

The Edge of the Wilderness Resort Area is a year-round family vacation destination in Northern Minnesota.

Visitors often tell us that the great variety of year-round activities found here coupled with our many lodging choices make the Edge of the Wilderness Resort Area their top pick for a vacation destination.

Our Northern Minnesota resorts, motels and campgrounds are cozy and welcoming, and the hosts are friendly and hospitable.  We know how to treat guests right! Just click here to choose from our many lodging choices.

Looking for some of the best Minnesota fishing resorts?  The walleye fishing, muskie fishing, and smallmouth bass fishing here are great.  And of course we have plenty of northern, largemouth bass, lake trout,  crappie, bluegill, perch and sunfish too.

Discover forests of pine, spruce, birch and maple, with many trails and logging roads to explore.  There are thousands of acres of undiscovered, unspoiled and undeveloped land here just waiting for your visit.  There is also direct access to hundreds of uncrowded lakes and rivers with clear, pristine waters.

One of the favorite activities of visitors is watching the abundant wildlife including deer, moose, wolves, bear, raccoons, squirrels, chipmunks, beaver, bobcats, fox, rabbits, mink, otters, lynx, and wolverines.

Birding enthusiasts can find many of Minnesota's most sought-after birds right outside their cabins.  We are home to the largest concentration of bald eagles and common loons in the continental United States.

  

The sky blue waters of hundreds of area lakes and rivers provide a perfect backdrop for family vacation fun.

Come explore the small friendly communities along the Edge of the Wilderness National Scenic Byway.  You'll find plenty of shopping, dining and other activities to enjoy.

This area is all part of the Edge of the Wilderness National Scenic Byway, a 47 mile drive along breathtaking scenery and interesting historical areas from Grand Rapids, Minnesota to Effie, MN.  To find out more about where we are located and how to get here, please click the "Directions" button above. The Edge of the Wilderness National Scenic Byway has been listed as one of the Best Fall Color Drives in America.

Each fall the hunting opportunities abound for ducks, geese, ruffed grouse, whitetail deer, bear and moose. (The Fall fishing is great too!)

In the winter, you can relax in a resort cabin or comfortable motel room complete with a fireplace, then get a bite to eat at one of our area cafes or restaurants before heading out to take advantage of our well-groomed snowmobile and cross-country skiing trails. Our trails intersect with trails leading to every part of the state.

We hope you'll find the lure of our Northwoods too irresistible to pass up.  We want to help you start your own Edge of the Wilderness Vacation Tradition - one that will last for many years to come.
