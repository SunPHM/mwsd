New Recording of Ewazen Bass Trombone Music »

Composer Eric Ewazen has done his part in the advancement of the bass trombone by writing numerous pieces for the instrument. Now these works have been recorded by world class musicians on Bass Hits, a recording from Albany Records. The all-star group of players includes John Rojak, American Brass Quintet; Stefan Sanders, Buffalo Symphony; David Taylor, New York Freelance artist; and Charles Vernon, Chicago Symphony.

ITA Members will be receiving a coupon within the next few weeks to purchase this CD from Albany Records for $10. Check back for more details.

On this CD:

  1. Concertino for Bass Trombone and Trombone Choir
    David Taylor, bass trombone
    Glen Cortese, conductor

  2. Ballade for Bass Trombone, Harp and String Orchestra
    Charles Vernon, bass trombone
    Jessica Zhou, harp
    International Sejong Soloists

  3. Concerto for Bass Trombone and Orchestra
    Stefan Sanders, bass trombone
    Czech Philharmonic Chamber Orchestra, Paul Polivnick, conductor

  4. Rhapsody for Bass Trombone and String Orchestra
    John Rojak, bass trombone
    Czech Philharmonic Chamber Orchestra, Paul Polivnick, conductor

  5. Capriccio for Bass Trombone and Trombone Choir
    David Taylor, bass trombone
    Glen Cortese, conductor

You may purchase the CD from Albany Records. Many of these pieces are available from the ITA Press.
