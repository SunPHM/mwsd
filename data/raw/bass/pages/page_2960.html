Experience the very finest Smallmouth bass fishing in the entire Northeast. South Branch Lake, being a warm water fishery, is ideally suited for smallmouth bass. The lake is dotted with numerous islands and countless submerged rocky shoals. The rock and gravel composition of the shoals and islands make ideal spawning sites for smallmouth bass. Bait fish and crayfish are abundant and supply the bass with adequate forage. Three to five pound smallmouth are common. We do practice catch and release to maintain this wonderful fishery. We have modern up to date equipment

Adventurous? Bring along one of our experienced guides to fish on the Penobscot, Piscataquis Rivers, or Seboeis Stream. Fish with the most experienced and well qualified river Smallmouth Guides in Maine. We use only the best when it comes to equipment and tackle, we are one of only a very few select places to carry Yamamoto Custom Baits, as well as AC Shiners. Our guides are prepared to supply all the fishing needs of our clients except fly fishing tackle although our clients are welcome to bring their own. The guides have been working out of our camp for twelve seasons, frequently on an every day basis.
