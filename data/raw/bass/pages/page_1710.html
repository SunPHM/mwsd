EJ's Tub Bass Is Autographed By "Riders In The Sky"

The Grammy Award Winning band "Riders In The Sky" stopped by Chillicothe, Ohio on October 26, 2002.
They performed show #4579 in the auditorium of Smith Middle School.
"The Riders" are band of musicians that appeal to American Western music lovers of all ages.

Among the people at their performance, was a long time fan and one of The Highpoint Brothers, our tub bass player, EJ.

After the show, EJ waited patiently in the line for an autograph.
But an autograph on one of the typical items ( the program, a CD, etc. ) was not good enough for EJ.  No.
When his turn came at the autograph table, EJ presented his tub bass.
The Riders seemed quite pleased that a fellow musician would bring in such an unusual instrument to be autographed.
In fact, after they autographed the neck of the tub bass, a couple of The Riders played it for those gathered around!

Shown below, Too Slim ( on the right ) autographs EJ's tub bass.



The temptation to tickle the neck of EJ's bass was too much for Too Slim.
Below, he slaps out a tasty tune on the instrument, to the pleasure of those gathered in the area.



After Too Slim had his fun, Woody ( their fiddle player ) took a turn.



So the next time you're at a show with The Highpoint Brothers, look down low on the neck of EJ's tub bass
( below where you normally place your hand ) to see the autographs of "Riders In The Sky"!
