Popcorn Barrel Beagle Bass or The Dog House Bass
Alan gave me the can around Christmas time and said hey make something with this. 
It's one of those cans that caramel, cheese and regular popcorn come in. 
Jon Mcgee Once showed me an old wash tub bass his granddad made for him, so that's where I got the idea to use weed wacker string.  They work great, just like cat gut!  But I opted for two lines of varied diameters. 
The puppy on the can was sooo lonely that I couldn't bare to paint over him, which I started to do.  But I got to thinkin' about the whole dog house bass name, which is some times the name for the big upright double bass.  It has a pickup inserted in the bridge. 
This instrument has always been a problem to get to sound just right.  we are constantly tweaking things and every night it sounds different.  Playin' it through a crappy jeeter rig don't help matters much...But Alan really whales on this thing.
