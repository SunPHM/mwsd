APPETIZERS: Ken White

It was a storybook, whirlwind romance that brought French restaurateur Hany Zaky to Las Vegas.

Zaky was working in his Paris restaurant L'Apostrophe one night about two years ago when a Las Vegas woman walked in the door. It was Kim Anderson's first trip to Paris, her first trip outside the United States, in fact.

They struck up a conversation and "she came back seven nights in a row. I think it was the food that brought her back," Zaky says with a laugh.
