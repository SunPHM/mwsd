Zazen Bungalows is a charming small resort that has existed for more than 25 years. Nestled on Koh Samui's quieter, more traditional northern shore, it stands on the west coast of Bophut beach amid lush gardens facing its own palm-shaded beach. Bophut beach itself is 3 kilometers long and is considered the most typical fishing village of the island. Although there are several shops, bars and restaurants in Bophut village, this beach is famous for its relaxed atmosphere

        

This Boutique Hotel enjoys a secluded setting at the end of an unspoiled bay. In this tropical setting, the low-rise resort has been especially designed to reach peacefulness in harmony  with an irresistible combination of Thai-style decorated bungalows in beautiful natural surrounding and discreet service.

Guest Rooms

The bungalows at Zazen are famous for their orange sun-washed Mediterranean color with traditional red-tiled roofs nestled amid a fragrant garden, lotus pond and balmy coconut groves beside a palm fringed stretch of golden sandy beach.

Each room is designed like a small and elegant Thai home with beautiful wooden and terracotta tiled floors, wide glass fronted doors and a large veranda overlooking the beach or gardens.

Restaurant And Bar

Dining at Zazen's is a delight as much for the romantic setting as well as for the choice of culinary styles. The restaurant and its terrace covered by a wooden pergola offers breathtaking views of the nearby islands of Koh Phangan and Koh Som as well as the whole bay of Bophut. Delicious fresh seafood speciality dishes from around the world or traditional Thai fare are part of your dining options. The Restaurant offers the freshest seafood, including Sea bass, Red snapper, Barracuda, Shark, Squid, Prawns and Lobster. Tropical cocktails and an extensive selection of wines provide something special for everyone.

    

The  beach bar is located right on the edge of the water. This is the place where you can enjoy a great pool game with your partner or play table soccer with some friends. This "very lounge" bar plays great music, serves fantastic drinks and, if you are into snacking, some light meals.

    

Facilities

In the middle of the resort, between the gardens and the sea, lies a magnificent free-form tropical pool with waterfall, sparkling jacuzzi and children's pool. All around the swimming pool, you can still appreciate the sea view from your sun-bed while eating a salad for lunch or drinking a refreshing cocktail.

This little boutique hotel is also the place where you can choose between the pleasures of a peaceful lifestyle holiday retreat and the excitement of the endless leisure activities such as: windsurfing, kayaking, diving, snorkeling, beach volley, ping pong, petanque or playing pool, darts or table soccer at the ocean view bar.

Zazen Bungalows are pleased to announce the opening of Zazen Spa in September 2004, offering a whole range of relaxation and pampering facilities, from centuries old traditional Thai massage to a stress-relieving fragrant oil massage, from facials to manicures and pedicures. All hosted in quiet, elegant surroundings on site, Zazen Spa will become your benchmark for relaxation and care of both mind and body.
