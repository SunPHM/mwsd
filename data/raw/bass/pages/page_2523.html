Florida Bass Fishing Brag'n Board
10 3/4 lb'er - May 21 & 22, 2001

Florida wild shiner trophy bass fishing anglers Rick Perry (Indianapolis, IN - ph: 317-823-8775 - e-mail: rperry@mibor.net) and (left) Dean Trennepohl (Monticello, IN - ph: 219-583-6060 - e-mail: dtren@monti.net) caught 28 bass that included a 10 3/4 lb'er during 2 half days (May 21 & 22, 2001) of wild shiner bass fishing with Cap'n Ron. Dean caught the 10 3/4 lb'er. They boated 10 bass up to 5 lbs in the Harney Pond Canal on their first afternoon of shiner fishing. The next morning I fished the Point Of The Reef out on Lake Okeechobee where we boated 18 bass up to 10 3/4 lbs. These anglers released all bass except the 10 3/4 lb'er that was kept for the wall.
