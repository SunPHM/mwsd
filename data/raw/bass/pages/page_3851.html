How to Read a Dendrogram
Dendrograms provide a visual accounting of how closely related one animal is to another.  The more alleles two animals have in common, the closer they are related. 

BACK

The combined distance of horizontal lines indicates relative genetic similarity between animals   (vertical lines not included).

  • The shorter the distance, the greater the number of shared alleles
  • The longer the distance, the fewer the number of shared alleles.
