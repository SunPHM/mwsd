Lucien Bass III


When he was a University student, Lucien Bass (Engr ’63, Darden ’65) walked across the Lawn every evening on his way to the Corner for dinner at Jim’s Restaurant. "No matter how many times I walked there, there was always something special about it," recalls Mr. Bass, now serving as chair of Jefferson’s Circle, the annual giving society that provides discretionary funds for many restoration efforts. "I believe the Academical Village is a national treasure we have a responsibility to maintain for future generations." As reunion co-chair for the Class of 1963, Mr. Bass led the successful class effort to raise $250,000 for restoration of the Reading Room of Pavilion VII. The gift was made in honor of the class’s thirty-fifth-year reunion in 1998.
