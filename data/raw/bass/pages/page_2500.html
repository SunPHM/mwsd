Log Cottages near Seneca Rocks in Cabins, West Virginia
Perfect for families and honeymooners!


Our 16 Log and Cedar Cottages are located on the banks of a trout stream, the North Fork of the South Branch of the Potomac River. These riverside log cottages have a spectacular view twice featured in Wonderful West Virginia Magazine.

2001-2002 Hopeville Canyon Expansion

Hopeville Canyon Bridge Completed

1 mile of private stocked stream access

Within the Monongahela National Forest, our cottages are located just 12 miles from Seneca Rocks and 2 miles from Smoke Hole Caverns in the heart of the Spruce Knob-Seneca Rocks National Recreation Area. Nestled in the foothills of Dolly Sods this location offers access to outdoor activities such as hiking, mountain biking, canoeing, rock climbing, skiing, golfing, trout/bass fishing and hunting.

Family & Honeymoon Cottages

Cottage #1, #3, #14 & #15 (maximum 6 person) cottages have three bedrooms, a loft, two baths, kitchen and a great room with a rock fireplace.  The spacious loft contains the master bedroom and a bath.  The two bedrooms down stairs share a bathroom.  These cottages include a Private Outdoor Hot Tub and washer/dryer.

Cottage #9 & #13 (maximum 4 person) have two bedrooms, kitchen, 11/2 baths, and a spacious great room with a rock fireplace.  The loft contains the master bedroom and a ½ bath. These cottages include a Private Outdoor Hot Tub and washer/dryer.

Cottage #2 & #7 (maximum 4 person) cottages have two bedrooms, a full bath, Jacuzzi and a spacious great room with a rock fireplace. Each master bedroom has a queen-size bed. These cottages include a washer/dryer.

Cottage #4, #5 & #6 (maximum 2 person + child under 5) cottages have 1 bedroom. Each cottage has a queen size bed, a full bath, kitchen and a great room with a sleep-sofa and Jacuzzi facing the rock fireplace.

Cottage #10, #11 & #12 (maximum 2 person) cottages have 1 bedroom. Each cottage has a queen size bed, a full bath, kitchen and a great room with a rock fireplace and Jacuzzi.

Cottage #8 (maximum of 4 persons + child under 5) is privately owned. The cedar cottage features hard wood floors throughout; two bedrooms one with king size bed and the other with a standard bed, a full bath, kitchen, a great room with a sleep-sofa, rock fireplace with gas logs and covered deck with 2 porch gliders.

Cottage #16 (maximum of 6 persons) is privately owned.  This
cottage is located 1 mile from the Hopeville Canyon location.  It contains two
bedrooms one with king size bed and the other with two queen size beds, two
full baths, a kitchen, a great room with a sleep-sofa and rock fireplace.
Also, it includes a Jacuzzi and washer/dryer.

Cottage #17 (maximum of 8 persons) is privately owned. This recently completed riverside cottage is located 3 miles from the Hopeville Canyon location. It contains three bedrooms, a loft, 1 1/2 baths, kitchen and great room with a sleep-sofa. The spacious loft contains the master bedroom and a bath. The cottage has two downstairs bedrooms (one with a queen bed and one with a set of bunks and a twin bed). Fireplace and Hot Tub features are planned for the near future.

Cottages include: Rock Fireplace, Jacuzzi (except Cottage #8) or private outdoor hot tub, DSS Satellite TV w/VCR, A/C, stove and refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, toaster oven, coffee maker, dishes and utensils, pots and pans. We provide linens, towels and washcloths for cottage usage.

Office and GiftShop:  The office and giftshop are located directly across Rt 55 from the cottage entrance.  The gift shop has been expanded to include antiques, tee shirts, spin fishing and fly fishing rod/reel rentals, fishing supplies and an assortment of flies.

POLICIES

Smoking:No Smoking in cottages (Allowed outside only)

Pets: No pets allowed.

Maximum Occupancy: Each cottage has a maximum occupancy. The cost for each person above the maximum occupancy is $50 per night.

Payment: Cash, WV only check, Mastercard, Visa & Discover.

Reservations: At the time of a reservation request, a deposit will be required. The deposit will be 50% (at least $100.00) of the total cost of the reservation. The balance due for the entire reservation will be required at check-in. Written confirmation will be mailed to you with directions to the cottages.

Cancellations:We offer two options:
1. If your cancellation occurred 30 days or more prior to your arrival date, we offer a refund of your deposit less $20.00 booking fee.

2. If your cancellation occurred less than 30 days prior to your arrival date, your deposit will be forfeited. We do offer a refund of your deposit less $20.00 booking fee with the additional requirement that the accommodations re-rent for the entire period of resevation.

DIRECTIONS

Click to view map

Harman's North Fork Cottages is located on Rt. 55/28W 10 miles west of Petersburg, WV or 13 miles east of Seneca Rocks. We are two miles from Smoke Hole Caverns. (Signs to Smoke Hole Caverns are everywhere) We are located 300 yards from Jordan Run Road (Dolly Sods).

From DC: Take I-66 west to I-81 South. Exit 296 Strausburg on Rt. 55 W. Follow Rt. 55 W to Wardensville, Moorefield, Petersburg, WV.

From I-79: Take I-79 to Rt. 33 east. Follow Rt. 33 E. to Elkins then on to Seneca Rocks.

From South/East: Take I-81 north to Harrisonburg, VA. Exit on to Rt. 33 to Franklin, WV. At Franklin take Rt. 220 to Petersburg, WV.

From North/East: Take I-81 south to Winchester, VA. Exit on 37 bypass to Rt. 50 W. Take Rt. 50 W to Romney, WV. Take Rt. 50 W 8 miles from Romney to Rt. 220 S. Take Rt. 220 S to Moorefield, WV. Take Rt. 55 W from Moorefield to Petersburg, WV.

From I-64: At the White Sulphur Springs exit, take Rt. 219 north to Rt. 28 N. Follow Rt. 28 N. to Seneca Rocks.
