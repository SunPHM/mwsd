Come along with North American Outdoors. We'll take you in the boat, on the stand, in the blind and to the streams … all to make you feel part of the action as it takes place.

Airing January through March 2003 at 7:30 a.m. ET Saturdays on ESPN2


Broadcast Schedule

Dec. 30 & Jan. 4 — Costa Rica Sailfish and Bahamas Bonefish

Steve Pennaz is fishing with Crocodile Bay Lodge's Todd Staley. Todd is the director of fishing at this beautiful Costa Rican lodge. Steve and Todd are using the classic bait and switch technique for the acrobatic sailfish. Following that is Dan Larson fishing for bones on the flats in the Bahama Islands. A great saltwater experience to lead off our first show of North American Fisherman.

Jan. 11 — Mille Lacs Smallmouth

Springtime smallmouth bass fishing could be one of the most exciting fishing experiences any angler will ever experience. Why? SIGHTFISHING! Join Steve Pennaz and Mille Lacs Guide Dave Bentley on Minnesota's best-known walleye lake for aggressive smallmouth bass. You won't see this kind of action anywhere else! Steve and Dave pitch Yum's new Crawbug right on the beds and hang on as these smallies attack the bait.

Eddy's Lake Mille Lacs Resort
320-532-3657
www.eddysresort.com
Jan. 14 & 18 — Red Lake Pike

In search of a trophy pike, head to Canada right … wrong! Steve Pennaz heads to northern Minnesota for this trophy adventure. Red Lake is well known to anglers in the upper Midwest as a great crappie destination but as Steve finds out, trolling an A-Salt Bomber, you don't have to travel to Canada to find pike weighing more than 20 pounds.

Hudec's Resort on Red Lake
218-647-8291
www.hudecsresort.com

Gonzo's Guide Service

Jan. 21 & 25 — Alaska Salmon and Halibut

Dan Larson travels to Seward, Alaska for this ultimate Alaskan adventure. He teams up with fishing fanatic Raleigh Werking and Grande Alaskan Lodge owner Tim Berg for ocean run King Salmon, Silver Salmon, and giant Halibut. It has it all, breathtaking scenery, wildlife, and of course great fishing action. Don't miss this one!

Tim Berg's Alaskan Fishing Adventures
800-548-3474
www.alaskanfishing.com
Feb. 1 — Nueltin Pike and Lake Trout

Steve Pennaz and Berkley's Clay Norris head to this wonderful Canadian lodge in Northern Manitoba. Steve and Clay caught numerous Lake Trout on Cotton Cordell's Big Wiggle "O" but it was the monster pike Steve and Clay came to Nueltin for. Starting with surface bait like the Red Fin and Zara Spook provided lots of excitement but a single Daiichi hook with a seven inch Yum Ribbontail worm put the monster pike in the boat.

Nueltin Lake Lodge
800-361-7177
www.nueltin.com

Travel Manitoba

www.travelmanitoba.com
Feb. 8 — Venice, Louisiana Seatrout & Redfish

Steve Pennaz joins up with Brent Ballay of Sportsman's Outfitters for an awesome saltwater adventure on Louisiana's Gulf Coast. Brent Ballay will guide the North American Fisherman team out of Venice, Marina and the Mississippi Delta for sea trout and redfish.

Sportman's Outfitters

Feb. 15 — Alaska Trophy Pike

It's a pike fisherman's dream to land a pike over 50 inches. Few places in the world can provide that opportunity and the record books are proof. One of those places is the Innoko River located in the Alaskan interior and that's where we find Dan Larson. Dan is aboard the Midnight Sun, a floating palace owned and operated by Leon Randermann. Bomber Long A Magnums, Jitterbugs, Zara Super Spooks … you name the bait and the pike couldn't resist!

Midnight Sun Trophy Pike Adventures
1-800-440-7453
www.mstpa.com



Feb. 22 — Mexico Largemouth Bass

Lake El Salto is the hottest big bass producing lake in the world right now! Steve Pennaz is joined by Rebel's Andy Carroll for a Mexican bass adventure. Host Billy Chapman of Anglers Inn puts Steve and Andy on fish that will battle fish nearly 10 pounds. If you love big bass, this is a fiesta for you!

Hook Sportfishing Charters
1-800-583-8133
www.hooksportfishing.com

Anglers Inn
1-800-468-2347
www.anglersinn.com
Feb. 25 & March 1 — Cabela's MWC Walleye Championship

Join Steve Pennaz as he guides us through this exciting championship on the Mississippi River near Prairie du Chien on the Wisconsin/Iowa border. Fifty teams will compete for this prestigious walleye championship. Competitors will use a variety of techniques from trolling crankbaits to dragging live bait rigs to land big walleyes and hopefully land a big check.

Brisbois Motor Inn
800-356-5850

March 4 & 8 — Mille Lacs Smallmouth and Walleye

March 4 at 9 a.m. & March 8 at 7:30 a.m.
Steve Pennaz hooks up with Terry McQuoid, owner of McQuoid's Resort near Isle, MN. The smallmouth are caught using Yum's crawbugs and the walleyes are taken with a Rebel Minnow. A great show to get anglers warmed up for the spring bite.

McQuoid's Inn & Guide Service
800-862-3535
www.mcquoidsinn.com


March 15 — British Columbia King Salmon

Steve Pennaz enjoys one of Oak Bay Marine Groups outstanding King Salmon lodges on the beautiful British Columbia coastline. Steve experiences the Salmon Seeker found anchored in the Kano Inlet off the Queen Charlotte Islands. Big fish, beautiful surroundings, and a unique home base make this a great fishing experience. Also, Kurt Beckstrom of NAF and Larry McQuarrie of Sportsman's Cove Lodge fish for Coho Salmon in southeast Alaska near Ketchikan.

Oak Bay Marine Group
1-800-663-7090
www.obmg.com

Sportsman's Cove Lodge
1-800-962-7889
www.alaskasbestlodge.com
March 18 & 22 — Fall Smallmouth

March 18 at 9 a.m. & March 22 at 7:30 a.m.
Steve Pennaz and bass pro Paul Neumann fish a tributary of the Mississippi River near Minneapolis. Pitching jigs tipped with Yum's Crawbug or casting a Vibra King Tube proves deadly for these bass getting ready for winter.











March 29 — Lake Michigan Salmon

Steve Pennaz hooks up with Captain Chip Porter outside Racine, Wisconsin for salmon and lake trout. These two good friends troll Vibra King Tubes behind dodgers for kings, cohos, and lake trout with a few taken on Rebel Minnows to add some flavor to a fantastic day of fishing! A fun day full of action as only Lake Michigan can provide.
