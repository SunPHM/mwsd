Jeff Berlin Signature Bass from Dean Guitars

March 6, 2000 -- An instrument two years in the making is finally available - The Jeff Berlin Signature Series.

This instrument provides the most effortless playability of any of the Dean bass series to date. The three piece alder body coupled with a maple neck and thick ebony fingerboard provide warm sweet tones that match Jeff's strict standards.

Twin pickups from Bartolini are utilized being the pickups that Jeff has personally designed and selected for his instrument. A gold plated Badass bridge and gold schallers complete this instrument and whether you are looking for warm jazz tones or punchy rock tones you will find your sound in the Berlin Signature bass.

"It's like playing in melted butter on a teflon surface in zero gravity. And with killer tone," exclaims Jeff!

Also available is the Berlin Exotic utilizing a hand picked custom wood veneer.

For more information, visit Dean Guitars on the web at www.deanguitars.com.
