Gary Jeffery, owner of Right Bite Baits, fishes for both.  Landing this 3 lb. bass on Greer’s Ferry Lake was “almost” as much fun as when he and his fishing partner, Ric Cothren, won the Governor’s Trophy and Ranger Bass Boat in the Spring of 2002, using bass fishing lures from Right Bite Baits.

Gary started Right Bite Baits in 1992 and initially focused on making spinner baits and buzz baits for his fishing buddies and a few local tackle shops.  From that, the business has grown to where he currently supplies retail bait and tackle shops in 25 states and Canada. Right Bite’s plastic fishing lures are made here in Northeast Arkansas.  Our commitment to our customers has never wavered.  Our goal is to provide the best quality, best selection, and best price possible.  VMC Hooks, McCoy Mean Green Line, Tube and Worm Rattles, Lead Heads, Fish Attractants and Dye are among the many other items available. Each product is tested and measures up to Right Bite Baits rigorous standards.

Great pride is taken in the quality of all our fishing lures. Gary is the type of person who pays attention to detail -- both when fishing, and of course, in making his artificial lures. All of our spinner baits are hand made to order, using only the finest materials. They are also available in a variety of weights.  All Right Bite plastic baits come in a large assortment of colors, several sizes, and are all salt impregnated.  He knows that while there is no substitute for skills learned through experience while fishing, the next biggest factor is probably the bait or fishing lures you are using.
