Saturday, May 22, 2004

Food food food!



So much for dieting and going to the gym. I couldn't resist food when it's free! Steak, lobsters, sea bass, lamb... the best that Cafe Ysabel and Red had to offer. Of course shooting the food before consuming them only delayed my pleasure, but when you're with four Youngstar ladies on a food trip, you can't do much but shoot fast before the food gets cold. Cool way to spend a rainy Saturday huh?

Oh, Karla has a new boy. Uuuuuuuuuuyyyy!
