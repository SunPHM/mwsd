THE RIVER CABINS!

These cabins have three bedrooms, 21/2 baths and sit only 120 feet from the water! Each cabin will sleep up to 12 quests, has a private beach and a deck overlooking the beautiful Eminence bluff hole. Call now for details and reservations!


If you're looking for a fun filled vacation, relaxing at the river's edge, floating, fishing, hunting, tubing, swimming, horseback riding, walking nature trails, hiking, cycling, golfing, canoeing, or picnicking, come to the River's Edge Resort.



Our private beach includes porch swings, hammock, picnic tables, barbecue grills, chairs, campfire site, (firewood provided), swing set and 1/4 mile of private riverfront to enjoy, fish, tube, and relax.





Come see us in Winter! Special off-season rates. Watchable wildlife all year. Best time to come is when things are nice and quiet. Week after Memorial Day and week before Labor Day. Also spring, fall and winter. See our off season and week-day rates. Ask about our April Dogwood Special!





It's new and cool! Trophy small mouth bass fishing like nowhere else on the upper Jack's Fork River. We're also in the heart of the state's premier trout streams.



"Alley Spring Mill is the most picturesque spot in Missouri." It's only about 5 minutes from River's Edge to the Old Mill. Storytelling, demonstrations, native music, and a variety of arts and crafts will be offered on various weekends throughout the summer.

 

Our Resort offers use of the hot tub and Gazebo overlooking the river, also innertubes for floating, firewood for a campfire on the beach, ping pong, volleyball, horseshoes, barbecue grills, use of private beach facilities. Coffee maker with coffee in room, cable TV.
