Meet DEPARTURE

DEPARTURE is more than a band. It is made up of a dynamic group of individuals.

Let's get to know them a bit better. Starting with Matt....

 

NAME : MATT - AKA "Gator"

INSTRUMENT : Bass Guitar

ROLE IN BAND : Matt acts as the booking agent for departure. Why? Because he is the one with a phone...

MUSICAL INFLUENCES : Entwistle, Sting, Bruce, Craig, Kaye

QUOTE : "It's not really that way..."

 

 

Here comes Sean .....

NAME : SEAN

INSTRUMENT : Drums

ROLE IN BAND : Besides drumming, Sean acts as the PR / Marketing guru for the band.

MUSICAL INFLUENCES : Gadd, Cobham, White

QUOTE : "Do we all start together on this?"

 

And then there is Bev.....

NAME : BEV
