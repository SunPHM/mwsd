 Welcome to White Oak Shores Camping & RV Resort, Folks!

Nappin' Nanna here to continue your online tour!

White Oak Shores Camping & RV Resort sits along North Carolina's pristine Crystal Coast. Surrounded by the tranquil, flowing waters of White Oak River and set amongst coastal marshes, this resort offers guests peaceful surroundings and a perfect "base camp" for exploring the many attractions of this beautiful area!

This is the first year White Oak Shores is open to the public, so please excuse the little growing pains that may occur from time to time. I assure you folks that this park will grow up fast to become one of the premier parks in the Carolinas!

Anglers listen up, because White Oak Shores might become your second home! Whether you cast a line from the soon-to-be-built onsite pier, from the shore, in the surf, or from your boat, you'll be in "fish heaven!" Reel in "prize size" fish from the river, Bogue Sound or the ocean!

Even if your idea of paradise isn't found behind a fishing rod, there's plenty to enjoy right here with the resort's planned amenities. For kids of all ages, splash your way through the White Oak Shores Mini Water Park, featuring a slide, swimming & kiddie pools with a few water features. Host your reunion, rally or other gathering in the Recreation Hall or Pavilions. Turn the little ones loose at the playgrounds while you enjoy basketball, volleyball or, if you're like me, a nice long snooze at your spacious campsite!

Nanna knows a secret for all you canoe and kayak lovers: The White Oak River is one of the premier canoeing and kayaking areas on the Crystal Coast. Plus, within minutes of White Oak Shores are many areas set aside for those who like to paddle their way around. Come and check out the many water sports activities at and around White Oak Shores.
