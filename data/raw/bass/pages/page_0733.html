1921 Conn New Wonder "Giant" EEb Bass
This is one of Conn's famous New Wonder "Giant" or Contra EEb tubas (model 20J) which Conn claimed "possesses the same calibre and volume of tone as the Monster Bb Bass".   It is indeed a monster with a 20 inch bell,  and 37 inches height overall and weighing 17 pounds.  It is silverplated with overall gold wash, most of which is surprizingly still intact. Conn provided options for finish and 4th valves  as stated in their advertisements:  "Beautifully engraved, triple silver plated over blast velvet finish, inside of bell burnished, pearl inlaid finger tips Price $132.00; with 4 valves, $145.00".  The engraving on the bell (shown below) is  truly elegant and consistant with the professional level tubas made by Conn in the 20's.   This tuba, (no.175879)  shows almost no signs of dents or repairs despite its 83 years of service.
