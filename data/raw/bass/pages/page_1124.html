Have you seen the headlines?

'Guitar rock is back.'

We're happy to hear it. Except at Ibanez, guitar rock never 'left' - and if you're the type of player who follows Ibanez news, you've been too busy playing bass guitar to worry a whole lot about the media's take on music. However, the papers are right about one thing: there are more new bass guitar styles than ever before. That's why there are more different kinds of basses in the Ibanez line up.
