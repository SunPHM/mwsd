Fly-in Fishing Fun For Everyone

Our Ontario Fly-in Fishing Packages have attracted sportsmen and women for many years. Come, enjoy the vast wilderness, here in the heart of Northwestern Ontario!

See the new Google Earth Maps of our location!

We have 12 remote lakes to choose from.  Our Guests often boast of 8 - 10 lbs walleye, and northern in excess of 20 lbs! We also offer lake trout and excellent small mouth bass fishing.

The traditional wilderness experience was, at one time, considered a rugged adventure. Over the past few years however, we have added many amenities to our facilities to increase the comfort of our guests.

As a result, we have noticed a marked increase in the number of couples, families and retired travelers who return, time and again, to experience our beautiful wilderness.
