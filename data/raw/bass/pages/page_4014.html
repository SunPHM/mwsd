The Philosophy behind our menu is quite simple. We select the freshest, finest, most wholesome ingredients available and create recipes to present them at their best. Every element, from the sauces to the desserts, are made on the premises, including the ice cream.

Whenever possible, we use local sources, organic produce, artisan producers, and specialty suppliers. There are no canned "bases" or "prepared" ingredients in our pantry. This is reflected in a menu that changes from week to week and often, day to day. Our menu is not extensive, but each item is a specialty, individually prepared to order and presented as if to please royalty.

We pledge to offer a wide variety of foods and to include meat, fish, fowl, and vegetarian entrées. There are favorite and signature dishes, but there is no formula or preconceived notion of things we must have on the menu that would supersede our mantra of freshness and quality. Fresh new foods stimulate the creative passions of our chefs. We strive to present wholesome dishes that are also delicious and exciting.

The combined experience of our kitchen staff equates to over 60 years and draws from a wide variety of influences. There are no titles. Each member of the Upstairs Downtown staff is respected as a professional and encouraged to express individual creativity on a continuous and ongoing basis.

In keeping with our philosophy, we have assembled an innovative and consumer-friendly wine list. There is a variety and value from West Coast producers, both large and small. Our bottled wines and those we offer by the glass are selected on the basis of how good they taste and how well they match with our cuisine.

We hope you enjoy your dining occasion with us and will share your experience with your friends and acquaintances. If you are pleased, we want to hear from you. If you are disappointed, we especially want to hear from you. We aspire to provide a constantly-changing and improving venue, influenced by all those who participate, most of all our patrons.
