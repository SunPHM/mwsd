That guitar is ok but you'll never make a living with it
We had Christmas at Fred and Karen's house yesterday. We all had a great time just being together, feasting and opening presents. Charles took pictures of the festivities.

Christmas day is also my brother Fred's birthday. We draw names now for Christmas presents but I got Fred a birthday present. About a week ago I heard snippets from the new Beatles' album Let It Be...Naked, I thought what I had heard amazing even though some reviewers didn't like it. I just knew Fred would love it. Back in 1970 Fred bought me the first Let It Be album, handed it over and said, "Hold on to this, this is the last album they will ever make." He was right. I still have that album.

So one of the highlights of the day for me was when Fred put the CD into one the machines in his music studio, fired up his bass guitar and started "jamming" with the fab four. It was a very special moment indeed.
