Pop's Pride

 

Inshore
Enjoy a half-day of bottom fishing for fluke, blues, sea bass, flounder, stripers, weakfish, tog, and cod. Leave the dock at 6:00 a.m. for a morning trip or at noon for an afternoon. We supply all fishing tackle, bait, and instruction. $360

Stripers
Stripers have recovered and are again providing great fishing. A cabin and enclosed flybridge makes the half-day trips a comfortable fishing adventure. $360

Inshore Full Day
Blues, bonito, false albacore, dolphin, and stripers are caught trolling. Sea bass, porgies, and tog are caught in quality numbers when fishing New Jersey's best wrecks and artificial reefs. Fluke, weakfish, and croakers abound. $585

Shark Mania
Drifting for Jaws can be done between May and November. Makos are most plentiful in June. Full day sharking includes bait. Offshore $650

Offshore Sea Safari
Full day trips out to 40 miles catch bluefin, tuna, yellowfin tuna, dolphin, wahoo, and white marlin in season. Includes bait and tackle. $795

Canyon Adventures
Troll or chunk for big game, Tuna, Marlin, Wahoo, Shark & Dolphin
One day trips provide an unforgettable experience fishing the edges of the continental shelf, 70-80 miles offshore. These 15 hour plus adventures offer fantastic fishing for tuna and other big game fish. $1,295.

Overnight 2 Day
A two day trip includes trolling plus overnight chunking and assures a productive trip. Leave and return to dock at agreed upon times. $1,895.
