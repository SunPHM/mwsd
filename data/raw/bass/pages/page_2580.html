Florida Wild Shiner Bass Fishing
Brag'n Board: March 2001

The month of March came in like a lamb. Some good catches were made during the warm spell. Okeechobee's bass were preparing to go on the beds to spawn on the March full moon. A cold front which brought our morning temperatures down in the low 40's and one morning it was a very chilly 37 degree moved the bass off the beds and shut down the fishing. The forecast for the rest of this month is expected to be excellent. We should not have any more cold fronts until next winter - I hope...

Lake Okeechobee's low water conditions which has congregated the bass in the deep water areas of the lake, making them easier to catch, is really helping my guide parties land some good size bass. Trophy bass fishing forecast for the March and April spawn under the low water conditions should be the best ever.
