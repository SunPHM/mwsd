Millennium Bass Fishing Reports

View "nine years" of Florida bass fishing reports on the renowned Lake Okeechobee. These Florida bass fishing reports are about bass that were caught while wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee by anglers from across the United States. Our Florida bass fishing reports are also cataloged under each month they were caught during the 2006, 2005, 2004, 2003, 2002, 2001, 2000, 1999 and 1998 calendar years. A fishing report on amount of bass caught, area caught on Lake Okeechobee and other information is listed with each Florida bass fishing report. To ensure validity the angler's name, address and e-mail address is also listed with every fishing report. E-mail testimonials received from our clients are also posted just as we receive them.
