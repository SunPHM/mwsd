FRESHWATER FISHING
Ask some of the world's top anglers and they'll tell you that there are some freshwater fishing experiences that can't be beat. How about when you see that bass grab hold of you line and jump out of the water? How about when you feel the pull of an angry catfish or bullhead and the battle begins? How about when you've gone the whole day without catching a thing and you suddenly come across a blue gill nest? How about that rare but beautiful moment when that tip of the pole bends and you know there's something mammoth and record setting on the other end?

Yes, freshwater fishing is a lot more than sitting in an aluminum boat, having a couple beers and burning your neck and back. For true adventurers, it's a hunt in the heady waters of Lake Anywhere, America. And true anglers know there are a lot of fish to be caught and a lot of stories left to tell.

Below you will find adventure companies offering the very best in freshwater fishing adventures.
