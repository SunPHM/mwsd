Postcard of the Bass Rock

Front

This postcard of the Bass Rock in the Firth of Forth is No 2 in a series of over 500 produced by Reginald Phillimore.  It is based on one of his paintings.

Reginald Philimore painted many views of this rock, which he cold see from his studio in North Berwick.  The sailing boat in the lower right, LH376, is from Leith.
