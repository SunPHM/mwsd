Play Guitar

You know the guitar music that you'd like to play. Now you can stop dreaming and start doing it!

Maybe you already know some and would like to play it better. Maybe you'd like to toughen up your style, improve your leads, find some new sounds or have the ability to push the limits more.

If you want any of the above and have a desire to learn we can help you take care of the rest. Don't stop now!

Our interactive software is just like having your own personal teacher but better. Better because it is a teacher that can help you as long as you need it to, when ever you need help. It never gets tired or critical and is always constructive.

You can also learn your way when ever it suits you without anyone looking over your shoulder.

Our difference. We give you the whole picture so you can get a sound you'll love.

Our audio and video, finger and music diagrams will give you the feel of the music. Not just the fingering for the notes but the rhythm, the phrasing, and all the touches that make it sound fantastic. All tricks you can use to make you sound fantastic that will help you with whatever you play later on.

What will you learn in our guitar lessons?
  • Learn 5 different guitar styles - Folk, Rock, Alternative, Punk, Grunge and Blues.
  • How to play guitar by ear, work out how to play your favourite songs.
  • All the chords required to give you a solid foundation to try the fancy stuff.
  • The tricks that Rock legends use to make their guitars squeal sing thump and purr!
  • Simple finger exercises to get you started.
  • The top 3 mistakes most beginner guitar player's make and how to avoid them.
  • Guitar holding techniques and how to minimize aches and pains.
  • How to read guitar tablature (tabs) without the need to be able to read music.
  • Two simple tricks to make you sound like a seasoned pro in less than one hour!
  • How to play any major or minor chord in three different places on the guitar.
  • Secrets to playing lead guitar revealed!
  • Master the techniques needed to play fast, thumping cascading lead breaks!
  • Simple guitar "picking" tips.
