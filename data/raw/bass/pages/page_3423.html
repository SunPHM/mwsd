Weaks/SeaTrout fisherman
Frustrated by the number of times you hook weakfish (a.k.a. SeaTrout) but can't get them into the boat because the hook pulls free?
Weakfish are notorious for their soft (weak) mouths, hence the name Weakfish. Many of them are what are called 'short strikers'. They do not take enough of the bait into their mouth to make the hook set properly. I designed a jig that uses a two toned worm that gets their attention and added a second hook that is inserted into the worm at the tail to hook those short strikers. You add a thin squid strip that equals the length of the worm to the main hook.
