LAKE OKEECHOBEE BASS FISHING REPORTS
1999 - 1997


Lake Okeechobee women anglers bass fishing reports and pictures from the calendar years of 1999 - 1997. This Florida freshwater lake over the 17 years I have operated a trophy bass fishing guide service on has produced on a person to person (male to female) ratio "more trophy bass to women anglers" It seems the girls are able to put more Okeechobee trophy bass in the boat far more offen then the experienced men anglers!
