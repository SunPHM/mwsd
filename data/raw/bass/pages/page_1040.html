Jam # 1

The first annual Les Paul Jam and Tone Conference December 13th 2003 was a huge success.

Tone brothers from as far a way as West Virginia braved the ice to come and enjoy Les Paul friendship.

The jam took place in the Revi Technical Wear Factory.

We had a great tme showing our LP's and trading chops for about 5 hours. Special thanks to Hambone Alley for laying down a tight background groove.

I hope we can all do this again next spring and continue the great times.

Thanks to all for coming. Thanks also to the Les Paul Forum for enabling us to find each other.

I will be uploading MP3's of the jam soon.
