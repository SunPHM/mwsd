Lake Dancy is located in the center of TheSouthern Legend Outfitters Game Preserve(6500 acre) game preserve, providing anglers with an unmatched esthetic surrounding. Located in Central West Alabama, bisected by the Alabama-Mississippi state line, Lake Dancy is 15 miles SW of Aliceville.
Convenient all weather access, including gravel roads and a concrete boat ramp insures a hassle free day on the water.
Numerous design features were "built in" to create structural diversity. The lake was built around a long and winding creek, surrounded by hardwood timber and brush. None of the woods have been cut down, making an ideal habitat for large fish. Acres of standing timber will not allow a bassboat to "get in", but at the same time provides hiding places were large bass can keep on growing and growing.

Extraordinary care has been given to forage fish. The crawfish population, already native to the creek, has exploded and provides a high protein addition to all the other available forage. Two species of bream and threadfin shad as well as tilapia have been released and are doing extremely well in this properly fertilized impoundment.
Most of the bass that is being caught are fat and are known as "football fish", due to well rounded girth, which makes a 14inch bass weigh approximately 3 1/2pounds!
