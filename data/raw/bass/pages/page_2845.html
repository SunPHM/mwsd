FLORIDA BASS FISHING BRAG'N BOARD TROPHY BASS FISHING REPORTS:
MAY 1999

Florida trophy bass fishing during the month of May yielded plenty of bass fishing action with good catches of 20 or more bass landed per each day of fishing. Florida trophy size bass were also taken that scaled from 6 to 8 1/2 lbs. Wild shiner fishing of course was the most productive method. s fished under a float along the weed edges was the best method used.



Florida bass fishing pair of 6 lb. bass
Brian Ouderkirk (Davenport, IA - ph: 319-324-2733) and brother Ed (Davenport, IA - ph: 319-323-4939) caught about 80 bass up to 6 lbs during 5 days (May 10 - 14, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Brian caught two bass that weighed 6 lbs each and an 11 3/4 lb. mudfish. His brother Ed landed a 5 3/4 lb. bass and several others in the 4 - 5 lb. range. A pickerel that weighed at least 4 lbs was also boated by Ed. The best catch of the trip was a 10 1/2 lb. huge bass that I noticed floating on top of the water struggling to stay submerged. Upon netting this beauty after several tries, we discovered that it had a very large fish in its stomach that caused it to be unable to stay submerged. This trophy bass as of this time is recovering in one of my shiner tanks. I have been treating it with antibiotics and will release it in Okeechobee soon. I fished the Monkey Box and North Shore areas of Lake Okeechobee. All bass caught were released.


Florida bass fishing 6 lb. bass
Frank Bandel and son Brendan (Lake Worth, FL - ph: 561-798-6961) caught and released 16 bass that included a 6 lb'er during 2 half days (May 8 & 9, 1999) of Florida wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee with Ron's Guide Service. Bass where caught in the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee.


Florida bass fishing 6 1/2 lb. bass
Peter Josef and brothers Joe and Tom (Manchester, NH - ph: 603-623-6454 - e-mail: pjosef@rpmcs.com) caught about 35 bass up to 6 1/2 lbs during 3 days (May 7 - 9, 1999) of bass fishing with Cap'n Ron. They fished with wild shiners and wade fished using artificial lures. Joe caught the 6 1/2 lb'er on a wild shiner. He also had a 5 lb. pickerel, a great white egret and a 2 foot alligator that hit his shiner also. All bass, as well as the pickerel, egret and alligator were released.


Florida bass fishing 8 1/2 and 7 1/4 lb. bass
Joe Klaire (N. Palm Beach, FL - ph: 561-624-7448 - e-mail: JKlair@aol.com) and Moish Novick caught and released 29 bass that included a 8 1/2, a 7 1/4 and a 7 lb'er during 2 half days (May 6 & 7, 1999) of Florida wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee with Ron's Guide Service. Joe caught the 8 1/2 and 7 lb'er while Moish hooked and landed the 7 1/4 lb'er. Bass where caught in the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee.


Florida bass fishing 7 3/4 lb. bass
Darren Young (Charlestown, NH - ph: 603-826-9735) his dad Dan Young (Charlestown, NH - ph: 603-835-6525) and Ron Klink (Acworth, NH - ph: 603-835-2996) caught about 50 bass up to 7 3/4 lbs on wild shiners during the 3 days (May 2 - 4, 1999) they fished with Cap'n Ron (Ron's Guide Service). Ron caught the 7 3/4 lb'er which he is having mounted. All the other bass (many of which ran in the 4 - 5 lb range) were released. Fish were caught in the Monkey Box and North Shore areas of Lake Okeechobee.


Florida bass fishing 7 1/4 and 6 1/2 lb. bass
Tyson Lee (Tuscumbia, AL - ph: 256-314-6346), Clayton Brown (Little Rock, AR - ph: 501-661-9843) and David Dollar (Hector, AR - ph: 501-284-5021) caught and released 61 bass that included a 7 1/4, a 6 1/2, a 5 1/2 and a 5 lb'er during 3 days (Apr. 30 - May 2, 1999) of Florida wild shiner fishing on Lake Okeechobee with Ron's Guide Service. Tyson caught the 7 1/4 lb'er. Clayton caught the 6 1/2 lb'er and David caught the 5 and 5 1/2 lb'ers. Bass where caught in the Monkey Box area of Lake Okeechobee.
