ABOUT OUR LODGE

Clearwater Lodge, is the resort for those
who love Canadian fishing, hunting or relaxing.

Clearwater Lodge, accessible only by boat, is located at the far north end of Clearwater (Burdiff) Lake and next to Quill Lake, which leads into the fabulous Piperstone chain of lakes. These sheltered waters harbor many species of gamefish, which include Musky, Lake Trout, Small and Largemouth Bass, Walleye, and the Great Northern Pike. These lakes are all crystal clear and pollution-free.

The scenery in our area is absolutely beautiful with its inter-connected waterways and high, rocky bluffs covered with stately pines and white birch trees. It'll make you forget about the pressures, burdens and problems of a fast-moving world. This is the place to go for fishing, hunting, relaxing or just plain getting away from it all.

The cabins you will be staying in are all housekeeping, mostly of log construction, solidly built and in good condition. These private cabins are all equipped with electric lighting, refrigerators, coffee brewers, toasters, microwave ovens, and gas ranges, plus the necessary items such as cooking utensils, dinnerware and linens, although we do not furnish towels. We also have a centrally-located shower house and fish freezing facilities at our main location. Our resort is a wilderness camp that we are very proud of.

A short distance from our main camp is the Clearwater Mechanical Portage. This portage will carry you and your equipment into or from the Upper Lakes.

Besides our main camp, we have two outpost cabins, one of which is accessible by boat, and the other accessible by boat and by portaging.

Quill Lake: Boat-in, close to main camp, all remodeled, cabin sleeps 4-6. Private, but not isolated.

Dash Lake: Portage in, small rustic trapper cabin, sleeps 4, excellent Lake Trout and good size Northern, easy access to Piperstone Lkae.

When entering Canada, you are allowed to bring with you all necessary food supplies, (within reason) plus 1 carton of cigarettes and 40 ounces of alcohol per adult duty free. Additional food supplies may be acquired at Fort Frances, Emo or Sportsman Landing on Clearwater Lake where we will then take you by boat to our resort. Licenses and alcoholic beverages may also be purchased at the landing.

Items not to be brought into Canada are handguns of any type. Also on the banned list are plants or any vegetable which can be transplanted.

Items which you should bring:

Fishing tackle, flashlight, camera, raingear, insect repellant, personal gear, warm clothing in case of cool weather, and, of course, your food supplies. If you have a fish locator, it would be a great help when fishing new waters.
