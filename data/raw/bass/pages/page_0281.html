Captain Bob

Captain Bob Wadsworth along with his father Capt. John Wadsworth own

and operate the Sunbeam fleet out of Waterford CT. Captain Brown is a third

generation fisherman behind his father and grandtofather.  With over 20 years

of operating various vessels in the fleet Captain Bob is well versed in both

inshore and offshore fishing.   The Sunbeam fleet offers half-day fishing trips

 daily (except wednesdays) for blues and baths.

 

Wednesday is fluke fishing day.  Fluke fishing is from 7 AM to 3 PM.

We also offer night bass trips every Wednesday, Friday and

Saturday nights.  Private charters are also available for groups of 6

to 149 passengers. To contact us call or write:
