Music and Entertainment

My oldest son Mark, is a member of Midget, an alternative band from Indianapolis Indiana. Midget fuses elements of heavy rock with funk and psychedelia. Their songs travel unconventional paths of tension and release that elevate to peaks of intensity and sedate into valleys of narcotic groove. Incessantly evolving their sound, Midget has only begun to tap into their musical potential. In October 2003, they released their first CD, a four song demo entitled “Person, Place, or Thing?” This demo was recorded and produced entirely by Midget. Black Sturgeon Lake Camp in Kenora, Ontario

If you like the photo above of our favorite vacation spot, I've got links to additional photos so you can check out more of the beauty of Black Sturgeon Lake in Ontario Canada. We have been vacationing at Black Sturgeon Camp since 1973 but we just can't seem to get enough! The camp is run by Carl and Debbie Snow, and is a great place for kids of all ages. Bald eagle and Golden eagle sightings are common! I enjoy fishing for smallmouth bass and they have some of the strongest I've ever caught. My wife owns the family record for largest smallmouth, 4.25 pounds! It was caught in the Winnipeg river, which is reachable by boat from the campground. The Winnipeg river is also known for it's great Walleye fishing! I can send you more information and directions to Black Sturgeon Camp resort if you are interested.
