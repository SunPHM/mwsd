JP Jigs - Quality Jigs for everything BASS

One of the most components of bass fishing is a quality jig attack. Quality hooks paired with a jig style to trick the bass whatever the situation. JP Jigs touch just about every angle of bass fishing with jigs imaginable. From tube jigs to brass Texas rigs, the can fit into every situation on the water.

BassDigest worked with these jigs for the past month and use each jig in situation after situation. Although some styles caught more fish than others, one thing was clear - Quality. The jigs offer high quality Gamakatsu hooks and precision poured weights. Each jig came out of the bag ready for battle.

One jig we took a liking to was the JP Spot getter. The Gamakatsu EWG hook paired with an inset mushroom head (below) was a big winner. this jig is unique and can tear apart a boat dock in no time. Paired with a 4.5 worm this jig can touch and skip into places under docks and around boats other jig combinations just dream about. Hitting the locations means - MORE FISH. We took fish out of the deepest areas of docks with great success. The Gamakatsu hook stuck fish and held them to the boat, important when you work the fish out around pilings.

The second style we liked a ton was the Senko keeper hook. This has a small screw assembly into the eye of the hook so you can get a Florida rig going without any weight. The Senko fits perfectly to the hook and is extremely weed less. Another great dock pattern.  JP Jig's has teamed up with Al's worms to offer a great one-two combination to your tackle box. We suggest trying the spot getters, but really check out the whole line of products JP Jig's has to offer.
