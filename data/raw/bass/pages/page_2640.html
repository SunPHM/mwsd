Lake Okeechobee: Honeymooners Bass Fishing Adventures 2005 - 2000


Who come to Florida do go bass fishing on their honeymoon! Wife's usually surprise their husband with a fishing trip to Florida while they make plans for a boat cruise in the Caribbean. I have put together a collection of pictures and other information about honeymoon couples who have Florida trophy bass fished with Ron's Guide Service during their honeymoon.



4 lb. bass - June 1, 2003 & Bluegill Fishing Report - May 31, 2003

HONEYMOON
Bass & Bluegill Fishing
anglers Mark & Erin Maloy (Noblesville, IN - ph: 317-770-7200) caught 6 bass up to 4 lbs during a half day morning of bass fishing with Cap'n Ron on June 1, 2003. This couple also caught about 60 bluegills on live crickets during a half day afternoon of bluegill fishing with Cap'n Ron on May 31, 2003. Bluegills were caught in the Twin Pond Canal area of Lake Okeechobee. Bass were caught in the North Shore area of the lake.

Pair Of 5 1/2 lb. Florida Bass
Florida Bass Fishing Trip September 20, 2001

Honeymoon Bass Fishing

Honeymoon bass fishing couple Josh and Sara Zenner (Athens, WI - ph:715-829-2952 - e-mail: saraannzenner@yahoo.com - e-mail: joshz@negconsulting.com) caught 13 bass up to 5 1/2 lbs during 1 day of wild shiner bass fishing on September 20, 2001 with Cap'n Ron. They each caught a 5 1/2 lb'er which is the biggest bass she or her husband ever caught. They also had a 5 1/4 lb, 5 lb, 4 1/2 lb, 4 lb and seven others from 3 - 3 1/2 lbs that they caught and released. Fished the Monkey Box and Point of the reef areas of Lake Okeechobee.
