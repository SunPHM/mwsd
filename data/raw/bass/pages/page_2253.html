A Partial List of Exhibitors

The Second Annual Puget Sound Stringed Instrument Exhibit will be open to visitors from noon to 6:00 p.m. on Saturday, October 4 and noon to 5:00 on Sunday October 5, at Renton Technical College near Seattle.

Below is a partial list of exhibitors, with pictures and links to websites where available. Please check back for updates!

David Krause has twenty years' experience in archtop guitars, mandolins, and violins, and is a teacher besides.
David Haxton's innovative techniques for making rosettes have made him well known among Seattle-area luthiers.
The oud, an instrument both ancient and modern, is the focus of Seattle builder Scott McKee.
Mike Doolin, inventive designer and builder of functional and beautiful guitars.
Jay Hargreaves , author, bassist and exponent of Kasha design.
James Wilson, Seattle.
Kerry Char, Portland.
James Campbell-Drury, Seattle.
Lyndon Kidwell, Preston, Washington.
