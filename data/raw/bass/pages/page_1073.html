They are actually pretty accurate about the beliefs of Islam. The only thing I don't like is then the pastor starts pointing out why the religion is "wrong" - "wrong" meaning it disagrees with the teachings of the Presbyterian church. But the pastor is pretty open-minded and asked afterwrds if anyone was offended by the class, which was polite. Afterwards I helped this little old lady my mom has Bible study classes with to move into her new apartment. Why can't agnostics be considered moral, too? ***smacks head for trying to debate deep, moral issues*** Okay, well, after that I was forced to wash the car. It reminded me of a simpler time, when I didn't have to worry about college and extracurricular activities and my future and all that crap. I went inside... and here I am.
