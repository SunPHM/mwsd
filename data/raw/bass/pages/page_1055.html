Introducing ...

Stuart Speight

Bass guitarist and newest member of the band, Stuart unfortunately suffers from "Fender Spenderitis", a rare condition that causes sufferers to spend all of their money on bass guitars.

Stuart currently owns 365 bass guitars ( "... one for each day of the year. "), and is in the process of building a new apartment block to house the 365 he expects to purchase next year. 

Stuart has the largest collection of Alma Cogan records in the country, and hopes one day to prove that she, and not Noel Redding, played bass on Jimi Hendrix's "Hey Joe"

Stuart and wife Nikki live with their 365 children in a converted double bass in Bass-ingstoke.
