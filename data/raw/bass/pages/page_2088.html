Orpheon Masterclasses and Summer Courses

Hvar (Croatia)
12 - 22 August, 2004



View of the City of Hvar and harbour



The course will focus on chamber music of the Renaissance and the Baroque Periods. Masterclass for viola da gamba and baroque strings. Viol consort, ensembles with violins and viols, chamber works with other instruments. Participants will take part in concerts in Hvar and on some of the nearby islands. Historical instruments will be made available to interested students upon request: Baroque violins, violas, violoncelli, violas da gamba.
This course offers an excellent opportunity for players of traditional string instruments to get acquainted with Baroque performance practice.

Application fee: 30 Euros
Course fee: 250 Euros
Accommodation: There are hotels with single, double and tripple rooms. However, we usually rent
apartments or get private quarters for the students at much more reasonable prices. This amounts to
about 13 to 15 Euros per night.
