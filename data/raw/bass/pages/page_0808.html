"Fishing for Striped Bass - Live Bait" features Al Lorenzetti catching stripers with live bait in the area of Fire Island Inlet near Babylon, New York. Al takes you on several outings during the course of a season and shows how to work all the variables to insure your fishing success. This video is a must for anyone interested in catching big striped bass.

 

Fresh live bait is the key to success. Al covers techniques for catching and maintaining bait for the day of fishing. Snagging and cast netting are featured.

 

 

Electronics help to provide the extra edge for success. Al covers their operation, focusing upon the use of sonar recorders. Interpretation of what you see is critical and Al discusses the finer points using high quality video graphics and animation.


.

Terminal rigging is always critical while bait fishing. Tying knots commonly used for bait fishing are shown in close up detail. Al shows how to set up and use his special snap attachment for rigging big live baits. Storing, handling and rigging live eels is also covered in depth.

 

When you’ve done your homework, catching fish is a sure bet. Al reviews angling techniques that are most effective. Boat handling, reading the water, using ranges and electronics to return to productive spots are covered. Proven methods for landing, handling and releasing fish are also discussed.


To top it all off, Al takes us on a memorable trip with a couple of his charter customers. Great shots of wild striper fishing on a cool fall day are enough to make you want to jump in the boat and join in the fun!
