Reel Mexican Adventures - Peacock Bass Trips


change font size

Although largemouth bass fishing in Mexico has been a specialty of ours for years, we demonstrate this same passion for peacock bass and other exciting gamefish of South America, having more than 13 years of experience sending anglers to the Amazon/Orinoco regions of South America.

Whether you're a first-time peacock bass angler or a seasoned South American veteran, we hope that PeacockBassOnline will provide you with factual, no-nonsense information that will help you locate and catch peacock bass. The material contained within the pages of our sister peacock bass site was gleaned from our company's forty- plus cumulative visits to South America in pursuit of this fantastic gamefish, as well as from personal correspondence and firsthand accounts from many clients, guides, biologists and lodge owners.

Since the late 1980’s, an ever-increasing number of adventurous anglers have trekked to South America to target peacock bass and other aggressive, predatory gamefish that reside in these waters. A peacock bass adventure to South America is one that necessitates careful planning and preparation.

Our companies, Reel Mexican Adventures and X-TREME ANGLING, will help you with this planning and preparation - every step of the way. You'll feel confident about booking your South American angling adventure, because we'll provide you with the most up-to-date information on peacock bass. We'll advise you about recommended inoculations and medications to take prior to your trip; the most comfortable clothing designed to protect you from the equatorial sun; and help you select the best rods, reels, line and lures you'll need to do battle with the peacock bass and other sport fish unique to the Amazon/Orinoco watersheds. As far as booking your peacock bass adventure, there's no one that will offer you as many prime destinations throughout South America. We've eliminated those lodges and fisheries whose best fishing days have passed them by, in favor of the most productive current peacock bass fisheries in South America. With our years of personal, firsthand knowledge, fishing nearly every prime peacock bass watershed in South America, we've gleaned the experience to offer you a fantastic adventure.
