Come Fish Minnesota's
Edge of the Wilderness Resort Area

We make it easy to catch the lunkers!  The "Edge" is home to more than half of Itasca County's 1400+ lakes and some of the best Minnesota Fishing Resorts in the state.  With all that water here you know the lakes are teeming with lunkers lurking in the depths - waiting for you to outfox them.


Family fishing vacations are very popular here.

Many of the lakes within the "Edge" are spring fed and stocked regularly by the Minnesota DNR with walleye, muskie or trout. 

A wide variety of Minnesota gamefish inhabit the lakes and rivers here. These include Walleye, Northern Pike, Muskellunge, Yellow Perch, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Rock Bass, Black and White Crappie, Bluegill, Sunfish, Brown Trout, Rainbow Trout and Bullhead.

Many of the resorts here can provide you with fishing guide service.  Just ask your hosts about it when you are making your lodging reservation.
