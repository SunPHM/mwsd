Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's Report: October 20 & 21, 2001

Florida wild shiner bass fishing anglers Dave Pawlicki and his 7 year-old son Mario (Weston, FL - ph: 954-349-8551 - e-mail: pawlicks@mindspring.com) caught and released 5 bass up to 4 1/4 lbs, 5 crappie and 3 channel catfish up to 5 3/4 lbs under very windy conditions during 2 half days of wild shiner bass fishing on October 20 & 21, 2001 with Cap'n Ron. We fished the North Shore and Monkey Box areas of Lake Okeechobee.

Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's Report: October 9 & 10, 2001

Florida wild shiner bass fishing anglers Bob Craley and his 12 year-old son Lee (York, PA - ph: 717-757-7696 - e-mail: Butch918@aol.com or TeresaC@yorktwn.com) put up with gale force winds during the first afternoon of their wild shiner bass fishing trip on October 9, 2001 with Cap'n Ron. To stay out of the wind we fished the cuts of Harney Pond Canal where Bob's son Lee caught 2 bass up to 4 lbs (bottom right. Bob accidentally hooked into a manatee that required the line to be cut before Bob was pulled overboard! The wind calmed down overnight to allow us to get out on the lake the next morning October 10, 2001. Bob and his son Lee caught 7 bass during their morning trip that included a 6 1/4 lb. (top left & right), 5 1/4 lb. (bottom left) and 4 1/2 lb'er that were caught by Bob's son. Two channel catfish were also boated. All fish were released except the 6 1/4 lb'er which is being mounted. Bass and catfish were caught in the Harney Pond Canal and North Shore areas of Lake Okeechobee.

12 year-old Lee with a 5 1/4 lb'er (bottom left) and a 4 1/2 lb'er (bottom right).
Lee with his dad and the airboat they fished out of with Cap'n Ron.

Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's Report: March 27 & 28, 2001

Florida wild shiner bass fishing father and son anglers Anthony Lee Sr. and 5 1/2 year-old Anthony Jr. (Vero Beach, FL - ph: 561-770-2793 - e-mail: aclee64@aol.com) caught and released 15 bass up to 6 1/4 lbs during 2 half days (March 27 & 28, 2001) of wild shiner bass fishing with Ron's Guide Service. Little Anthony caught the 6 1/4 lb'er (left) and a 4 lb'er (right) as well as most of the 15 bass put in the boat. They fished the Harney Pond canal and Sportsman canal areas of Lake Okeechobee.
