Each sunny morning you will wake to an exquisite breakfast, replete with freshly brewed Costa Rican coffee, succulent mango, papaya, pineapple and bananas from the Inn’s own orchards. The Inn is renowned for its fine cuisine, prepared with care and creativity from the bounty of its fruit orchards and organic vegetable and herb garden. Chef Pedro Alas welcomes the curious gourmand into his kitchen to share the secrets and techniques of nouvelle Costa Rican Cuisine. The Chef draws on Costa Rican and international culinary arts, using local fresh seafood and organic meats and poultry to create a menu that changes daily. The elegant four-course dinners set around the inviting, intimate fireplace are offered to guests exclusively.
