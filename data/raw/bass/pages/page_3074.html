Welcome to Anglers Inn / Casa Blanca Lodges and Mexico where our team works hard to make sportsmen's dreams come true on a daily basis in the middle of the most incredible fishing and hunting in Mexico, while surrounding them with the kind of world-class service that has to be experienced to be believed.

We can take care of the sportsman's every need and desire, whether that involves catching a giant Lake El Salto bass fishing or hard-fighting saltwater gamefish like marlin, sailfish, dorado, snook and roosterfish. If wingshooting is your game, we own the keys to the kind of red-hot duck and dove shooting that Mexico has long been famous for.

The crown jewel of our operation is Lake El Salto, where our luxurious lakeside lodge sits just a few yards from the most famous big-bass waters in the world. In fact, no less than BASSMASTER Magazine has called El Salto the very best trophy bass lake in the world. And when the BASSMASTER editors and writers come to El Salto, they stay with Anglers Inn where they know the service always matches the impressive big-bass action.

Anglers Inn is now in the process of putting a new lodge in one of the most promising new lakes in Mexico: Lake Aguamilpa. During this season, we'll be observing the lake and fishing conditions to make sure this lake is ready to live up your expectations. This is one of Mexico's newest and largest bass lakes for sheer numbers. You should be able to experience hundred-fish days in the 3- to 7-pound range with the opportunity of a 10-pound plus.

Next season, this lake will have one more year of growth and Anglers Inn will be ready to impress you with fishing, lodging and service. We'll keep you posted about this new lake's developments.
