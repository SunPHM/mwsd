Fishing for Large Mouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish and Perch in Kansas.
Fishing and Hunting Prices

Our Fishing Ponds: Throughout the ranch there are nineteen ponds for your fishing enjoyment. Most of our fishing ponds are spring fed, meaning they don't go dry.

In 1986, we stocked the bigger nine of the fishing ponds with large mouth bass, crappie, catfish, and perch.

These nine bigger Kansas fishing ponds run about 1/4 acre in size with two running 10-12 acres. Your chances of catching a trophy fish are very good.

Kansas Bass Fishing: When fishing for our Large Mouth Bass, we have several that can run into the 10 to 12 lb range, so you had better make sure you can endure a fight.

Our catfish can run into the 15 to 16 lb range. It is not uncommon to also catch a stringer full of hand size crappie, or perch.

We are selling only a limited number of Kansas fishing lease's to ensure that you will always have a good time on the ponds.
