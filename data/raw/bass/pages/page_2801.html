SMALLMOUTH BASS FISHING

Smallmouth Bass Fishing at Marten River

Smallmouth Bass are one of the most under fished resources at Marten River. The River is host to a large supply of "smallies" and catching one in the 4 to 5 lb range is not uncommon. They tend to prefer live bait and like walleye enjoy a minnow or a worm on a go-getter. Top water lures as well as spinner baits work well to attract big Smallmouth Bass.

Marten River Lodge features a back lake that specializes in Smallmouth Bass; it is a day trip to this catch and release lake where up to 3 people can go with a guide on an exciting Argo trip (that’s an 8 wheeled all terrain vehicle). This is an extremely remote lake where you will not see another soul and the fishing is usually awesome. There is nothing more exciting than a large smallmouth leaping through the air trying to shake your hook before being netted by one of our experienced guides.
