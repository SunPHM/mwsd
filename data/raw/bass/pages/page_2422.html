Felix Austria

The splendid musical heritage of the Austrian Court during the reigns of Leopold I (1640-1705) to Joseph II: Sonatas, concerti grossi and vocal works by Biber, Muffat, Legrenzi, Fux, Bononcini, Attilio Ariosti, Marc' Antonio Ziani. (8 musicians)

Also in our repertoire (in collaboration with various choirs):

Johann Sebastian Bach: Matthäuspassion, Johannespassion, Christmas Oratorio, Mass in b-minor, Easter Oratorio, Magnificat

Georg Ph. Telemman: several passions, larger cantatas

Georg F. Händel: Utrechter Te Deum, Messias, Brockes Passion, Israel in Egypt, Judas Macabbaeus
