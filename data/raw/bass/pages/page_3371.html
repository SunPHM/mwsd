Sea Bass

     

Sea bass trips are fun for all. Trips are usually 8 hours long and can be fished inshore, over wrecks or out in the Old Grounds. The action can be so fast that our charters often get tired even before their limit is caught. The sea bass caught by our charters offer many great tasting meals for our clients. We almost always catch our limit on sea bass, as well as a selection of other fish when conditions are right.
