A  bass fishing professional, Pete specializes in Table Rock Lake bass fishing trips for the amateur fisherman and the experienced angler alike. Pete will guide you through the techniques that have helped him win major tournaments, giving you the pointers and tips on presentation techniques, rigging, types of baits, depths, structure fishing and much more!

On your guided trip, we'll provide all your fishing necessities and conveniences, including

                    Pick up and return to your hotel or resort on the lake.

                    Fishing lures and  bait provided

                    Rods & reels, life jackets, and gas included.

Whether you want to personally improve your fishing skills or want to teach the whole family how to fish for bass.  Pete will provide you with the knowledge that  has helped him win numerous bass fishing tournaments on Table Rock Lake.
