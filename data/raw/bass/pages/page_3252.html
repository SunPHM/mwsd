Chilean Sea Bass Fillets

Delicious mild flavored fish fillets!

SeaMazz® Chilean Sea Bass are harvested from the deep, pristine waters of the South Atlantic. These flavorful all natural fillets have a succulent, white flesh, bringing forth a mild yet delicious flavor. Chilean Sea Bass Fillets are extremely versatile, are individually packaged, and can be baked, broiled, microwaved or sautéed to your liking.
