Description: Recently imported all the way from the USA - this beautifully modelled (Strad outline) instrument with its violin corners and swell back has been hand carved with incredible neatness and precision from good quality maple and spruce wood. Presented with quality fittings such as fingerboard, bridge, endpin and strings this instrument represents absolutely astonishing value for money.

Playability: Nicely raked shoulders means that this is a very easy instrument to get around. Aluminium bridge adjusters means that you can set the strings as high or as low as you like.

Sound Quality: I'd describe it as a good, even, pleasant sound.

Would I go for this instrument?: This instrument does have a lot of appealing qualities. Its good looking, its hand made, its easy to play and it sounds pretty OK. Best attribute of all – we think - is the price.
