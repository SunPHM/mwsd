Located 350 miles southeast of Caracas, Venezuela; Lake Guri is where Venezuela peacock bass fishing and payara fishing were first popularized among international anglers. The Peacock Princess is bringing this fabled peacock bass and payara fishery back to its glory days, with mobility unmatched by any fixed lodges. The 65-foot houseboat traverses the 80-mile-long Lake Guri with five roomy Ranger bass boats.

(The angler goes Venezuela peacock bass fishing or payara fishing while the "Peacock Princess" moves to meet you at the fishing location).

Experienced guides take you to unexplored waters, without long boat rides cutting into your peacock bass or payara fishing time. Catch three species of peacock bass and trophy-class Payara, then return to the Peacock Princess for dinner and relaxation inside your air-conditioned all American Standards House Boat.

Location: Guayana Highland Guri Lake, East of Amazon Territory State, Venezuela, South America.

Type of Boats: Fully equipped 21 foot Ranger Bass Boats.

Fishing Season: all year long.
a) High water level: September ~ January
b) Low water level : February ~ May

Numbers of Fishing Packages:
a) 4 nights 3 Days of Fishing
b) 6 nights ~ 5 Days of Fishing

Best Time to Fish: When ever you can get away from responsibilities!
