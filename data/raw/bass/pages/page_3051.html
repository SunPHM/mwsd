FLORIDA BASS FISHING BRAG'N BOARD BASS FISHING REPORT:
APRIL 1999

Florida bass fishing this April has really opened up with some of the fastest bass fishing experienced this year on Okeechobee. Big Florida trophy bass as the below pictures and reports note have really been caught in good numbers by our guide parties. Bass are bedding all over the lake. There are beds with bass on them in just about every open water area on the North Shore. Schooling bass are starting to bust shad in the open water. Seeing lots of bass chasing minnows along the weed edges just about everywhere on the lake. The lowering of the lake water now and throughout the year will greatly improve the bass fishing this year spring and summer. Get into this great bass fishing now by taking advantage of our Florida bass fishing April "Special Rates" as noted below!
