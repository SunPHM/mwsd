Rates for Guide trips with Capt Richard Vance.

 

Richard will furnish rods and reels, but if you are fishing artificial lures you may want to bring your own favorite gear. Richard will have rods and reels also for shiner fishing as they are heavier gear than most people use normally.

Be sure to bring the following items along with you:

  • Lunch, snacks, and drinks

  • A iced cooler is provided on board for your convenience

  • Sunglasses, sunscreen, cap or hat, rain coat, and camera.

  • In the winter months be sure to bring along warm layered clothing, as it can be chilly on the water of a morning, when the wind is blowing, or when traveling by boat.

  • Our weather during the winter is the best to be had in the entire country, but even then we can have a cold front that drops temperatures into the 50.s of a morning.

Standard Rates:
