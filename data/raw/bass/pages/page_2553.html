Lake Okeechobee women anglers bass fishing reports and pictures from the calendar year of 2002. This Florida freshwater lake over the 20 years I have operated a trophy bass fishing guide service on has produced on a person to person (male to female) ratio "more trophy bass to women anglers" It seems the girls are able to put more Okeechobee trophy bass in the boat far more offen then the experienced men anglers!
