Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's Report: November 28 & 29, 2002
5 1/2 lb'er

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Kevin Roath and his 10 year-old son Kevin Jr. (Metamora, MI - ph: 810-678-2837) caught 10 bass up to 5 1/2 lbs during 2 half days of wild shiner fishing with Cap'n Ron. Kevin Jr. caught the 5 1/2 lb'er. They also caught a mudfish in the 8 lb. range, pickerel and a cat fish. All fish were released. Bass were caught in the Horse Island area of Lake Okeechobee.

Big smile!

Florida Bass Fishing Dad's & Son's Report: October 26 & 27, 2002
8 1/4 lb'er

Lake Okeechobee bass fishing anglers Rocco and his 16 year-old son Kyle Gabriele (Baltimore, MD - ph: 410-732-2426 - e-mail: Engine280@aol.com) caught 20 bass up to 8 1/4 lbs during 2 days of wild shiner fishing with Ron's Guide Service. This father and son team due to poor fishing conditions caught nothing on their first day (October 26, 2002) with one of Capt'n Ron's guides. On their second day (October 27, 2002) with Cap'n Ron they caught the 20 bass up to 8 1/4 lbs. They boated 10 bass in the morning session of fishing and 10 in the afternoon session with the 8 1/4 lb'er being caught that afternoon at 5:00 p.m.. Kyle caught the 8 1/4 lb'er and his dad's biggest out of the 20 bass they boated was a 4 1/4 and a 4 lb'er. All fish were released except the 8 1/4 lb'er which Kyle is having mounted. Bass were caught in the Horse Island area of Lake Okeechobee.
