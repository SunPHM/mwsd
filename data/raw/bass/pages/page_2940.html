Fishing in Brazil... An Amazon Adventure 
The Amazon Rain Forest of Brazil is home of the current world record Peacock Bass caught in 1994 on a tributary of the huge Rio Negro River. The Rio Negro is truly one of the very best rivers in the world for turning out Peacock Bass in excess of 20 lbs. The Rio Negro, the largest tributary of the famed Amazon River , gets it's name from it's black water. This dark tannic acid water has very few mosquitoes or other annoying insects which is truly a blessing considering it is located in the heart of the Amazon.
You will fish from TRITON BOATS newest fleet of 17' all welded, heavy gauge aluminum bass boats. These boats will be equipped with the new Yamaha 60 HP outboards and Motorguide trolling motors as well as marine two-way radios for communication between other boats and back to base. These are the very best boats in the Amazon for fishing and comfort. Many times you will encounter a situation of trying to enter into a lagoon through a narrow and shallow canal that will require a very shallow draft on the boat.
Fishing
Anglers fish two per boat with a talented Brazilian guide well versed in the nuances of bait casting, spin and fly fishing. He is intimately familiar with the waters and will put you into impressive numbers of big fish.

Peacock bass fishing

Imagine a bomb going off when you cast your Big Game Woodchopper next to some brush, the majority of peacock bass are caught on top water lures and their explosive strike is unsurpassed in the bass fishing world. The three major species of peacock bass are abundant along with payara, piranha and many other exotic fish. Peacock bass range from 5 lbs. to over 20 lbs.; several fish 10 lbs. and over are caught each day.
The current IGFA all- tackle world record of 27.0 pounds came from the region you will fish!
Come break the record!

Accommodations
Amazon Angler Yacht
This boat will cruise tributaries of the Amazon River in search of the very best fishing as well as the best water levels. The houseboat concept is the best way to see the most remote regions of the Amazon as well as the most productive for fishing.
Your home away from home will be aboard a mother ship, the AMAZON ANGLER. This yacht style boat offers air conditioned cabins with private bath, dining area, two bar facilities, satellite tv and phone as well as laundry service. This boat is perfect for cruising the most remote regions of the Amazon in order to produce the best fishing but without giving up all the comforts of home. The service aboard the Amazon Angler will also be second to none.  The guides are all top notch and have extensive background in guiding American fisherman in the Amazon.

Dine
The food is a blend of American/Brazilian cuisine prepared by an experienced chef who has worked on other boats for nature tours. The staff has over 30 years experience in the Amazon tourist business and is knowledgeable in logistics and the surrounding culture.

Be sure to take your camera.
Besides the exciting fishing, this tropical fantasyland is home to huge alligators, ducks the size of turkeys, parrots, macaws, toucans and hundreds of other colorful and unique birds and animals.
