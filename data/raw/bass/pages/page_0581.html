I am Carol the Cherry Hill NJ half of the team. My sister and I have been redesigning all our lives.  We just never had a name for what we did.. we love using the things a homeowner has and “weaving our magic” with them.  We decided to turn our passion for redesign into a business. While the business aspect of what we do is new we are still doing what we do best. Redesign.  

In the last 33 years we have redesigned homes in Tennessee, New Jersey, California, Florida, Georgia, Montana, New York, Connecticut, Virginia and Mississippi. 
 

Luann Black

I'm Luann, the Nashville, TN half of this partnership. My sister, Carol and I have realized our dream to have a business together, even though long distance.  We've been "playing" at re-design for so many years that we were nicknamed "THE GORILLA DECORATING TEAM" by our husbands.  We decided that a more appropriate name was needed-thus-"ROOMS RETOUCHED".  Carol works her "territory" and I work mine. When we get really big jobs, we fly to each other and team up.  When we work on our own we make use of today's technology to share redesign ideas and help each other problem solve.

As my mother always told us, "Where there's a will there's a way." Well, we have the will and have made our way. Nothing like an empty nest to start those creative juices flowing. Every time I redesign a room I have trouble sleeping because it is such a creative high.
