McCarten LTD.

At McCarten LTD., I produce hand crafted strung instruments of which each one is individually unique. I began 20+ years ago by customizing electric guitars/basses by upgrading or adding electronics and/or hardware, and in some cases carving patterns ect. into the top. Over the years since I have gained knowledge by building a variety of different types of instruments, each one having it's "quirks" which allows me to learn and appreciate that there are different ways to approach a given situation.


I make use of a variety of woods both exotic and domestic. I feel that each type as well as piece of wood has it's own character both physically and visually. I am open to try new varieties and discover what each has to offer myself as the maker, and the player, and the music.


I make solid, semi-hollow, and hollow body, electric guitars and basses, as well as some "unique" conglomerations/deviations thereof. The focus of late is on archtop acoustics. They are truly a versatile, beautiful, underated, and often misunderstood instrument. There are a number of gifted luthiers out there inspired by amongst others, the late Jimmy D'Aquisto, who are stretching the boundaries for this instrument and I hope at some point to at least in some small way contribute to that end.


I also enjoy the challenge of collaborating with mother nature on projects like the mandolinlike instrument (above and below)using a gourd for the body. This requires virtually redesigning each instrument to match/compliment the naturally individual body. This reinforces the fact that every piece of wood, every instrument is unique and must be approached as such.
