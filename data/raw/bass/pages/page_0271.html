Colin Bass

New George's is a notoriously difficult place to shoot pictures -- most of the lights are behind the musicians.

The only reason this picture turned out at all is because Colin stepped in front of the light. See the halo?

Taken at New George's, San Rafael, CA, 3/6/97.
