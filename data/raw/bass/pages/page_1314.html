Manhattan Trio Jazz Bands

The Manhattan Trio is one of St. Louis' most popular Jazz Combos. Jazz Standards, Big Band & Swing, Sinatra, Duke Ellington, Cole Porter, George Gershwin -- the perfect music for a cocktail party, a cocktail/dinner party, convention opening receptions, open houses for companies & rehearsal dinners. 

Piano/Keyboard, Bass & Drums or Guitar, Bass & Sax are two of our most popular trios. A Vocalist can be added to the Manhattan Trio to give an extra dimension.
