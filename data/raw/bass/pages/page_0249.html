Stringed Instruments

Fender Stratocaster USA 1992 neck SN E941386, after-market Japanese ash body in two-tone sunburst and nitrocellulose lacquer, with EMG DG-20 pickup assembly. Volume pot pulls out to activate bridge pickup for tele configuration. The flagship.

Fender Stratocaster Japan '54 reissue, refinished in sonic blue, neck stamp ST54DEX2 March 1997, SN A001544, V-neck, gold anodised pickguard, Fender Custom 54 pickups volume pot pulls out to activate bridge pickup.

Fender Telecaster Japan '52 reissue. Lightweight ash body. V-neck. Fender Texas Special pickups. SN Q044639.

Rickenbacker 4001 bass guitar. SN UJ3475 which makes it an '82 I think. Someone unbelievably modified this by putting in a kahler vibrato bridge. Has since been restored to a Rick bridge. Refinished in Chris Squire colours (it was fireglo red..)

Monterey Stratocaster Stage Series, with EMG S pickups from the 80's. Someone sanded the black paint off them. Master tone control. Bottom tone control is a blend for the neck pickup. Gigging guitar!

Status Stratocaster Silhouette Series. Fender Lace Sensor pickups and master TBX tone control. Bottom tone control blends the neck pickup. Other gigging guitar.

SX (Essex) Telecaster Traditional Series, with Fender 4-way selector switch. To be restrung in baritone configuration any day now (B E A D F# B low to high)
