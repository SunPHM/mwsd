PRESS RELEASE

Big Fish Audio Announces Old School Funk Bass

February 27, 2002

Session bass player and producer Des Pres takes you on a complete tour of essential bass guitar lines that helped to drive the great Funk/R&B classic records into the hit record history books. With over 14 bass instruction books (and combined sales of over 100,000) to his credit, Des Pres is no stranger to tutorial presentations and relies on his extensive record producer/songwriter/arranger background to make sure this package into the most essential and funkiest loop collection yet offered to songwriters and musicians. All the style, vibe and feeling of old-school, funk bass guitar playing is captured on this two CD set with over 500 loops all performed live using vintage basses, equipment and recording techniques of the '60s, '70s and '80s.

Advertisement

All of these loops are duplicated in both Acidized .wav format and as unlooped, standard music files on two CDs with complete cross-referenced names. There are three sections: Old School Funk Bass patterns, 70's Funk and Disco grooves and Hip Hop Bass patterns. Selling for $99 MSRP, Old School Funk Bass-The Ultimate Funk Bass Library is produced by Track Star Audio Samples and distributed by Big Fish Audio.

For more information, visit their web site at www.bigfishaudio.com.
