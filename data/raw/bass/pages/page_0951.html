Hard Rock Cafe Cologne Memorabilia

HRC Cologne has a really nice collection of Rock and Roll Memorabilia. Items of some of the biggest stars in the rock music business are on display at HRC Cologne.

Here are just some items - you will find a lot more cool stuff on the walls of HRC Cologne:

  • Black and white Fender Precision Bass Guitar of Tom Hamilton (Aerosmith)

  • Kramer Baretta I Guitar with custom Bon Jovi New Jersey paint of Ritchie Sambora (Bon Jovi)

  • Gibson ES350 Double Cutaway Guitar of Elliott Easton (The Cars). The guitar was custom made for him from Gibson's Nashville plant

  • Gold Gibson Guitar of Eric Clapton

  • White Telecaster Guitar of David Gilmour (Pink Floyd)

  • Yellow and white Telecaster Guitar of Hot Tuna

  • Red Gretsch Guitar of The Monkees signed by all band members

  • Light blue BC Rich Guitar Guitar of Poison signed by all band members

  • Cream and white ESP Guitar of Ron Wood (Rolling Stones)

  • Black and white Gibson Flying V Guitar of The Scorpions signed by all band members

  • Custom Made Ibanez Guitar of Steve Vai

  • Black and white Fender Stratocaster Guitar of Sting

  • Acoustic Guitar of The Travelling Wilburys signed by George Harrison, Tom Petty, Bob Dylan and Jeff Lynne

  • Red and white Statocaster Guitar of U2 signed by two band members 

  • White Kramer XL I Guitar of Warrant signed by all band members

  • Harley Owners Group Leather Jacket signed by Bob Dylan, Billy Idol and Bruce Springsteen

  • Gold buster of Madonna 

  • Drumhead of U2s Larry Mullen jr used during the recording of 'Angel of harlem' for the 'Rattle and hum'-album

Click on the item you are interested in to see a scan of it.

As you can see HRC Cologne is a real HRC Museum - so come on over and check out these and other cool items at Hard Rock Cafe Cologne.
