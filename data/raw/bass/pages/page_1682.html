The Freenotes Bass/Contrabass Marimba is unlike any other, and has significant acoustical and structural advantages over conventional instruments. The Marimba is available in chromatic, diatonic or pentatonic scaling, and the notes may even be purchased individually. All notes are freestanding, separate units, however, we can supply a support framework to contain any array of pitches you require.

Contrabass range notes are made with four inches wide bars and are $150.00 per note. Bass range notes are made with three inch wide bars and are $115.00 per note.

Please email us your requirements and we will provide pricing on a complete set made to your specifications.
