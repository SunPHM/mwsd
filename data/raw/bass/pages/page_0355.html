Bass Fishing with Bill Cannan

 

Bill Cannan offers bass fishing to several of Texas’ top lakes- Lake Conroe, Lake Livingston, Lake Raven, and two private lakes called Bieri Lakes.  Bass fishing on Lake Conroe with Bill in the Spring is going to be your best bet for a giant bass.  Every Spring Bill puts clients on giant bass in shallow water.  Cannan’s specialties are jig fishing and sight fishing.
