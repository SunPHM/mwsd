AQUACULTURE Systems

Aquacultured product available

for export.

 

        AQUACULTURE Systems network our own farms and others to pool product for the supply of export markets. We supply premium fish to wholesalers, restaurateurs, exporters, or the public. While we specialise in plate size barramundi (350 - 500g), we are able to tailor our product to the buyer's specific requirement. The bulk of our product is sold either whole dead, or live. Our barramundi are similar to Asian sea bass, with no muddy taste, and firm, textured flesh.

 

         AQUACULTURE Systems Pty. Ltd. promotes the use of intensive growout methods. Most anaysts agree this is the way aquaculture must be conducted in the future; if for environmental reasons alone. Regardless of the way it is marketed, all of our product is of the highest quality. This is achieved because we have absolute control over our entire growout process. We are not at the mercy of predators or the weather, we use no chemicals other than salt, and our fish are grown in a clean, stress free, constantly optimal environment. Unlike many fish grown in farm dams or net cages, our stock does not need to go through the damaging and stressful process of being transferred to purging or holding tanks. Our fish are already in their purging tanks, we simply stop feeding. We exclude parasites, disease is controllable, and there is no mud whatsoever in our systems.

 

        All AQUACULTURE Systems farms aim for quality in their production process. Product is networked to provide reliable supply to larger markets; which is becoming more reliable as more of these farms are established. Our farming system now has access to ISO9002 QA certification.

        We are aware of the negative image that farmed fish has had in the past; generally a muddy flavour, high fat content, little texture to the flesh & dark skin coloration. To combat this we "finish" all stock before marketing. Fish are placed in smaller, dedicated salt water tanks for seven days. During this time the stomach is purged, flesh flavour improved, and the dark colour changes to silver. We also speed the circular current in the salt tanks, which takes off body fat & firms the flesh. It is body fat which holds muddy or undesirable flavours.

              AQUACULTURE Systems also has an additional finishing treatment which further improves flavour of the flesh, and improves the way barramundi defrosts after freezing. This is called the AQUACULTURE Systems "Freeze Treatment", and is completely non toxic. This treatment has been developed over the past three years in consultation with Japanese importers - in an effort to allow sushi quality barramundi after freezing.

 

        AQUACULTURE Systems farms are very well suited to the sale of live fish. Our completely stress free finishing process allows the highest live transport survival rates. Buyers are able to back their vehicle to within one meter of the finishing tanks; we then transfer the fish with minimal disturbance.

        Buyers are able to view the stock in our tanks long before purchase, and appreciate the clean growing conditions. This facilitates the supply of product to specialist niche markets. Our process is relatively risk free and predictable, so importers have time to develop their own markets for our fish. Export of small quantities thus becomes possible.

        For the reasons above our process is very well suited to export (live or dead). Our entire farming process has in fact been developed with export the final aim. Our fish farms have the quality required, however we currently do not have the quantity. This is changing as more farms are established, and we predict that within twelve months there will be a large volume of premium quality product available for export from various regions within Australia.

 

        It is important for us to make export contacts now which can grow into business relationships in the future as this larger quantity becomes available. We thus invite expressions of interest from importers or agents world wide.

 

        We consider ours to be the most technologically advanced fish farming process in Australia. This is evident in the degree of control we exercise over our entire growout process. This level of control has only one purpose; to produce aquacultured product of the highest quality, tailored to the precise needs of our buyers. The future of aquaculture is here.
