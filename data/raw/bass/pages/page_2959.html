Lake of the Woods, with it's world
renowned fishing and hunting reputation
is waiting on your door step.

Whether you are fishing for bass, walleye, northern pike, monster Muskie or lake trout, Lake of the Woods will not disappoint you. Bring your own boat or rent one of our fishing rigs. We can provide you with tips of where the fish are biting. Guides are available with prior arrangements. Be sure to book them well in advance as they are busy in the prime fishing seasons. Licences may be purchased in our convenience store along with maps, ice and other supplies. Ask us to pack a bag lunch for you or cook your own shore lunch from your "catch of the day".

If you prefer not to fish and would like to explore the beautiful waters of Regina Bay and Lake of the Woods, rent one of our boats and motors for a truly memorable experience.
Our sand beach and sand volleyball court are always a favourite for the whole family. Use our Hotel as a base and enjoy the local atmosphere of our friendly area. Summer festivals, well stocked gift shops and some of the most beautiful country in the world beckon you.
