Sailing from picturesque Rock Harbor, the "Madame B" fishes beautiful Cape Cod Bay for trophy striped bass and bluefish from May through September. As one of
the largest tidal flats in the world, Cape Cod Bay consistently provides some of the best striped bass fishing there is. The "Madame B" is a custom, 36' Harris, powered by a Cummins diesel. The "Madame B" is equipped with all the latest electronics and safety gear. Come join Capt. Dick Woodland and crew for a day of striper fishing that you won't soon forget!

 
The Madame B features:
  • Custom Harris 36
  • Cummins diesel engine
  • State of the art electronics
  • Penn reels and Custom rods
  • Comfortable salon
  • Enclosed head
  • Top of the line safety equipment
  • Maximum 6 passengers
Whether you are an experienced angler, or a father taking his child on his first fishing trip, Cape Cod Bay is the perfect destination. With the abundance of fish within in a short distance of the harbor, and the usually calm seas, a great time is assured for all.

With over 40 years of experience fishing Cape Cod Bay, Capt. Dick Woodland knows where the fish will be when, and how to get them in the box! Let the crew of the "Madame B" be your guide to an unforgettable striped bass and bluefish trip.
