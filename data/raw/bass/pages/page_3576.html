Pan Seared Sea Bass with Vegetable Hash -
This is a very colorful dish and can be made all year round.

Ingredients:

Sea Bass
 6 Sea Bass Steaks 6 to 8-ounces each
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 cup of minced fresh herbs, use any combo of parsley,
basil, thyme, rosemary, or other favorite herbs.
Salt & pepper to taste

Vegetable Hash
½ cup sweet corn, frozen is fine
½ cup red pepper
½ cup yellow pepper
½ cup asparagus fresh
½ cup red onion
½ cup black beans, canned and rinsed
1 medium sweet potato diced
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
Salt & pepper to taste
Prep all ingredients ahead of time. This is an easy recipe and doesn’t require a lot of cooking time once you get started. Have your guests ready to eat!

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Sprinkle each side of the Sea Bass with pepper and salt. In non-stick sauté pan heat oil. Dip top of Bass in minced herbs. Place herb side down in oil and pan sear, approximately 3 minutes. Flip for three minutes. Remove and put on Baking pan. Put in oven for 10 minutes.

Heat Large non-stick sauté pan. Place in pan butter and oil until blended and hot, add brown sugar, honey, pepper, and stir. Add all vegetables and cook for about 4 to 5 minutes or until tender.
Take large dinner plate and spread hash out, place fish on the center of the plate. We spooned a tomato basil cream sauce over our fish. You can use any type of sauce or leave it alone and enjoy the natural vegetables and fish by itself.
Try this with chicken, shrimp or any other main entrée you like. This vegetable can go with just about anything and you can add you favorite vegetable and herbs as well. Have fun and enjoy!!!!
