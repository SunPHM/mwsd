Takamine Guitars are crafted from the finest handpicked tonewoods, hand-crafted by expert luthiers, all providing players with an unmatched excellence and surprising affordability. Play one today and you'll play one for a lifetime.

The Takamine EF341-SC has dramatic looks with superb dreadnought sound. New upgraded features for 2002 include a solid cedar top, solid maple back and sides. Schecter:

The 'Diamond' Acoustics are constructed from with a composite 'oval' back with a highly figured maple veneer over a spruce top. This semi-shallow body design makes the Diamond Acoustics an excellent choice as a live stage guitar, which in conjunction with the 4-Band active circuit, make this acoustic/electric very 'feedback resistant', even at high volumes! The Elite version adds an upscale elegance with the abalone binding.

Features include a spruce top with highly figured maple veneer, a set neck, 'sonically tuned" oval back, a maple neck with rosewood fretboard, diamond inlays, and 20 extra-jumbo frets. Also includes Grover tuners (chrome or gold hardware depending on color) and a feedback-resistant 4-band piezo bridge pickup.
