 2003 ALBC-Burger King/Optimist Club Kids Tournament
Bayou Bassmaster's of Alexandria wishes to thank all the sponsors of one of the most successful fishing tournaments of the year. This was not an event with huge paychecks or boat give-away like the pros have, but a simple fishing tournament where the participates were out to have some fun. Each child received a free Zebco rod & reel, a T-shirt, and a trophy for participation as they passed through the gate. Some of the sponsors who graciously helped out in various ways were: Burger King who supplied the tournament with over 1,000 hamburgers, Cenla Beverage Company which supplied drinks for all participants and parents, the Optimist Club of Alexandria for their help with the launching,   parking and various other organizational functions, Bill Lewis Lures, Rapides Parish Police Jury, Super 1,  Louisiana Tourism, Tom's Potato Chips, Trophies Unlimited, Dare officers of Rapides Parish, Sayes' Office Supply, and radio station La 103.5 . To all these sponsors and supporters Bayou would like to give you all a hardy thanks for making this tournament run so smoothly and making it the success it turned out to be. Even with these sponsors the tournament would not have been such a success if the parents, grandparents, aunts, uncles, and various other kin had not taken the time to take a kid fishing. Over 500 kids turned out for the event and I think all had a wonderful time. I was lucky enough to capture some of the joy on film of some of these excited children. Take a look at these pictures and I think you will see what I mean.
