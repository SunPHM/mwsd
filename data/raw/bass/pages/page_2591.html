FLORIDA BASS FISHING BRAG'N BOARD: JANUARY 1999

Our Florida bass fishing during the month of January got off to a good start. Big Florida trophy size bass were moving into the spawning areas all around the lake. Good catches were made using lively wild shiners fished under a float or free lined either by drifting or anchoring down along a weed line. Okeechobee's North Shore, Moonshine Bay and the Monkey Box areas seem to be the hot spots to fish.

Florida bass fishing: 7 lb. trophy bass.
Ronnie Conard (Canton, NC - ph: 828-648-8062) and Alan Pressley (Clyde, NC - ph: 828-629-0887) caught 80 bass which included a 7 lb’er during 3 days (Jan. 25 - 27, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Ron’s Guide Service. Alan caught the 7 lb’er. All 80 bass were caught in the North Shore and Moonshine areas of Lake Okeechobee. All the bass were live released.

Florida bass fishing: 7 1/2 and 6 1/2 trophy bass.
Bruce Neal (Johnstown, OH - ph: 740-967-1621 - e-mail: bassman277@yahoo.com) and Randy Barclay (Amlin, OH - ph: 614-873-4212) caught 170 + bass during 5 days (Jan. 18 - 22, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Ron’s Guide Service. Bruce caught a 7 1/2 lb’er while Randy boated a 6 1/2 lb’er. They also had numerous bass that were in the 5 lb. range during the 5 days they bass fished. All of the 170 + bass caught were live released. The bass were caught in several areas on Lake Okeechobee with the North Shore, Monkey Box and Moonshine Bay producing the bulk of the fish.

Florida bass fishing: 6 3/4 lb. trophy bass.
Ron Watson, son Bobby (Lansdale, PA - ph: 215-412-9796 - e-mail: rlwesq@dplus.net) and grandfather Bill Kader (Rock Hall, MD - ph: 416-639-2649) caught 49 bass up to 6 3/4 lbs. during 3 days (Jan. 16 - 18, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Ron’s Guide Service. Bobby’s grandfather caught the 6 3/4 lb’er while Bobby caught a 5 and a 5 1/2 lb. bass. Bass were caught out of the North Shore area of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.


Florida bass fishing: 7 1/4 lb. trophy bass
Duanne and Ann Swift (Green Bay, WI - ph: 920-437-8704 - e-mail: pres@titletown.org) caught 11 bass up to 7 1/4 lbs. during a half day (Jan. 12, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Ron’s Guide Service. Duanne (President of the Green Bay area Chamber of Commerce) caught and released the 7 1/4 lb’er. Fish were caught on the North Shore of Lake Okeechobee. All bass were released.


Florida bass fishing: 6 lb. trophy bass
Charlotte Kemmler and Charlie Miller (Louisville, KY - ph: 502-893-8648) caught 15 bass up to 6 lbs. during two half days (Jan. 11 & 12, 1999) of wild shiner fishing with Ron’s Guide Service. Charlotte of course caught the 6 lb'er. Fish were caught and released on the North Shore of Okeechobee.
