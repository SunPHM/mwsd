Fishing the Northwest Angle
For the novice fisherman or the professional,
Lake of the Woods creates excitement.

The Northwest Angle area is a premier fishery for Walleye and Muskie. In these pristine border waters you can fish two countries in one afternoon. Catch the excitement of … Northern Pike. . . , Sauger. . . , Smallmouth Bass. . . , Crappies. . . , Jumbo Perch. . . , and even Burbot thrown in for good measure!

Our region has some of the hottest reef fishing around in summer. After a delicious shore lunch of freshly caught fish, fried potatoes and baked beans, sit back and enjoy!

Explore an island or two, swim at a beautiful secluded sand beach. Create a laid back, gentle, quiet vacation or an adventure-filled fishing trip.

Winter ice fishing on the Northwest Angle and Islands reefs is hot! We catch Walleye, Sauger, Jumbo Perch, and Northern Pike. Come let our area's experienced guides take you out to a heated well lighted fish house, provide you with bait, and get you on some fish. Please check our local resorts.
