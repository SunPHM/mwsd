Kevin
bass guitar

 

Kevin has been playing bass guitar since middle school.  His musical influences range from Roger Waters, Flea, Trent Reznor and a wide variety of Hip-Hop artists.  Kevin has played a wide variety of music in his past bands such as reggae, funk, and psychedelic rock.  His diverse style brings the bottom-end pulse needed to create the SEV’N 9 sound.  Kevin’s talents are not limited to music alone.  He uses his highly announced technical skills to further the production and development elements of SEV’N 9.
