Great Big Bugs . 8.5 and 5 pound lobsters I caught off the Panty wreck in 120 feet water. I was on the Jean Marie Dive Charters with Captain Ken on August 20, 2004. I also caught 3 other lobsters that day but I could not hold them all for the picture. They were smaller, just 1 3/4 - 2 pounders.

Lobsters in the Bag.Counting up the bugs after a good dive off the North shore in the Port Jeff area. Don't discount the coastal regions of the sound. They turn out lots of game on shallow dives.

Monster Blackfish . Took this one in early March at the Ponoquogue bridge. Had to follow him across 2 channels and finally got him in the rocks. 14 1/2 lbs. He was 30 inches long.

Conger Eel .Took this big boy in late November from a jetty in Montauk. I was able to get a head shot and I hit him right behind the head and severed his spine. Be careful about shooting these because they can kill you by wrapping themselves ,line and chaos all over you. You must be able to break the spine or don't take the shot.
