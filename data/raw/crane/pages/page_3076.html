September 23, 2003

Hiroshima cranes



At the beginning of August, a university student set fire to the origami cranes displayed near to the statue of Sadako in the Peace Park in Hiroshima. Apparently he did this because he'd been unsuccessful in getting a job; presumably the stupid fool didn't realise that arson, especially in such a high-profile spot just a few days before the anniversary of the atomic bombing, wasn't likely to increase his future job prospects.
Anyway, the stands where the cranes are hung were finally repaired and reopened yeserday. This photograph is a close-up of some of the many cranes around the statue of Sadako.
