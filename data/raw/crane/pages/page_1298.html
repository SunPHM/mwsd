PHOTO: FLOCK OF CRANES


     

Cranes dot the skyline of the Paris construction site on the Strip looking north toward Bally's. The $760 million resort will join the Park Place Entertainment Inc. group of properties when it opens in September. Next door, work continues on the $1.3 billion new Aladdin, which plans to open early next year.
