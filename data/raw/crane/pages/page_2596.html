 UPLAND BIRDS 11/12/05

Our population is definitely up over the last 4-5 years. Spring rains provided much needed relief from the drought we've experienced the last few years. Quail were recovering last year ,and they appear to be increasing again this year. OPENING WEEKEND 2004 was a success with great weather & some quality dog work and excellent shooting! 

 
A Full Day's Hunting Upland Birds with Wild Wings Lodge Guide and English Pointers, is limited to 8 Hunters and 8 guests at the Lodge! A Combo Hunt including Geese can   also be provided.

Sandhill Cranes

We now have the opportunity to hunt Sandhill cranes on our property .In the past only a small portion of the state was open to crane hunting, now most of western Kansas allows crane hunting. A large number of cranes are again expected to stop over at Kirwin. In 2004 they arrived around the l1st. 2 weeks in October.The 1st. Cranes arrived here Sunday,Oct. 3rd. 2004!
