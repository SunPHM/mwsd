Thames & Severn Canal In Water            Back to T&S Canal Index

Much of the Thames & Severn Canal east of Chalford is dry but in just a few places, the canal can be found in water and after exceptionally wet weather other parts fill to give just a hint of what the canal must have once looked like and what it might look like after its restoration.
