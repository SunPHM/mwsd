About AGD

Crawler Cranes, Piling Equipment and Micro Tunnelling Systems
 

Welcome to AGD Equipment Limited.

AGD are a well-established UK supplier of construction equipment specialising in crawler cranes, mini cranes,
piling equipment & micro tunnelling systems.
Equipment can be supplied on a sale or rental basis,
either new, used or reconditioned in our workshops.
All machines are available for hire with
or without operator. Centrally located in
Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwickshire.

Overseas enquiries are welcomed as we are experienced exporters.

Please browse our web site to learn more about our
products. Each product line has specification sheets available for download in PDF format.

Please contact us today if you have any further enquiries.
