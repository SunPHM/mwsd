Book 4 in the Four Winds Saga
 Ree Soesbee

First Published in December 2003
Genre(s):
Fantasy - Sword and Sorcery


From the Publisher

The ending of this title will actually be decided by the Legend of the Five Rings readers and fans in a tournament during the Summer of 2003, allowing players to have direct impact on novel content.

The Crane:
Clan War: Third Scroll
Book 3 in the Legend of the Five Rings
 Ree Soesbee

Genre(s):
Fantasy - Sword and Sorcery


From the Publisher
 Legend of the Five Rings is full of high action and adventure, set in a mythical Eastern world. The Crane continues the epic story begun in The Scorpion . This series of novels chronicles the ongoing Clan War, a story told through the Legend of the Five Rings trading card game, as well as these popular books.

The Crane Clan champion has everything a young samurai could want - power, riches, glory, and honor - until he seeks an audience with the Scorpion empress, as beautiful as she is deadly. Though a master of courtly politics, he loses everything.
