This crane was used to lift the roof trusses in place for the carpentry crew that was framing this house. In this picture, the 1/2 inch plywood roof sheathing is being lifted up and placed on the second floor. Later, after all of the trusses have been set and braced, the plywood will be lifted up by hand to carpenters working on the roof.
