Crane Business b.v. in Moerdijk imports crawler cranes from S-E Asia and certifies these machines according to the standards that are applicable in the markets of our buyers. Crane Business has its own service- and parts network, with which, from Moerdijk, service can be guaranteed throughout the world. Crane Business exploits a rental fleet for efficient and timely delivery: also for temporary needs. Rental is as important to us as sales of equipment: we appreciate your inquiry.

Our current crawler rental fleet consists of crawler cranes in 35t~160t class. If you consider renting a machine for a longer period, we will buy the machine and rent it out to you. Whatever the class/application may be.

We also give 24 hour service for parts and support on our crawler cranes. Next day service.

One of a large projects of Crane Business is a Kobelco model BM700-c developed in cooperation with Euro-Rigging in Holland and Kobelco Japan, the strongest 80t machine in the market. Crane Business b.v. supplies these cranes with the 80t modification package.

The management of Crane Business B.V. has a vast experience in the crane business and in transport. The above is a selection of deliveries which took place in the years 1997~2001. We are currently drastically expanding our rental fleet and stock of trade machines to meet increasing demand of esp. contractors that require rapid delivery throughout the world.
