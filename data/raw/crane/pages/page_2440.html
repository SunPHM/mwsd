Professional Fishing and Waterfowl Guide Specializing in Walleye Fishing, Canadian Goose Hunting and Sandhill Crane Hunting

Curts Guide Service is a professional fishing and waterfowl guide specializing in Walleye fishing, Canada Goose hunting, dove hunting, duck hunting, and Sandhill Crane hunting. All fishing and hunting is in central and southern Oklahoma. Our guide service offers quality, exciting trips without the high cost. We are fully insured for your protection and strive to make your outing the most enjoyable.

Lodging: available at Howard Johnson's of Lawton, OK. Rates - $50.00 per night.

Sandhill Crane Hunting: Professional fishing and waterfowl guide specializing in Walleye fishing, Canadian Goose hunting and Sandhill Crane hunting. Serving sportsmen for ten years in Oklahoma, Texas, Kansas, Arkansas. Prime season for sandhill begins in November and lasts thru December. I provide all sandhill crane decoys, calls, and blind material. You must have a Oklahoma hunting license, and both a Federal waterfowl stamp, and a Oklahoma state waterfowl permit. The stamp and permit are not required but many types of waterfowl will also land in the decoys. You must also have a sandhill crane permit. (It is free) You can obtain the Oklahoma hunting license from 1-800-223-3333. You must get the permit from the Oklahoma Wildlife Department. Tel# 405-521-2739 or 2730 this usually takes a few days so do it in advance. Reservations should be made well in advance.
The cost per person is $85.00 tax included.

Goose Hunting: Prime season for geese begins in September with the special season for the local giant geese. Then again December thru January. I provide all goose decoys, goose calls, and blind material. Geese usually taken are Canada's, lesser and mediums. Occasionally some giant Canada's are taken. We also take quite a few Snow Geese, Blue Geese and Specks. If time permits we also take a lot of ducks. You must have a Oklahoma hunting license, Federal waterfowl stamp, and a Oklahoma state waterfowl permit. If you do not have an Oklahoma hunting license you can purchase one at 1-800-223-3333. Reservations should be made well in advance.
The cost per person is $85.00 tax included.

Duck Hunting: offered in season only. All Blinds, decoys, calls and dogs are provided. Hunts are over leased water tanks and ponds. You may book one to five people for duck hunting. Hunts are in the morning or afternoon. Price is $85.00 per person.

Dove Hunts: offered in September and October. You can book one to eight people. Hunts are scheduled for morning or evening. We hunt over Milo, corn, wheat or water. You may book hunts for morning or afternoon. You should bring your own bucket or stool for the hunt.
Hunts are $25.00 per person.

Walleye Fishing: Professional fishing and waterfowl guide specializing in Walleye fishing, Canadian Goose hunting, Dove hunts, Duck hunting and Sandhill Crane hunting. Serving sportsmen for ten years in Oklahoma, Texas, Kansas, Arkansas. Prime season is March thru June and then September thru October. Walleye's caught in the early spring can be of tremendous size. The same goes for late fall when big females make their way into the shallows. All walleye bait, graphite walleye rods, northland fishing tackle and equipment is provided. You may book two to three people, but must have a minimum of two. Guided Walleye trips are morning or afternoon. You must however bring raingear and any cold weather gear. Oklahoma fishing license is also required. If you do not have a fishing license you can purchase one at 1-800-223-3333. Reservations should be made well in advance. Cost per person is $60.00 tax included.
