Crane Inspection

Periodic professional crane and material handling equipment inspections provide every business an avenue to both a safe working environment for your employees and a resource to eliminate costly downtime or loss of production. QCTL's crane inspection services are accredited by the U.S. Department of Labor under 29 CFR part 1919 to inspect, test and certify cranes. We are not a repair service or a manufacturer, which enables QCTL to provide you with an impartial assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of your lifting/material handling equipment.

Our Quality Promise
QCTL is committed to enhancing customer safety and reliability by providing services and responsiveness that sets industry standards for quality and value. We relentlessly strive to provide the finest in testing and inspection capability by fostering a culture of responsibility, trust and high expectations.

    • Load and Lift Fixture Testing Services
    QCTL Crane Inspection provides multiple proofload testing solutions. Included in our broad variety of test weight options are 10 to 200 ton portable water weights, vertical test fixtures with a 100-ton capacity, and 25 tons of compact weight blocks.

    Certified Technicians
    Every inspection professional mobilized to your site is not only qualified but also certified to the strictest standards: ASNT, OSHA, SNT-TC-1A… Each individual must meet our stringent demands for classroom training, hands-on experience, and safety before they are eligible to take the industry's most demanding series of certification examinations. Only then do they begin the long probationary road, which ultimately leads to QCTL providing you an inspection professional that meets your demanding needs. Ask your current inspection service supplier this question: "Have the inspection personnel you intend to supply been certified?"
