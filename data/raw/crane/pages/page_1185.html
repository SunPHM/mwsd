Portable Toilets
Our portable toilets are available for short-term or long-term rentals. Whether you need hand wash units, handicapped units or regular units, S. B. Cox can schedule your service days based on your special requirements. Our service trucks are in all areas of Richmond on a daily basis.

Roll-off Containers
We provide competitively priced roll-off container services with 10, 20 and 30 cubic yard containers to dispose of construction and demolition debris. Our fleet of radio dispatched trucks serve the entire Richmond metropolitan area.

Landfills
If you have construction or demolition debris that needs a home, we have two permitted construction and demolition debris landfills. Our Southside customers can now dump their debris in Chesterfield County at our Taylor Road landfill. (turn left at Grange Hall Elementary)

Crane Rentals
We are equipped with cranes ranging from 22 to 70 tons with booms up to 250 feet. S.B. Cox is in the business of rigging and picking in and around construction sites and has over 40 years of experience. Contact us today to scope your site requirements.

Hauling
We deliver our products and materials "going to the distance" to serve your needs. Our experienced commercial drivers are equipped with GPS technology which serves to expedite the dispatch of your materials and products. These experienced operators and drivers can save your time and staff hours by providing prompt and dependable service.

Storage Vans
Secure your valuable equipment "on site" with one of our storage vans. You will have a safe, dry, secure space to store your materials and tools. S. B. Cox can provide storage trailers for your job site at a minimum monthly rate. Rest assured knowing that your belongings are safe, out of the weather and away from prying eyes.

Materials Recycling and Mulching
S. B. Cox is interested in conserving our natural resources, and to that end has operated a concrete recycling center for the last ten years. Stumps are processed into mulch after passing through our stump grinder. Please conserve our environment by hauling your debris to our recycling facility.
