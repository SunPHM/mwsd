AME, Inc., Recognized as Significant Supporter
of York Technical College Foundation


(L-R)Dennis Merrell/York Tech, Bill Kimbrell,
Danny Funderburk/AME, Inc. and Lloyd Case/AME, Inc.
At a press conference held on Tuesday, June 25, York Technical College highlighted the importance of the York Technical College Foundation's Dawn to Dusk Golf Marathon and the substantial contribution of those who made this year's event successful. This year's Golf Marathon, spearheaded by Foundation President Frank Campbell, President of AME, Inc. and board member Bill Kimbrell, raised more than $40,000, which will be used to provide scholarships for needy students. The 26 golfers (six teams,) who played on five area courses, represented the support of major donors such as Comporium, Founders Federal, Chester Telephone, AME, Inc., as well as the faculty and staff of York Technical College and hundreds of individual donors.


(L-R) Lloyd Case/AME, Inc. and Dennis Merrell/York Tech.
The college gave special recognition to AME, Inc., a company headquartered in Fort Mill, which is an industry-leading provider of a wide variety of industrial and general contracting services. AME, Inc. was a significant supporter of the Golf Marathon and a major partner in the college's Institute for Manufacturing Productivity.

The endowed scholarships provided by the York Technical College Foundation continue to be important even after the advent of lottery-generated tuition assistance. Currently, lottery money is covering only half the cost of tuition. For many students, Foundation scholarships meet the critical need for the remainder of tuition as well as books and relevant costs.


AME, Inc., Wins Recognition For
Top Industrial Project In The Carolinas


Rick Wyatt, sharing comments after receiving
the award and Frank Campbell looking on.
AME, Inc. of Fort Mill, SC has been awarded the 4 Star General Award for the top industrial project in the Carolinas. GSI General Materials, LLC, a major supplier of building materials for the construction industry, annually presents the 4 Star General Awards based on the category, size and prestige of construction projects in the Carolinas. AME, Inc. was recognized in the industrial category for the Freightliner Expansion Project, Mt. Holly, NC. The presentation was made at a ceremony at the GSI General Materials offices in Charlotte, NC on Thursday, October, 11.


(l-r)Tim Stewart, Rick Wyatt, Frank Campbell, Jeff Campbell

AME, Inc. was founded in 1960 and has received numerous awards in safety and construction services, as well as being recognized in multiple regional and national trade publications for industrial construction excellence. AME, Inc. offers a wide range of specialized general and industrial contracting services including equipment installations, crane rentals, building systems and precision concrete.




AME, Inc., Makes South Carolina's Top 100

AME, Inc., has landed on South Carolina's list of the top 100 privately held businesses.

The list, which has been compiled by Arthur Andersen for 15 years, ranks the companies according to revenues from the most recently completed fiscal year.



AME, Inc., Featured in Top Trade Publication

AME, Inc., was recently featured in the November, 1999 issue of Dixie Contractor magazine. The magazine is distributed to over 11,000 industry subscribers throughout South Carolina, Georgia, Florida and Alabama.
