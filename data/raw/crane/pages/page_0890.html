Planning and building a custom log home demands attention to many details and options. To ensure our customers are thrilled with their complete log home system, we perform a site analysis as we begin designing. Our individually designed plans may be a combination of an existing plan and your dreams or 100% your imagination.

Natural full size Eastern White Pine logs averaging 18”-20” butt diameter (larger sizes available) create structural integrity and greater thermal performance. Your log home stays cool in the summer and warm in the winter.

Hand picked, hand peeled, hand scribed and hand cut equals a Timber Wolf Handcrafted original!

Tree selection is critical for a well-built log home. We hand select every log to ensure the best quality timber in our buildings. The logs are hand peeled with a drawknife one pull at a time. Peeling is often a taken for granted art that requires attention to the log and physical strength, without a good peel the character of the log is lost. Take a close look at the detail of our logs, we are confident you will be impressed with the natural contours maintained after peeling.

In addition to producing handcrafted log homes using fresh-cut, Michigan-native Pine, we are pleased to offer our customers fully handcrafted log homes using kiln-dried Pine, Douglas Fir and Cedar. As one of only a select few handcrafters in the world to offer customers the option of kiln-dried logs, our log homes experience less shrinkage and less surface checking than homes built with air dried logs. Besides significantly reducing the adjustment period of the home, kiln-dried logs have the added benefit of simplifying subtrade construction and providing a log surface that is ready for staining. Like our fresh-cut logs our log homes are environmentally responsible and use tree length logs with sizes of over 20 inches in diameter.

Immediately after peeling, our logs are treated with a non–toxic coating to maintain their natural brightness and discourage any fungi or molds. Nylon straps are then used to transport the log throughout the building process, keeping that freshly peeled surface nice and smooth. We take these extra steps to assure you the finest looking logs you can find.

We take great pride in our hand cut double scribed lateral grooves and notches. Our attention to detail shows. Flexible and weather tight, we run EPDM rubber gasket, imported from Sweden, along all long grooves and notches. On site, natural wool insulation fills the long groove insuring the best, least toxic weather barrier. Our double scribed long grooves and notches provide an airtight fit lasting for generations to come.

All window and door openings and exposed log end details are cut, ground and beveled. Appropriate keyway splines (angle/iron) for windows and doors are also included. Log drilling, mortising and necessary flats for electrical box covers and fixtures are done in our log yard prior to shipping. We supply and properly install through-bolts, adjustable screw jacks for vertical posts and required steel for structural log work including plates and washers.

While most of the work to your log shell is completed in our log yard, a few things are left to complete on site. Items such as slots for interior walls, ceiling grooves and various roof details are better finished when the shell is set on your site.

Once your shell is completed in our yard, it is carefully loaded using the same non-marring nylon straps.Our experienced drivers deliver your log shell on our trucks and trailers.The reassembly of your Timber Wolf Handcrafted Log Home is performed by the same crew who first built it in the log yard.

Real log possess the beauty nature gave it – even if that means nature’s imperfections like burls, “cat faces” and scars. Timber Wolf Handcrafted Log Homes supplies and installs every log component of your log shell to insure quality and the precision necessary to make your home weather tight. All this, plus our years of experience and attention to detail assure you the finest in handcrafted log construction.
