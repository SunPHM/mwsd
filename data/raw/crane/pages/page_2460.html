PHOTO GALLERY #2

American The Beauty - Panel 2

All of the slip add-ons are attached to the background canvas. The palm, cypress, and

branch from Panel One are here and the Northern pine trees will arrive soon.

 

After 500 hours of stitching, more background is covered than bare canvas showing. Birmingham Needlearts chapter continues to schedule work sessions. One day five members from the Alabama chapter in Huntsville drove down and worked on the panel.

 

I will be attending National EGA in Minneapolis in October where all panel coordinators will meet and discuss our joint progress. Panel Two will be in the Exhibit area at National 2005 in Atlanta.

                              Druscilla Defalque

                              October 1, 2004
