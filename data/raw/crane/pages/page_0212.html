R/P Johnson, Inc.'s construction services includes a hydraulic truck crane and hydraulic boom truck to service industry, contractors,farmers and others in Ohio. These versatile cranes are available for hourly rental with a qualified operator and are designed to travel quickly and safely to work sites. Offering 24 hour service to our customer, R/P Johnson, Inc. crane service is available 7 days a week. Our operators are equipped with mobile telephones for rapid deployment to your site.

The 30 Ton Hydraulic Truck Crane has a Basic Boom Length of 94.75' with Swing Away Offsetable Jib Erected and Fully Extended Tip Height of 143'.

The 14 Ton Hydraulic Boom Truck has a basic Boom length of 66.75', with Swing Away Jib Erected and Fully Extended Tip Height of 188'.

Set up time at the job site is kept to a minimum owing partially to the self storing hydraulic outriggers and stabilizers. Extended tip height is easilyobtained through the use of the swing-away boom mounted jib. Heavier lifts are accomplished by rapid conversion from a headache ball to a multi-part line tackle block.

Insuring the safety of lifting conditions, our cranes are equipped with anti-two block devices with a 20,000 lbs. load dynamometer on the boom truck and a computerized load Moment indicator on the 30 ton for questionable load weights. Our cranes are OSHA approved, and R/P Johnson, Inc. personnel are fully trained for safe operation.
