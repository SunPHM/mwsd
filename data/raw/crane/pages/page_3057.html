Introduction
Origami-Tsuru:

Folding Origami Cranes has been an Island tradition symbolizing long life and happiness. One thousand and one cranes properly folded and mounted becomes a beautiful work-of-art that you and your family will enjoy for years to come.

You can have your 1001 cranes mounted to the design of your choice and framed for weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, (yakudoshi) or any occasion.

The mounting includes the arrangement of your choice on a black velvet background or rice paper and framed to your specifications.

Prices vary according to the complexity of your design. From 1989 I have provided professional, knowledgeable and personalized service. We crate for Insured Shipping.
