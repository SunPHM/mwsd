Sandhill Crane


The sandhill crane is a tall gray bird with a long neck and a red cap. They can get to be 34-48 in. tall.

They will eat any type of berry, wheat or insects. Sometimes they will eat amphibians.

Their habitat is fresh water marshes, fields, prairies and lakes.

The sandhill crane's predators are raccoons, foxes, crows and bobcats.

They meet their partner in the spring it takes them 24 hours just to lay their eggs.The sandhill crane lays their eggs in the tundra in Canada.
