Carson Helicopters can pour concrete and erect steel structures in areas where ground based cranes are not suitable.
Soft or swamp ground, steep mountain terrains, environmentally protected areas and offshore islands are easily accessed by our air cranes for delivery and placement of construction materials and equipment. Large and heavy materials and equipment can be moved and placed in a short period of time.
 
Expensive road construction and environmental restrictions can be overcome by our helicopter cranes allowing cost effective construction in places where installations would otherwise be impossible. Our experienced pilots can perform these tasks with the necessary precision to provide a cost effective alternative to more traditional construction methods.
