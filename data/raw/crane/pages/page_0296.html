With a tradition of quality service we are committed to customer satisfaction!

Able Crane has been servicing New England for over 25 years.

We have modern hydraulic cranes ranging in size from 25 tons up to 210 tons.

Our crane rental service is measured by the repeat business and referrals over the years.

Able Crane offers the latest technology in its fleet of cranes; all are rented with a skilled operator. All of our cranes are mobile truck cranes and service a 50 mile radius from our New Hampshire base including Massachusetts, Vermont and Maine.

Crane service and rental is the core of our business. We appreciate servicing the lifting needs of our present customers and look forward to working with new customers.
