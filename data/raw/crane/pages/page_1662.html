Modification and adjustment of new or existing fork-lifts, cranes, earthmovers and other means of transport

Houcon Construction Services turns standard products into custom-made products, specific to the importer, dealer or end-user. This is always cheaper than developing a completely new product for a one-off application.

Houcon Construction Services is often the discussion partner and the party that carries out the work on behalf of the purchaser. Examples are the alteration of fork-lifts, cranes and earthmovers according to customer specifications, adjusting turning circles, shortening masts and so on.
