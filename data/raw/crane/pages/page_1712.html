K-10000 tower crane
COUNTERWEIGHT TROLLEYS

 


The Counterweight Trolleys, as well as the Trolleys, are suspended in multi-level fully boggied supports, i.e. the wheel loads are evenly shared, whether or not the roller path is straight.
The Traction Cable dead-ends on a Ratchet Winch facilitating easy take-up of the gradually released constructional stretch of a new cable.
