We provide Haulout & Launch services for yachts up to 32 ft wide using our specially designed 70-ton Marine Travelift. The yard area amounts to 6 acres which is dedicated to storage, repair and painting. Osmosis treatments, metal fabrication, mechanical and electrical departments are also available on site.

WoodWork
Woodworking applications are directed to Fred Thomas who operates Shipwrights Ltd also located on the compound.

Marine Supplies
Our marine supplies are provided courtesy of Island Water World who have a satellite store onsite in St. David’s, as well as a main store on Lagoon Road in the capital of St. George's. The store caters towards items for repairs with the main store stocking similar items and accessories such as clothing, fishing equipment etc.

Battery Maintenance
Grenada Marine offers a complete line of battery maintenance including the use of pulse Technology. Battery life can be doubled or tripled by fitting pulse units during installation of new batteries. Visit www.pulsetech.net, then select POWER PULSE 12 (for batteries on constant use) or INDUSTRIAL SOLARGIZER 12 (for use on batteries used occasionally).
 

Rigging
Mast Stepping
With a 70 foot boom, our crane can remove the masts of most of the yachts we are able to haul, the ones that our crane can't handle can be handled by a locally available mobile crane with a 120ft boom, mobilization fees apply in the case of the latter.

Areas of expertise in this department are:- Splicing all exotic lines, wire to rope splice, both traditional and modern rigging. Presently, parts are imported to complete jobs however, as soon as the machines arrive we will be able to manufacture the parts ourselves.
