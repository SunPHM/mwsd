Crane construction

Design, production, delivery and assembly of:
Hoisting facilities with electric and manual operation, e.g.

  • single-beam and twin-beam bridge cranes
  • single-beam and twin-beam ceiling travelling cranes
  • wall slewing cranes and slewing pillar cranes
  • monorails
  • overhead cranes
  • hoisting units for freight transport through shafts
  • special hoisting facilities without rope migration

Additionally: Retrofitting of remote controls into cranes (radio-controlled and infrared-controlled). So crane control will be simply and fast.

This portable hoisting unit (on the right) is to be bought only once and can be used then at many places of action. It replaces expensive beams and cranes. Only one connection plate is to be installed at the possible place of action.
