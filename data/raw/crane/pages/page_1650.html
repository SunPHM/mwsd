K-10000 tower crane
MAIN JIB

 
The Jib is assembled from generally 40 ft jib sections, requiring a couple of bolts in the two Top Chords and in the Single Bottom Chord.
Because of the large dimensions of the K-10000 Jib, there is ample space for a supremely safe internal walkway.
