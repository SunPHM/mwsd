A paper crane is a symbol of hope and the possibility of fulfilling all of your dreams. Print out and follow the instructions below to make your own paper crane to keep as a symbol of hope.
Making a paper crane requires a square piece of paper. If you don't have one, we have provided a template for you to print and cut out a square.
