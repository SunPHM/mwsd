TESTING OF POWER HYDRAULIC SYSTEMS AND UNITS

THE STAND FOR WINCH TESTING

The stand is designed to test the winches of lifting system in cranes, hoists, pipe-layers etc., and complete hydraulic systems of those machines by simulation of actual work conditions by means of weight of variable value lifted and lowered on a special tower.
    The range of tests:
  • measurement of characteristics,
  • brake adjustment,
  • durability tests,
  • modelling the power transmission and control systems of winches under conditions simulating their actual work.

Stand parameters:
Single line pull 125 kN
Max. single line speed 142 m/min
Tower height 35 m
Max. single line pull up to 200 kN
Max. single line speed up to 60 m/min

STAND FOR HYDRAULIC CYLINDER TESTING

Testing of all types hydraulic cylinders, both standard type and special long stroke type (length up to 9 m) used for telescoping the mobile crane jibs.

The following tests are provided, in the range of pressure up to 42 MPa:

  • tightness,
  • characteristics,
  • durability of seals,
  • durability of articulated joints,
  • others depending on needs.
    Stand parameters:
    Stroke up to 9m
    Piston diameter up to 240mm
    Max. output up to 1200l/min
    Max. pressure 42 MPa
    Full automation of the tests.
