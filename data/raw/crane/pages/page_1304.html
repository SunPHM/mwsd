Service

Workshop
3,000m2 workshop featuring seven bays for the repair or refurbishment of mobile truck, rough terrain, crawler cranes or tower cranes. Includes an underbody service pit.

Parts Store
Carrying a large stock of MAEDA and NATIONAL CRANE components and various other crane spares, in addition to a comprehensive range of filters.

Washbay
EPA approved washbay with high pressure detergent/steam cleaner is available to ensure that equipment is thoroughly cleaned and waste disposed of in the authorised manner.

Holding Yard
10,000m2 holding yard suitable for servicing large tower cranes and the like, as well as storage for insurance work and load testing prior to delivery.

Field Service
Available at call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week with fully equipped service vehicles able to travel throughout NSW servicing and repairing cranes on site. In addition, we have travelled to Papua New Guinea, Samoa, Soloman Islands, Vanuatu, American Samoa and Indonesia to carry out field service.

Safety System Installations
A complete range of safety systems can be fitted to various models of cranes, including Favco (tower cranes), Franna (articulated), telescopic and lattice boom cranes.
