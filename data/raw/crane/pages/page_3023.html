Betsy Popp
Originals

The Northwoods of Wisconsin have always been home for Betsy.  This has given her the opportunity to study and sketch her favorite wildlife within an easy drive of her home.  Betsy works in a variety of mediums which include fine pencil drawings, colorful acrylic paintings, vibrant pastels, unique wood burnings, and detailed wood carvings.  Such a wide variety of original art work, yet all uniquely the work of Betsy Popp.

Betsy's pencil work has earned her a spot at the Leigh Yawkey Woodson Art Museum in their prestigious annual "Birds in Art" exhibit.  She has received "Best of Show" awards as well as many "First Place" ribbons for her wood carvings and her wood burnings.  Recently she was selected to exhibit in the Third Annual Exhibition "For Pastels Only on Cape Cod".

Some other honors include:
- Accepted and placed three years in a row in the Rocky Mountain Arts Assoc. "Rocky Mountain Carvers Cup," Kimball Art Ctr., Orem, Utah.
- Juried into the "Birds of Prey in Art" at the Millbrook Gallery, Millbrook, NY.
- Exhibited at the Indiana "Wildlife Artist Exhibit," Indianapolis, IN.
- Exhibited at "Art Wyo" at West Winds Gallery, Casper, WY.
- Exhibited at the "Bald Eagle Conference Art Show," Klamath Falls, OR.
- Took 2nd. place at the "Endangered Species Society Show," Denver, CO.
- Voted "Artist of the Year - 2002" by the Northwoods Wildlife Center in Minocqua, WI.
