The Gain, It's Plain, is Mainly in the Crane

Mid-August saw an unusual vehicle in the MOAH driveway and parking area. SummerHill Homes brought in a crane to install reinforcing bar assemblies in the structure of the adjacent building under construction.

For a few hours on a sunny afternoon, steel soared through the air. Its job done, the crane departed and calm descended instead of rebar.
