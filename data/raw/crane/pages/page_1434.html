Iran

Disaster in Iran, I have difficulty in comprehending loss of lives of that magnitude. Help is needed and it is time to set religion and political believes a side. This is a good place to start The international Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies It is good to see the people unite and help. I can remember when disaster struck In Darwin on Christmas Eve 1974 (Cyclone Tracy) I was travelling on the Pacific Hwy north towards Brisbane and encountered a traffic jam. Volunteers collecting money in buckets somewhere around Taree I know we did dig deep in to our pockets at that time. I like to think we can help people in Iran in the same way. Don't Type Skype

I downloaded Skype a few weeks ago but since I had no one to test the program with it just sat there for a while. So the other day I went to work and installed Skype on or computers at work and we started to use it. A great program and the quality of the phone calls are fantastic. It has a few hiccups now and then but after all this is a Beta version. Almost 4 million downloads of the program to date. It almost reminds me of early ICQ days. VOIP is getting better and better and the clever use of a P2P makes this program stand out from the rest or as Mark Andreessen said in a reascent interview "they have nailed it".

Lost your phone lately?

Ok you know when you lose your mobile phone at a party or in the office or where ever
You start calling your own number in hope of finding your trusted devise.

This happened to me the other day . I had looked everywhere and my phone was gone.
The only possible place I thought was in the garbage, accidentally tossed my phone in the garbage!
Stupid I know but …. I am in luck the bins has not been emptied
Easy phone the number m…… well what is the number? Never really use it so I can't remember
Phoned a friend. Can you tell me what my phone number is ? By this time you feel pretty stupid
But not as stupid as when you together with another friend are walking up and down rows of garbage bins your ear close to every lid ………NO not there……


I would have needed this, An application that would on my remote command make my phone take a photo and send it back to me via MMs or email . This is it MMS CAM for Series60. "Just send the command SMS '//YMMSCAM' and in the twinkling of an eye it takes a photo and sends it back via MMS or via e-mail." If I had that app that day I would have seen a photo of the rig of my yacht Yes the phone was on the deck.

I think you could have a lot of fun with this app Santa please………
