DANIEL SANBORN HANSEN SR.

HANSEN, Daniel Sanborn Sr., 28, of Minot, Maine, committed suicide July 2.

Although Mr. Hansen was brought up in the church, he wasn’t attending at the time of his death. In an article in the Portland, Maine, Press Herald, Daniel’s parents, Neal and Kim, noted that they are talking about his suicide in hopes that it may prevent someone else from committing suicide.

Could Daniel’s suicide have been prevented? the article asked. Did his parents lecture him too often about the consequences of his impulsive (and sometimes destructive) behavior, or did they not lecture him enough? His six-year-old son is angry about his father’s suicide. In tears, his father, Neal, asked how Daniel could do this to his family.

Daniel is survived by his wife, Tara; their sons, Daniel Jr. and Jordan; parents, Neal and Kimbalee (Booth) Hansen; a brother and sister-in-law, Erich and Cathy; grandparents Fannie and Kenneth Harriman; and many aunts, uncles and cousins.
