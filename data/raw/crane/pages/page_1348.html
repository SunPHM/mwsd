Location

Chengxi Shipyard, located in Jiangyin City at the lower reach of the Yangtze River, is one of the largest shiprepairing and newbuilding enterprises under CSSC. It has a good advantage in road and waterway transportation as it is of short distance to port cities like Shanghai, Nantong and Zharlgiiagang, P. R. of China.

Slipways: Two newbuilding slipways for 30,000 and 15,000 DWT equipped with one set of 120-ton and two sets of 40-ton gantry cranes.

Quays: The river front stretches over 2000 meters. The yard has a 1180-meter long and 20 m wide outfitting quay , the front water depth being 8-14 meters and the main navigation channel 30 meters

Cranes: On this quay there are 7 sets of 25-ton and 30-ton shore cranes and 2 sets of 100-ton and 60-ton floating cranes.

Shiprepair & Conversion

Chengxi Shipyard is mainly engaged in shiprepairing, newbuilding, steel construction, offshore units repairing and electromechanical equipment fabrication. The yard is rich in shiprepairing and newbuilding facilities and equipment.

Some of our services are:

Hull & Dock Repair: Large Steel Renewal, Hull derusting, Hull Painting, Hatch covers, propellers, CCP & Rudder dismantling.

Machinery & Electrical Repair: Electric Equipment, Main Engine repair, Meteorological Instruments installation, Propeller and Rudder machining, cranes, Large Boiler repair and replacement.

Piston machining, Crankshaft grinding, Metal Thermal Spraying, Turbocharger, RHD Series Governor Maintenance, Chemical cleaning, etc.

Ship Conversion:

Conversion of 20,000 ton Fishing base vessel M/V Slavjansk.

Conversion of 18,000ton Bulk Carrier M/V Fujisan Maru, Darya Shaan, M/V Darya Jaan into Selfloading & Unloading Cement carrier

Special Repair: Oil Water Treatment, Tank Coating.

Ship Spare Parts: Gangway, Lifeboats, Rubber packing, Anchor Chain, Sacrificial Anodes, GMDSS Equipment.

Ship Building

Chengxi Shipyard has two slipways for building the vessels up to 30,000 DWT and 15,000 DWT respectively, and the yard also has one set of 50 ton crane and one set of 120 ton and 40 ton gantry cranes. CS is capable of building different class of vessels and has built around 100 vessels till now.

Some shipbuilding equipment: Steel pre-treatment Line, Plasma cutting Machine, 150 ton ship section transporter, NC Pipe bender, 600 ton Hydraulic Press, Paint Cell, Block site for shipbuilding, CAD for shipbuilding.

Quality Management

The yard is pursuing Business Concept of " one vessel as an advertisement, and one advertisement as one market", while strengthening the Quality Management.

The yard was accredited and certified with lSO 9002 Certificate by CSQA and LRQA respectively in September 1996 and October 1999 for shiprepairing, newbuilding and steel construction.

Chengxi Shipyard is actively implementing the Strategic Policy of "improving the shiprepairing, developing the newbuilding and expanding the Non-Marine products" , executing the management policy of "Human resources basis, scientific management and science & technology in the lead" and developing the shipyard's enterprise spirit of "Unity and Harmony, Honesty and Sincerity, Practicality and Reality".
