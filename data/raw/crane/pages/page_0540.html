Crane Fly

Growing up I knew this kind of fly as a mosquito-eater. After moving back to Sitka and seeing lots of them, I decided to see if I could find out whether they really eat mosquitoes. So far, it seems unlikely that they do.

Location: Sheldon Jackson College, Sitka, Alaska
