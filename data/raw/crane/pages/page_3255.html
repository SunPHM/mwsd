Crane

This is a classic fold, and one of the most popular. It is believed that if you fold 1000 in a year you will have good luck for the rest of your life.

2. Fold the paper in half along the dotted line shown.

16. Do another fold in the other 'leg'.

17. Do a small reverse fold where shown, to make the head, then:

18. Pull the two points at the top apart, and blow some air into the fold to puff out the body.
