About CHC, Inc.

Since 1979 Coastal Hydraulic Cranes, Inc. has been designing and manufacturing hydraulic marine cranes, hydraulic equipment, and handling systems. Our emphasis is on flexibility to meet our customers' specifications and applications.  We adhere to the latest API 2C, ABS, OSHA, ANSI, USCG, AWS, DNV, and many other specifications.

Another aspect of our business in addition to new construction is providing 24-hour service for all types of pedestal mounted and truck mounted cranes. We provide an array of parts for all cranes. We also have specialized experience in rebuilding valves, pumps, cylinders, winches, and gearboxes.

CHC, Inc. represents several types of truck mounted, jib, and knuckle cranes, and is proud to announce the addition of FassiÒ truck cranes to our product line in March 1999.
