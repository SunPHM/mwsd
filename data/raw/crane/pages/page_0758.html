So much to see, so much to do. The Children's Museum of Virginia is a feast for young eyes, ears, minds, and imaginations. We've highlighted just a few of the many, many fun things you'll find here, so dig in…then come visit us and see it all for yourself!

The Lancaster Antique Toy & Train Collection
If you like trains, this'll stoke your firebox. Our Lancaster Antique Toy & Train Collection, among the world's finest, features four working layouts and thousands of engines and cars in a variety of scales.

 

Bubbles
Stretch a Bubble • Volume • Step Into a Bubble • Bubble Table

The Bubble Room is a guaranteed instant favorite. Experiment with different tools and techniques to make the biggest bubble possible, lots of small bubbles at once…even try a bubble on for size.

Planetarium

For entertainment, not even the sky is the limit. Explore the universe and other fascinating subjects in our state-of-the-art planetarium, featuring star-studded performances daily.

The City
Police Motorcycle • Fire Engine • City Bus • High Street Market •  TowneBank • Mail Box • Parking Meter • Olde Towne Clinic • Traffic Light

A kid's imagination is an amazing thing. The City is filled with fun props and real vehicles that shift those imaginations into overdrive.

Norfolk SPCA Exhibition

Want to know the best way to hold a puppy? Learn how to be a good pet owner and just have fun in the Pet Park. The first collaborative exhibit between the Norfolk SPCA and The Children's Museum of Virginia, the Pet Park offers a gazebo for reading books about animals, a plasma screen TV showing pet care tips, photo opportunities with animal face cutouts plus much more.
