HTC-8670 70 ton Hydraulic Truck Crane

New for 2061 is the 86 series of hydraulic lift cranes from FMC-Linkbelt. Capitalizing on their long experience in manufacturing construction equipment, the 8670 and its smaller brother the 8650 represent the ultimate in mobile, maneuverable heavy lift power. Guided by precise onboard electronics, the crane operator can position the unit to the most exacting of tolerances. With a boom height of 56 meters, and a swing clearance of only 2 meters wider than the chassis, the 86 series are indispensable when operating in tight urban confines.
