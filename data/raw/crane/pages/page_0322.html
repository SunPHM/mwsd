Plant and Equipment

Strategic Plant Location

Locating our Greenfield plant in California's Central Valley has positioned J.I.T. directly between the western USA flat rolled steel mills. Main line rail service permits receiving competitive rail shipments from all North American producers. This strategic positioning allows us to serve the California markets with same day delivery and permits next day deliveries into the other western states. Proximity to major interstate highway routes provides our customers a wide choice of both intra and interstate common carriers for on-time truck deliveries

Plant

Each of the three 80-foot coil storage bays is equipped with a 35 ton recieving / production crane for mill coils and a 12 ton finished goods crane. A 60 ton coil transfer car allows for safe and efficient movement of the coils from the storage bays to the appropriate production units. Each slitter has a mono-rail packaging line with an in-line digital scale. Inside rail and truck accesses provide weather protected receipts and shipments.

Equipment

J.I.T. Steel has three slitters offering a capacity range from .010" through .400" x 72" maximum width coils, two level cut-to-length lines with capacities from .014" min. through .500" max. x 96" wide and .625" max. x 72" wide, and five precision shears capable of blanking sheet products form .010" min. to .250" max. thickness x 144" long.
