My one year work experience at Siemens in Germany has provided me with the opportunity to gain a better insight into the structure and function of the Personnel Department. It helped me to improve myself in several ways and most of all I have become more confident in my language use. Now I have a better perception about my future career, than before I started my placement and I believe that this twelve months practice I have completed can open up a wider scale of career opportunities for me.
