Crane Update

    November 19, 2001

    President Garter's Visit
    Terrell County, Georgia

    The Migration Team and whooping cranes are staying put this morning in Terrell County, Georgia. Because of the windy conditions, it would make the flight to the next stop too long for the cranes.

    Former President Jimmy Carter paid the crew a visit at our last stopover. He and wife Rosilyn are avid birders, having more than 1000 species on thier life-list.
