Pillars, high ceilings, arched porticos and decorative niches give elegant classical styling to this splendid open-plan living room with access to the pool area and deck. Comfortable seating for six people with views to the golf course and lake. Fully air-conditioned with cable television and telephones providing access to free local calls.
An attractive formal dining area overlooks the landscaped front garden making an ideal setting for that special evening in.

For more casual dining a separate ceramic tiled nook with seating for four has views of the pool and direct access to the deck area.

Spacious open-plan, well -stocked and fully - equipped kitchen with central breakfast bar.

Appliances include microwave, fridge freezer, gas cooker/oven and dishwasher.

A separate utility room has full- size family washer and dryer.

Master bedroom with views across the golf course and doors directly to the pool terrace, queen - size bed, with walk-in closets, and luxury
en suite bathroom with separate toilet, twin hand basins, oversized bath & fully tiled walk in Roman shower.
Two further bedrooms are located adjacent to the dining area, one twin bedded, one double with queen- size bed.
Both have large closets and share a separate fully tiled bathroom with 
over - bath shower.

All bed linen and towels are provided.
