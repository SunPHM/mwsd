Andy Penrose

Me and My Shadows
Jonesville, Michigan
517-849-9623

Natives of Michigan, Andy and Jan Penrose have always had a love for birds. They have translated this passion into an art form, precisely and delicatley cutting by hand bird silhouettes from copper, brass and wire.

Perched on branches, poised over a flower, standing among cattails or drifting on water, their creations include chickadees, cardinals, titmice, nuthatches, hummingbirds, loons and cranes. All of the work is life-like. Their birds have literally flown all over the world.

Their art form also includes some unique specialty designs just for fun!
