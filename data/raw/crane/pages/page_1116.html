"Company History"
 
Continental First Federal, Inc., "CFF", formally know as the CFF Agency, is a privately held equipment leasing/finance company located in Mt. Juliet, Tennessee. We specialize in financing Independent Contractors, Distributors, and Owner Operators for logistics companies across the continental U.S. and Canada. The company currently promotes financing for logistics specialty vehicles, straight truck, mobility vans, etc. (OTR semi's financed only within program accounts), and related benefits for this industry.

Since its inception, CFF has carved a niche for itself as a highly service oriented and progressive funding source with very competitive terms and rates. With over 45 years accumulative experience in equipment finance,  CFF's main focus is providing fast service and quick credit decisions to these companies when qualifying their independent contractors/distributors/owner operators which hire out to these companies, while providing these contractors better rates and terms through these programs.  Additional benefits can be added such as occupational accident, life insurance, and extended service contracts with special group rates.   CFF's new "Health Care Program", offered through C1 Benefits, is one that has everyone talking. 

However, any category of equipment is considered for financing.  As finance specialist, CFF can approve many transactions that banks and other financial institutions decline. 

CFF now provides financing for vehicles to all "Mobility Dealers".  These vehicles are equipped with a lift to pick up wheelchairs or other means of transporting wheelchairs.  

Also, at Continental First Federal our goal is to provide an easy to use, reliable, responsive and economic financing source for the commercial HVAC market. All costs associated with this market can be financed, including soft costs such as installation, engineering, extended warranties and maintenance agreements. Want cash flow to operate your business, CFF can finance your accounts receivables or finance your equipment under a sale leaseback to cash out your equity... 

Another of CFF's main lines of business is factoring receivables for companies, which has made CFF a very diversified finance company.
