Further Information about this issue:

To emphasize the importance of wildlife conservation, the Whooping Crane was selected. Whooping Cranes have dwindled from thousands in number to a small flock of thirty birds.

Cachet design for the Fleetwood® First Day Cover by Fred Labitzke. Actual size: 6-1/2 x 3-5/8 inches.
