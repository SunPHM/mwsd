Crane Services

In effort to better service our clients needs, Fairweather Roofing, Inc., has two cranes equipped with a Terex Stinger Model 4792 (47000 LB Capacity).  These units have a four section main beam capable of reaching 101 ft. and a 44 ft. side fold two stage jib creating a maximum tip height of 144 ft.  These machines have a 360 degree working load chart and are capable of lifting loads over the front of the cab therefore increasing it’s amount of reach with certain setups.

 The bed carrying capacity of these cranes are approximately 7500lbs.  These units and operators are radio dispatched and should enable us to service all your lifting needs.  Our lifting rates for these machines include both the crane and operator.

 If you have not used our crane service in the past, our original crane is equipped with a model 2863 14 Ton Simon RO.  It has 73 ft. of main tip height and a 24 to 40 ft. jib, which enables us to reach a maximum tip height of 113 ft.   This machine also has a 360 degree working load chart increasing the amount of reach with certain set-ups. 

 

The hourly rate for all machines and operators is $85.00 per hour with a (3) three-hour minimum. 

All rates are billed on a port-to-port basis.

 As always, we appreciate the opportunity to service all your lifting needs.
