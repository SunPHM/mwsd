Upcoming Events

April

Entire month of April - DestiNature, Dallas, Texas - Audubon will partner with more than twenty organizations to present DestiNature, a month-long series of field trips, exhibits, lectures, guided tours, and special events designed to educate and raise awareness of the natural world in North Texas. The events will also serve to celebrate the 100-year anniversary of the National Audubon Society, and announce the launch of Audubon's education projects in Dallas County. For detailed information, visit www.DestiNature.com

April Art Events - Release of Billy Hassel's Third Edition Lithograph "Bobwhite Quail" in conjunction with John James Audubon's birthday - El Paso, Dallas, San Antonio and Kingsville. Area art galleries will host the art unveilings and invite their client list, along with local Audubon members.

Ongoing

Birdathon is similar to a walkathon, but with a twist - participants count bird species and collect pledges from friends, family and co-workers. Some people pledge money for each species identified; others donate fixed amounts. Funds raised stay in Texas, supporting Audubon conservation programs throughout the state, and can be earmarked to support a special project if you choose. Click here for more information.
