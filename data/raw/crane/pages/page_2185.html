Southern Texas 2001 photos

Click on the small picture to see a larger, more detailed version. I toured the southern tip of Texas. The trip was led by Jan Hansen (dba Otus Asio Tours otusasiotours@bellsouth.net). He leads a variety of trips, all of which I would recommend. I apologize for the quality of those pictures taken in low or difficult light conditions.

Jan organized and Mark Korducki led a trip to the Texas Hill Country and Southern Rio Grande areas in April 2003. Those pixtures are available. Jan Hansen organized and Mark Korducki led a trip to the Texas Hill Country and Big Bend National Park areas in April 2004. The 2004 pixtures are now available.
