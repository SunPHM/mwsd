About the book:
Here is a new collection of unique origami designs, first created by the nineteenth century Buddhist priest, Rokoan Gido (1761-1834). Twenty-four designs are explained in this beautifully produced book, using full color photographs and easy-to-follow diagrams. Paper craft enthusiasts will learn to create figures including up to 100 connected cranes using this ancient cut-and-fold origami technique. The colorful and textured washi paper, included with each book, adds beauty to each completed figure. Origami: Rokoan Style #2 is the perfect gift for paper craft artists of all ages.
