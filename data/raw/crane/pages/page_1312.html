The "Spanopoulos Bros" Group of Companies is a dynamic and flexible maritime services venture.

Our fleet of floating vessels is continuously renewed, while the already existing ones are reconstructed and upgraded to meet the needs of each single project. Our fleet is comprised of the Group's privately owned floating cranes, flattop and split barges, floating drills and excavators, as well as other auxiliary means or special construction equipment, which are adapted to the needs of each project and subsequently benefiting each client. The ultimate reward for us is the satisfaction of our clients.

The "Spanopoulos Bros" Group of Companies is characterized by 24-hour readiness and its ability to carry out any operation and solve any emergency problem, with speed, safety and efficiency, while reducing the time and cost as much as possible.

The fields in which our Group of Companies specializes in are:
  • Harbor and Marina Construction
  • Towages
  • Refloating of Wrecks - Salvages
  • Underwater Projects
  • Cable and Pipe Laying
  • Cargo Shift
  • Excavation
  • Shipyard & Repairs
