Construction Machines PAVERS
We provide consulting and procurement services
to our clients to help them obtain proper machines
for their projects with economical prices.

We supply brand-new or used machines depending
on the feasibility of the project and the request.

Used machinery can be supplied "AS IS", or as
overhauled, tested and painted to Clients' colour
requirements with Warranty Certificates.

After completion of the project, we arrange resale
of the machinery at site with their actual values.

We organize packing, insurance, and shipment to
site providing the required documents to ensure
trouble free clearance at the customs on arrival.

Operator training is also included at no additional
cost to our Clients at the suppliers' premises.

We can also arrange, repairs, maintenance or
overhauls at site, if requested.

Our Clients always have our full technical support
for the machines we suppied.
