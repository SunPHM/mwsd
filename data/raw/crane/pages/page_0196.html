Crane Hunting

 
PANHANDLE'S BEST, INC. offers Sandhill Crane hunts over mounted decoys. (We do not offer plastic hunts for sandhill cranes because of the extreme wariness of these birds.)

Typically, hunters can expect to shoot limits (or near limits) of Sandhill Cranes, and quite often, capitalize on some "bonus" Geese during the same hunt!
