The Necedah Lions Fourth Annual 'Whooping Crane Festival" takes place in Necedah on
September 18, 2004.  This is fun for the entire family.  Lions pancake breakfast
and bus tours of the Necedah National Wildlife Refuge hourly from 7a.m. to 4p.m.
Activities begin at 10a.m. and run throughout the day.  Food & Beverages all day.
There will be Arts and Crafts, Raptor, Bird Carving Demos, Wild Life Displays,
Rendezvous Displays, Youth Activities, Kids Art Contest, ATV raffle, Souvenirs
and Bluegrass Music.  Don't forget to try some Whooper Brew!

All proceeds will be used to support Lions and the Whooping Crane Re-Introduction Projects.
