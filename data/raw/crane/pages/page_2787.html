Davenport - Vander Veer Park

Vander Veer Botanical Park, in Davenport.

The Vander Veer lagoon is a popular spot for fishing or feeding the ducks, or simply taking a walk along the water's edge.

Some of the Vander Veer Botanical Gardens near the Conservatory.

Another beautiful view of the gardens.

A 15 foot tall sculpture of a Sandhill Crane, carved by local artist Tom Gleich from a 120 year old Sycamore tree.

The newly created "Grand Allee" leads from the lagoon area to the recently renovated historic fountain at the south end of the park.
