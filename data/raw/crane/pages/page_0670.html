View from the Tower Crane

  Click on image for larger picture
On May 11, I climbed the tower crane.  The view is awesome.
The crane is 160 feet tall.  It took me 10-15 minutes to climb 16 stories of ladder.  Strangely, it was my arms that ached, since most of the ladders are pitched negatively, and your body weigh tends to fall away from the ladder.
Chad is the crane operator,  He learned from his Dad.  Chad, his father, and his brother all operate tower cranes.
This is the roof of the Graduate Science Research Center.  You can see lots of structural steel which will be used to support the roof.
Here is a view facing North.  You can see the roof of the Physical Science Center, the radio telescope, and the Capital dome in the distance.
