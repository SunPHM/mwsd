Crane West, Inc. was created in 1977 to provide professional and competitive service in all aspects of crane, lifting and heavy hauling operations. Capitalizing on our extensive experience and concentrating on what we do best, Crane West, Inc. has built a reputation for dependability, responsiveness and customized solutions. From designing critical lift plans to complex moving projects we have the manpower and equipment to get the job done.

We offer complete crane, lifting and heavy hauling management packages. This ranges from design and implementation of large lifting projects to specialize equipment transport. When you hire Crane West Inc., you receive highly trained operators, state-of-the art equipment, experienced management and all necessary insurance. Our investment in new equipment results in the most modern and comprehensive fleet of boom and hydraulic cranes, rigging, trucks and flatbeds available.

Crane West, Inc. is a member of the Associated General Contractors and the Idaho Trucking Association.
