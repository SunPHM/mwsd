Warning Level Descriptions

     While you all may know what the current Album Advisory Warning level is, you may be asking yourself, "Self, what in the bluest of blue hells does a U2 Album Advisory Warning Level of Yellow mean?? What should I do to prepare myself? Does this guy have too much time on his hands or what?"
     Well, now the answers to all those questions can be found right here... and for that last question, yes... I do have waaaaay too much time on my hands.

 Technically there's no new album on the slate for a few more years, but then again it's just waaay too busy in the U2 camp to slip the Advisory System down to Green.  This is the ultimate time for U2 fans... a year long celebration of all things U2, before we slip back into the pre-album doldrums.  Enjoy.

The release of the new U2 Album is imminent. Batten down the hatches, hide the women and children. The new single will be out shortly, and is probably being leaked over the Internet already. Bono has dyed his hair black and The Edge has gotten himself a new beanie. Bvlgari stock soars through the roof.

The album and release date is all but confirmed, and the countdown has begun. Adam has been pulled out of the basement and dusted off, given a shave, a hair cut, a new pair of red velvet pants, and a new spoon Bono picked up in Africa.

We know the album is close, but we don't exactly know how close. Various news reports are circling, mostly from ever so reliable sources such as The Edge's cousin, Bono's half sister, and the drunken hobo who lives under the bench outside of Hannover Quay studios.

Bono is in Africa bringing attention to the plight of the East African Whooping Crane, Adam is at the World Spoon Lover's Convention in Henniker, New Hampshire, Edge is posing for Mr. Clean advertisements, and Larry's sitting in the studio wondering where in the hell everyone is.

New album? What new album? The next U2 album is tentatively scheduled for release right after "Chinese Democracy" by Guns N' Roses.
