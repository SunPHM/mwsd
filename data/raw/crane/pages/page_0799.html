Gas sampling system

Perma Pure’s Micro-GASS Gas Analysis Sampling System reduces the water content of gases from humid environments to optimal humidity for electrochemical sensors.

It does this without altering the concentration of analysis gases such as carbon disulphide, chlorine or methane in the sample. This overcomes the problems with chiller/condensers that remove water by condensation and so reduce the concentration of water-soluble gases. Perma Pure’s system is continuous and self-regenerating.

Field gas monitor

Innova introduces the new gas monitor 1412 based on the photoacoustic infrared detection method. The 1412 can have up to five optical filters installed, allowing simultaneous measurement of the concentration of up to five component gases and water vapour in any air sample.

The detection limit is gas dependent but typically is in the ppb region. The accuracy of measurements is ensured by the instrument’s ability to compensate for temperature and pressure fluctuations, water vapour interference and influences from other gases known to be present.

Vapour analysis

The InfraRan specific vapour analyser from Wilks provides real-time, on-site measurements of sulphur hexafluoride (SF6) down to below 1ppm. SF6 is an important industrial gas but has a potent greenhouse potential.

The InfraRan has a measuring range of 0-10ppm with a resolution of 0.01ppm and a 5-10 second response time. This makes the instrument ideal for tracer and leak detection applications.

The portable InfraRan system weighs 8.2kg and can run for 8 hours on its internal rechargeable battery. It can also run on mains power.
