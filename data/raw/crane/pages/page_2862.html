STOCK IMAGES

My collection of 35mm color transparencies numbers over 90,000 and covers a wide range of North American nature subjects. Birds are a favorite subject of mine, with hummingbirds being a specialty. Insects are well represented in my collection. Mammals, reptiles, amphibians, plants, and scenics are also included. Areas with extensive coverage include the deserts of the southwestern U.S., the Rocky Mountains, the Texas gulf coast, south Texas, Yellowstone National Park, Colorado, Arizona, Alaska, New Mexico, Florida, and Canada.

Credits include magazines such as Audubon, Birder's World, Bird Watcher's Digest, Colorado Outdoors, National Geographic, National Wildlife, and Ranger Rick. Calendar credits include Audubon, Avalanche, Golden Turtle, Landmark, NorthWord, Sierra Club, Smith-Southwestern, and Terrell. Book publishers include Academic Press, Chanticleer, Houghton Mifflin, Kendall/Hunt, Key Porter, Little Brown, Meredith, NorthWord, Stackpole, Storey , Sunset, and Voyageur.

For information on licensing images and the availability of subjects, please contact me by e-mail, or by regular mail at PO Box 592 Hereford, AZ 85615

Birds

More than 300 species of U.S. birds are included. Extensive coverage of most species of hummingbirds that occur in the U.S. Images include adults in flight feeding at a wide variety of flowers, portraits, nest building, eggs and young, adults feeding nestlings, and at feeders. Many species of desert birds. Cavity nesters such as woodpeckers, bluebirds, chickadees, wrens, nuthatches, and swallows are well represented. Also many images of waterfowl, waders, songbirds, owls, and bald eagles.

INSECTS

Large selection of insect images depicting behavior, camouflage, predator/prey relationships, biological control, life histories, and pest status. With a Master's degree in entomology I am able to provide accurate identifications and knowledgeable captions.
