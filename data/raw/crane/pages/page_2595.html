Sandhill Crane Hunting

Professional fishing and waterfowl guide specializing in Walleye fishing, Canadian Goose hunting and Sandhill Crane hunting.

Serving sportsmen for ten years in Oklahoma, Texas, Kansas, Arkansas.

Prime season for sandhill begins in November and lasts thru December. I provide all  sandhill crane decoys, calls, and blind material. License requirements are a OK hunting license 1-800-223-3333 (24 hours a day) or directly from the Wildlife Department by calling 1-800-949-6392, Monday-Friday, 8:00 a.m. till 4:30p.m. (Visa or MasterCard only. Setup time is about forty minutes, and everyone helps with setup and teardown. Setup includes walking into the field if we are not able to transport into field due to crops or mud. We are able to transport impaired or handicapped hunters into the field. Application of camo on individual blinds, digging a grave pit (Shallow pit for hunter to lay in)if required. Setting up decoys, and the ability to lay down and then sit up to shoot birds. Allow for mailing time to receive your license). Non resident is $42.50 for five days, $92.50 for an annual, a sandhill crane permit which you can obtain at the above numbers for $3.00 and a HIP permit which is $3.00.You must have a Oklahoma hunting license, and both a Federal waterfowl stamp, and a Oklahoma state waterfowl permit. The stamp and permit are not required but many types of waterfowl will also land in the decoys.  Reservations should be made well in advance. The cost per person is $110.00 per hunt.
