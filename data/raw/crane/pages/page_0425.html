  Lindsey Crane
Class:
Senior
Hometown:
Fairpoint, N.Y.
High School:
Fairpoint
Height:
5-8
Event:
Free/Back

One of the Cavaliers’ leaders in the sprint freestyle and 100 backstroke ... terrific relay swimmer ... helped set the school and ACC record in the 200 free relay (1:30.98) ... ranks seventh all-time at Virginia in the 100 back and eighth in the 50 free ... a first-team All-American.

2001-2002– Earned All-America honors for the first time as a member of UVa’s 200 free relay that placed eighth at the 2002 NCAAs ... helped set the school record in the 200 free relay with a time of 1:30.98 in the prelims at NCAAs ... was a member of UVa’s runner-up 200 free relay at the ACC Championship ... also swam on the team’s fifth place 200 medley relay ... a consolation finalist in the 100 free (15th) ... swam the 50 free and 100 back at ACCs as well ... varsity letterwinner. 2000-2001- Served as a team captain ... consolation finalist at the ACC Championships in the 50 free (11th), 100 back (11th) and 100 free (13th) ... swam on UVa’s 200 and 400 free relay teams that finished third, as well as the team’s fourth place 200 medley relay ... varsity letterwinner.

1999-2000: Was the team’s surprise sprinter during the season ... ACC consolation finalist in the 50 free (10th), 100 free (13th) and 100 backstroke (12th).

Prep & Personal: Four-year varsity swimmer for Fairport High ... two-time New York State High School champion ... MVP at 1998 state meet ... 1998 Fairport High School Athlete of the Year ... daughter of Susan and Jeffrey Crane ... born August 29, 1981 in Rochester, N.Y. ... majoring in psychology.
