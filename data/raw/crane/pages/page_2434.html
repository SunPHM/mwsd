Sandhill Crane
 

Radio-tagged Sandhill Crane (Grus canadensis), near the Town of Alabama, Genesee County, New York. Video-grab copyright of Angus Wilson © 2000

One of four banded/radio-tagged Sandhill Cranes that originated as part of the "Operation Migration" study based at the Tom Yawkey Wildlife Center in South Carolina.
