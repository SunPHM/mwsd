Origami

Our partners from Notsuharu Junior High School sent us a string of peace cranes and some origami paper for us to make our own. We did want to do this, but there was always so much work to be done, we didn't have time. That is, until the last day of school!

A patch of desk cleared to create the cranes.
Careful concentration and a lot of helping each other and soon we progress from one step to another.

Blow the body full of air and SUCCESS!

Of course, because we're in the Tech Lab, and surrounded by computers, we just had to find a sky picture and have the cranes fly out into the sky.
