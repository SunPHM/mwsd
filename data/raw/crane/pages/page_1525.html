The Summer Palace is a palace that was built for the Empress Dowager Cixi. It has a man-made lake called Kumming lake and a mountain made from the dirt from where the lake is.  We got to take a boat ride on Kumming lake.

     

        

         

         

         

       

Beijing is a city that is under so much construction, it's unbelievable.  I took lots of pictures of cranes just to demonstrate how much construction is actually going on.  People in the group thought I was weird for taking pictures of construction cranes in China, but I thought it was interesting!

 

Because we were somewhat of a dignitary-type group, due to our large group, and being that we were a choir (and the tour planners having connections), we got to eat a wonderful dinner at the Great Hall of the People. It is sort of like our White House, being that it's the national reception hall for China and a place where a lot of Chinese policy decisions are made.  Our tour guide Jenny told us that Beijing is the political capital of China while Shanghai is the economic capital of China.
