Construction on New
Administration Building
Completed March 2005

Pedestrian Access Easier From Wall Street or via Garage Elevators

 

Work on the new Administration Building was completed in March 2005. 
     The new Administrative structure is located next door to the existing Admin Building, 3000 Rockefeller, at the corner of Pacific Avenue and Oakes Avenue. Employees began moving into the 9-floor structure in March 2005. Moves were completed in June 2005.
     The new building covers what once was the County's parking garage. Citizens coming to the Snohomish County Administration Building can access the new building via the County's new underground garage. Entrances are off Pacific Avenue at Rockefeller, and off Oakes Avenue just south of Wall Street.
     However, if you're walking to the Administration Building, come in from the Wall Street side of Campus, cross the Plaza and enter the far-left building through its double, ADA accessible, doors.         
       Or take an elevators from the new parking garage to the "PLAZA" level. Entrances to the new Admin building are located to the left of the elevators.
        A new Plaza Cafe is open just to the left of the Elevators, as well.
