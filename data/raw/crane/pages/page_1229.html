Rigging Services: Plant Relocation

Maybe your need isn't quite as small as the installation of one piece of equipment. Maybe you need a whole plant relocated. State-to-state, coast-to-coast, AME is the best choice when your operation shifts locations.

AME can provide a turnkey relocation project experience. We are more than precision millwright, heavy and specialty rigging, and equipment installation. We are also industrial construction and plant relocation experts. We can:

  • Remove and relocate the existing equipment
  • Install existing and new equipment in the new facility
  • Provide you a working operation according to schedule


    We have the personnel, equipment, and total capabilities to make your project successful.

    • Full construction services - general, industrial, commercial, and institutional - to match your requirements.
    • One of the largest mobile crane fleets in the Carolinas, offering the ability to closely coordinate and control work schedules.
    • Precision and specialty concrete forming, pouring, and finishing services that allow the preparation of equipment foundations, pits, trenches, mass structures, and large pours or slabs to rigid tolerances and specifications.
    • Optical alignment for precise installation and integration with other systems.
    • Special rigging and lifting equipment to match any situation or restrictive work condition you require.
    • Dedicated field personnel, supervisors, and project managers with the experience to make certain you are operational and productive on schedule, and on budget. We know how to coordinate our work with your on-site activities.
    • Architectural and design/build services to help you plan your project.

    Relocating a plant can be a complicated issue. AME has the experience to help you design and plan a successful project.
