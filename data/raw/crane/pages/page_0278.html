Cynthia Crane

"Our group was fascinated by Cynthia Crane's stories of women about whom we had heard so little in the past. She brought history to life through her enthusiastic presentation, and we deeply felt the struggles of women of Jewish/Christian heritage trying to survive in Hitler's Third Reich. Cynthia provided a most interesting and informative lecture and graciously answered audience questions sparked by her revelations. Obviously, her research was painstaking and sensitive, providing great insight into the human spirit. We truly appreciated the time she spent with us!" --C. A. Miller, President, WIPO

" Cynthia Crane was a fascinating speaker for our literary club... Her emotional connection to her subject matter in Divided Lives is clear...We can feel her excitement and awe... She makes us feel there." --Ellen Reister, Program Chair, Trouveres
