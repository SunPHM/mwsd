Meridian Construction Company Limited is based in the British Virgin Islands, a group of islands located in the Northeast Caribbean, and specialises in civil engineering and marine construction.

Formed in 1996, the Company has expanded rapidly, particularly in the field of piling, after being awarded major prestressed piling contracts in Road Town, Tortola. Acquisition of construction plant, including cranes and barges, has led to work in the construction of bulkheads, docks and structural steel erection. Its strong civil engineering background allows the Company to compete for traditional construction.

Work has not been confined to the BVI and Meridian Construction has successfully completed projects on other Caribbean islands including St. Thomas, Nevis and Anguilla.

Meridian Construction is proud of its ability to undertake technically difficult projects normally outside of the capacity of construction companies in the region.
