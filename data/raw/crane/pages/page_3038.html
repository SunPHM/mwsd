Origami has been a hobby of mine for some time now and continues to fascinate me, so much so, that I started this website and business to share this traditional art of Japan with others.This is truly a website made by a folder for the folders!
I am very pleased to be able to bring to you a selection of authentic origami papers and handmade washi from Japan.
I will endeavour to add new products to our range whenever possible and if you have any origami paper that you would like us to carry, please let us know. All suggestions will be considered and I will try my best to find sources for them.

I believe our prices are very reasonable and international orders are tax free! (10% discount on listed prices).

Orders are processed promptly and we have received much praise for our good service & speedy delivery from both domestic and international clients.

So start folding and discover the fun and the challenge you can get from origami.

Origami wa sugoi desu!
