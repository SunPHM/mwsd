Lifting Capacity

On the spreader for container handling:

  • 38 tonnes (29m radius)

  • 20 tonnes (41m radius)

On the ropes:

  • 47 tonnes (10-29m radius)

  • 29 tonnes (29-41m radius)

Attachments

  • Fully automatic telescopic 20/30/40 ft. spreader

  • Load rotator for all applications (hook, grab, spreader)
  • Load leveller
  • 10 cubic metre Piener grab for bulk cargoes

Special Features

  • Video camera mounted on crane boom to aid visibility into the ship's hold

  • Operator's cabin fully adjustable for height above wharf between 14.5m and 19.5m

Wharf Loadings

  • Maximum static pad loading 149 tonnes

  • Maximum dynamic pad loading 186 tonnes
  • Wheel loadings, maximum without load 25 tonnes
  • Outriggers, 4 independently operated star pattern arms
  • Axles, six steerable with double tyres

Crane Details

  • Maximum road speed 6km per hour

  • Crane width 4.76m
  • Crane length 21.9m
  • Height when travelling with boom lowered 5.8m
  • Turning radius for crane Carrier 18.30m
  • Crane Engine, Daimler Benz OM444LA diesel engine, 12 cylinders, 491 kw engine set to 1900 rpm
  • Diesel hydraulic crane drive
  • Fuel consumption 48 litres per hour
