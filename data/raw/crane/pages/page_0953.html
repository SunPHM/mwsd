More About A & B Industries of Morgan City, Louisiana
A & B Industries of Morgan City has two well-equipped facilities specializing in new marine construction, restoration and repair, marine fabrication, and dry-docking facilities.  We have an excellent record of quality marine construction and on time delivery.

    

With two floating dry-docking facilities centrally located, we can handle several vessels at the same time.  We understand very well about your downtime and will try hard to accommodate our customers' need to get their vessels back on the job.
