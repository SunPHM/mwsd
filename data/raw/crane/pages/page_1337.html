Process of Granite Sculpting

My involvement as a monumental mason, sculptor and many years of operating a stone quarry, working with earthmoving equipment and explosives, has given me the skills to undertake the making of monumental sculpture. Besides the artistic merit, the art of the possible played an important part in their development.

 

 

At the moment, one person can produce a large sculpture, using pneumatic tools, diamond cutting equipment and non-explosive demolishing agent, in somewhere close to 1000 hours. In times past the same project could have taken decades and a team of workers. As technology moves on, other developments could make the process still easier and faster. I am thinking about laser beams, already in use but, at the moment, prohibitively expensive.
