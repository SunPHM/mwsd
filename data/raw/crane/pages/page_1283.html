ECONOMIC SECTOR

Construction of radio-electronic equipments

SPECIFIC SECTOR OF APPLICATION

Designing, construction and application of industrial radio control systems for long range guidance and operation of:

Construction cranes, bridge cranes, dock cranes, erectors

Machines for hoisting and handling of containers

Self-propelled drills, cutters for road and railways tunnels

Pumpers, aerial ladders, tow trucks

Locomotives for intermodular switching

Machine tools employed by metallurgical and

Chemical industries as well as in the processing of wood and plastic materials

Thread rolling mills, transfer carriages, stowing equipment, belt conveyors,

Rolling grills and shutters, platforms, electrovalves, electropumps used by cement mills and Plants for production and stocking of cereals and flours

Activation of floating drilling platforms and jetties

Self propelled irriggation systems, agricultural machines

Motorised carriages, dollies for TV and film shooting

Machines to be operated in dangerous areas with remote video control and replication of machine signals (bidirectional radio command).
