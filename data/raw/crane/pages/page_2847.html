CHETEK		  

 
Snug Harbor Resort is cradled in a lazy cove of Prairie Lake among Great Blue Heron, Sandhill Crane, and rare Pileated Woodpecker.

During your stay, you'll discover...

  • true solitude in our five wooded acres...
  • dazzling beauty along our 1500 feet of
    level shoreline...
  • the comforts of home in our spacious
    cabins...
  • the convenience of our on-site store,
    carrying bait, tackle, ice, groceries,
    clothing and more...
  • this is the place you'll want to return to!

If fishing is your thing, there's no place better
than Snug Harbor. At 1550 acres, Prairie Lake
is the largest of the Chetek chain of six lakes, providing boat access to the entire chain. It is
home to an abundance of panfish, bass,
northern and walleye.
