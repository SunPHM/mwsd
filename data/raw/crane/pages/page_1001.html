Political Graffiti

Berlin: a city in turmoil. The central region of Berlin was under construction when we arrived. We counted 17 large construction cranes within the downtown city blocks. The Berlin wall was down, and the economy was now the driving force. This building's wall depicted the political climate perfectly: a ravaged skyline filled with soldiers, tanks, nuclear waste, and a business man clinging desperately to a Mercedes Benz logo.
