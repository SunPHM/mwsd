Under Construction

Potsdamer Platz, Berlin

This reminds me of home! I live in the middle of a big construction zone that will look really great when it's finished. Whenever that will be. And this is a big construction zone, and it will look really great when it's finished. Everything from past the Brandenburg Gate all the way past Potsdamer Platz is under construction. It's truly stunning.


Construction next to the Brandenburg Gate

The next picture is taken from the "Info-Box" - a 6-story-high observation/exhibit space near the S-Bahn/U-Bahn station.

Since I can use the S-Bahn for free with my Eurail pass, I've tended to stay away from the U-Bahn, which is a pity, since it seems to cover more of Berlin.


Potsdamer Platz Cranes

DaimlerChrysler is moving into their new world headquarters here, Sony is working on an absolutely enormous entertainment/office/residential complex, there's new libraries, galleries, theatres, and lots and lots of cranes. It's quite a sight to see, how quickly the city heals itself.
