1999 Berlin Vacation


This monument is known as the Victory Column.  It was used by Allied pilots during World War II as navigation point.

These pictures are of the Reichstag.  This building was finished a month after we left- they must have got a lot done in that time!  You can see all the sky cranes.  There is an incredible amount of construction going on in Berlin these days.  I counted over 40 sky cranes in this part of the city alone.

This is what you would expect to see when you visit Berlin- the infamous Berlin Wall.  This was the biggest chunk left that we could find.  I've seen a bigger piece on display at one of Microsoft's cafeterias in Redmond, WA than this piece!!  A true victory for capitalism.
