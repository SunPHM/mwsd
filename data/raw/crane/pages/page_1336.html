 Vulcan Engineering Works
is one of the largest and most experienced industrial manufacturing concerns in Israel. Active in numerous projects throughout the country, Vulcan specializes in cranes, construction, pressure parts & equipment, tanks & pressure vessels, electric poles and train wagons. Vulcan is dedicated to maintaining the highest international quality levels in all its products and processes. The company holds a number of quality certifications, including ISO 9002, and is authorized to use the U and S ASME stamps.

 Timely Delivery Time and Again
Throughout the years, Vulcan has become known in Israel and internationally for its reliability. In project after project during the past 50 years, Vulcan has been able to draw together the required combination of resources - know-how, modern equipment and facilities, expert professionals and qualified workers - to complete hundreds of successful projects in a timely manner.

The core staff includes a wide range of expert designers, detailers, quality control engineers, welders, fitters, assemblers CNC operators, mechanics and electricians. This valuable group of professionals is supplemented by teams of hundreds of workers, who are employed as required to meet the needs of each project.

 Extensive Manufacturing Capabilities Under One Roof

Vulcan is located in Haifa Bay, a short ride from Haifa Port and Haifa Airport, and with links to the national railroad network. The company's large modern facilities span 44,000 square meters, including 15,000 square meters of indoor areas. Vulcan also counts with a wide range of complex machinery, among the most modern in the country. These resources enable Vulcan to provide immediate solutions to sophisticated requirements - all under one roof.

 
Few Israeli companies can match Vulcan's production facilities. This translates into unparalleled flexibility and the ability to provide immediate solutions to sophisticated requirements - all under one roof.
