International Friendship Meeting

Many people from other countries and schools came to enjoy our International Friendship Meeting on November 9, 2002.

We had many interesting cultural activities, including origami. Even the boys got involved.

Here I am with some of my works! I made two kinds of cranes and a puppy.

Some middle school students were pros at this art!

I got to enjoy some wonderful tea…

along with my cute Japanese sweet…

that I got to decorate myself!

Here is the whole tea setup. It tasted wonderful!

　

　

I played a game with some of the locals, deciding what aspects of Japan we most wanted to introduce to people of other countries. It was quite fun.

Some people had the chance to learn about Japanese toys.

We had lots of visitors to the American booth where we tested their skills in geography and fed them.

A member of our English Club made a fabulous speech! We all had a great time.
