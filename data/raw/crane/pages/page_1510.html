SUNDIAL MARINE is conveniently located at river mile 120 on the Columbia River, just west of the mouth of the Sandy River, near Troutdale, Oregon. Our facility, comprising 26 acres of land, is within 2 miles of Interstate 84, 6 miles of the Portland International Airport, and 15 miles of down-town Portland. The facility has approximately 1200 feet of pier space, one (1) 248 foot long floating drydock with a capacity of 2100 tons.

SUNDIAL MARINE has a 300 foot long by 60 foot wide set of end-launch building ways. We have a 2,000 square foot lay out and erection slab, with portable covers, which is serviced by a 30-ton track mounted crane. In addition, we have 5,000 square feet of covered shop area, serviced by overhead cranes, to support additional sub-assembly.

Our facility has one (1) 50-ton mobile truck crane, one (1) 50-ton crawler crane, and two (2) mobile hydraulic cranes with capacities of 20 and 25 tons. In addition, we have a track mounted gantry crane with a capacity of 34 tons, and two (2) pneumatic tired cranes with 18 and 24 ton capacities. Our main office complex houses our management, accounting, engineering, contracts, purchasing, and estimating staff.
