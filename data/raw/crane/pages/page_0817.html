Vale of Glamorgan Railway's Other Locomotives & Rolling Stock

The following selection of photographs are scanned from some old slides lent to me for reference. Unfortunately, none of the slides have any details regarding the identity of the individual locomotives so at present I can only identify as best as I can from the remaining numbers still evident in the photos. If anyone can more accurately identify any of the locomotives please contact me. If anyone has any photos that they would like to share I would also be very interested in displaying a copy on this site with the aim of posting a photo of every locomotive to have survived through the forward thinking of the Legend Dai Woodham. All photos are courtesy of various members of the VoGR but primarily Jeff Morgan, the current Chairman.
