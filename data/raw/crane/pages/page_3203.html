Sakura Matsuri 2002!

   
    In March of 2002, fifteen JETAA-DC volunteers ran a booth at the annual Washington D.C. Sakura Matsuri.  The hundreds of cherry trees that line the Potomac tidal basin were a gift from Japan, and bloom every springtime, bringing thousands of visitors to the Potomac shores.
