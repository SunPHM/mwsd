January 13, 2004

”Into the fold” at Cynwyd: 1,000 origami cranes!
According to Japanese legend, wishes come true for those who fold 1,000 origami cranes. With the help of parent volunteers, every student at Cynwyd School will create three cranes (one for home, one for school and one for the world) during the next two weeks in a collective effort to accomplish this impressive feat. Before the 10-fold process begins, students will also include a written wish inside each crane. Some students, for example, have asked for “peace in the world” and for “everyone to have enough food to eat.” Here’s hoping that like the cranes that serve as inspiration for the school’s project, Cynwyd’s origami dreams will fly.
