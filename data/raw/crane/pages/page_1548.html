We are a local Training Organisation, based in Invergordon, established since 1989, specialising in Quality Instruction Testing and Certification of all types of Construction and Industrial Plant Operator training, bringing each operator to H. S. E. Codes of Practice Standards.
We offer Plant Operator C. I.T.B./CTA Certification of Direct Entry, Foundation Training and Safety Awareness for Plant Operators. Foundation Training can be arranged and conducted on site (minimum of 3 trainees by special arrangement).
Being Instructor registered also with the National Plant Operators Registration Scheme Ltd and LANTRA National Training Organisation we can offer alternative registration on all Plant Operator courses including all Fork Lift Truck types/groups, courses ranging from Novice (4-5 days) to Experienced Operator (2-2.5 days). We also incorporate a 1 day (recommended) refresher course plus re-test for previously certificated operators - all of these courses are usually conducted on the client's premises.
Other Instruction Courses that we can offer are, Abrasive Wheels, Gantry Type Overhead Crane, Lorry Loader, Sea Crane, Pier Crane, One day Slinger/Signaller; Mobile elevating Work Platform, Excavators, Loading Shovel, Manual Handling, Crawler Cranes, Mobile Cranes, Wheeled Cranes.
If we can be of any assistance to you or your company with any training needs in the future please do not hesitate to contact us and we will be delighted to give you competitive quotations, or indeed help with any other information that you may require.
