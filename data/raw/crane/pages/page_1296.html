Company Profile

TELCO

Leader, Always

Telco Construction Equipment Company Limited manufactures construction equipment that is used in major infrastructure projects in India. It has remained a market leader for the past five years, despite stiff competition. It has revolutionised the Indian construction equipment industry, with the introduction of the V series of hydraulic excavators. The company has an extensive customer base that includes government and institutional buyers, and contractors. The company was the country’s first construction equipment manufacturer to receive ISO 9001 certification.

Areas of business

The company has used state-of-the-art technology to manufacture excavators and backhoe loaders. It enjoys a 90 per cent share of the crawler crane market in India. It is the only Indian manufacturer that produces 100 tonne cranes. These are the largest machines made locally. The company was the first to introduce mini-excavators in India, and its brand EX60, is the most successful machine to be made in India so far, with more than 1,300 machines being sold in the last three years. It is the largest manufacturer of hydraulic excavators in India, with over 6,000 machines in the market. It offers the widest available range of hydraulic excavators, eight models ranging from 2 tonnes to 60 tonnes in size. The company can indigenously design and develop products. The company’s revenue in 1998 was Rs. 3.81 billion. It has an average annual growth rate of 21 per cent.

Collaborations

The company has collaborations with Hitachi Construction Machinery Company Ltd., Japan, for hydraulic excavators and cranes; and John Deere, USA, for backhoe loader technology.

Locations

The company has a countrywide network of sales, service and spare parts offices. It has modern manufacturing expertise with the latest machine tools and automation facilities, such as robot welding and machining centres, at Jamshedpur and Dharwad.

Telco is committed to maximising customer satisfaction through the development, manufacture, sale and service of a complete range of reliable, cost-effective and quality construction and earthmoving equipment, manufactured to international standards.

Telco’s range of construction equipments also include Hydraulic Excavators in eight models, Crawler cranes in 4 models, John Deere 315SE Backhoe Loader and Wheel Loader TWL3036.

The operating weight of the backhoe loader is 6700 kg. Its engine is of Perkins 1004.4 THR type. Transmission system is ZF four speed helical cut gear, collar shift transmission synchronised in all gears with hydraulic reverser. Front tyres are 9-16, 16 PR type and rear tyres are 16.9-28, 10 PR type. The brake is hydraulic wet disc, mounted inboard; self-adjusting and self equalising type. Backhoe is of 0.09-0.35 m3 size and loader is of 1.00 m3 size.

The engine of the wheel loader TWL3036 is of Tata 697 TC type. Transmission system is with ZF 3WG 150 hydrodynamic torque converter and reversing transmission of counter shaft design. The machine has the dump height of 1.5-3.0 m3, and has reach at maximum dump height of 1.21 m.

Maxmech Equipments

Incorporating German Technology

Maxmech Equipments, Ahmedabad, is one of the pioneers in the manufacture of construction equipments. The company has introduced many "firsts" in the country. Maxmech now launches, first time in India, Concrete Pump with boom placer with latest proven German parts and technology, available in models MB50A 17.3, MB 50A 25.3, MB 90 A 25.3, MB 90 A 33.4, and MB 90 A 37.4.

These models offer a range of conveying capacity, concrete pressure, conveying cycle diameter, conveying cycle stroke, hydraulic system, hydraulic pressure, water tank capacity, horizontal boom length, vertical book length, boom kinematics, number of boom section, length of end hose, front outrigger width, rear outrigger width, electrical control unit, drive from propeller, and different weight of boom and pump.

The company also offers trailor mounted Portable Concrete Pump built with German parts and technology, with ‘S’ valve system. The conveying capacity of this machine is 49 m3/hr; maximum pressure in concrete is 60 bar; conveying cycle diameter DN 180 mm; conveying cycle stroke 1300 mm; number of strokes 25 maximum; concrete hopper with extension has 550 litre capacity and 1450 mm height; aggregate size 40 mm (max) and 50 mm edge length; hydraulic system is open circuit; maximum hydraulic pressure is 318 bar; oil tank volume 480 litre; water tank volume 300 litre; water pump 150 litre/minimum (20 bar); electrical control unit 12 V; and the total unit weight is 3200 kg.

The product range of Maxmech Equipments include: Maxmech tandem vibratory roller, soil compactor, static tandem roller, wet mix macadam plant, cement storage silo system, bitumen sprayer, Maxomix concrete batching/mixing plant (fully automatic electronic), and conmix transit mixer.

CAPIOUS AIRTECH

An Integrated Approach

CAPIOUS Airtech is committed to provide automation excellence in road making machines, and has earned a foremost position with total approach to the needs and problems of the road construction industry. At Capious the buzzword is "dependability". From concept to completion, the company serves the need of the customers precisely, keeping in mind the individuality and locational problems of its area of operations. Capious is committed to manufacturing machines that are durable and user-friendly, and uncompromising quality adhering to the M.O.S.T specifications. Timely execution of orders, customer-friendly policies, consistent innovation to develop newer and improved products by incorporating the latest technology, and quick after-sales-service to maximise customer productivity are the areas the company is focussing on.

Capius Drum Mix Plants meet all the specifications laid down by M.O.S.T. The plants are designed and engineered to give trouble-free performance with enhanced fuel efficiency using the latest technology. Available in capacities ranging from 40 to 90 tph, Asphalt plants offer automatic control on production through centralised controlled operation from the control cabin. Capious plants consist of four-bin feeders for aggregates along with single deck vibratory screen for removal of over size aggregates. Insulated dryer drum is made from 10 mm/12 mm thick heat and abrasion resistant steel plants to give excellent durability. It is equipped with high pressure electronic jet burners with auto cut-off to prevent overheating and economise the fuel consumption. Hotmix material from the thermodrum is carried out by special heat resistant rubber belt conveyor. Automatic electro-mechanically operated surge hopper at the end of the load-cut conveyor is provided to minimise the segregation of mix materials.

Capious range of product also includes tandem vibratory roller, air pollution control system for asphalt drum mix plant, and hydrostatic sensor paver finisher.

At Capious, each machine goes through many quality checks during design and development involving people from all the concerned department to produce high quality machine that gives satisfactory performance. Customer feedback forms an important tool to give better and improved products. Computer-aided designing enables to design most critical parts in such a way that they are capable of withstanding the severest of shocks and vibrations during operation. Almost all parts are manufactured at the work office enabling to provide high quality after-sales-service to the valued customers. Ready availability of all the frequently needed spares along with quick service response helps the customers to achieve maximum productivity.
