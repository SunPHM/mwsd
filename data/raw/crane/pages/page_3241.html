Summer SolstiCelebration 2003
Held in the Big Thicket of White Rock Lake in Dallas, Texas







A Note from Amy:
Summer SolstiCelebration as a tradition of nine years went through many incarnations, from a pagan poets consortium, to a huge ethnic drum and dance festival, to a somber labyrinth walk and spiritual procession, to an eclectic gathering in the spirit of humanity's utopian goal.
This year's was a fine evening, made more poignant by it being the final one led by me. But from such sweet dreams as the pictures below illustrate, new traditions are made.
