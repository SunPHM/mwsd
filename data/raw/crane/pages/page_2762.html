"In the Presence of Sandhill Cranes -Book II"
  
Peter R. Gerbert © 2006 This original design has been copyrighted by the artist and the image has been digimarked. Not for copy or web use unless permission is granted by the artist.
"I spent more time researching this piece than any other painting before it. I wanted to make sure that the setting had the correct time of year for the chicks to be present; with the correct wildflowers and such. Direction of sunlight became a big factor with this piece as well. I had to resort to the use of some fluorescent paints to get try to get the foreground to look like the sun is touching it. Spending a great deal of time observing a family of Sandhill Cranes was a pleasure!"           -Peter R. Gerbert
