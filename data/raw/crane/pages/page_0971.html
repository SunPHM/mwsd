Sounds Expo 2004

On the walk to the show from the notorious London Underground one gets a great view of the amazing construction work that is underway (albeit rather slowly) on the new Wembley stadium. These cranes are HUGE. the whole thing is simply massive! I look forward to returning next year, when the construction is completed.

Once inside the show, one is immersed in the latest noises in the industry. Hall 2 is the Sounds Expo show, hall 1 is the Video Expo. Digital Village is the show's official retailer, and consequently has the busiest stand by far. An interesting offshoot of DV is the new DV Pro side. A welcome thing. A reaction against the trend during the past few years where pro audio has become cheapened to the extent that quality pro gear is not really available any more in the main stream. DV Pro redresses the balance by offering quality equipment - that you can expect to pay for!

see www.dvproaudio.com for more information

A close up of the DV stand with their plasma screen showing their special on the little P3.

The show was busy busy busy. Lots of feedback. Thank you to all those that stopped by our stand, it was a great, although tiring show; hence this update has taken a while to get on the web!

Thanks again, Danny Fletcher
