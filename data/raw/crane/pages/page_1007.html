HEATON RENTS CRANES…AND A WHOLE LOT MORE

What motivates contractors and owners, time after time, to choose to rent cranes from Heaton Erecting for their complex as well as routine rigging or erecting jobs?

First, we provide the right crane for the job. We make sure it can do the job safely and efficiently. Our full inventory of cranes, from rough terrain, boom truck, hydraulic truck cranes, larger crawler, and conventional, range in size from 13 to 230 ton capacities. All necessary rigging equipment, including shackles, beams, chokers, personnel baskets, etc. are furnished to assist you in getting the job done.

Our equipment is fully manned to suit your job needs. Operators, oilers, and riggers are provided as needed. Our crane operators have had, on average, in excess of 15 years experience setting structural steel, precast concrete, and mechanical/electrical equipment. Each of our employees is well trained with extensive emphasis on safety and regulatory compliance. Our operators are certified by the National Commission for Certification of Crane Operators (NCCCO).

Our management team is a dynamic and respected part of the construction community, always putting our customers' production requirements first. Our estimators not only quote the job accurately, they often manage any difficult or unique lift to ensure the successful completion of the project. Many contractors have relied heavily on our professionalism and experience, from pre-planning through job completion, for over twenty years.

Heaton Erecting is committed to investing the necessary capital to augment our fleet in number, size, and capacity so that we can provide our customers with the latest and most productive cranes in the industry. In addition, we invest significant money into our preventive maintenance program each month to ensure maximum efficiency, safety and availability of our fleet.

With over thirty-five years of rigging and erecting experience, Heaton Erecting is a leader in the Atlanta crane market. We've done thousands of jobs, either as a subcontractor or crane supplier, for many of Atlanta's top general contractors on a multitude of premier construction projects. Our reputation for service, quality, and safety is our most valuable asset.
