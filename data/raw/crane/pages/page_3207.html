Japanese Origami &

the Sadako Story

 

In 1945 an atomic bomb was dropped on Hiroshima when a young girl named Sadako was 2 years old. When she was 11 she got leukemia and died a year later. There is a Japanese legend that says if you can make 1000 cranes you will be granted a wish. She had believed that her wish of staying alive would be granted if she could make 1000 cranes. She managed to make 964 cranes before she died.

 

We learned how to make paper cranes just like Sadako and her friends and we taught it to the other students in our class.
