Jones Lake

Distance: < 0.1 mile
Accessibility: Easy

Depending on local rainfall, Jones Lake may be lake, pond, pool, mud, or grass. This photo shows about one third of the total area of the lake which is home to several alligators, frogs, and birds of all kinds. After spotting all the bay ducks, Jones Lake is a good place to visit to continue adding to your list. Although I haven't done it yet, I have always wanted to bring a lawn-chair for an extended stay on the pier.
