Armsel MHE

Leader in Lifting Gear

Armsel JIB Cranes are big money savers for every Industrialist and Business man. Armsel has a solution for any localised material handling with a specific jib crane and lifting tackle from a wide range of types and capacities.

For localised handling of any load or material economcially, safely efficiently in a minimum floor space, nothing beats a standard ‘Armsel’ JIB Crane.
A full range covering entire lifting needs is now available with Armsel-the nation-wide leader in lifting gear.

Configuration :

Three main varieties of Jib Cranes are available. Free standing (Self-supported), Wall bracket mounting, and King Post Mounting, in capacities upto 10,000 Kg.

Design Features :

All Jib Cranes are designed maintaining working stress well within the limits as per available and relevant I.S. specifications. Deflections of Jib Arm can be restricted as per customers actual need, based on cost and design. Armsel Jib Cranes are designed to occupy minimum floor space and maximum approaches.

Easy erection features are in-built characteristics. All self-supported models can be wired internally from isolators to Jib Arm through Slip rings, thus making the crane safer.

Easy Accurate Alignment :

Predetermined machine locations and Pilot pads make it easier in erection and alignment of every Jib crane. In most cases, Jib arms are adjusted by single nut, tie rods.

Bearings :

All Jib cranes are fitted with Self-aligning anti-friction ball/roller bearing for efficient and smoother service for long periods.

Special Models :

Special Tailor-made Jib cranes can be designed to suit the exact requirement of lifting application.

Accessories :

A full range of accessories like Slip-ring units, swivel limiters, adjustable stops, out-door covers, motorised swivels, manual/electric, chain/rope lifting tackles all types of slings, clamps, lifting beams etc. can also be supplied.

Erection Testing Inspection and after-sales service :

Full support of their independent service/after-sales wing specialised in erection and maintainance of these equipment is offered with every sale.
