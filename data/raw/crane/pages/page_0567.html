Anchor Crane & Hoist Offers Various Types of Cranes

     

Anchor Crane & Hoist is a manufacturer of overhead bridge cranes, gantry cranes, monorails and jib cranes. Their services include project engineering and design, 24-hour parts assistance, installation, service, load tests and OSHA inspections.

     

"We have been in business since 1969, and are committed to quality built products and 100% customer satisfaction. Anchor Crane & Hoist, an ISO-9001 Certified company, offers a combination of strengths including design, fabrication, delivery, installation, field service, inspection, and parts from one single source. This combination of value added services has led to a reputation of reliability and competitive pricing," stated Lloyd Smith.

     

Anchor Crane provides turnkey solutions to material lifting requirements. Anchor Crane personnel provide the following: ¥ engineering ¥ design ¥ fabrication in 115,000 square foot manufacturing plant ¥ complete installation ¥ service.

     

Products include the following: ¥ single and double girder top running bridge cranes ¥ underhung bridge cranes ¥ runway systems ¥ patented track systems ¥ gantry cranes ¥ jib cranes ¥ specialized material handling equipment ¥ work station cranes.
