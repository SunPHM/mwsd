CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY

We Manufacture a Range of Sign Products designed to meet the specific needs of the Construction Industry.

Customer requirements are analysed and assessed on an ongoing basis to enable us offer the most appropriate and competitively priced products. As a result we now supply over 200 Construction companies throughout Ireland and the UK.

Being an ISO 9001 Quality Assured Firm it is our policy to provide a fast and efficient service to our customers.

  

Below is a brief description of the products and services we offer to companies involved in the Construction Industry, all at very competitive prices!

Construction Site Health & Safety Signs
We produce Signs specifically tailored to meet on site signage requirements with regard to Health and Safety regulations. Our range of Off The Shelf Health & Safety Signs have been designed to comply with Council Directive 92/58/EEC and S.I. No.132 of 1995. To meet customer demand we have recently developed an Off The Shelf Site Entrance Sign that can be personalised by adding your Company name. We can also manufacture non standard signs with wording to suit your specific requirements.

Plans & Drawings Laminated
Protect your plans and drawings from water damage and everyday wear and tear. We now provide a laminating service which encapsulates them in a clear protective film.

Development Signs
Large Scale Development Signs for Advertising and Promoting Housing and Commercial projects can be Designed and Produced in-house. These can include your Full Colour artists impressions and Photographic Images.

Company Name
Signs with your Company Name and Logo etc. can be produced on a selection of materials such as Aluminium, Aluminium Composite panel and Corriboard or as Self-adhesive Stickers in whatever size you require.

Crane Signs
Internally Illuminated Crane Signs, manufactured as single or double sided units, for fixing to, or suspending from the jibs of tower cranes.

Helmet and Plant Identification Stickers
 Self adhesive stickers suitable for outdoor use, designed and screenprinted.

Road Works Signs
Reflective and non-reflective temporary Road Works Signs, with standard text and symbols or produced with the specific wording you require.

Vehicle and Plant Graphics
Self-adhesive Lettering designed and produced for all types of vehicles and machinery.

Sign Systems
External and internal directional signage systems for Factories, Hospitals etc.
