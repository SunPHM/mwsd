Available as a vertical lift or canopy (tilt-up) design, IDI crane doors are used for passage of overhead cranes (top running, underslung and monorail type). IDI crane doors can be built as single, stand alone units or as part of a vehicle access door. Electrical control panels are designed to interlock the control circuits for the door and crane operating system.

Applications

  • Steel mills
  • Foundries
  • Warehouses
  • Test Cells
  • Simulation Rooms

Options

  • Vehicle/material handling door units (lower)
  • Electrical interlocks (lower and upper doors)
  • Customized application - please consult our sales department with your requirements
  • Weatherseals
