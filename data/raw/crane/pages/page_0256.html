Robles Diversion Dam

I got up early this morning and walked over to the Robles Diversion Dam to see the progress they have make on the new fish ladder. The temperature was 35 and frost was on the ground. The air was smokey and the sun was out. My route took me up the Ventura River bed. When I arrived a crane was unloading a large piece of steel from a flatbed truck. I believe the steel is used as a form for the new concrete walls.
