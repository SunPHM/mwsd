Bays construction

Our firm erects bridge and overpasses structures of following types:

 

  • prefabricated reinfoced concrete beams;

  • cast in-situ post-tensioned concrete;

  • metal and metal-concrete structures.

 

Prefabricated reiforced concrete beams are mounted with the help of different mobile cranes. In present time we carry out a construction with the application of such beams on th efollowing objects: Kolomna bypass on the Motor road Ì-5 "Ural"; on the objects of Motor road Ì-4 "Don" in Tula Region. The following objects have been finished: bridge on 264 km Motor Road "Don"; overpass on 113 km Motor Road "Don".

 

 

 

Cast in-situ post-tensioned concrete structures are constructed with the application of continuous and beam scaffoldings, in any climatic zones, in the any geometric form both in plane and in profile. Our firm is the leader of russia transport cast in-situ construction. We have mastered both foreign and russian technologies of cast in-situ construction.

In present time we are carrying out the construction of unique bridge crossing across Angara River in Irkutsk. There is suspension concreting method for river part construction.

The following objects have been finished: bridge crossing across Oka River in Kashira; trestle on the intersection of Leningrad highway and MKAD; overpass "Pyrochi".

 

 

Metal and metal-concrete bays are erected by two methods: longitudinal launching and continuous scaffolding assembling. Our engineers have experience in producing the any weldings and high-strength bolts joints. In present time we are carrying out construction of the following objects: bridge across Moscow River on Kolomna bypass; approaches of stay-cabled bridge across Neva River in St.-Petersburg.
