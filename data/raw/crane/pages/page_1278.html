INDUSTRIAL RADIO CONTROL


INTERMARK SERIES SAGA 1 - TC - TC/E

Industrial radioremote control suitable for all types of contruction cranes, overhead travelling cranes, lifting and transporting equipment.
Up to 30 relay commands, contact current: 10 A impulse programming, PASSO/PASSO, witholding, with RITARDO IN ATTACCO, releasing, inching function, speed increase/reduction by automatic impulse, with RITARDO.
Working frequency VHF 410/490 MHZ (433,050/434,790 MHZ VE).
Channelling passage: 5 - 10 - 12,5 - 25 - 50 - 100 KHZ (12,5 - 25 KHZ VE).
Modulation FM - MSK, DISPERSO spectrum 245/460 MHZ (FM-MSK VE).
Power RF < 10 MW (ETS 300 - 220 LPD VE).
Transmitter supply: 7,2/9 VOLT CC.
Reciever supply 12 VCC 12 VCA - 24 - 48 - 55 - 64 - 110V CA.
Homologation: PPTT Italy - France - Spain - Portugal - Rumania - Czech Republic
9001 Cert. n° 7E2Y007-00 - FCC USA - DOC Canada.

INTERMARK SERIES SAGA 2A - 2A/S JOY

Radioremote control suitable for all types of construction cranes, overhead travelling cranes, lifting and transporting equipment.
Electrical characteristics identical to the SAGA 1/TC series.
2 joysticks version with 1 - 2 - 3 - 4 - 5 speeds on each ASSE (according to clients' needs).
The series SAGA 2A - 2A/S can be used under special circumstances when complex movement are necessary. SAGA 2 JOY has been built to be bi-directional, with dispaly of signals and weights being lifted.

INTERMAK SERIES F21 A/B/C/D

Industrial radioremote control for radio control of machnine tools, agricultural machinery, automatic irrigators, platform scale commands, roll-up shutters, electropumps, electrovalves.
The F21 series is not made for lifting equipment, construction cranes or overhead travelling cranes (VE).
