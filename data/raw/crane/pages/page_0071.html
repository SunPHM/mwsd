Delivery of new Mobile Harbour Crane

This August, ThPA S.A. received its new Mobile Harbour Crane to be used for the loading/unloading of general cargo with motor grabs, containers with spreaders and bulk cargo (coal, iron scrap) with 4-rope grabs.

The crane was supplied by Gottwald and has:

  • Lift capacity 100t in a 17m radius
  • Maximum radius 44m

It should be noted that long-term investigations have shown that the mobility of Mobile Harbour Cranes increases utilisation of port handling equipment by approximately 60% over that of rail-bound cranes. This means for the company:

  • lower investment in the machine park
  • lower investment for and better utilisation of the wharves
  • lower maintenance costs resulting from a smaller machine park
  • lower personnel costs
