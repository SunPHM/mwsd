Machinery Foundation Work

Bogner Construction Company has the ability to design and/or build your equipment foundations. The installation pictured at left is a large combination press pit for five underdrive Danley presses. This project involved working in the heart of an existing facility. The pit was 22 feet deep and totally protected by sheet piling. Over 2500 yards of concrete were used in the installation.

During this three-phase project, Bogner completed the foundation, then the presses were stacked so that the superstructure of the building could proceed. While the superstructure was being completed, the presses were aligned and mechanical services were hooked up. The structural steel pit flooring was then completed. This five-month project was delivered to the client three weeks ahead of schedule and on budget.
