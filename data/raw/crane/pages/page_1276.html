Construction Services: General Contracting

The General Contracting Division of AME serves all major industries, handling projects from complicated to easy, large to small. In 2001, AME won the prestigious Four Star General Award for the top industrial construction project in the Carolinas.

  • Turnkey building services
  • Steel erection and modification
  • Traditional brick and mortar construction
  • Demolition
  • Precision foundation work
  • Specialty and mass concrete construction
  • Design/build expertise
  • Self-performed major trades
  • Multi-level project accounting and reporting


    • One of the main AME strengths is the integration of construction services. AME self-performs major trades and maintains one of the largest mobile crane fleets in the southeast, allowing solid control over scheduling. And AME personnel are experts in specialty steel fabrication and erection, rigging, and equipment installation. Our logo is a hallmark of excellence in construction.

    AME maintains tight management control over every project to save you time, money, and effort.

  • Critical path scheduling
  • Schedule of values
  • Detailed accounting and timely cost reporting
  • Monthly invoicing
  • Labor and materials reporting
  • Percentage of completion reporting
  • Cash flow curve analysis based on construction schedules
  • Unlimited General Contracting licenses in force in GA, NC, SC, TN, and VA

    Our experience ensures that your project is completed on deadline and on budget. Our safety program is accredited and managed by an in-house Safety Coordinator, and our crane operator training and certification program is the best in the industry. The AME team is dedicated to a higher standard of excellence for your project.
