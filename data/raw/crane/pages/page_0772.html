Section Of East Kemper Rd. Closed After Crane Accident

RELATED PICTURES
Click for larger images.


Road blocked in Symmes Twp
(WCPO/WCPO.com)


Crane toppled down bank
(WCPO/WCPO.com)

Reported by: 9News
Web produced by: Neil Relyea
Photographed by: 9News
Last Updated: 9/1/04 11:35:18 PM

Part of East Kemper Road in Symmes Township was closed after a crane overturned.

The incident happened around 4 p.m. Wednesday afternoon between McKinney and Loveland-Madeira Road.

The driver of the truck carrying the crane and a large drill, Robert Jester, 21-years old, of Hamilton, was able to escape injury.

The crane fell down an embankment towards a creek bed.

Officials were still working to clear up the mess.
