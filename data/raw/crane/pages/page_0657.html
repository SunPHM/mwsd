During the first two weeks of construction:
 
  • All 2812 seats were removed.
 
  • All of the existing acoustical banners and discs were removed.
  • The existing stage was stripped bare to accommodate a construction crane.
 
  • The 52-foot-diameter oculus was lowered from the ceiling and removed.
 
  • All of the carpeting was removed.
  • A 22-tonne construction crane was delivered to the site in pieces, inched through the small opening off the loading dock and then reassembled in the hall.
 
  • Scaffolding was erected and work has begun on assembling steel frames for the wooden bulkheads, which are being installed in the upper chamber of the auditorium.
