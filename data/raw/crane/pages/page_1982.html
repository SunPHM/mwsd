Bahia Bay is a unique upscale canal-subdivision located in Aransas County Texas, about halfway  between the cities of Rockport and Aransas Pass. We are adjacent to the Intracoastal Waterway, and overlook the world renown fishing ground on Redfish Bay known as Estes Flats. It is only a few minutes by boat to Port Aransas and the Gulf of Mexico.
We are  at Lat. / Long Coordinates: 
N27o 58.020 X W097o 05.849
