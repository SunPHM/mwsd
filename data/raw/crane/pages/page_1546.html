Specialists in original equipment

Intergomma was established in 1967 as specialist in the supply of complete wheel assemblies for the original equipment market in the field of agriculture, industrial and building machines.

During the years Intergomma develops its activity and gains ever-growing market shares until reaching the leadership in this sector.

Among Intergomma's customers there are the most important manufacturers of agricultural and harvesting machines and the main producers of cranes and building equipment.

Particularly important has become during the last years the original equipment market of earthmoving and construction machines, where Intergomma gained as its customers the most important italian manufacturers supplying, besides its quality products, several additional services unique in this sector.

Intergomma exports its products both for replacement and original equipment, cooperating with foreign companies already present on the markets.
