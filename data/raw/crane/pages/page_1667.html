Boat transportation and yacht relocation specialist Harbor & Lake Boat Transport is proud to announce the recent launching of the custom built sportfish "Nubbs"!

"Nubbs was built using the cold-molded plywood/epoxy method of construction to afford a superior weight to strength ratio.  The combined  horsepower from her twin diesel engines was to be in excess of 1,000 hp.  She is 48'-0" in length and has a beam of 16'-6".  At a loaded height of 20'-6" she was classified as a "superload" and displaced 25,000 lbs.  This recent launching by Harbor & Lake Boat Transport took place in South Miami, Florida.  The company based out of South Carolina was called in for the job based mainly on their experience with the transportation of oversize boats and specifically for their knowledge of water launching.

 

Due to the location of high tension power lines, two cranes were used to lift the boat from her construction cradle onto the transport trailer. The above photo shows the load being suspended between the electrical lines as the trailer is being maneuvered into place.

The combined efforts of the local utility companies as well as police and company escorts were necessary to ensure that the yacht reached the water safely and without damage. Because of the size of the boat special routing was needed. The picture above shows the crew replacing a sign that was removed to allow the load to pass a traffic signal.

Congratulations to the owner and the builders of this fine new yacht for a job well done.
