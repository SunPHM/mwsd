Introduction to Harp Vending

                                                  Harp Vending Inc. was founded in September of 1953 by Mr. I.L. (Pee Wee) Harp.
                                                  Mr. Harp owned and operated the business until September of 1983,
                                                  when it was purchased by Ronald L. Linebaugh and the late Rubye F. Linebaugh.
                                                  Ronald Linebaugh started wiht the company in 1965.
                                                  Ronald L. Linebaugh, Jr started with the company in 1987 and became a partner in 1995.
                                                  Harp Vending, Inc. provides coin operated amusement machines
                                                  to various locations in the Big Country area.
                                                  Our equipment includes: Juke Boxes, Video Games, Pinballs, Pool Tables,
                                                  Electronic Darts, Cranes, Air Hockey, and other amusement machines.
                                                  Harp Vending, Inc. strives to provide service with extreme high quality and integrity.
