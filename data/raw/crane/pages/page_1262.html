A LEADING COMPANY

Set up in 1980, VENDAP, is leading company on the national market for the hire of industrial equipment, especially for large-scale construction jobs, such as:

  • Telescopic cranes
    • Rough-terrain from 18 to 40 tonnes
    • Truck crane from of 18 to 90 tonnes
    • All-terrain from 15 to 500 tonnes
  • Lattice Boom Cranes
    • Crawler Cranes from 35 to 113 tonnes
  • Prefabricated accommodation units

We are the largest and most dynamic company in this line of business, with a solid economic and financial situation. We also enjoy a high level of technical and commercial credibility.

We are able to respond to our Customers' requirements in any part of the country through a wide network of offices in Matosinhos, Mangualde, Pombal, Sines, Crato, Ferreiras e Funchal.
