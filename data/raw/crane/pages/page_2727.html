Area Events and Attractions

Area Attractions:

 

History buffs will enjoy a visit to Casa San Ysidro  located in Corrales, or strolling

Old Town Albuquerque.  Or, combine history and hiking with a visit to Bandelier National Monument, home of the cliff dwelling Anasazi.
Bandelier National Monument
On the road to Los Alamos

New Mexico's famous vistas can be viewed from the Sandia Peak Tramway located just east of Corrales, along the Turquoise Trail, or while driving through the Jemez Mountains on the way to Los Alamos.

The Indian Pueblo Cultural Center is a good place to learn about the many Pueblos in the Albuquerque area and a visit to the Petroglyphs National Monument gives you a close up view of ancient cliff drawings.  Museum buffs might want to take in the Albuquerque Museum of Art and History and the Atomic Museum.  While visiting Santa Fe, a visit to the Georgia O'Keefe Museum and the Loretto Chapel Museum are a must for fans of art and architecture 

 

Galleries featuring work by local artists abound in Albuquerque and Santa Fe. Corrales is home to several galleries featuring everything from jewelery to pieces forged from iron.
The dates for the 2006 Albuquerque International Balloon Fiesta are October 6th through October 15th.  Balloon ascents, races and evening glows are featured during the Fiesta.  We book up quickly for this event so reserve your room early.
