Loading Cane with a Crane


Loading the cane on the trucks became the job of the crane and crane operator. Many were highly skilled, and amazingly graceful to watch. The less skilled could make mistakes, and find themselves overturned as the weight of the cane pulled the crane over. Or worse, the claw, clumsily swung with a full of a load of cane, could swing back and crush the cab and the operator...
