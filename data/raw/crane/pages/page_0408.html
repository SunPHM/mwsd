Crane Scales

We supply robust heavy-duty industrial crane scales suitable for use with most cranes or hoists with safety factor of upto  500 %. The load cell overload protection of the crane scales is upto 200% of their capacity (depending on the crane scale model). The housing is designed for protection against dust. So they can be used in harsh working areas / environments.

Wireless infrared remote control untits offer extended application abilities and let the crane scales be operated upto a distance of upto 15 m.

All crane scales are CE- certified and can
additional be calibrated (DKD- Certification).
We can do that procedure for you.

The scales will be delivered with rechargable
battery, recharger and infrared remote control.

Warranty on crane scales: 2 years 
User guides ever are in english, french, italian
german language.
