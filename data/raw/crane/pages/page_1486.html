Hoists & Cranes From

Hercules

Hercules Hoists Limited was promoted in 1962 for manufacturing material handling equipment like hand hoists, electric hoists and cranes in technical and financial collaboration with M/s. Heinrich (GmbH), Dusseldof, federal republic of Germany. The company belongs to Bajaj group, one of the large business houses of India. This group is having plants to manufacture Steel, Sugar, Scooter, Motor Cycles, fans etc. and marketing electrical goods and since then the sales of the company have increased continuously. The company is managed by the Managing Director under the superintendence direction and control of the board of directors. The company’s products are widely used througout India by small, medium and large enterprises. Special mention can be made of various steel plants, automobile manufactures, cement, fertilizer, paper and sugar plants, state electricity boards, irrigaton department, mining, construction projects etc. The company recently acquired knowledge for automated storage, retrieval machines from M/s. Davy Morris Limited. United kingdom some of the company’s products are

INDEF Chain Electric Hoist : INDEF electrically operated chain hoist is a simple but robust and safe hoisting machine incorporating only a few parts which are perfectly machined and assembled to ensure safe, reliable and trouble free service for along time. The load chain wheel is fully machined from alloy steel and appropriately hardened and tempered. The hoisting chain is wear resistant, heat treated and has adequate safety.

The gears are lubricated with high viscosity and high temperature resisting grease and mounted on splinted shaft instead of keyed shaft. The brake is fitted on non-driving end of the motor to avoid dismantling of motor for brake adjustment. Robustly built, totally enclosed. Squirrel cage induction motor is truly modular. The directly actuating limit switches prevent over-hoisting and over-lowering. The overload clutch is provided inside the gear box to prevent lifting of overload and protect the hoist from mechanical damages specially developed pendent switch for hoisting and lowering. The totally enclosed control panel houses airbreak type contractors for motion, reversing, overload relay, control transfer and limit switches. For storing the loose chain, chain collector is supplied. Each hoist is tested to 25 % overload, it is supplied with a trolley. Aguarantee is provided for the repair and replace.

INDEF WIRE ROPE ELECTRIC HOIST :It is a medium duty and medium speed hoist. These hoists are modern in design incorporating many new special features. Hoist is of fabricated construction and consists of separate individual units such as brake, motor, drum, gear box, electrical control panel and travelling trolley. All these units are easily accessible for repairs and maintenance. The main frame consists of mild steel plates to which the other units are assembled. The rope drum is made of steel on which wire rope grooves. The rope guide consists of hearing material connected by steal bracket to make it strong. The gear box is modular in construction and can be easily separated from the hoist. The brake is disc type fail-safe of reputed make is provided for trouble-free operation and easy for maintenance and adjustment. Heavy duty steel-forged hooks are supported on thrust bearing for free swiveling safety latch on hook is also provided. The trolleys can be adjusted for various beam sizes for preventing over-hoisting and over-lowering, limit switches are provided, robust, heavy duty push button switch with safe handling voltage is suspended from the hoist. Aguarantee is provided for the repair and maintenance.

INDEF Chain Pulley Blocks :INDEF short headroom chain pulley blocks occupy less headroom, as they are compact, maximum height of lift can be utilised. Blocks trolleys are built in one unit. These blocks are used for jobs requiring robust, safe and high efficiency. Unique design of threaded load pressure-brake make these blocks completely self-sustaining in all positions. The hooks are steel forged-designed to withstand shock loads and free swiveling. The load wheel is made of heavy duty malleable castings with accurately cast chain pockets for smooth running. All the gears are accurately machine cut from special alloy steel and case-hardened.

The hand chain wheel made from pressed sheet steel eliminate all possibilities of breakage. Alloy steel chain uniformly electrical welded from alloy steel and heat-treated to give ductility, toughness and wear resistance. The blocks conform to the testing requirement. There is aguarantee for repair and maintenance. The other products of the company are : Travelling Trolleys, Storage and retrieval cranes and H.O.T./E.O.T. Cranes.
