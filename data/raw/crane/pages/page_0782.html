Images of Development:
Scenes from the People's Republic of China

A large crane stands over a modern container port in Zhangjiagang, a "model city" located near the mouth of the Yangzi River, South of Shanghai. In 1980, Zhangjiagang was a small fishing village with 10,000 inhabitants. Today it is a modern, bustling city of over 800,000. The city, modeled after Singapore, is situated in a special economic zone. It boosts modern manufacturing facilities, clean and well designed public spaces and rich neighboring farmland.
