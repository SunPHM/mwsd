Military Hangar, Nigeria

120 metre span Hangar, for the Nigerian Air Force Hercules C. 130 Aircraft, at Kainji, Northern Nigeria.

 

This large hangar has been designed to allow its doors to be stacked inside at either end, which gives a full 90 metres clear opening with no obstructions. There are two under slung overhead traveling cranes, one of 10 tonnes capacity and one of 5 tonnes.

 

We designed, made and erected the complete structure.
