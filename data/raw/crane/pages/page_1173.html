  Seven K5/50 cranes for the construction of the Millau viaduct- November 2001
These cranes' performances had been calculated for the installation of the reinforcement steel bars and pouring the concrete with 9.5-tonne skips, and they were proposed with or without anchorage accessories for building the bridge piers at underhook heights of 85, 102, 131, 154, 160, 240 and 260 meters.

Potain should be supplying and installing the 7 cranes between now and June 2002, equipped with the new 150 LCC hoisting winches and accompanied by their respective crane-driver elevators.
The K5 cranes will be equipped with cameras in case of bad weather, and for the drivers' long working days, the counter-jibs will equipped with rest areas in which there will be a toilet and an eating area with a refrigerator.
