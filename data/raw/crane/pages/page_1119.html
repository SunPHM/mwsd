History:


          Began operations as a farm shop in 1978 for repairing Elkridge plantation's own equipment.  Prior experience in machine work and fabrication along with the gradual accumulation of machine tools, began to interest farmers and commercial interests for repairs and modifications to various equipment.  This scenario existed for several years until 1989 when Tensas Machine and Manufacturing was established with full time machinists and welders.  At this time equipment was purchased for the manufacture of a line of U-Bolts for the agricultural industry.  These are still being marketed to equipment dealers and distributors along with many other lines of equipment.

Plant:
