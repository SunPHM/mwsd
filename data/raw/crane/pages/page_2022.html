The Skimmer is Back!

"The Serious Birders Tour Boat"

Rockport Birding and Kayak Adventures continues the tradition of Captain Ted's Whooping Crane Tours aboard the Skimmer. The Skimmer is fast and quiet. She drafts 2.5 feet and cruises comfortably at 25-30 mph making her the fastest birding tour vessel in the area. Built for birding, she carries up to 46 passengers and will maneuver into remote birding areas which gives you an unparalleled vantage point to observe and photograph the abundant bird and wildlife species our area has to offer.

Whooping Crane Tours

Our goal at Rockport Birding and Kayak Adventures is to provide serious birders with a top-notch vessel and professional operation they can count on year-round.

We hope you and your friends will join us many times this year. In fact, we now offer an annual pass that will allow you to enjoy unlimited trips for one low price. We promise we will always show you something you haven't seen before, that's what makes it an adventure!

We are based at Fulton Harbor, an area that is quickly becoming a focal point of the Rockport/Fulton area. There are 2 new hotels, 8 great restaurants, shopping, and a lighted fishing pier all within a few blocks.
