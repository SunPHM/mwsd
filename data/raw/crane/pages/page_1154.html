Machinery

 

 

Machinery has been a core component of ASA's trade activities over the years.

The nature of equipment has ranged from bulldozers and cranes, for the construction industry. Pumps and generators for irrigation and power generation. Tractors, harrows, laser graders and other implements for the agricultural industry.

 

In the specialized industrial area ASA has supplied things such as CIP (Clean in Place) equipment for vaccine plants, centrifuges, sterilizers and compressors for the pharmaecutical industry.

ASA also supplied some of the first equipment for small business computer networks into countries like Poland.
