Large White Heron

The large white heron is our most popular bird.  We get many compliments about how life-like these birds look. It's not unusual to see these birds throughout Southwest Florida.  These models are $30.   


 
Large Blue Heron

The large blue heron is also one of our most popular birds. There are quite a few blue herons around this area.  These are $30.  They stand about 36" tall (w/o a base).  


 
Sandhill Crane 

Believe it or not, we started making these birds at the request of a customer!  People absolutely love the Sandhill Crane.  The sandhill is $35.  It also stands approximately 36" w/o a base.  There are slight differences in dimensions from the two previous herons.  

These birds are popular around this area of Florida and in other states, from California to Georgia.
