This is our fully equipped, modern, purpose built workshop, measuring over 5,000m2. The workshop contains 8 metre clear height bays and an 11m clear high third bay.
With over 50 boilermakers/welders on the floor we are capable of fabricating well over 10,000 tonnes (120,000hours) of steelwork and metalwork a year. Strategically located in Wingfield in the northwestern industrial sector of Adelaide we are able to efficiently service all areas of South Australia and interstate.

Facilities include;

  • - Drilling
  • - Section rolling
  • - Mig, submerged Arc facilities
  • - In-house shearstud application
  • - In-house priming application
  • - Section saws
  • - Eight overhead cranes with up to 20tonne lifting capacity
  • - Close links to SA's largest abrasive blasting & treatment works
  • - In-house drafting facilities
  • - Metalwork - staircase and balustrading fabrication & erection

Samaras maintains multiple in house erection crews, our large fleet of cranes and plant, enables us to tackle any scale fast track project.

SSE prides itself in its abilities to diversify and with its strong history and expertise in steel fabrication, allows the company to successfully perform on unusual projects to the clients satisfaction.

Samaras recent completion of over 750t of structural steel to Pelican point Power Station has proven our ability not only to produce high quality fully pre-fabricated and pre-treated steelwork to a highly demanding quality assured site and well within the time constraints.

We know Samaras Structural Engineers commitment and service to the building industry is second to none and our endeavour is to maintain the prestige and history of this proud company.
