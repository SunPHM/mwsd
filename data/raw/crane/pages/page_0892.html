CRANE HIRE

 

WITH RIWAL YOU BUILD ON OVER 35 YEARS LIFTING EXPERIENCE

Since 1968 Riwal is a specialist in heavy lifts and runs a fleet of some 42 mobile cranes ranging from 30 to 300 metric tons. It includes all terrain, construction, truck-mounted, rough terrain and crawler cranes from well-known manufacturers as Liebherr, Grove/ Krupp, Peiner, Linmac, Demag and Tadano/Faun. To meet our clients demands on efficient and fast delivery we run our crane operations from two locations: Dordrecht and Rotterdam.
