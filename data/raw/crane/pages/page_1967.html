Birds(cont)


Penguin Family 


This is designed as Antarctica's Emperor 

Penguin.  It is a very good example of 

air brush decorating creating realistic 

textures.  3600 4/5 and 3000 minis were 

released in 1978











   


          Whooping Crane






This bird is currently on the endangered 

species list, therefore the decanter is 

marked with the endangered species symbol.  

932 minis and 804 750 ml. were issued.







   






Fighting Game Cock






3600 750 ml. were issued in 1980 and 

sold primarily in South Carolina. 

Donald Malick was the artist for the 

design.  2400 minis were released in 

1982.  Both sizes included a wooden 

base.
