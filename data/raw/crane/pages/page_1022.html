Rack Supported Stacking System with integral overhead crane

"Stacker cranes store & protect valuable tools, dies, fixtures, components, subassemblies & work-in- progress." We design, manufacture and install rack supported storage and retrieval stackers with integral crane, stack racks, stacker cranes, tool and die storage racks & fixture storage racks for tool rooms & maintenance departments. Similar to Aurora Systems stackers, we offer a stacking rack with a variety of carriage attachments. Here's what the Ridg-U-Rak Stack-U-Rak system can do for you…

  • Efficient use of valuable floor space.
  • Provides high density at key locations throughout plant.
  • 100% immediate accessibility saves time.
  • Better location and organization of component parts.
  • Keeps work-in-progress areas cleaner and safer.
  • Saves space- custom tailors location opening to load size.
  • Standard rack heights available up to 16' in 4" increments.
  • Simplifies handling of heavy items- simplifies transfers too.
  • Fast storage and retrieval- ends long wait for lift trucks.
Here's why Stac-U-Rack is the best system you can buy :
  • Better Structure : Unmatched structural integrity.
  • Better Stacker : Standard stacker crane complies with CMAA and is rated at 2000 lbs. cap. & 4,000# cap. rotates 360 degrees. Optional man aboard.
  • Better Pallets : Solid steel decks, full depth fork entry pockets, rigid steel bracing, foolproof pallet engagement.
  • Better Overall System : Superior design, materials and construction give you stacker system of outstanding value, providing you with many years of rugged, trouble free operation.
