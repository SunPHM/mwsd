The Double C Ranch offers many opportunities for family outings, reunions, wedding receptions, company meetings, etc.. .and the great environment and the excellent staff makes every trip special and relaxing. The Double C Ranch, which is located in Dimmit County, is about 3 1/2 miles west of Crystal City, TX. The ranch features beautiful lodging with 11 bedrooms all with private baths, full service kitchen with dining area and game room, HD TV with satellite connection, DVD/VCR , swimming pool with hot tub and fountains, a lake with a pier for great bass fishing, and it can accommodate up to 20 people. The Double C Ranch is located right in the heart of some of the greatest hunting country in South Texas with access to over 14,000 acres, all within 10 to 25 minutes from the lodge.

 

Family Outings and Reunions
During the day, guests can go on wildlife safaris to see and photograph the many species of animals that make their home on the Double C.
Skeet shooting is always available and since the range is lighted, it can be done at anytime during the day or night.

 

Parties and Fun
The kareoke bar is a fun addition for any party.
Cool off during the day and relax at night in the climate controlled pool that over looks the lake.

 

Weddings and Showers
This is my daughters wedding that was held during late March. The perfect weather and beautiful scenery added to the already special occasion.
The ranch is still close enough to have excellent catering and all modern conveniences, but still allow for the uniqueness that is the South Texas brush country.
Bridal luncheons, batchelor parties, wedding showers, teachers meetings, family reunions are all possible at either the Double C Ranch or the Lone Star Lodge.
