Fabrication

At Jeff Hort Engineering we have a fully equipped fabrication shop with high head room serviced by a 10 tonne, 10 metre high overhead travelling crane.

All types of materials and welding techniques are catered for. Equipment includes Oxy, stick, MIG and TIG welders, Oxy and plasma cutting, wire and spray hard facing.

Our expertise allows us to fabricate almost anything, including Vessels & Silos, Pipe & Fittings, and conveyors to name but a few.

Whether as a "supply and install" contract or "supply only" contract, we are in a position to provide overall supervision/co-ordination of all work associated with your project.
