Widespan Structural System

While the Widespan™ structural system is our most basic building system, it is also one of the most versatile. The proven practicality of a rigid frame is combined with almost unlimited design flexibility to meet your needs. Framing options for your Widespan building include LRF, MRF and WX framing systems.

The LRF framing system provides variable roof slopes of 1/4:12 to 4:12 for a contemporary exterior profile and large clearspan interiors for maximum use of interior space.

The MRF framing system, which is just as flexible as the LRF, is specially designed for wide buildings using a minimum of interior columns.

The WX framing system provides additional space for extensions or expansions. It accommodates all eave heights and roof slopes of the LRF and MRF systems. WX frames are available in widths up to 60'.

Single slope, double slope and offset ridge designs are available. You may also select tapered columns for maximum economy or straight columns for functionality.

A variety of roof and wall systems are also available, allowing you to find the perfect fit for your new building and your budget.

Landmark 200 Structural System

The Landmark™ 2000 structural system employs a special truss purlin (an open web roof secondary structural member) that is exclusive to Butler. This offers you the benefits of systems construction along with the longest bay sizes in the industry.

The system employs solid web primary frames and pre-punched truss purlins to form a very attractive and economical long bay framing system. The entire system was designed with flexibility in mind. Bay sizes up to 60' may be specified in 6'' increments. Eave heights are available in 1/8'' increments.

Single slope, double slope and offset ridge designs are available with a roof slope of 1/4:12 to 5/8:12. You may also select tapered columns for maximum economy or straight columns for functionality.

Multi-Story Structural System

The Multi-Story structural system is an extension of Butler’s pre-engineered systems. It offers the benefits of our low-rise building expertise and the single-source advantages of a Butler® Multi-Story structure.

Butler offers a variety of roof and wall options with this proven structural system. Your Multi-Story building comes with your choice of any Butler standing-seam roof system. The exterior wall treatment may include brick, masonry, block, curtain wall or metal wall panel options.

The Vistawall Architectural Products by Butler are also available for all your storefront, entrance, window, curtain wall and skylight needs. This single source supplier provides value, convenience and architectural enhancement to your Multi-Story building.

Skyline Structural System

Now, there is a simple solution to complex hip, valley and dormer framing-the Skyline(TM)structural system. Butler® engineered and factory assembled light gauge steel trusses allow unlimited truss configurations and design flexibility. The first one-hour, one-layer, UL fire-rated assembly in the industry, the Skyline structural system is a cost-effective choice. In addition, it is an excellent alternative in certain re-roofing and fascia applications.


Hardwall Structural Systems

Concrete wall construction and the world's finest standing seam roof system can be easily combined by using this unique hardwall structural system. The Delta Joist(TM) system provides diaphragm bracing and is designed for load bearing or non-load bearing masonry or concrete wall projects.

Pre-punched joists are assembled on the ground to form completed roof structural sections which are then raised into position and bolted to the pre-punched building frames. Prepunching is the key element in assuring alignment of the structural system and performance of the standing seam roof system. Joists are assembled with a specially designed Scrubolt(TM) fastener which installs quickly and is more reliable than field welding.

The Delta Joist structural system provides an economical alternative to bar joist construction by combining the cost efficient use of materials with time and labor savings. The Delta Hybrid(TM) structural system is also available to provide even greater structural economies. Plus, you get all the benefits of the MR-24® standing seam roof system - a proven system which is performing successfully on two billion square feet of roof area.
