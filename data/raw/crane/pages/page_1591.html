LATTICE BOOM CRANES
The all new Link-Belt Lattice Boom Crawler lift cranes feature many improvements, yet retain the basic design that made these the standard of the industry with a reputation for dependability and versatility. From 50 to 250 tons, all lattice Boom Crawlers have a sealed for life track system and an all welded, precision machined lower frame that provides optimum stability for lift crane applications.
