Jean Crane, M.D.

Board Certified Family Practice

Dr. Crane loves the "biographies" of her patients.

"I love to hear their life stories. For me, medical practice is listening to someone’s story and using my medical training to come up with a plan to solve a problems and  maintain the good health."

Dr. Crane is married with four stepchildren and five grandchildren.  She enjoys digital photography, gardening, quilting, and exercising with their three Labrador Retrievers.
