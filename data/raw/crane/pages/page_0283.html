Storms at Gulf Oil Rigs Hit Pump Prices

by Christopher Joyce 

 
Minerals Management Service

A crane lowers part of an oil plaform at an offshore rig.

 
 

All Things Considered, October 12, 2004 · Hurricanes contributed to a 66 percent rise in oil prices this year. Hurricane Ivan damaged or destroyed many oil platforms and pipelines in the Gulf of Mexico. That's helped make oil scarce, and costly. NPR's Christopher Joyce reports.
