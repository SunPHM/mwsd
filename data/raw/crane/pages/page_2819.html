Directions to Saint James G.C.

Leave South Hutchinson Island at the north end of the island and make a left onto US1 South and travel 5 1/2 miles south to the stoplight at Midway Road (aka Route 712). Make a right turn onto Midway and travel west about 1 1/2 miles. At the 2nd or 3rd stoplight you'll see South 25th Street. Make a left onto S 25th street. The name of this road quickly changes to St. James Boulevard, but just follow it for no more than 2 miles and the entrance to St. James G.C. will be on your right. (772)336-GOLF (About 25 minutes from Ocean Village.)

An alternate route is if you are coming up or down I-95 get off at the Midway Road Exit #126 and travel east on Midway Road (aka Route 712) for about 3 1/2 miles. When you come to the stoplight for South 25th Street make a right. The name of this road quickly changes to St. James Boulevard, but just follow it for no more than 2 miles and the entrance to St. James G.C. will be on your right.
