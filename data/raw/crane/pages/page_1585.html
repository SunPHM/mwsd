Mining & Steel Industry

For many years, Lakehead Constructors has provided major plant reconstruction and rebuilding, along with key construction projects to improve the effectiveness of taconite mining. In recent years, Lakehead has completed projects at each of the region's taconite plants, which are owned by the nation's top steel producers.

  • Our services and projects Include:
    General construction services
  • Grate/kiln/cooler systems repairs and maintenance
  • Grate/kiln/cooler systems refractory and brick linings
  • Concentrator line rebuilds
  • Structural steel modifications and repairs
  • Conveyor systems installations and repairs
  • Ore storage bins rail lining replacements
  • Coarse crusher conversions
  • Limestone unloading facility
  • Mobile equipment shop facility (for new large equipment)
  • Water intake structure modifications
  • Alignment services - grates, kilns, conveyors
  • Continuous grate system repairs and maintenance
  • Continuous grate system refractory and brick linings
  • Equipment installations
  • Heavy rigging services
  • Asbestos abatement
  • Precast tunnel construction
  • Overhead crane installation
  • Stack installation and repair
  • Emergency repairs

The services we have provided the mining industry have been consistently safe, cost effective and of the highest quality. Each year our customers have new and more challenging projects. We look forward to meeting these needs and challenges in the future.
