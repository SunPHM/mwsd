  Tower-crane forecasts

Site-specific wind and weather forecasts
 
For a site of your choice, our tower-crane forecast service provides you with mean and gust wind speeds (m.p.h., at required heights) wind direction and weather for the next five days. Delivered via fax or e-mail, on a one-off basis or routine service, the forecast is easy to understand and is a great help in saving you time and money when planning the allocation of your resources.

For more details on this service, to request a quote, or place an order please send an e-mail to construction@metoffice.gov.uk or call our 24-hour Customer Centre, stating your interest in tower-crane forecasts.
