Slewing rings are depending on purposes widely used for construction machines and loading and unleading machines e.g. all sorts of cranes, excavators and etc in their rotatry parts. Contrary to the widespread bearings or the bearings with conventional rotary system the slewing rings of Shin il Precision have an exceptional feature: a simple structure that can take up with one bearing axial force and moment at the same time. Thus the maintenance and inspection is easier.

Due to extension of standard products we prepared new technical data for the range of slewing rings of Shin il Precision. Please take advantage of our data. Moreover Shin il Precision has a great deal of experience in designing and manufacturing large-sized slewing rings with special forms, which are not listed in this leaflet. By request we are at your disposal. please contact us and we will make a selection of appropriate slewing rings for your purpose.
