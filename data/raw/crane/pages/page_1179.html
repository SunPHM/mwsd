 July 8, 2002: It's been a long time since our last update--and sadly, not much has changed. We've noticed no new activity inside the area (now hyped as "Area 51"), but either its our imagination, or there really are new dirt piles and machinery sprouting up on Millennium Island (visible while riding Iron Dragon). Also, since the last update a new sign has gone up, similar to what was done before the announcement of Tomb Raider: The Ride at Paramount's Kings Island. Here is a photo:

Another quick update...as of Monday, May 20, 2002, we have not seen any changes at the former Dodgem site at all...As always, in the event something happens, we'll keep you informed.

New update...as of Friday, May 10th nothing really has happened at the site. Here are a few new photos, nothing really that important. Also...there seems to be some digging going on in Millennium Island...there are large dirt piles, really nothing to see, and they could be for something completely different. Anyway, here are a few of the photos:

There is some mysterious construction going on in the area around the former site of Dodgem I. A large fence has gone up around the "site" and the climbing wall located almost adjacent to the present construction site has been moved next to Witches' Wheel. It is interesting to us that there are already footers going in...Have a theory? Let us know in the forums or by email.
