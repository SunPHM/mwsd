Origami by Tiana

Origami means folding paper. You can fold this paper into many shapes. Some of the shapes are birds called cranes, house, man, and a flower. You can make anything you want. If you make a thousand cranes, you can make a wish or give them to people who are sick. You can give them to a team for good luck.
