At WFSI we apply our engineering and manufacturing expertise in supplying our customers with specialized structural fabrications.

We are capable of manufacturing complex assemblies, particularly in special applications where architectural and structural requirements are blended to form unique combinations of structural shapes and material.

Our welders are qualified to AWS D1.1 and have experience in working with a variety of materials including stainless steel, aluminum, and high strength low alloy steels. Our experience in power generation, pulp and paper, and general fabricating has given us the knowledge and experience for fabricating process equipment as well.

With 15 ton crane capacity and two 100 foot bays, we are capable of various sized projects, and our two shift operation lends itself to short and compressed cycle time projects.

Our quality system ensures the work coming out of the shop meets our customers high standards for precision, excellence and competitive pricing.

WFSI supplies a superior structural product with the efficiencies needed by our customers for the successful completion of their project.
