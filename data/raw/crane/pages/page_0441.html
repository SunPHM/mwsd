ENHC'S Top Picks

Saturday, August 7, 8:00 am to 4:00 pm

Crane Beach Sandblast!

Description: The Trustees of Reservation invites you to the annual Crane Beach Sand Sculpture Competition! This year's theme is Under the Sea. Join as a Master Builder, Family, Adult, Teen, Kids or Corporate/Business team. Let your imagination soar with Starfish, Mermaids, Seahorses or even Neptune himself! Registration includes parking for one car. Spectators are welcome at regular beach admission.

Location: Crane Beach
Argilla Road, Ipswich, MA

Price: Advance registration recommended - Member team, $10, Nonmember team $20. Day of registration - member team, $15, Nonmember team $25.

For more information: 978-356-4351or E-mail: castlehill@ttor.org

Sunday, August 15, 3:00pm

Shakespeare in the Barn: Richard III

Description: New England Shakespeare Festival returns to the Barn at Castle Hill for two performances of Richard III this year. Nothing, not even family ties keeps Richard from taking the throne that he believes to be rightfully his. Seizing power is one thing, holding onto it is another, and he becomes more ruthless in the face of challenges to his rule! Patrons are encouraged to arrive early and picnic in the tower gardens across from the barn area.

Location: Castle Hill
The Crane Estate, Argilla Road
Ipswich, MA

Price: Members: $12. Nonmembers: $16

For more information: 978-356-4351 or E-mail: castlehill@ttor.org

Noises Off! by Michael Frayn

Labor Day, September 6, 12:00pm to 6:00pm

20th Annual Bread and Roses Festival!!

Description: Don't miss the 20th edition of Lawrence's free, fun, family-oriented Labor Day festival celebrating our unique heritage. Musical performances culminate with headliner C.J. Chenier & the Red Hot Louisiana Band, the top act in Zydeco, the irresistible Cajun R&B musical gumbo. Also featuring The Mammals, performing bluegrass & folk for the 21st Century.
Experience the history of the mills and the Bread and Roses Strike with reenactments and demonstrations, plus free trolley and walking tours. Enjoy sampling our International Food Court. Visit craft makers and information booths. Take the kids to our children's area. And for the truly ambitious, start the day with our 5K Road Race.

For more information: www.breadandroses.net or call 978-794-1655, or email breadandroses99@hotmail.com
