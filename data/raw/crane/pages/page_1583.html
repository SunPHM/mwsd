Myshak Sales & Rentals was incorporated in February of 1996. The company has a staff of twenty-eight experienced fulltime employees with three part-time students. All of our staff are committed to providing a safe quality product with service to ensure reliable products for our clients.

The founders, Mike & Jim Myshak of Myshak Sales & Rentals Ltd. have over 65 years experience in all aspects of the business. The Sales and Rental department is managed by Jim Myshak and Corey Mitchell with combined experience of 38 years. The Warehouse department is managed by Terry Kochan with over 24 years of warehouse and procurement experience. Our Service department is Managed by Noel Reilly with 30 years in equipment repair & maintaintence.

We have a wide variety of specialized construction equipment available for rent including Mobile Cranes, Rough Terrain Cranes, Picker trucks, Fuel & Lube trucks, Water trucks, Site Tractors, Trailers, Wheel Loaders, etc. We provide Rig Mats & Crane matting and/or swamp matting specially built to suit our customer's needs. We can custom build different lengths and widths of mats for your requirements. We also manufacture and supply for both rental and sale Engineered designed and Certified Specialized Rigging ; Man & Material Baskets, Spreader Bars from 5 - 300 Ton and a variety of other specialized crane & rigging needs. We can offer the following services to our customers:
  • Dry monthly rental rates on all rental equipment.
  • Rental with Options to purchase and/or lease.
  • Hourly, daily, weekly and monthly rates with operators (FOM).
  • Maintenance and service can be provided on or off site at customer's request. We have a large repair facility located in the Acheson Industrial Park and mobile service in Fort McMurray.
  • Currently, we have warehousing and storage facilities available with rail access. We provide indoor and outdoor storage complete with material handling and categorization for all your requirements.
  • Myshak Sales & Rentals Ltd. offers Certified journeyman mobile crane operators, and Certified boom truck journeymen operators. Our shop consists of Certified journeymen Heavy Duty Mechanics, apprentice mechanics, Certified journeyman welder and two apprentices.

We feel that with the combined experience of all our employees we can offer the industry competitive rates, up to date equipment and technology, and excellent personal service.
