TAP Publishing Company


Rock & Dirt is owned and operated by TAP Publishing Company in Crossville, TN. The company began in 1937 with the launch of its flagship publication, Trade-A-Plane. In addition, TAP publishes Rock & Dirt en Español and Tradequip International. All publications have websites. They are www.rockanddirt.com, www.trade-a-plane.com, www.rdespanol.com, and www.tradequip.com.

The company's Commercial Printing Division produces magazines and catalogs for other clients. TAP's first Internet-only product, the WeatherTAP website, is at www.weathertap.com

Rock & Dirt

Rock & Dirt has been bringing buyers and sellers together in the heavy construction industry since 1950. Known as The Equipment Marketplace, Rock & Dirt offers the largest selection of heavy equipment for sale found anywhere. Construction contractors all over the globe rely on Rock & Dirt as their single best source for heavy equipment and related services. The color tabloid is published three times monthly and features the largest equipment auction section in the industry. The publication can be accessed on the Internet at www.rockanddirt.com

Rock & Dirt en Español

Published since 1992, Rock & Dirt en Español consists of classified and display advertising, (in Spanish) offering all types of new and used heavy equipment, related products and services. The color tabloid reaches Latin American construction and mining companies and heavy equipment users. Rock & Dirt en Español is published monthly, and is distributed in 22 Latin American countries plus Mexico and the U.S. For Latin America, Rock & Dirt en Español is The Equipment MarketPlace. The publication can be accessed on the Internet at www.rdespanol.com

Trade-A-Plane

Trade-A-Plane is the world’s largest general aviation resource. Pilots and aircraft owners around the world rely on Trade-A-Plane to bring buyer and seller together in the general aviation market. Since 1937, the color tabloid has been published three times each month. Trade-A-Plane offers an enormous classified section along with display advertising where aviators can find virtually any type of new or used aircraft and a host of aviation products and services. Distributed in over 100 countries worldwide, Trade-A-Plane holds the well earned title of "The Aviation MarketPlace." The paper can be accessed on the Internet at www.trade-a-plane.com

Tradequip International

Tradequip International is the world’s most comprehensive energy equipment resource. Published since 1978, Tradequip consists of classified and display advertising offering all types of new and used energy equipment, related products and services. From drill pipe to gas processing equipment, Tradequip covers all facets of the upstream petroleum industry. The color tabloid is published three times monthly, and is distributed in the U.S. and over 100 other countries. Tradequip International is The Energy Equipment MarketPlace. The paper can be accessed on the Internet at www.tradequip.com

WeatherTAP

WeatherTAP.com is an online subscription weather service with over 6,000 graphic products covering the continental United States. The "fastest weather on the web" has been available since 1998 at www.weathertap.com. Its major components are Local Weather, NEXRAD Doppler Radar, Satellite, Lightning and Aviation Weather. Free, sample images are accessible. With high-resolution East and West satellites, the site features the exact images and data provided to the Federal Aviation Administration, with no delays. The closest thing to real-time weather on the Internet, weatherTAP delivers the quickest, most current radar and weather available.
