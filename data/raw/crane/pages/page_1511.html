Our fully equipped floating cranes, with a lifting capacity ranging from 30 to 300 tons, offer varied solutions.

They may be used to complete high standard harbor projects or to carry out loading, unloading, transhipping assignments, heavy load transportation and lifting or replacing anchors.
To ensure immediate intervention, wherever that may be needed (marine accidents, vessels, groundings etc.), all our vessels are available on a 24-hour basis.
