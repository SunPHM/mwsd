The new plant facility in Plaridel, Bulacan

          The new Plaridel plant facility became operational last year producing foam with superior cell structure and plush feel, and improved comfort and support factors. The imprved foam quality is mainly due to the state-of-the-art Multiflex Foaming Machine made by Hennecke of Germany, the global market leader in foaming machines. Among other factors, the development of the Multiflex Foaming Machine led to Hennecke being awarded as one of the "Top 100 medium-sized Innovative companies in Germany" of the year 2000. With the new machine, the RGC Foam Group will also be able to expand its manufacturing programme to include highly technical foam grades conforming to the
