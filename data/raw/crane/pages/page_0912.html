About Us

Deep South Crane Rentals, Inc. has been serving the entire Gulf Coast and Southeastern region with quality crane rentals since 1979.  Our fleet of hydraulic cranes is available with fully qualified operators.   We also offer A/T - R/T pickers with or without operators.  All operators are qualified in accordance with ANSI B30.5 and our crane fleet is inspected and certified in accordance with OSHA 1926 and ANSI B30.5.

Our fleet ranges from 14 and 17 ton boom trucks to 300 ton all terrain hydraulic crane with superlift and luffing jib.  With a fleet of 45 hydraulic cranes we are ready to provide you with the proper crane for your job.

Our complete services include qualified riggers along with trucks and trailers for your heavy hauling needs.

Our in-house safety department and Risk Manager assure that all of our employees are properly trained to complete your job in a safe and timely manner.  They all have completed the OSHA 10 hour construction safety & health course.  In addition, our Risk Manager is an OSHA authorized trainer to conduct the 10 hour and the 30 hour safety course.  Safety and OSHA compliance are our way of doing business. 

We do not meet the standards...  we set the example for others to follow!

We demonstrate this by investing in new, safe equipment and training our employees in safety in order to give you the best and safest job.
