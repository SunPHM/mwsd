BEFORE UMASS: Transferred to UMass from Furman... A three-year letterwinner in basketball (forward) and a two-year letterwinner in baseball at Woodstock Union H.S... Baseball team captain his senior year... Named team MVP in 1996 and '97... American Legion squad was Southern Vermont Champions in 1996 and '97... Named to American Legion All-Star Team in 1997 and All-Tournament Team in 1995, '96 and '97...

PERSONAL: Enrolled in the College of Arts and Sciences... Born on March 2, 1979 in Hanover, N.H... Son of Jonathan and Susan Crane.
